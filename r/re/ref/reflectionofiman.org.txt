Privacy Statement
_We at Reflections of Iman.com respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. You can visit our website without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information unless you decide to buy.
We are the sole owner of the information collected on our site. All of the information given to us will be confidential and will not be share with any additional parties for any reason.  So under no circumstances will we ever share, sell, rent, or loan personal information about our customers to third parties for e-mail marketing or for any other reason.
_
_We only need the required information to process your order, respond to your email /chat request, or return a phone call per customers request.  _
_Reflections of Iman may use the information we obtain to provide you with information on our products and services and any ongoing promotions through email and newsletters. We will not send information by mail/email if you request us not to do so.
The type of information we will collect may range from:
_
your name and/or business name
address
telephone
email address
other information required in order for us to process your order
records of transactions with you
records of communication with you
Your Credit Card Information:
We are paypal verfied, and for the security of our customers, Reflections of Iman processes all online payments through Paypal. Paypal is well-known worldwide for processing credit card, Paypal, and E-check payments securely. For information on the privacy policies of Paypal, please visit ** PayPal.com** . By using PayPal to process all online payments, none of our staff have access to any of your financial information.
Links to Other Web Sites:
When you click on a link on this Web Site that takes you to a web site operated by another company, you will be subject to that web site’s privacy policies
If you have any questions or comments about our  privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
