RedLeafAds - Privacy policy
RedLeafAds has established its privacy policy to explain how your information within the purview of RedLeafAds is protected, collected and used. This policy may be updated by RedLeafAds. RedLeafAds will post notices of its policy changes on the RedLeafAds site at: https://www.redleafads.com/help-privacy.html
PRIVACY PROTECTION
RedLeafAds does not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes, or engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs with other similar sites. RedLeafAds recommends its customers to keep their password safe and do not remember it on public (shared) computers (Internet-cafe, college, library, family computer etc). RedLeafAds does not knowingly collect any information from persons under the age of 13. If RedLeafAds learns that a posting is by a person under the age of 13, RedLeafAds will remove that posting. RedLeafAds, or persons who post on RedLeafAds, may provide links to third party websites, with different privacy policies. RedLeafAds is not responsible for, nor have any control over, those third party websites.
INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE
RedLeafAds collect traditional contact information such as your name, mailing and/or billing address, phone number and email address for few purposes: to send selfpublishing and confirmation emails, to authenticate user accounts, to provide subscription email services, to register for forums, to process any credit card charges, etc.
RedLeafAds does not store full credit card information but RedLeafAds may store the information partially, such as only last 4 digits of your credit card number for transactions authenticating, monitoring and fraud prevention purpose. All credit card transactions initiated by RedLeafAds are transmitted through a financial gateway using encrypted Secure Socket Layer (SSL 128 Bit) protocol.
Your personal information may be collected by RedLeafAds if you provide it in the feedback or comments, post it on our classifieds, forums, guestbook or other publishing areas of the website.
Should you expect your personal information to be private please do not post any personal information on RedLeafAds site. Standard web log entries for each page served, including visitors IP address, page URL, and timestamp are collected by RedLeafAds web logs in order to help diagnose problem with RedLeafAds servers, to administer RedLeafAds site, and to otherwise enhance visitors use of RedLeafAds sites and services.
DATA STORAGE
RedLeafAds stores in its database all user generated content (classified ads, forums, guestbook postings etc.) even after they are deleted, as well as can archive such data elsewhere. RedLeafAds indefinitely stores web logs and other records. Registered members can access and update personal account information through the account homepage.
DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
RedLeafAds may disclose information about its users if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process. RedLeafAds may also disclose information about its users to law enforcement officers or others, in the good faith belief that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to: enforce our Terms of Use; respond to claims that any posting or other content violates the rights of third-party; or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of RedLeafAds, its users or the general public. RedLeafAds complies with the provisions of C.R.S. § 6-1-716 pertinent to the breaches of security notifications effective September 1st, 2006.
GLOBAL USE
By providing us with data and by visiting RedLeafAds you acknowledge and agree that due to the global use of RedLeafAds we may use the data collected in the course of your use of our site and/or services for the purposes identified in this policy or in our other communications to you, including the transmission of information outside your resident jurisdiction. Data provided to RedLeafAds may be stored on servers throughout different jurisdictions. User agrees to consent to transfer of such data to different locations.
IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH RedLeafAds PRIVACY POLICY PLEASE LEAVE THIS WEBSITE
