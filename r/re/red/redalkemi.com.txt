Privacy Policy - RedAlkemi
This site is owned by RedAlkemi Online Private Limited. This Privacy Policy is designed to tell you about our practices regarding collection, use, and disclosure of information that you may provide via this site. Please be sure to read this entire Privacy Policy before using or submitting information to this site.
Consent
By using this site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Whenever you submit information via this site, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Information Collected and subscriptions
In general, you can visit this Website without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself.
RedAlkemi may, if you so choose send subscriptions to you at the address given by you. You have the option to 'opt-out' of this subscription by way of links provided on Website. We respect your privacy and in the event that you choose to not receive such subscriptions, we will take all steps to remove you from the list.
Use and Disclosure
Except as otherwise stated, we may use information collected via this site to improve the content of our site, to customize the site to your preferences, to communicate information to you (if you have requested it), and for any other purpose specified.
It is to clarify that RedAlkemi Online Private Limited never issues communication (E-mail, SMS or call) asking for bank account details from any consumer. RedAlkemi Online Private Limited appeals to members of public not to respond to such mails, SMS or calls and not to share their bank account details with anyone claiming to be from RedAlkemi.
Changes
RedAlkemi Online Private Limited reserves every right to add, modify or delete portions of the privacy policy at any time without prior notice. Please check back periodically, and especially before you provide any personally identifiable information. This privacy policy was last updated on March 05, 2018.
