Privacy Policy - Retirement Homes, Retirement Communities & Senior Housing - RetirementHomes.com
RetirementHomes.com is firmly committed to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for RetirementHomes.com.
This site contains third party links. RetirementHomes.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web Sites.
Our Home Request service asks visitors for contact information (such as their email address) and demographic information (such as age, gender). Demographic and profile data is also collected at our site upon registration. We use this data to tailor our visitors' experience at our site, tailoring our content to match their interests. We do share your name and email address with programs when you voluntarily request further information from the program. By submitting this information you are opting in to receive communication back from the program you have contacted and from RetirementHomes.com about retirement home related products and services.
Some properties on RetirementHomes.com use a third party to manage their inquiries. In the event that you have contacted a property who's inquiries are managed by a third party, your information will be forwarded accordingly.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. RetirementHomes.com uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, utilized by most popular browsers, including Netscape and Microsoft Explorer. This technology allows us to use the industry standard encryption tools necessary to protect information transmitted between your computer and our server.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor.
