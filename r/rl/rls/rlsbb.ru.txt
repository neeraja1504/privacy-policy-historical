ReleaseBB
ReleaseBB (aka www.RLSBB.ru), a World Wide Web site owned and operated by ReleaseBB ltd, recognizes its readers rights to privacy.
**Storing information  **
ReleaseBB doesn’t collect any information about its visitors. The only stored information is IP address of readers who submit a comment to particular article. These records are strictly confidential and will not be reproduced, sold or misused in any other way. Storing IP address is strictly for safety reasons, so we can determine eventual attacker or spammer.
This site does not sell, rent, lease or allow any third parties to view or use the information collected. ReleaseBB does not maintain any mailing lists and does not send unsolicited emails.
**Links  **
This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. When you leave our websites we encourage you to read the destination site’s privacy policy.
Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: rlsbb.ru (at) gmail.com
