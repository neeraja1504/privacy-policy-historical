Privacy policy | RSS MAD
By using RSS MAD you have read and agree to all of the terms and conditions of our Privacy Policy.
Any information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web site, used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor, and is NOT shared with other organizations for commercial purposes
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, and also the email of those users who register on our site. Your email and other personal information will NEVER be shared with other organisations.
We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record session information, and also record past activity on the site in order to provide a better service when visitors return.
Please note that we do not take any responsibility, nor can we be held responsible, for the content of the feeds located on this website, as they are provided by 3rd parties.
Please contact us at team@rssmad.com if you have any questions.
