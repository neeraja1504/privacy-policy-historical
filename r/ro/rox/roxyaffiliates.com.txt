Privacy Policy
PRIVACY
Roxy Affiliates highly values and respects the privacy of all our affiliates.  This document is designed to demonstrate our strong commitment to your privacy, and discloses how we gather and disseminate information from our website, and security procedures in place for protection of your information.
INFORMATION
When you register at our site, we request specific information from you, including contact information (your name, e-mail address, telephone number etc.).  We use your customer contact information from the registration form to send you information about our affiliate program.  No information will be sold, given or in any way transmitted to any person, corporation or other entity outside Roxy Affiliates and associated companies.
SECURITY
Roxy Affiliates has strict security measures implemented to protect against unauthorized use, loss and/or alteration of the information under our control.
