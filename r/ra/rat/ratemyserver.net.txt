RateMyServer.Net :: RO Database & Server Reviews
RateMyServer.Net knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, we appreciate your trust. We would like you to feel confident in our services and security as it relates to your personal information. The following information describe types of personal information we receive and collect when you visit RateMyServer.Net, and how we safeguard your information.
Log Files
As with most other websites, RateMyServer.Net collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocal) address, your ISP (internet service provider, such as AOL or Shaw Cable), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.
Cookies and Web Beacons
We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site. This could include only showing you the features you enabled from previous visit, or the ability to login to some of our member only system or forum.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit this page to opt out of the DART cookie used by Google Adsense or consult the third-party advertising companies for any possible options. You may also visit this page to learn more about how Google uses information from our site and how you can control the information collected. Another third party company that you can choose to opt out from can be found here. We are not responsible for the content of any third-party websites.
Your IP address, ISP, or any other information is NOT shared with our in-house server banner advertisers. In-house server banner advertisers are those that bought advertisement banner directly on RateMyServer.Net without going through a third party agency.
You can chose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs that you use to manage your internet security. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites. This could result in the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts. For more information on configuring your browser, please see AboutCookies.org or the documentation for your browser.
Contact Information
Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or other policy related material can be directed to our support staff by emailing us at: admin@ratemyserver.net
