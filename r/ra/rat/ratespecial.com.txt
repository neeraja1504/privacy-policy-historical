Privacy Policy - RateSpecial Interactive
About This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy describes information that we collect from you as part of the normal operation of our service and what may happen to the information we collect.
Application of this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy applies to RateSpecial and all websites ("Site" or "Sites") owned and controlled by RateSpecial,LLC. ("RateSpecial", "we", or "us"). Please note that this Privacy Policy applies only to Sites owned and controlled by RateSpecial and not to websites maintained by other companies to which we may link.
By registering with us (i.e. through the submission of the information requested, including your personally identifiable information) on this Site or otherwise using or accessing this Site, you agree that the following terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy shall govern our use and disclosure of your personal information. If you do not agree with the terms and conditions of our Privacy Policy, please do not complete any information request forms (i.e., do not register) hosted on this site or otherwise use or access this Site.
This Site is not directed at persons under the age of 18. Persons under the age of 18 must not send any personal information to Ratespecial under any circumstances, including but not limited to name, address or email address.
Your Privacy is Important to Us
Our Privacy Policy explains what information we obtain on the Site and how we use the information you disclose to us, including how we may provide your information to our Third Party Providers who have expressed a willingness to provide you with the products and services associated with this Site, as well as other products and/or services that we and they believe may be of interest to you.
By entering your contact information, you are authorizing us, or a Third Party Provider, to contact you with exciting new offers and information, via email and automated telephone messages, even if you are listed on a Do Not Call Registry. Users may opt out of the Companies disclosure to such third parties by following the instructions in the opt-out section of this privacy policy. We may also disclose nonpublic personal information about you as permitted by law.
