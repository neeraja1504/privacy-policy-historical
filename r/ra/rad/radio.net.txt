Personal data provided by the user
We collect, process and store your personal data when you access our Website. In order to enable effective use of the Website, radio.de collects data about your use of our Website in the form of so-cal ed “website log files” (hereinafter also referred to as “ Log Files ”). Each attempt to access our Website is therefore logged. The Log Files generated from this process contain the following data:
- Your IP address;
Data protection officer - Time, type and number of station requests;
- Your browser type and other similar information, where applicable.
The collection and processing of information stored in the Log Files wil only be used for anonymised analysis for statistical purposes, i.e. to analyse your use of our Website and ensure continuous improvement of our services. Such data are not attributable to you as an identified person. This data will not be merged with other data sources. In order to be able to make full use of the content provided on our Website, including in particular audio services, you may create a user profile with us. This gives you the opportunity to create a favourites list of stations and songs that you can retrieve in your user profile. This allows you to access your favourite stations quickly. We can also recommend other stations to you in accordance with your favourites list. You can amend or delete your personal data or favourites lists stated in your user profile at any time. When you create a user profile, we wil collect and process the following personal data as the minimum information required:
- Your email address;
- Your user name;
- Your password.
Once you have created a user profile, you wil have the option to add additional personal data to your profile on a voluntary basis.
- Your name;
- Your place of residence;
- Your gender;
- Your profile picture.
For certain functions on our Website, it is necessary to collect location data regarding specific use of the Website in order to be able to offer you functions and services on the Website tailored to your location (such as to choose local stations, etc.). Our app therefore uses your IP address in order to determine your rough location. No conclusions can be drawn at any point about your specific location. radio.de will only use the collected location data in order to provide its services. In order to provide certain functions of the Website, radio.de must therefore process your IP address in order to be able to offer you these functions when you use the Website. Your location will only be sent to us if, when using the website, you make use of the various functions that radio.de is able to offer you once we know your location. This so-cal ed “geolocalisation”, i.e. the assignment of the opening of the Website to the location it is opened, is therefore performed using the IP address as the basis. This data wil not be merged with other data sources.
When you use the content offered on our Website, including in particular audio services, we wil collect and process the following personal data: - Your user profile, if created, consisting of the minimum information required and any potential additional data;
- Log Files;
- Your location.

Cookies
radio.de uses so-called cookies. Cookies are small text files which are stored by your browser on your computer. As a result, the collection of cookies makes it easier for you to use our services and al ows us to better understand your user behaviour and be able to offer you suitable content and tailored services in line with your requirements.
radio.de uses so-called technically necessary cookies on the one hand. Some elements of radio.de require the browser you are using to be identified even after switching pages. The purpose of using technical y necessary cookies is to make our Website easier for users like you to use. Some functions of our Website cannot be offered without the use of cookies. These functions require the browser to be recognised again after switching pages. The data stored by the cookies include, among others, your choice of stations. As part of the various services offered, these cookies allow us to receive your personal settings over a longer period of time. radio.de also uses cookies which enable analysis of users’ browsing habits. This relates in particular to the utilisation of different functions on our Website and the frequency pages are accessed. These cookies are used for the purpose of continuously improving the quality of content provided by radio.de and aligning this content to the user behaviour. The cookies tell us how the Website is used, enabling us to constantly optimise our offering. The data collected in this way are pseudonymised by technological means. Therefore, assignment of the data to the requesting user is no longer possible. The data are not stored together with users’ other personal data. The legal basis for collecting and processing the data collected and processed using the cookies is Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR. Al cookies are stored on your computer and then sent from your browser to radio.de. You, as the user, therefore have ful control over the use of cookies. You can disable or restrict the transmission of cookies by adjusting the settings in your web browser. Cookies which have already been saved can be deleted at any time. This may also be done automatically. However, radio.de wishes to point out that if you do so it is also possible that certain content on the Website cannot be used or cannot be used in full.
