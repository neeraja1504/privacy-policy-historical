RuneHead - RuneScape clan memberlists, news, highscores, and forums
RuneHead is run by the people in the RuneHead Crew . Your privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. We want to make your experience online satisfying and safe. Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy surrounding the collection and use of that information.
Collected Data
Certain features require registration and this is a one time only registration for the whole range of RuneHead services. Data collected from this process is used only for the running of said services and will not be given to any third parties ever.
Cookies
We may place text files called a "cookies" in the browser files of your computer. The cookie does not contain Personal Information although it will enable us to gather anonymous information. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites.
You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use this site. However, you do need cookies to participate actively in some interactive features.
Usage Logs
RuneHead server logs record information about visitors, including IP addresses, date and time visited, referring website, length of stay, etc. This information is used for site statistics only and is not correlated to individual user information. It is absolutely anonymous.
Forums
We provide the use of our Forum for support and general discussion. Please remember that any information that you post on these forums is at your own risk and you should keep this in mind at all times. Especially in regards to personal information.
Advertisers
Most advertisements on RuneHead are provided by outside advertising companies and may contain cookies. These cookies are collected by their respective companies and are not accessible by us.
Links to external websites
RuneHead may link to other websites on the internet. Content and Privacy Policies are the responsibility of their respective owners.
If you have any further questions then feel free to contact us via our Support Forum.
