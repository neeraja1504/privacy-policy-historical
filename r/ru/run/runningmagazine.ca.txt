Privacy Policy - Canadian Running Magazine
June 3, 2013
Canadian Running Magazine is published by Gripped Magazine Inc.
(Gripped or Gripped Publications). Gripped does not share subscription information with third parties.
All information collected for single issue sales and subscriptions within the Canadian Running App is held by Gripped and/or our digital publication partners and is not shared with any third parties.
Any data collected and/or stored on the infrastructure of our digital publication partners will not be shared with anyone other than Gripped Publications. Nor will it be shared, transferred, or sold to any third party. The data collected for Gripped Publications, in any of the above mentioned scenarios, is for the use of Gripped Publications only.
Only information necessary to provide single issue purchases and one-year digital subscriptions are collected.
Subscribers who choose not to renew their digital subscriptions will have their information deleted after 6 months.
Cookies
