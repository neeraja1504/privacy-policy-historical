Privacy Policy
Last revised on 22 July 2015
Gamma Entertainment Inc. is the service provider of 21members and shall be known as "We", "Us" and "Our". We understand that the privacy of 21members Users is very important. During the navigation of the website and the registration process Users may provide personal identifiable information about themselves, therefore We have developed this policy to protect User privacy while they enjoy 21members.
Only adults who are at least 18 years old (21 years old in some jurisdictions) may navigate, purchase a membership or use 21members. All other persons under this age must leave the site immediately.
What is collected
By navigating the site, signing up or communicating with the Our Customer Service department We collect personal information provided such as name, email, zip and Approved Facility (payment) details. We may also send you a cookie which collects some information about your use of 21members. When Users visit and surf the site, anonymous demographic information may also be collected such as browser information and IP address. If Users choose to post information, messages or comments in public areas on 21members then any information about the User will be kept on 21members. Lastly, if Users send personal correspondence via email, ticket or chat such information will be maintained as a record of correspondence.
The collection of this information allows us to process your requests, provide 21members in the best format for your computer or device and provide targeted information.
Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information generated by web servers and stored on the computer or device used to access and surf websites for future access. 21members uses cookies which are essential to the operation of the site in order for Users to view the content. Other cookies that may be used include analytical cookies which monitor the performance of 21members and help Us provide better products or identify areas that need maintenance. Tracking cookies are used in relation to those who help promote 21members. Functional cookies are helpful to record User preferences when returning to 21members; for example, they are used to remember the scenes or photos stored in a favorites' folder or the preferred language to use when navigating 21members. Finally, advertising cookies or third party cookies may be used to provide specific advertising to Users which We believe are relevant to User interests. Please note that We cannot control cookies placed on your computer or device by third party advertisers.
How to control cookies
Users are not obligated to accept cookies and browsers can be modified to refuse cookies. Users may also delete cookies. If all cookies are refused, many websites may not function properly, including Ours. It is possible to modify browser settings to only accept cookies with consent before each cookie is placed on the computer or device used. This provides more control over what is placed on the computer or device, however it may slow down the web experience.

Web Beacons
In order to effectively deliver email communications and perform website analysis We may use web beacons. Web beacons are electronic files that count the Users who access 21members or a specific page and can help Us identify if a cookie has been activated. Web beacons also allow Us to confirm that an email message was successfully delivered and read. It is not possible to decline web beacons however if by refusing cookies this can also prevent web beacons from being used.
Consent
Users should enjoy the full experience of 21members. By signing up with personal information or by the continued use and browsing of 21members Users are consenting to Our processing of personal information, communication with and analysis of the information provided by Users.
Security
We take reasonable precautions against external threats to maintain security of any information obtained from and about Users, and to protect against the loss, misuse and alterations of information under Our control. Our third party payment processors provide Users with a secure web page through which billing information is submitted when purchasing a membership.All personally identifiable information held by Us is stored in a secure location (behind a firewall with limited administrative access).
User login information must be protected by the User. We advise all Users NOT to share username or password details with anyone. It is the User's responsibility to keep password information confidential. A User's account may be deactivated for abuse if We assess that a User's login details have been used by someone other than the registered User to 21members.
How User information is used
We use personal information to process payments for Users, communicate receipts, troubleshoot problems, resolve disputes, enforce any agreements or policies, and to facilitate viewing of 21members.We may use the information collected to improve marketing, analyse site usage, improve content offerings, customize site layout and provide information about 21members to its Users.
We are constantly striving to deliver the best to Users and We may use the information collected to deliver information targeted to User interests. Such information may be shared or licensed to Our partners, affiliated or unaffiliated third party web service providers who display banners or links on 21members. We encourage Users to read their policies, terms and conditions as they are not the same as Ours.
We may use the Users and former Users' email addresses to communicate new services, subscription information and special offers from Our partners, third parties or 21members. When Users and former Users are presented with special offers from Us, our partners or third parties they may include consent to receive email solicitations, communications, newsletters, commercial advertising and other promotional or special event materials. All emails from Us include simple instructions on how to unsubscribe and this may be done at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email, by visiting http://www.td3x.com/ or by contacting Our Customer Service. Users may NOT opt out of receiving emails regarding account details such as responses to questions or membership purchase receipts.
Sharing information with third parties
We may disclose personal information collected if required by law, court order or other governmental authority or when We believe in good faith that disclosing this information is otherwise necessary or advisable, including to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interfering with the rights or property of 21members or Us.
In the event of a change of ownership (merger, acquisition, sale of assets) We reserve the right to transfer all User information collected in connection with 21members to such separate entity.
Legal
Personal information collected on and/or through 21members is stored and processed in Canada. By using 21members Users consent to any transfer of such information outside the country in which they reside or access 21members and to be bound by the laws of Canada.
This policy does not apply to any linked websites to 21members. Please consult the privacy policy, terms and conditions of the website to which redirection may occur if clicking on any banner or advertisement.
By using/navigating 21members Users acknowledge they have read, understood and agree to be bound by this privacy policy and the terms and conditions of 21members. Headings are for ease of reference only.
