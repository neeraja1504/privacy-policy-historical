JB VIDEO PRIVACY AND SECURITY INFORMATION
JB VIDEO PRIVACY, SECURITY, AND CONTACT INFORMATION Our policy regarding sharing of information:
Our policy is simple: we never sell or rent your personal information to any third parties under any circumstances. How we protect the security of your personal information:
The security of your personal information is very important to us and we are committed to protecting the information we collect. JBvideo.com uses firewalls and state-of-the-art encryption to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information that you have voluntarily given our company. How to update or correct your personal information:
To modify or delete your personal information, simply send an e-mail stating your request, and we will make the requested changes. You may also call us, fax us, or contact us via mail.
