Privacy | John Lewis Finance
You don’t have to provide any information to us to use this website, however, certain sections of the site or services within the site will not be accessible if you choose not to share any information with us.
We collect information about your internet connection and website use, e.g. the web page you came from, IP address, domain type, browser, the country and telephone code where your computer is located, the web pages viewed during your visit and the advertisements you clicked on. If you take part in any promotions or competitions, give us feedback or complete any profile forms, we may collect additional information.
When you use our website to apply for a quote, we will collect the data that you have provided.
How we use your personal data
We use your information to provide and personalise the products and services that we offer. We may use and/or analyse your information to offer you discounts that reward your loyalty.
We may also contact you by phone or email to discuss a quote and whether there is anything we can help you with.
With your permission we may contact you by post, email or SMS to let you know about new products, services and offers provided by us or our third party suppliers.
How can you stop the use of your personal data for direct marketing?
There are several ways you can stop direct marketing communications from us. Please note that you may continue to receive communications for a short period after changing your preferences while our systems are fully updated.
If you have an online account, sign in into your John Lewis or Waitrose account, visit the ‘My Account’ area and change your preferences.
In our apps, you can manage your preferences and opt out from one or all of the different push notifications by selecting or deselecting the relevant options in the ‘Settings’ section.
To stop emails, click the ‘unsubscribe’ link in any email communication that we send you. We will then stop any further emails from that particular division.
