Privacy | Joomlatools
Joomlatools guarantees that your personal information will never be shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Joomlatools makes sure your information is sufficiently secured and respects Belgian privacy laws.
You have the right to view, modify or remove your personal information. Please contact us if you'd like to make modifications to your information.
Joomlatools reserves the right to track usage of the joomlatools.eu site and subdomains, its services, software and content through means such as cookies and access logs. No personally identifiable information will be recorded.
