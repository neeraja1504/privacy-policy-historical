Privacy Policy
Jamestown Distributors
17 Peckham Drive
Bristol, RI 02809
info@jamestowndistributors.com
(800) 497.0010
We appreciate the trust you place in Jamestown Distributors. We are committed to respecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your personal information. We always welcome your questions and feedback.
Welcome to our community.
We maintain the information that you provide to us, along with a record of your purchases, in a secure database. We collect only the email addresses of those who communicate with us via email and collective information on which pages consumers access or visit and in what order they are requested. The information we collect is used only to improve the content of our website. Jamestown Distributors does not share, sell or trade email addresses.
Information volunteered by consumers, such as catalog request information, is shared from time to time with a few responsible companies. You can have your name put on our do-not-share list by indicating so in your catalog request or by contacting us.
Consumers who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding their orders, but not for any other reason. Jamestown Distributors does not share, sell or trade telephone numbers.
