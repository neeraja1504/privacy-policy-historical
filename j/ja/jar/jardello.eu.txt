What personally identifiable information is collected on this site?
Visitors may supply personally identifiable information (such as name, e-mail address) for various purposes on this site. These purposes include receive e-mail newsletters or simply asking a question about .::AS::.Jardello. .::AS::.Jardello does not collect personal information from you unless you are a member. This means that you can visit our site without telling us who you are or revealing any personally identifiable information about yourself.
Security
Personally identifiable information is stored on our Web server and is not publicly accessible. Further, personally identifiable information is only accessed by SurfJardello employees on a need to know basis. All .::AS::.Jardello employees must go through privacy training upon starting with .::AS::.Jardello.
E-mail
All e-mail lists maintained by .::AS::.Jardello contain the names of people who have requested to be a member of SurfJardello. The e-mail address collected by .::AS::.Jardello will never be rented to outside firms for any purposes.
What anonymous information is collected on this site? Anonymous information is collected for every visitor to this site. This includes login count, date and time, IP numbers and browser type.

How does this site use cookies? .::AS::.Jardello uses cookies to identify you when you log in. This cookies contains only the entered login information (e-mail address and password).

Links
