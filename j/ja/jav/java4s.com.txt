Privacy Policy - Java4s.com
Privacy Policy
Takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.
Posting
We are sharing lot of things by searching various sites and different sources regarding java technologies. If you feel any of our posts in this site are opposite to the others privacy we will delete those particular posts from our records immediately once we have responded. But please tell us with your valid identity, please feel free to contact regarding this matter.
Comments
We are working really hard in order to maintain quality of this site. Actually this will help us serve you better…
We love comments, we do provide email follows-ups of the conversations by default so you can get all answers/follow-ups directly into your inbox to be in touch with your query
We won’t remove any links from comments, provided they are relevant to the current conversation
We love freedom of speech, You have all rights to express your intention but do it politely.  Strictly abuse will not be tolerated
All duplicate comments will be removed, like asking question which is already being answered
We have right to edit your comments, to avoid spell checks & broken links
Routine Information Collection
All web servers track basic information about their visitors. This information includes, but is not limited to, IP addresses, browser details, timestamps and referring pages. None of this information can personally identify specific visitors to this site. The information is tracked for routine administration and maintenance purposes.
Cookies and Web Beacons
Where necessary, uses cookies to store information about a visitor’s preferences and history in order to better serve the visitor and/or present the visitor with customized content.  Advertising partners and other third parties may also use cookies, scripts and/or web beacons to track visitors to our site in order to display advertisements and other useful information. Such tracking is done directly by the third parties through their own servers and is subject to their own privacy policies.
Controlling Your Privacy
Note that you can change your browser settings to disable cookies if you have privacy concerns. Disabling cookies for all sites is not recommended as it may interfere with your use of some sites. The best option is to disable or enable cookies on a per-site basis. Consult your browser documentation for instructions on how to block cookies and other tracking mechanisms.
Special Note About Google Advertising
Any advertisements served by Google, Inc., and affiliated companies may be controlled using cookies. These cookies allow Google to display ads based on your visits to this site and other sites that use Google advertising services. Learn how to opt out of Google’s cookie usage. As mentioned above, any tracking done by Google through cookies and other mechanisms is subject to Google’s own privacy policies.
Contact Information
Concerns or questions about this privacy policy can be directed to webmaster@java4s.com for further clarification
