Web Privacy Statement
Jefferson City Schools
The Jefferson City Schools web site exists to inform the community about and to engage
it in our joint mission to provide a quality education to all children. We use the site to
publish as much information about Jefferson City Schools as possible and to enhance
two-way communication between the district and community. Your privacy is important
to us. Please take a moment to become familiar with our privacy statement.
•  Management
This website is hosted and managed by Jefferson City Schools in Jefferson,
Georgia. The site is comprised of many smaller sites representing schools and
offices, many with their own website administrators. All but a few of these sites
are hosted by Jefferson City Schools. Those schools with their own hosting
arrangements must also conform to this privacy policy. Principals and office
administrators are responsible for content published to their sites.
•  Statistical Reports and Cookies
Like publishers of most Web sites, the Jefferson City School system may collect
data from visitors to our site. The purpose of this data collection is to analyze
how people navigate the site and then use that analysis to make changes that
improve the user experience. This information is not used to track individual
users or create user profiles. The data collection process does not enable
Jefferson City Schools to discern who individuals are, only that a computer with a
specific number (IP) is accessing the web server. Typical information gathers and
analyzed by our statistical software includes:
Ø The date and time of your visit
Ø Pages you visited
Ø The web address (URL) of the link you followed to reach the site
Ø The domain of your internet service, e.g. www.tds.net
Ø The type and version of your web browser, e.g. Internet Explorer 5.0
Ø The operating system of your computer, e.g. Windows XP
Ø The site also includes third-party applications that may rely on cookies to
enable users to save their information and recall it later. The applications
that use cookies will expire the cookies when you leave the site.
•  Gathering of Your Personal Information
Some schools and offices use interactive tools on their web sites to gather
comments and feedback from visitors. The most popular interactive tools include
email feedback forms, guestbooks, and surveys. Typical information visitors are
invited to submit to us includes:
Ø Name
Ø Mailing Address
Ø Email Address
Ø Local School
Ø Affiliation, e.g. parent, teacher, etc.

Some of these forms and surveys also include a blank space the visitor can use to
send us any type of comment or information. Do not use this field to convey
highly personal or sensitive information.
•  Use of Your Personal Information
Information submitted by users to a specific school or office website is managed
and archived by that office. If the occasion warrants, it may be shred with other
appropriate staff. The Jefferson City School system does not share visitor
information with or sell it to third parties.
•  Links to Other Websites
The Jefferson City School system website links to many web resources
considered educationally valuable. Each school’s webmaster also may choose to
link to external sites, but these sites must have an educational purpose. Principals
and office administrators are ultimately responsible for links published on their
sites.
•  Children
The Jefferson City Schools website does not directly solicit or collect information
from children. If a child wishes to share information via a form, we recommend
that he/she obtain parent permission and that the parent supervise the activity. If
the information is published to the site via a guestbook, we urge that no
personally identifiable information be shared.
•  Security
The Jefferson City Schools website does not solicit personal, highly confidential
information such as social security or credit card numbers and is not equipped to
guarantee highly secure transactions at this time.
•  Contact Information
As the site continues to grow and improve, Jefferson City Schools may update
this privacy statement. We will note the change on our home pate to alert you to
the modifications. Please contact the superintendent if you have comments or
questions.
