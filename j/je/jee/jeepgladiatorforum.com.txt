Privacy Policy for This Site
Cougar Gulch.com is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of visitors to our web site.
Cougar Gulch.com for the sake of this contract is defined as Cougar Gulch.com, affiliated sites, parent organization and sponsors.
FOR VISITORS: We have structured our web sites so that, in general, you can visit us on the web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information.
FOR CUSTOMERS: Once you choose to provide us personally identifiable information (any information by which you can be identified), you can be assured that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with Cougar Gulch.com. We will not share your email address with third parties for any reason.
What information do we collect?
When you visit our web site, our web server collects basic user information about you that includes your ISP's domain, the time you accessed our web site, and the pages from within our web site that you visit.
We may at times also use cookies, which are small pieces of information that a web site can store in a designated file on your computer.
Cookies allow a web application to respond to you as an individual. By gathering and remembering information about your preferences, the web application can tailor its operation to your needs, likes, and dislikes.
Our web server does not collect your e-mail address or aggregate information on what other pages on the Web you have accessed or visited.
How do we use this information?
The basic user information collected is used to help us analyze web traffic and to improve the content of our web site. Cookies may be used to facilitate your customer interaction with us. For example, we may use a cookie on the landing page of products sold online which record the customer information that is include_onced on the order form, and then forward this information to our internal sales tracking database.
Note that our site provides links to products and services offered by other companies; the company's web site that you link to will be able to determine that you linked from our web site.
Cougar Gulch.com subscribes to one or more affiliate programs. These programs have their own means for identifying you as you travel from our site to their affiliate merchant's site. This is important because that is how they know that we have earned our pay. If you do not carry the info with you, it is the same as stealing from our hard work and we are not able to offer you this great (and fun) service.
With each and every affiliate program, the merchants and affiliates have pledged in writing to a code of ethics and that code promises to protect your information across all channels.
