Privacy Policy | 855-345-0656 Privacy Information from Comcast
We respect and are committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy will provide information to you regarding how we collect and use your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. From time to time, we may revise this Privacy Policy to address new issues or changes to our Web sites or practices. These changes will be posted on this page so please check this Privacy Policy periodically to see if there have been any revisions.
The effective date of this Privacy Policy is November 1, 2009.
Scope of this policy
This Privacy Policy applies to JT's method of collection and use of information gathered.
This Privacy Policy also applies to your use of the following Web Site: www.jt.org
If you leave these sites via a link or otherwise, this Privacy Policy does not apply and JT cannot and is not responsible for how data and information obtained from you on other Web sites is used by the operators of such Web sites. Please be sure and check out those sites Privacy Policy to ensure you are aware of their practices.
JT may set and access our own cookies on your computer to allow you to buy and interact more effectively with our sites. We also use web beacons to gather anonymous, aggregated statistical information (e.g. unique hits, page views, etc.) about our website traffic. This information is used to optimize your online experience by simplifying the process is which you order or interact with our sites.
JT will not share, rent, lease, or sell your personal information (e.g. your email address, your credit card information, your name, your address, etc.) to any third party for their independent use or benefit.
To unsubscribe from our mailing lists, please click the link at the bottom of any email sent to you by JT. These links will give you complete instructions on how to remove yourself from the mailing lists.
