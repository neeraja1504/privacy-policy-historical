PRIVACY POLICY
1.1        Your private information required for registering an account on this website will be kept in the strictest confidence by Us and will not be sold or made known to third parties.
1.2        We may electronically collect, store and use your:
1.2.1    name and surname;
1.2.2    e-mail address;
1.2.3    birth date;
1.2.4    your city;
1.2.5    non-personal browsing habits and click patterns;
1.2.6    preferences regarding voucher offers;
1.2.7   IP address.
1.3      This information will only be used to do the following:
1.3.1   subject to your consent, to inform You of facts relating to your access and use of this website;
1.3.2   subject to your consent, to inform You about Offers;
1.3.3   to compile non-personal statistical information about browsing habits, click-patterns and access to this website subject to the conditions in clause 1.6 below;
1.3.4   to verify your identity when transacting with Us.
1.4      Information detailed above is collected either electronically by using cookies or is provided voluntarily by you. You may determine cookie use independently through your browser settings.
1.5      We may collect, maintain, save, compile and share any information collected from you, subject to the following conditions:
1.5.1   We will not disclose your personal information unless You give Us your permission thereto or unless required for the purposes of any legal process;
1.5.2   We may compile, use and share any information that does not relate to any specific individual;
1.5.3   We own and retain all rights to non-personal statistical information collected and compiled by Us;
1.6.4   We own and retain all rights to non-personal statistical information collected and compiled by Us online.
1.6      We cannot be held responsible for security breaches occurring on your electronic device (personal computer or other electronic device used to browse this website), which may result due to the lack of adequate virus protection software or spy-ware that you may inadvertently have installed on your device.
1.7     We will send you regular communications via email with the hottest deals on fun things to do, see and experience in your city. Subscription to this email is free. Every email will have an 'unsubscribe’ link to facilitate easy opt-out if you no longer want to receive the emails.
1.8   This policy forms part of the Terms and Conditions for use of this website and forms part of the contract between You and Us. We review this policy from time to time, to ensure that it reflects how we use your information. The policy which was effective on the date you used this website will be applicable so please ensure that you are familiar with any changes.
1.9  In the event of any material change to this policy, we will announce the change either on the home page of this website or by email.
