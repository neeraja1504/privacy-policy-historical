Privacy Policy
This site is owned and operated by DankStop. As an online business, ensuring the privacy of your information is a top priority for us. At DankStop, we want to make your online shopping experience both safe and satisfying. Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.
Information DankStop gathers about you:
1. Electronic newsletters policy
We offer a free electronic newsletter to our customers. DankStop gathers the email addresses of users who make purchases on our site. Users may remove themselves from this mailing list by following the link provided in every newsletter that points users to the subscription management page that offers a simple one click un-subscribe.
2. Personal information gathered through on-line purchases:
First and Last Name
Billing Address
Email Address
Credit Card Billing Information - We NEVER actually see your CC number, only the last 4 digits. Our system is 100% PCI compliant
All of your personal information is protected by high quality security settings that include secure socket link (SSL) connection for a safe checkout process, 226-bit encryption throughout the entire checkout process, and 100% PCI compliance.
**Under NO circumstances does DankStop rent, sell or trade any information about an individual user to a third party that is gathered throughout the checkout process.  **
