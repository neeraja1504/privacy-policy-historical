The Daily Script - Privacy Statement
The Daily Script respects your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Dana Franklin and The Daily Script will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. Check out the details below.
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:
The Daily Script does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists. However, notice the ZapZone logos and check out their privacy statement for information on the free email services offered through this web site.
Browser information collected on the website:
The Daily Script analyzes website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Use of cookies:
I do not use cookies on this site.
Privacy of email lists:
The Daily Script maintains an email list to keep subscribers informed of new material on the site. These lists are kept on file by webpire.net. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone.
Advertisers:
You'll notice banners at the top of every page. These banners are supplied by Advertising.com. Their privacy statement can be found here.
The Rundown:
The Daily Script tries to keep your privacy a priority!
