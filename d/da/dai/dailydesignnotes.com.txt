Privacy Policy - Daily Design Notes
The privacy of our visitors is one of our primary concerns as DailyDesignNotes.com. This privacy policy document underlines the types of personal information which is received and collected by DailyDesignNotes.com and how it is used.
Please don’t hesitate to contact us for any possible questions about our privacy and policy.
Log Files
DailyDesignNotes.com keeping log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) address, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/timestamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track usersí movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information can be linked to information that is personally identifiable (i.e. when you comment or contact us via one of our forms and leave your name/email).
Cookies
DailyDesignNotes.com uses third party cookies, such as the ones of Google Analytics and social media networks. Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web bugs on our web site DailyDesignNotes.com
Third-party ad servers
Third party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on DailyDesignNotes.com to send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or web bugs) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
DailyDesignNotes.com has no access or control on these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers may store on your computer. DailyDesignNotes.com has no responsibility on this third party cookies aswell.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices, as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. DailyDesignNotes.com privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsersí respective websites.
