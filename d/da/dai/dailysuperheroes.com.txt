A. APPLICATION OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY
This Privacy Policy explains DailySuperheroes’s data collection practices that are applicable toward (i) our customers, which are our business partners, whether they are publishers, advertisers or any other type of business partners (“Customer/s”) and (ii) the data practices relating to visitors of the Site and/or our Customer’s websites and/or products (“End User/s”), Customers and End Users will be jointly be referred to as “Users”. Lastly, the Privacy Policy refers to general provisions, which are applicable to both Customers and Users. Capitalized terms will have the meaning given to the in the DailySuperheroes Terms or here, in this Privacy Policy. When we use the term "Personal Information" we mean information that may be used, either alone or in combination with other information, to personally identify an individual (natural person), such as a first and last name, an email address, phone number, a home or other physical address, and other contact information. In some jurisdictions and given the specific use we make with the information, IP address that your Internet service provider allocates for your device, may also be considered as Personal Information (in the European Union for example). In which case, we will be treating the information as personal in all handling and safeguarding aspects, however, DailySuperheroes does not make any use of such information for identification or personalization purposes and we do not combine such data with another in attempt to identify you.
B. If YOU ARE A CUSTOMER:
If you are a Customer of the Services, in addition to the User Information we collect, we may collect Personal Information that you are required to submit to us when you open an account with us, for purposes of engaging with us in a business relationship or to otherwise integrate our Services into your own sites, products, software and services. In which case, our data collection practices will be as follows:
1. Information we collect about Customers
In addition to information we may collect about End Users, (as detailed below), if you are a Customer and you choose to open an account with us, you will be asked to submit certain Personally Identifiable Information, such as first and last name, email address of the entity on which behalf you are engaging with, a password for accessing your account and billing information. We will also ask you to fill out a form in which you will provide your organization’s billing address, URL and some financial information. We will also collect other Information that Customers choose to provide to us, such as the contents of a message that a Customer submits through our Website (collectively “Customer Information”).
2. HOW WE USE CUSTOMER INFORMATION?
We use Customer information in order to (i) render our Services and contact Customers that express their will to communicate with us and/or engage with the Services; (ii) communicate with Customers and respond to any inquiry or any other form of communications from Customers; and (iii) providing Customers with newsletters, promotions, updates, and other information Customer may find useful, including via email;
3. SHARING CUSTOMER INFORMATION.
We may disclose or share Customer Information to a current or future (i) affiliate, parent companies and/or subsidiaries that help us with facilitating the Services or operating the Website; (ii) services providers, agents, contractors, vendors and any other individual or entity which requires access to Customer Information in order to perform their functions; (iii) other Customers, to the extent required to render the Services and consented by Customer.
4. YOUR CHOICES.
As a Customer of us, you have certain rights concerning your Customer Information, including: edit, correct, verify, update or delete your Customer Information. If you wish to exercise any of those rights, you may do so by contacting us directly at: support@dailysuperheroes.com. In addition, any email communication we send to you, will include an opt-out / unsubscribe option that you can directly exercise via the email communication. Please note that if you choose to unsubscribe from email communication, we may still hold your account information and Customer Information and may contact you for updates regarding your use if the Services or any other relevant account information.
C. If YOU ARE AN END USER:
If you are an End User of the Services, the following data privacy practices are relevant for you:
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT END USERS
When you visit the Site or access, interact or engage with any of the Platforms, we may collect some information about such use as dependence of the type of Platform (and respective technology) with which you interact. Such information generally includes the following, and may be attributed to our Platform or Service ID that we generate for each type of Platform or to a general user ID that we generate for each user on the system and is a completely random identifier: device information (such as type, operating system type, version and language), your user agent, that includes the browser type, version and language from which you access the Platform or consume the Services, the domain or URL of the website you browse in which the Platform is integrated and the Services are displayed or served [please advise if you take full URL even if the site is served over https]. We may also collect the IP address of the device that accessed to the Services for purposes extracting the End User geographic location. In which case the IP address is not retained. User Information and Customer Information will be jointly referred to as “Information”.
2. HOW WE COLLECT END USER INFORMATION?
Like most online services, we also use Cookies (also known as web beacons), pixel tags and similar web technologies to collect User Information:
(i) Cookies: A cookie is a small text file that is placed on the browser of the hard drive of your computer (or similar device) by websites that you visit. Most websites and online services use cookies. Cookies are typically used (together with web beacons) in order to make websites or web services function, or function more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the particular website or service. Cookies make your use of the Services easier to use and improve their functionality. Additionally, is some of the Platforms we operate, Cookies are essential for the proper functionality of that Platform and the Services that are available by it. Most browsers are automatically set to accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to disable cookies or to completely remove them. If you choose to disable cookies though, you will still be able to access the Website and use the Services, but some features and services may not properly function.
(ii) Web Beacons. We, or advertisers partners, may use standard Internet technology such as web beacons (or "clear gifs"), tracking pixels, and other similar technologies, to gather analytics, anonymous, aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about End Users that interact with the Platform or the content that is available on top. Please be aware, as a Platform, we tend to host third party content, features, elements and services that are not necessarily provided by us (“Third Party Content”). Such Third Party Content may include graphics, animation, video, ads, offers, links, buttons or any their similar feature with which you may engage. Any interaction you make with such Third Party Feature is govern by their providers’ policies, terms and conditions, so we advise you to refer to their privacy policies to learn more about their data collection and protection practices.
(iii) Log Files. Technical information from your browser is automatically collected into a log file, when a you visit our Site or interact with any of the Platforms. This information includes your device’s IP address (which we do not retain), User’s browser type, operating system, access times, the content of any undeleted cookies that the End User’s browser previously accepted from us, and the referring website address.
3. HOW DO WE USE USER INFORMATION?
Please note that we use User Information for purposes of operating the Platforms, providing the Services and constantly improve them. That includes the following uses:
(i) Operating and further developing and improving the Services;
(ii) For counting and tracking purposes when the Platform involves ad serving and campaign management;
(iii) Validation of users as humans;
(iv) Where you provided us with your email address, for example if you contact with us for an inquiry or request, we will use your contact details and inquiry content to process it and respond and other communications with you;
(v) Identify or authenticate your access and use of your account (in case of a Customer Information).
(vi) Personalized Content and Advertising. When a User interacts with our Media Player or Market Place, we may facilitate the transmission of certain device information and End User information. This information includes user agent, IP address, Cookie ID and additional parameters about the End User’s consent. We do not retain this information, however enable by it the serving of personalized content. This information is never used to personally identify any individual and is handled with compliance to applicable laws, as we strictly require our advertising partners.
(vii) Retargeting. Some of our third-party services may collect non-identifiable information about your interaction with the Platforms. Such information may be used by such third parties for serving and displaying ads and offers (“Ad/s”) when you browse other websites across the web (“Retargeting”). You will have the choice to opt-out at any time of such type of advertising, directly from the Ad when you encounter it, from the ad setting of the providing third party services.
(viii) For any legally permissible purpose in our sole discretion or law enforcement requirement;
4. SHARING OR DISCLOSING USER INFORMATION
We do not share Personal Information about you with Third Parties, except for the following circumstances:
(i) Data Storage and Security Platforms (“Processors”); We use third party cloud and storage services to store and retain your Information. It means that we send or otherwise share your Information to/with third party entities that are based in the U.S, for the purpose of storing such information on our behalf, or for other processing needs. Such entities include Amazon Web Services (which privacy policy available at: https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/). Our Processors may be based (and their servers may be located) anywhere in the world. We require each such Processor to maintain a strict privacy protection and data security policies, and strict compliance with applicable data protection laws. However, their practices and activities are fully governed by their own privacy policies and the jurisdictions in which they are based.
(ii) Enforcement. We may share your Information with any third party if we believe that disclosure of such information is helpful or reasonably necessary to: (a) comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; (b) enforce our terms and conditions, including investigations of potential violations thereof; (c) detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud or security issues; and/or (d) protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of our Platform, system network or technology, our affiliated entities, our users, yourself, and/or the public.
(iii) Merger, Sale or Bankruptcy. In the event that we or one of our affiliated entities is acquired by, or merged with, a Third Party entity, or otherwise sells all or part of our/its assets, we may (and hereby reserve the right to) transfer or assign the Personal Information and other information we have collected or received. In such case, we will require the acquiring entity to post its data practices and provide you with any of your rights as per the jurisdiction of your residency.
(iv) Other Third Parties. We may send or otherwise share User Information to/with various third parties that help us with our business operations (for example, customer service operators and email and push administration providers, where applicable and made available), that help us understand how our users use the Site and Services, operate them and to improve them. These third parties may have their own privacy policies that they adhere to and may also provide how to opt-out of such services, and we require them to make it available whenever their services are provided. We recommend you revisit such privacy policies when those Third Parties render their services directly to you. Also, these third parties may be based (and their servers may be located) anywhere in the world.
(v) Social Media Websites and Widgets. DailySuperheroes may include in its Sites (or related content), certain features of third party social networking platform such as Facebook, Twitter and Linkedin share functions and widgets (“Third Party Features”). The Third Party Features may collect information about your use of the content or such features, and may do so by setting a cookie to make sure the feature functions properly. Third Party Features may be either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our Services or a Publisher Site. Your interactions with the Third Party Features are governed by the privacy policy of the relevant operator and provider of such features. We have no control over the actions of the companies providing the Third Party Features or their users, but we do however require them and making sure they are compliant with applicable privacy laws.

6. INFORMATION SECURITY
We, and third party services/affiliate entities implement data security systems and procedures to secure the information transmitted, processed and stored in our computer servers and systems. For example, the Site is encrypted by using SSL and so as any other information at transport, we periodically review our information and data collection practices to ensure constant monitoring, we use third party processing and serving services that implement various information security standards and certifications (at rest and transmission), and we monitor and filter access to data when it comes to personnel in our organization. Such systems and procedures reduce the risk of security breaches, but they do not provide absolute security, as there is no such thing when it comes to the world-wide network. Therefore, we cannot guarantee that the Services and our servers are immune to unauthorized access to the information stored therein and to other information security risks. If you have any questions about our security and safeguards efforts, you can contact us at: support@dailysuperheroes.com.

We may transfer your Information (including Personal Information) outside the country in which it is collected or in which you reside, including to a country that may not offer the same level of protection for Personal Information as the country in which you reside. However, in which case we make sure to place all contractual safeguards to ensure your Information is processed in a way that offers an adequate level of protection when it is shared between or among our third-party vendors. If applicable to your type of consuming the Services, by providing Personal Information and using the Services, you expressly consent to the use of such Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and to the transfer of your Information to another country.
9. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
The Services are not designed to attract children under the age of 16. Accordingly, we do not intend to collect Personal Information from anyone we know to be under 16. If we learn that we have collected Personal Information from a child under 16, we will use commercially reasonable efforts to delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any such information, please contact us in accordance to the contact information set forth below.
