Privacy Policy Disney World Information
We cherish our privacy, you cherish your privacy, therefore we cherish your privacy.
Electronic Storage of Customer Information:
All data submitted to this site is retained for the sole use of DW Insider and is only displayed to the public at http://www.dwinsider.com.
Distribution of Records:
DW Insider does not intend to sell or distribute individual information gathered on this site, including but not limited to personal information, credit card information, demographic information, or information pertaining to the purchasing habits of individual customers.
Use of Email Addresses and Web Addresses:
Customer email addresses and web addresses are retained as a method of contact between you and DW Insider. We do NOT sell or rent email addresses nor web addresses.
