Thank you for visiting Doctors.at and, if applicable, choosing to use our Service. We try to make our Privacy Policy easy to understand so that you are informed as to how we use your information. This Privacy Policy, like our Terms of Service, is an integral part of using our Service; therefore you must completely agree to our Privacy Policy in order to use our Site or Service.If you are under 18 please stop using our Service immediately.
2. Information Collected
Identifying Information Submitted by You
You will not be required to submit any information when visiting our Site. When creating an account, we may collect your name, email, address and telephone number.When purchasing any of our Services you will be required to submit your credit card information to our third party payment processorRecurly. We do not collect or store your payment information. All payment information is processed and stored by Recurly.
Additionally, if you wish to link your account to third party social networking sites, we may collect your login information and any additional information you make available through those sites.
Non-Identifying Information
Whenever you visit our Site, we may collect non-identifying information from you, such as your IP address, interactions with the Site and Service,referring URL, browser, operating system, cookie information, usage, data transferred and Internet Service Provider. Without a subpoena, voluntary compliance on the part of your Internet Service Provider, or additional records from a third party such as your wireless provider, this information alone cannot usually be used to identify you.
