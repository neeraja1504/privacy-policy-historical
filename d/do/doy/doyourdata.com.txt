DoYourData Software Privacy Policy
DoYourData Software is committed to protecting the privacy of the information you share with us. Your personal information will only be collected for defined purposes. We will not sell, distribute, or rent information about you and your purchase information to a third party. We may use the information you share only for the purposes of improving your use of our web site, and/or, with your permission, sending you information about our products and services.
Information Collection
When you place an order on our website, your full name, email address, etc will be required. The information is used to complete the transaction and help us provide better support and services to you.
When you email or write to us, your email or name will be used for the communication between us.
When you submit a question or write a review on our website, your email or name will be collected.
When you become our subscriber, your email address will be used for receiving our email about product or service.
MyCommerce of Digital River
MyCommerce (a Digital River company) is an advanced secure online payment service for software reselling. DoYourData Software uses Mycommerce to provide you with very secure and fast online purchase.
About Cookies
Most of users use cookies. A cookie is a small text file that a Website writes to your hard drive. Cookies work as your identification card and may record your personal information. Cookies let the web page server know that you have returned to this site. Cookies are unique and can only be read by the server that assigns them. They cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. You can change the settings in your browser to prevent cookies if you do not want to have a cookie set when you visit our website. However, by doing so, you may not have full access to all the web pages.
Linked Websites
We provide links to third party sites. Since we do not control these web sites, we encourage you to review the privacy policies posted on these (and all) third party sites.
If you have any question about our privacy policy, please send an email to support@doyourdata.com
