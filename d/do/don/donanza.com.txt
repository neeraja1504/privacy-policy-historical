Our assets currently include our intellectual property, knowhow, software and technology, and the database related to our users which has been collected throughout the years of our operation, as permitted by the Donanza Privacy Policy ("Customer Data"), consisting of certain data and information related to Donanza customers, including without limitation, personal contact information, such as name, company name, address, phone number, and email address, blog/site URL; industry; navigational information collected through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and web beacons, such as standard information from customers' browser (such as browser type and browser language), Internet Protocol ("IP") address, and customer online behavior such as actions taken by customers on Donanza.com (e.g., web pages viewed and links clicked); and information available on third party social networking websites such as facebook and twitter (the "Social Networks"), through which Customer has in the past signed into his DoNanza account, including without limitation user profile details, friends and contacts list, the networks to which Customer belongs, etc. ("Donanza Assets").
Donanza is currently contemplating the sale or other disposition of any or all of its business, activity or Donanza Assets. We therefore want to bring to your attention the fact that as an integral part of such transaction we may enter into, we may dispose of all or part of the Donanza Assets, whether in one transaction, or in several transactions to various acquirers. Under such transaction, Donanza may review, share, sell, transfer, assign, rent, trade, or distribute to a third party, any or all of the Donanza Assets, including Customer Data. In any event where the ownership of all or part of our business or of the Donanza Assets (including Customer Data) shall change, Customer Data may be transferred to the new owner.
If you wish to remove your personal information from DoNanza's database, please notify us by no later than February 10th, 2015, by sending an email to: contact@donanza.com. [If you don't notify us by such date, your information will remain part of the database that may be transferred to the purchaser of DoNanza's assets.]
We thank you for your support.
