When accessing Discoroid we will learn certain information about you during your visit. While reading and / or downloading information on our pages, we collect and store the following information about you:
The name of the domain and IP address from which you access the Internet. The operating system you use. The browser you use to access the site. The date and time you access our site. Your 'Cookie' details (please see below) The Internet address of the website you used to link directly to our site. Discoroid may make anonymous use of this information to help diagnose debugging our site and to gather broad demographic information about our users in general.
This information will be stripped of all details that could identify youersonally. Discoroid will only collect personal information about you if you choose to provide such information to us. COOKIES Cookies are used to collect information and data automatically. These are small text files that hold information about you as a user - they allow discoroid to recognise you as repetitive visitor and provide you with a convenient means of access, without nrequiring your credentials again.
Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, where we would prefer to be transparent for our visitors to provide you all the means of using Cache
