Privacy Policy - Classic-Recipes
Our Privacy Policy
We do respect your privacy! All information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Classic-Recipes will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. If you submit material (via the recipe or cooking tip submission form) to be displayed on this site, only the information you specify will be used.
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties
Classic-Recipes does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.
Browser information collected on this Website
We analyze our logs to constantly improve the Website. Our Website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Advertising and Cookies
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
