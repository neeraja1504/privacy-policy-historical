DtDNS :: Privacy Policy
The following privacy statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.
Effective Date: September 5, 2007
IP Address Usage
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. IP addresses associated with hostnames and domains are also used in the DNS protocol, and will be publicly available to the Internet community. Since this is an IP redirection service, IP addresses may also be used in statistical tracking.
Personal Account Information
Our site's registration form requires customers to disclose contact information (like their name, e-mail address and phone number) and demographic information (like their zip/postal code). The customer's contact information is used primarily to contact the customer when necessary.
We respect your privacy and do not make this information available to other companies for any purpose unless required to do so by law.
Link Policy
This site contains links to other sites. DtDNS is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has physical, procedural and electronic security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All data is backed up in the case of an emergency or misuse.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides customers the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the customer. We also offer an option in the Account Settings screen to toggle the option to receive notices. Sometimes important information regarding the operation of this web site and its services may be sent to ALL customers, regardless of the profile settings.
3rd Party Anonymous Statistics Gathering
DtDNS makes use of Google Analytics ("Google") for statistical tracking purposes. Google may use cookies to track visitors based on incoming search terms and other aggregate statistical information. For more information on how Google stores and uses this information, please see their Privacy Policy.
Account Modification/Cancellation
This site gives customers the option of changing and modifying information previously provided by modifying it in the Account Settings screen.
This site gives customers the option for removing their information from our database, to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service by using the Cancel Account button in the Account Settings screen.
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with this Web site, please contact: admin@dtdns.com
