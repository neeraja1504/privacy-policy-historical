Privacy Policy | Dealsgaram
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at dealsindiabin@gmail.com
At dealsbin.in, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by dealsbin.in and how it is used.
Cookies and Web Beacons
dealsbin.in does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
dealsbin.in has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. dealsbin.in’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
