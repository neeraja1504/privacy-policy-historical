Privacy Policy | Death Penalty Information Center
The Death Penalty Information Center does not collect information on individual users of its Web site. On occasion, we have checked with other services that record the number and general types of visitors to our site. We do not obtain any personal information about users, nor do we give permission to others to obtain or share such information.
