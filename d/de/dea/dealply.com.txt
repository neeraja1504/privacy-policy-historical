Which information we collect on our Users? We collect two types of data and information from our Users:
a. Non-personally identifiable information pertaining to a User, which may be made available or gathered via the User's use of DealPly (“Non-personal Information”). We are not aware of the identity of the User from which the Non-personal Information was collected. Non-personal Information which is being collected consists of technical information and aggregated usage information, and may contain, among other things, the activity of the User on DealPly, type of operation system, User's 'click-stream' on DealPly's offers and any e-commerce websites operated by third parties (including Third Party Services, as such term is defined in the Terms), including search queries and related searches on search engines you make, browser type and version, language and keyboard language.
b. The second type of information is personally identifiable information, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort identify an individual, or may be of private or sensitive nature (“Personal Information”).
Personal Information which is may be gathered consists of the following:
The User's IP address or other persistent user identifier, which is allocated to your device by your Internet service provider and used mainly for enhancing the User's experience and for geolocation personalization and security purposes, as further detailed below;
Any Personal Information provided voluntarily by the User via DealPly, such as the User's e-mail address in order to be contacted or any Users interaction with us through DealPly, as may be updated from time to time by us.
We do not collect any Personal Information from you or related to you without your consent, which is obtained, inter alia, through your active acceptance to the Terms and this Privacy Policy.
How Do We Collect Information on Our Users? We use certain technologies and methods to collect information:
a. Cookies; a cookie is A 'cookie' is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website. We use cookies to recognize you as a User and so we will be able to provide you with customized Offers. You will need to have cookies enabled in order to use and enjoy our services. We use cookies to collect and correlate certain information about you as detailed in section 10 herein.
b. Keys and values in the Windows Registry; which include data about your operating system and relevant processes;
c. Installer Reports; when you install DealPly, the installer generates reports with information about the installation of DealPly on your computer;
d. Information which you provide us voluntarily; For example, we collect Personal Information which you voluntarily provide when you contact us directly by email for instance.
We store the Personal Information either independently or through the help of our authorized third-party service providers as detailed below.
What are the Purposes of the Collection of Information? We collect Non-personal and Personal Information in order to provide and operate DealPly and to make DealPly and related services customized as possible to your interests and preferences. We also use the information for statistical and analytics, research and technical support; this enable us to further develop, customize and improve DealPly based on Users’ common preferences and uses and to enable us to provide our Users with a better user experience, with more relevant products, offers and deals and other marketing materials.

We share Non-Personally Information with third parties, when this is required in order to provide, maintain or improve our services and operation. This is the case with third party offers; we share your Non-Personal Information with third parties for the purpose of providing you with offers we or such third parties think you may be interested in, including based on your statistical Non-Personal Information.

User Choices We value your privacy and control over your personal information, and therefore you may notify us that you wish to either review your Personal Information or delete it. You can notify us by sending an email to: support@dealply.com and we will make reasonable efforts to delete any such Personal Information pursuant to any applicable privacy laws. Note that unless you instruct us otherwise we may retain your Personal Information for a period of 12 months or a longer period as reasonably and legally required.
Further, at all times you can choose not to allow us or any third party to collect data about you, or ask to view or edit Personal Information that is stored about you. You may also choose not to accept cookies, including cookies associated with our services or third party services. However, you should take into account that many of the services or portion of them may not function properly if your cookies are disabled.
If you are a resident of California, United States, or of the European Union, you have the right to send us written request and ask a list of Personal Information categories that we may disclosed to third parties or to which we may give access to third parties, and the details of such third parties. To exercise such right you may contact us at: support@dealply.com.

Cookies When you access or use DealPly we use industry-wide technologies such as "cookies" (or similar technologies), which store certain information on your computer ("Local Storage") and which will allow us to enable automatic activation of certain features, and make your service experience much more convenient and effortless. The cookies used by DealPly are created per session you launch when the application starts for the first time, and may persist until the application is removed from your computer. We do not include any information about you other than your session and anonymous globally unique identifier key, the date and time at which you have started using the application, information about how you have downloaded DealPly to your computer and information necessary to serve you different content, such as when performing user-interface so-called “A/B tests". These cookies can be removed by using your browser’s cookie/Local Storage cleaning facilities. It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage altogether. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer's hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. However, if you block or erase cookies your online experience may be limited.
The list below provides additional information regarding the types of cookies we use and their purpose:
Functionality Cookies: used to identify you as an user and you preferences to provide you with customized offers and deals; we also use such cookies to label you as an opted-out user in case you will choose to opt out of our services;
Analytics Cookies: used to collect information about how users use DealPly. We use the information to compile reports and to help us improve DealPly. Such information is collected in an aggregated form, including the number of Users of DealPly, where Users have access to DealPly from and the webpages they visited.
