DesignVitality Privacy Policy
Information and Data that may be Collected
Design Vitality collects information about your computer (your IP address, browser type, referral visit and visit time) to provide a better user experience. This information will not be sold to any other parties.
Cookies
Design Vitality stores cookies on your computer during your visit and retrieves them upon your return.
These cookies are only used to determine if the website has changed since your last visit
Our advertisers may also use cookies, which determine whether you have been shown certain ads before.
Uses of your Personal Data
Your personal data will only be used to improve your browsing experience when browsing the website.
Other Disclosures
Design Vitality does not disclose our cookies to third parties
Security of your Personal Data
Design Vitality will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. We are not liable for any malicious activity which is out of our control.
Policy Amendments
Design Vitality may update this privacy policy from time-to-time by posting a new version on our website. Users are encouraged to check this page occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes.
Your Choices
You may decide to disable cookies for this web site if you so choose.
Recommended Reading
Visit the Electronic Frontier Foundation to find out more about your digital rights. The EFF is a nonprofit group working to protect your digital rights.
Contacting Us
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy you may contact us here.
