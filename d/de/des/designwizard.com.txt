DesignWizard: Graphic Design Software Privacy Policy
The privacy notice  describes how Wavebreak Media  Ltd T/A Design Wizard, of Unit 3, West Point Business Park, Ballincollig, Cork City, Ireland (and its affiliates) protects and makes use of the information you provide to the company when you use this website and services, for the purpose of carrying out its business activities.
The information you provide will only be used in the ways described in this privacy notice. The policy also describes the use of internet cookies. By accessing and using the website, e.g. reading our blog posts or visiting the website pages, it is implied that you have read and accepted the privacy notice and cookies in its entirety.
The latest version of the Privacy Notice is published on this page, with the revision date shown at the top. This will be updated from time to time, so we recommend you check the page regularly.  If you have any questions on the policy, please contact us. You will find company contact details on the ‘Contact Us’ page of the website but you can contact US by email at privacy @designwizard.com
Data We Gather
If you give us information about you, we will keep it and use it to provide our service to you and make our service better.
When you register on our website, for example signing up for a Design Wizard account or for a webinar or a copy of an eBook, you will be required to complete an application form containing personal information. The following are examples of the types of information we gather;
Your unique username
Name and Job Title
Email Address
Phone number
Demographic Information, such as postal address
Information that may be useful in providing a better user experience or relevant content such as industry or company name
We also gather the following types of data;
Internet analytic data – see also section detailing Internet Cookies
Logs of website/services/application usage data for product/feature development
Device identifiers
Location Data
Clear giffs/web beacons
As is true of most websites, we may directly collect analytics data, or use third party analytics tools, to help measure traffic , browser type, CPU speed, and other data such as usage trends. These tools collect information from your mobile device or your computers browser, which include items such as the pages you visit and other information which helps us to improve the service to you.  We collect this information in an aggregated way so it cannot be manipulated to identify a particular person.
Each time you access the service we also automatically log that information from your browser or mobile device. We do so in order to improve the service to you and help you login faster and improve the service to you. Log files may contain anonymous information such as web request, internet Protocol address ( more widely known as an IP address), browser type, device type, exit pages and URLs, interactions and clicks, pages viewed, domain names and other such information of a similar nature. The information that this log files sends us is stored securely in our serves.
When you access the service through a mobile device we may also use a device identifier to enhance our service to you. A device identifier is a small data file which is stored on your mobile device and which may convey information to us about how you browse or use the service.
When you access the service we may also collect information regarding the location of your device which we use to provide you with a better service.
When you use the service we may also clear GIFs ( also known as web Beacons) which are used to anonymously track the online usage patterns of our Users. In addition, we may also use Clear giffsin HTML-based emails sent to our users to track emails to see information such as opening rates and click through rates of links of recipients.  This information is used to improve the service to our users.

Although we generally collect personal information directly from you, on occasion, we also collect certain categories of personal information about you from other sources. In particular:
Financial and/or transaction details from payment providers located in the US, for legitimate interests, in order to process a transaction;
Third party service providers (like Google, Facebook) which may provide information about you when you link, connect, or login to your account with the third-party provider and they send us information such as your registration,  profile, user Id associated with your account, an access token necessary to access from that service. Essentially, when you use our service, third parties might give us some info about. The information we receive may vary and is controlled by that service provider or as authorized by you via your privacy settings at that service provider;
Partners or other third-party sources, whereby we receive additional information about you, only to the extent permitted by applicable law, such as demographic data or fraud detection information. E.g fraud warnings from service providers like identity verification service. We also receive information about you and your activities on or off the DeisgnWizard.

How We Use Cookies
We may send cookies to your web browser to help you use Design Wizard If you would like to adjust your cookie settings, please read your browser instructions.
Internet Cookies
An internet cookie is a small file placed on your computer, which enables a website to identify your computer as you view different pages on the website and when you return to the website at a later time/date from the same computer.
Design Wizard uses cookies for the following reasons;
Identify that you are signed in to our website.
Store information about your preferences. This can give a better experience of the website and services based on your personal preferences.
Retain your preferences and auto login on return to our website. You may wish to remain logged into to the website (by not logging out) when you next return, automatically applying your personal preferences.
Web traffic analysis. Data from website data aggregation services (such as google analytics) helps us improve our services and provides technical data (for example, versions of internet browsers accessing the website) to our development team.
We partner with a third party to display and manage advertising.  Our third party may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on this website or other websites in order to serve advertising to you based on your browsing activities.
Design Wizard Cookies
When logging on to  www.designwizard.com, a cookie is created to keep you logged in throughout your visit.
When accessing www.designwizard.com, a cookie is created and stored for one month to automatically log you back in when coming back to the website.
Please note that no personal information is stored in these cookies. These cookies can be manually destroyed by you by going in your browser’s settings.  If you do not want your computer to store cookies from our website, you can disable cookies in your internet browser but please be aware that this may limit your use of the website or impact the functionality of the website.

Controlling Your Information
You have control over your account settings, such as your account information and email notifications, but there’s some important updates we’ll always send you for as long as you have an account with Design Wizard.
You have the right to access and edit your account and profile information by logging into the Design Wizard application and choosing to edit your Profile. You are able to upgrade, downgrade, and cancel your Subscription. You have the right to close your account.
You will have an opt-out link available in promotional emails.  Alternatively, you can contact us directly under the ‘Contact Us’ page of our website.
We do not lease, distribute or sell your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or the law requires us to do so.
You have the right to ask us to port your personal information, to erase it, or restrict its processing. In certain cases these rights may be limited  where need to retain your personal information to comply with legal or contractual obligations, which may mean we have to retain your data, even though you withdraw your consent.  We may also require your data in order to comply with contractual or legal obligations.
We care about the safety of your data and, we have implemented industry recognized measures to ensure that your information is protected. However, we can’t guarantee that nothing bad will ever appen to it.
When your Account includes a Subscription, we collect, but do not store, your credit card number. We do collect and store your billing address. Full credit card numbers are never visible to Design Wizard  employees or contractors, and we will not modify your credit card number or billing address under any circumstances. We adhere to all controls required for handling cardholder data in accordance with Payment Card Industry (PCI) compliance standards. You agree that our Third Party payment processors may collect and store your billing address and credit card information on our behalf. We are not responsible or liable for the ways in which Third Parties collect, store, or process your Personal Information or Sensitive Information, or other details about you.
