Privacy Policy -
We’re committed to ensuring that we respect and protect our customers’ privacy
Privacy is extremely important at Dedi Networks and we are committed to ensuring that we respect and protect our customers’ privacy.
We only collect the data that we actually need and will not ask for information we do not need to serve you in some way.
Financial and personal information will always be encrypted before being transmitted from you to us via an SSL session.
Website Vistors
Google analytics collects your IP address, browser type and operating system on each visit to every page on this site on our behalf.This information is used only for internal statistic tracking purposes and is never shared with other entities.
