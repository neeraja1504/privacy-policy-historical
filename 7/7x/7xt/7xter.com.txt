Privacy Policy - Zero Hacks
Privacy Policy
At Zero Hacks, we take privacy of our readers very seriously. The following privacy policy describe what are the information collected and how it is being used.
User Contact Information
We never sell or distribute your contact information collected through contact forms and comment boxes.
Cookies
We use cookies to store information about user preferences, generate suggestions based on user interests.
Advertisements
We use adsense and other thirdparty advertisements, where they automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
