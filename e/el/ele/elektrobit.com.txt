Data privacy notice - Elektrobit
We appreciate your visit on our website elektrobit.com and your interest in our company. The protection of your personal data is very important to us.
In the following, we describe which information we collect when you visit our website and how this information is used.
For further information how personal data is processed at Elektrobit click here.
Since personal data are under special legal protection, they are only collected by us insofar as this is necessary for the provision of our website and our services.
Our data protection practice complies with the statutory regulations, in particular those of the Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG-neu), the Telemedia Act (TMG) and the EU Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We will only collect, process and store your personal data, as long as this is necessary for provision of this website, our content and services, as well as for processing inquiries and, if necessary, processing orders / contracts, but only to the extent permitted by legitimate interest, Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR, or another permit. Only if you have given your consent separately, you data may be used for the purposes stated in that consent declaration, e.g. for sending advertisement via newsletter.

Provision of the website and creation of log files
Every time our website is accessed, our system automatically collects data and information from your computer. The following data is collected:
(1) Information about the browser type and version used
(2) The operating system of your computer
(3) Your IP address
(4) Date and time of access
(5) Websites and resources (images, files, other page content) that were accessed on our website.
(6) Websites via which the user has accessed our Website (Referrer tracking)
This data is saved as a Log File in our System. It will not be matched with any personal information so that linking it to an individual is not possible.
Legal basis for the processing of personal data
6 para. 1 para. f GDPR (legitimate interest). Our legitimate Interest is to achieve the subsequent purpose.
Purpose of data processing
We create Log Files to maintain the compatibility of our website for all visitors, to prevent abuse and errors. For this purpose, it is necessary to log the technical data, in order to be able to react as quickly as possible to prevent errors, attacks on our IT systems and/ or errors in the functionality of our website. In addition, the data is used to optimize the website and generally ensure the security of our information technology systems.
Duration of storage
We delete this technical data as soon as it is no longer needed, but never later the 3 months after your visit.
Option to object and erase your data
Your rights to objection and erasure to this processing are described further down in this privacy notice.

Information about the use of cookies
Extent of processing of personal data
On various pages we use cookies to enable certain features on our website. Those ‘cookies’ are small text files that your browser stores on the computer. These text files contain an individual string that allows us to identify your browser when you visit our website again. The process of storing cookie files is also called ‘setting a cookie’.
Legal basis for the processing of personal data
6 para. 1 lit. f) GDPR (legitimate interest). Our legitimate interest is the preservation of the full functionality of our website, the increase in usability and the ability to provide a more personalized experience to the customer. The identification of individual site visitors is only possible, if by giving consent to the use of cookies, personal data is gathered.
Purpose of data processing
Cookies are set in order to maintain the full functionality of our website and to improve its usability. In addition, cookie technology allows us to set individual aliases (an unique ID) to visitors so that we can offer more personalized services.
Duration of storage
Our cookies will be stored until deletion in your browser or, in the case of a session cookie, until the session has expired.
Option to object and erase your data
You can set your browser according to your wishes in such a way that the setting of cookies is generally prevented. You can either decide on a case-by-case basis which cookies are allowed or allow them entirely. Cookies can be used for different purposes, e.g. to analyze if your PC ever had a connection to our website (permanent cookies) or to save recently viewed offers (session cookies). We use cookies to provide you with an better user experience. Your rights to objection and erasure to this processing are described further down in this privacy notice.
