INTRODUCTION
We have created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. By accessing any of the eRevollution sites, you are consenting to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes:
what information eRevollution collects from users;
how such information is used and stored;
when such information may be disclosed to third parties;
how users, parents, and guardians can control the use and disclosure of collected information;
and how information is protected.
INFORMATION THAT WE MAY COLLECT
eRevollution collects only the information that is reasonably necessary for the activities available on the particular website. In general, we collect e-mail addresses.
USE OF COLLECTED INFORMATION
We may use your e-mail address to uniquely identify you as a user of eRevollution. Subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy, we may use your contact information to process our newsletters and/or e-mail campaigns.
In general the information we collect may be used to:
respond to your requests,
measure analytics;
and manage and improve the services available on eRevollution
INFORMATION WE SHARE WITH THIRD PARTIES.
Depending upon the circumstances, we may share collected information with:
law enforcement or others as needed to protect or defend our rights and the rights of others,
third parties we hire to handle certain activities;
otherwise with your permission
We will not share your information with any third parties, except that you may disclose your personal information to third parties who perform services for us, such as PayPal system.
Those third parties have access to your information only as necessary to perform their functions, and for no other purposes.

COOKIES:
Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we may transfer to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser. We use cookies to enhance your online experience.
The Help portion of the toolbar on most Web browsers will tell you:
how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies,
how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie,
how to entirely disable
However, because cookies allow you to take advantage of some of eRevollution essential features, we recommend that you leave cookies turned on.
