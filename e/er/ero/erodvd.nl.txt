Privacy Policy
The privacy,and security of your customer information is very important to you. When you place an
order through our website you provide your name, your billing address, your shipping address,
your credit card number, credit card expiration date, phone number and your email address. We
consider this information private. We do not share this information with anyone.
All your customer information is kept on a secure server behind a firewall and is hacker safe. Your
personal information is protected from all outside intrusions. We encrypt all your customer information
using secure socket layer technology (SSL). To read more about SSL, follow this link:
www.geotrust.com.
Legal Disclaimer: BakhuisMedia reserves the right to disclose your personal
identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect
our rights and/or comply with a court order, judicial proceeding, or legal process served on our
Website.
