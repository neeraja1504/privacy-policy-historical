e-Miles: Earn miles for free and redeem towards your favorite reward program
This privacy policy applies to www.e-miles.com owned and operated by e-Miles, Inc. This privacy policy describes how e-Miles, Inc. collects and uses the personal information you provide on our web site: www.e-miles.com. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.
e-Miles, Inc. ("e-Miles") is firmly committed to protecting your privacy. Because protecting your privacy is important to us, you may always submit concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or our privacy practices to info@e-Miles.com. If you choose to write us an e-mail, please reference the Privacy Policy in your subject line. e-Miles® will attempt to respond in a timely manner to all reasonable concerns and inquiries.
e-Miles, Inc. has been awarded TRUSTe's Privacy Seal signifying that this privacy policy and practices have been reviewed by TRUSTe for compliance with TRUSTe's program requirements including transparency, accountability and choice regarding the collection and use of your personally identifiable
information. The TRUSTe program covers only information that is collected through this Web site, www.e-miles.com.
TRUSTe's mission, as an independent third party, is to accelerate online trust among consumers and organizations globally through its leading privacy Trustmark and innovative trust solutions. If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at info@e-Miles.com. If you are not satisfied with our response you can contact  TRUSTe here.
e-Miles, Inc. complies with the U.S. — E.U. Safe Harbor Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use and retention of personal data from the European Union member countries. e-Miles, Inc. has certified that it adheres to the Safe Harbor Privacy Principles of notice, choice, onward transfer, security, data integrity, access, and enforcement. To learn more about the Safe Harbor program, and to view e-Miles, Inc.’s certification, please visit  http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/
You Will Not be Spammed by e-Miles
The e-Miles Service (e-Miles Service) is an opt-in e-mail program. You will only receive e-mails from e-Miles based on information you supply to us.
