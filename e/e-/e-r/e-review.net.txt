Privacy Policy | E-Review.net
Your Privacy
Your privacy is very important to us. To efficiently protect your privacy we provide this information explaining our online info practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice simple to find, we make it available on our home-page and at each point where your personal data may be requested.
Collection of Personal Information
When browsing on this web site, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the times and dates of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the website, track user’s movement and accumulate broad demographic information for internal use. Most significantly, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to individually identifiable information.
Links to third party Websites
We’ve included links on this site for your use and reference. We’re not in charge of the privacy policy pages on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from our own.
Changes to this Privacy Statement
The contents of this statement may be altered at any time, at our discretion.
