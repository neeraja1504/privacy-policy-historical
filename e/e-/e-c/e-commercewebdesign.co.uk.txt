Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions - E-Commerce Web Design
Our company is the sole owner of the information collected from users on this web site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others. This website collects information from its users at several different voluntary points on our website. This web-site also gathers general usage statistics so that we can monitor our web traffic.
We only request information from our users on our enquiry forms. On our forms, a user must volunteer to provide contact information (like name, email and telephone numbers). This information is used solely to fulfil customer's enquiries. We respond to the enquirer to provide the information requested (as an example a quotation for services).
