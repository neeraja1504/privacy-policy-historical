Privacy policy
We respect and protect the privacy of our users.
Eurogates promises never to sell, exchange or release your personal information without your consent to any third parties.
Personal information includes name, e-mail address, and answers to our questions.
Eurogates does not monitor individual traffic patterns on our site - only aggregate reader behaviour is collected.
We use this information only to help us improve our site Eurogates to make it more useful for our visitors.
