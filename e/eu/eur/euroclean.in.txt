Euroclean
At Eureka Forbes, we are committed to protect the privacy of all our viewers and customers. Eureka Forbes does not sell, rent or loan any identifiable information at the individual level regarding its viewers and customers to any third party. Any information you give us, is held with the utmost care and security. This information is collected primarily to ensure that we are able to fulfill the objectives of the website.
When you visit www.euroclean.in and/or register with us for any other services or submit an enquiry, you agree to the option of receiving e-mails, SMS, Calls or personal visits regarding updates about your enquiry, special offers, new products and new services of Eureka Forbes who are the owners of this website. The information collected on the page includes Name, Address, Email id, Mobile number, State, City and Pincode. We are also bound to co-operate fully should a situation arise where we are required by law or legal process to provide information about the person who accessed our website. We may use such personal, individual, statistical or demographic information in aggregate form, for research and advertising purposes. This Privacy Policy can be changed at the sole discretion of Eureka Forbes from time to time and the same will be posted on the website. In case of any concern about your privacy, and to opt out of being contacted by Eureka Forbes representative and/or personnel, kindly write to us at: info@eurekaforbes.com. Your request will be responded to promptly.
