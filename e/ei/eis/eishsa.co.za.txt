Privacy Policy - EishSA
Eish SA Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how we safeguard your privacy regarding the information submitted by you.
The information you submit
When you submit your information on the Eish SA website it is to apply for your website to be listed on the Eish SA Website Directory.
How and why we use the information
We gather this information to allow us to process your request to be listed on the Eish SA Website Directory.
How your information is protected
The Internet is not a secure medium. However we have put in place various security procedures as set out in this policy. Eish SA takes the security and privacy of our customers personal information extremely seriously. We have strict internal procedures which protect the information submitted by you.
Your consent
By submitting your information you consent to the use of that information as set out in this Policy. If our Privacy Policy changes we will inform you personally.
How to contact Eish SA
Email us -   info@eishsa.co.za
