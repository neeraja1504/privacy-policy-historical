Privacy Statement
We care about your privacy and we hate spam. This website uses cookies to allow our subscribers to stay ‘logged in’ for up to 2 weeks. None of the information collected or stored will ever be used for any other purpose. We will never sell, rent, share, or by any other means give your personal information to any spamming websites or spam marketing entities. The only time we would ever give your information to any other party is either to conduct a transaction that you have explicitly authorised or where required to by a law enforcement agency. From time to time we host ads for products and services offered by third party websites. When you visit those websites you are subject to their privacy policies and you should read them before giving them your information.
