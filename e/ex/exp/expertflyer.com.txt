ExpertFlyer.com
Introduction
If you are visiting ExpertFlyer.com, this Privacy Policy applies to you.
ExpertFlyer.com is part of New Imagitas, Inc., which operates as Red Ventures. When we refer to "ExpertFlyer.com," "we," "our," or "us" in this policy, we are referring to Red Ventures.
We respect your privacy and this policy explains how we use and protect the information we collect about you when you use this website; including co-branded or white labeled versions of our tools and other products. It does not apply to third-party websites.
By accessing or using our Services in the United States, you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of Your Information (collectively, "Use of Your Information") as described in this Privacy Policy. If you are accessing or using our Services in the European Economic Area ("EEA") or UK, please see the EEA/UK Addendum below.
How do we get information?
To operate this website, and provide you with information about products or services that may be of interest to you, we (and partners on our behalf) collect several different types of information, as permitted by the law, in different ways:
Sometimes you give us information.
Sometimes information about you is collected automatically.
Sometimes information we receive information about you from other third-party sources.
In all instances we ensure, the information we collect about you is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
Account registration data
When you create an ExpertFlyer.com account with us, you provide your full name and email address.
If you create your ExpertFlyer.com account using your Facebook credentials, we will have access to some of your account information from that service, such as your name and email address.
Payment data
If you opt for a Pro Plan (Basic or Premium), you'll need to supply your address, zip or postal code, country of residence, as well as payment card information. Optionally, you may supply your telephone number.
Our third-party payment gateway provider will confirm to us that transactions have been processed in accordance with our terms.
Onsite forms
If you complete a form hosted on one of our webpages, for instance, the contact us form, we will retain a record of that correspondence together with your name and email address.
Website use data
Information is generated and collected when you use our website, including: log data, pages visited, time on site, IP address, location information, browser type, language settings, and operating system.
Some of this information is collected automatically, but the majority requires the use of cookies and/or other identifiers (please see our Cookie Policy for more information), and we can only collect it once we have the required consents (which vary by region).
For example, we use Google Analytics as described in "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps."
How do we use your information?
We use the information we collect in a number of different ways:
Access, payment and communications
We use your email address and your other personal information to help us efficiently operate the website, to contact you in connection with your transactions and other activities on the website (including, but not limited to, confirmation emails or important news that could affect your relationship with ExpertFlyer.com).
These types of communications are known as "Operational Communications." In some cases, Operational Communications may also contain commercial messages, such as banner ads and special offers.
Website improvements
We are continuously looking to ways to improve our website and services. We use your information in conjunction with third-party web analytics tools help us understand how visitors use our products, what they like and dislike.
Security and functionality
Maintaining the security of your personal information is paramount to us, and we use tools to constantly monitor session data and traffic for suspicious activity, to ensure your experience of ExpertFlyer.com meets your expectations. Read more about our approach to security below.
Public/Demographic Information
We use demographic information to tailor the website and our communications to your interests. We may also share demographic information with advertisers on an anonymous and aggregated basis (i.e., without identifying you). This helps us ensure our advertisers' goods and services will appeal to ExpertFlyer.com's users, and, where we have the required consents, so that we may target specific ads at specific demographic groups.
Our sharing of demographic information with advertisers is anonymous. When you respond to an advertisement, however, we ask you to remember that if that ad that is targeted to a demographic group and you decide to give the advertiser your personal information, then the advertiser may be able to identify you as being a member of that demographic group.
Who do we share your information with?
We use certain selected suppliers to provide our service - We have contracts in place with these suppliers to ensure they are held to the same exacting standards we ourselves hold.
Safeguarding data transfers
Because data protection regulations vary by country, it is important to safeguard data across international borders. As such, we ensure that all data transfers to third party organizations include appropriate safeguarding mechanisms and reporting requirements, as necessary.
How we protect your information
We use of security measures (processes and tools) to increase the safety of your information, and protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
Our secure server software (SSL) is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure commerce transactions.
All your financial information is encrypted as it travels over the Internet, and is stored in encrypted form.
Additionally, all partners who process personal information on our behalf are obliged to commit to ensuring they operate appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
How long do we retain your information?
We will only retain, and permit our data processors to retain, your information while your account (paid or free) with us is open, to comply with our legal obligations, to resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
Accessing Your Data
You can always review, update or change personal information from within your account. ExpertFlyer.com will also, when you request, provide you with information about whether we hold, or process on behalf of a third party, any of your personal information.
Please contact us at if you need assistance in reviewing your information. ExpertFlyer.com will respond to your access request to review the information we have on file for you within one month.

EEA/UK Addendum
Data controller
Any personal information processed by ExpertFlyer.com in connection with this privacy policy is controlled by Red Ventures, which is considered the "data controller" of your personal information under European Union and UK data protection law.
Our address is Expert Flyer, 1101 Red Ventures Drive, Fort Mill, SC 29707, USA
Legal grounds for processing
We can only use your personal data if we have a lawful basis for doing so. Here are the reasons why we process your data:
Consent
Where you have agreed to our use of your personal data (for example, when you sign up to receive our newsletter and promotional emails).
Legitimate interest
When it is in legitimate interest to collect and use your personal data, and our interests do not override your data-protection interests or fundamental rights and freedoms. (For example, for providing, enhancing, and securing our products and services, and for our marketing activities).
Contractual Obligation
When you create an account on our website or otherwise agree to our terms and conditions, a contract is formed between you and us. To carry out our obligations under that contract we must process the information you give us, as described above.
Data Subject Rights
EEA residents have certain other rights defined in the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
Consent Withdrawal
You have the right to withdraw your consent in relation to our processing of your personal data based on your consent. You can unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time using the "unsubscribe" link in any emails we send you.
Erasure
You have the right to ask us to right to request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
Restriction of Processing
You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Object to Processing
You have the right to object to the use or disclosures of your personal data.
Data Portability
You have the right to request we transmit your personal data to you or another company in certain circumstances.
We process all requests in accordance with the law, and there may be legal reasons why we cannot fulfil all requests.
Please contact us at if you wish to make a request. We have one month to respond to you. You are not required to pay any charge for exercising your rights.
