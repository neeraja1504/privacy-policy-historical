This statement discloses the privacy practices of Alyans LLC
Alyans LLC is a primary operator and owner of several websites and blogs from which this statement is linked.
Please direct questions regarding this statement to the Support.
Customer Information: All Alyans LLC customer account information is held in strict confidence. We do not sell or share our customer list with any other entities. Customer information such as name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number, and billing information is collected for the sole purpose of providing services described by the product purchased.
Subscribers Information: All information provided by email subscribers (e-mail addresses and other associated information) are held in strict confidence. It is only used to deliver follow up messages and promised related offers. Each email contains a one click unsubscribe option and a full address to request manual removal from follow up list. Alyans LLC does not sell or share your with any other party.
Affiliate Information: All Affiliate information, including address, phone number, e-mail address, and tax id number is held in strict confidence. This information is collected for the sole purpose of generating commission checks and meeting United States federal tax requirements.
Alayns LLC is independently owned and opperated by Alex Sysoef and this Privacy Policy applies to all sites that are part of the portfolio.
