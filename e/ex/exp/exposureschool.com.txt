Privacy Policy - Exposure School
ExposureSchool.com collects some personal information from visitors, such as your email address. Email addresses are only collected if visitors choose to subscribe to receive updates from us. Subscribers can unsubscribe at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link that is included at the bottom of each email we send. We use the email list to stay in touch with visitors who would like to receive updates about our site and content, and we do not sell customer information to 3rd parties. If you have questions regarding this privacy policy please contact us.
