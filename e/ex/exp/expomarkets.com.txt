Disclaimer :: Australian - Indian Exporters Directory,India
ExpoMarkets.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy of our customers and users. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for ExpoMarkets.com and all other applicable ExpoMarkets.com partner sites.
We log your IP address, browser type, access times, and referring site to help diagnose potential problems with our server, and to administer our Web Site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.
Our site uses cookies to keep track of your user account. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, to save your username so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site, and for other purposes. For our site to function properly, you need to have cookies enabled.
Our site's registration form asks users to give us contact information such as name, phone number and e-mail address. We use customers' contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company, the services we offer and relevant messages from our partners. Customers' contact information is also used for contact with regards to technical issues or information maintenance when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving non-technical mailings; please see the choice/opt-out section below.
This site contains links to other sites and locations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitors' contact information such as name, phone number e-mail address. Contact information from the order form is used to send orders, information about our company and relevant messages from our partners. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
An outside company is used to display ads on our site(s). These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, our ad company may also collect cookies received with banner ads, and we do not control, nor do we have access to or usage of this information.
Security
this site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control and we will make every effort to maintain the security and privacy of that information.
Supplementation Information
This site supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from our ExpoMarkets.com partners and us.
Removal/Discontinuance of Service
This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive services from our company.
