NameMedia: Privacy Policy
NameMedia collects and maintains certain information (including e-mail addresses, IP addresses, names, geographic information, etc.) on many of its Web sites and via price request and other forms (online and offline). This is done to help us provide our customers with the best customer service and with valuable information regarding relevant products and services from NameMedia and appropriate third parties.
NameMedia is dedicated to protecting personal information and will make every reasonable effort to handle collected information appropriately. All information collected, as well as related requests, will be handled as carefully and efficiently as possible in accordance with NameMedia's standards for integrity and objectivity. Email originating at NameMedia contains clear and efficient instructions for opting out of future emails of its kind.
If you need assistance in updating the information you have provided to NameMedia or you have questions or comments about these policies, contact NameMedia's manager of customer information.
