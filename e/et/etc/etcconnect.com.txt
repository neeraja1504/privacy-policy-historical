Electronic Theatre Controls’ Privacy, Cookie, Terms of Use, and Acceptable Use Policies
Privacy Policy
Last Updated: October 22, 2019
This Privacy Policy describes the information collected through this website “Site” and how that information is used and shared. Electronic Theatre Controls, Inc., and its affiliates, (“ETC,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) may collect or receive the types of information described below in connection with your access to and use of the Site, including your account registration on the Site and any information you may post to the site (together, “Collected Information”).
Please read this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding Collected Information and how we use and share it. By accessing, using or submitting information via this Site, you agree on behalf of yourself and any company that you represent (together, “you” and “your”) to this Privacy Policy, our Terms of Use Policy, and our Acceptable Use Policy, in addition to ETC’s use and sharing of information as discussed below. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use this Site.
This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and made a part of our Terms of Use.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may amend or otherwise modify this Privacy Policy at any time. All modifications will take effect immediately upon posting of the updated Privacy Policy on the Site. By accessing or using the Site after modifications are posted, you agree to those modifications. Material changes will be conspicuously posted on the Site. You are encouraged to revisit this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to review any changes that have been made. The date on which this Privacy Policy was last updated is noted at the beginning of this Privacy Policy.
1. ###### **General**
ETC respects the privacy of all visitors to the Site. Whether you provide us with your personally identifiable information is your decision. You do not have to tell us who you are or reveal anything further about yourself to visit this Site. We collect only the information necessary to maintain a satisfactory business relationship with you or your company.
Notwithstanding the above, we may collect and use other information that may be considered personally identifiable information in some jurisdictions (such as your IP address) in order to make our website accessible to you. Such information will automatically be transmitted by your computer. Without your explicit consent, we will not use such information for any other purpose than making the Site accessible to you.
This Privacy Policy does not apply to information that we may collect through any medium other than the Site (e.g., collected offline), or to information collected by any person or entity other than us (such as a third-party website or a third-party application).

When you access and interact with the Site, we will make temporary use of other information in addition to the PII mentioned above as technically necessary in order to make the Site accessible to you, including the IP address of the device you are using to access the Site. For example, our servers may receive and record information about your computer, device, and browser, including potentially the IP address, browser type, and other software or hardware information. This information is never used to identify you personally and never combined with other personal data such as PII stored in your MyETC account or otherwise sent to us through our website. Such information includes the following:
1. **Usage Details and IP Address.** As you navigate through this Site, details of your visits to this Site, including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, logs, the resources that you access, and information about your computer and internet connection, including your truncated IP address, operating system and browser type may be automatically transmitted by your computer. 3. **Web beacons**. "Web beacons" are electronic images, tags or scripts that allow a website to access cookies and help track general usage patterns of visitors to a website. Web beacons can recognize certain types of information, such as cookies, time and date of a page view and a description of the page where the Web beacon is placed. We may use Web beacons to compile information regarding use of this Site.
