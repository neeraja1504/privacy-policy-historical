Times Internet Limited, CIN U74999DL1999PLC135531, (hereinafter, referred as the "Company"), incorporated in India under the Companies Act, 1956, having the registered office at Express Building, 9-10, Bahadurshah Zafar Marg, New Delhi -110 002 and corporate office at #391, Udyog Vihar, Phase III, Gurgaon -122 016, Haryana, India.
The Company develops, manages and operates the website hosted as www.etmoney.com ("Website") and the mobile application 'ETMONEY' (the "App"). This Privacy Policy is a part of the Terms of Use and is also applicable to services provided by the Group Companies on Website and/or App.
Please read this Privacy Policy along with the Terms of Use linked separately to this Website/App. All terms defined in the Terms of Use have the same meaning used in this Privacy Policy. For the purpose of this Policy, wherever the context so requires "you" or "your" shall mean User and the term "we", "us", "our" shall mean the Company and its Group Companies.
This Privacy Policy is published in compliance of:
Section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000; and
Regulation 4 of the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Information) Rules, 2011 (the "SPI Rules") "Personal Information" and "Sensitive personal data or information" shall be as defined under the SPI Rules. supplied by Users and

The Users unconditionally agree that by registering on the Website/ App and/or using the services of the Group Companies, he /she signifies his/her unconditional assent to this Privacy Policy including the collection, storage and use of the personal information provided by him/ her for availing the various services.
We respect the privacy of the Users and are committed to protect it in all respects. Any dispute with the Company over privacy matters are subject to this Privacy Policy read in conjunction with the Terms of Use.

COOKIES
We may use "cookies", or similar electronic tools to collect information, to assign each User a unique random number as a User Identification (User ID) in order to understand the User's individual interests using your computer/laptop/notebook or mobile/tablet/pad/handheld device etc. We will have no way of knowing who you are, even if we assign a cookie to your computer/laptop/notebook or mobile/tablet/pad/handheld device etc., unless you voluntarily identify yourself (through registration, for example). The only Personal Information a cookie can contain is information you supply. A cookie cannot read data off your hard drive. Our advertisers may also assign their own cookies to your browser (if you click on their ads), a process that we do not control. We receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us via Website/ App through your computer/laptop/notebook or mobile/tablet/pad/handheld device etc.
Log File Information
When you visit our Website / App, we automatically collect limited information about the connection of your computer/laptop/notebook or mobile/tablet/pad/handheld device etc. to the Internet, mobile number, including your IP address. Your IP address is a number that lets computers attached to the Internet know where to send you data -- such as the pages you view. We automatically receive log information from your computer/laptop/notebook or mobile/tablet/pad/handheld device etc., including, but not limited to, your location, IP address, device name and its serial number or unique identification number, your device operating system, browser type and version, CPU speed and connection speed.
Information from other Sources
We may receive information about you from other sources, add it to our account information and treat it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide information to the platform provider or other partner, whom we provide services, your account information and order information may be passed on to us. We may obtain updated contact information from third parties in order to update our records and fulfil the services or to communicate with you.
Demographic and User Behaviour
We may refer other sources of demographic and other information related to User behaviour in order to provide you with more targeted communications and promotions. We use Google Analytics among others, to track the User Behaviour and derive product related insight for our Website/App. Google Analytics specifically has been enabled to support display advertising towards helping us gain understanding of our Users' Demographics and Interests. If you don't want Analytics to be used in your browser, you can install the Google Analytics Opt out browser add-on.

How collected data is used?
The information as supplied by the User enables us to improve the services (including advertising and personalization on the Website/App), provide you the most user-friendly experience and for developing new services.
We may share user personal information and/or transactional data with our Group Companies / associates/ vendors / business partners for promoting/offering their product and services.
We may use your email address for non-marketing or administrative purposes (such as notifying you of major changes, for customer service purposes, billing, etc.).
When you register with the Website/ App, we contact you from time to time for the purpose of updating of your personal information to provide the User, such features that we believe may benefit / interest you.
If you interact with social media in connection with the Website/ App, such as the Facebook Like button, or use your social media credentials to log-in or post content, these features may collect information about your use of the Website/ App or the product and services availed through the Website / App, as well as post information about your activities on the social media service. Your interactions with social media companies are governed by their respective privacy policies.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements when you visit our Website / App or use the product and services availed through the Website / App. When we present information to our advertisers -- to help them understand our audience and confirm the value of advertising on our Website/ App-- it is usually in the form of aggregated statistics on traffic to various pages / content within our Website/ App. These companies may use information (including, but not limited to, your name, address, email address or telephone number or any personally identifiable information) about your visits to or use of, particular website, mobile application or services, in order to provide advertisements about products and services that may interest you.

Accessing, correcting or deleting your personal information
When you use the Company's Website/ App, we make good faith efforts to provide you, as and when requested, with access to your Personal Information so that any Personal Information or sensitive personal data or information found to be inaccurate or deficient shall be corrected or amended as feasible, subject to any requirement of retaining such Personal Information or sensitive personal data or information by law or for legitimate business purposes.
We encourage you to review, update and correct the personal information that we maintain about you, and you may request that we delete personal information about you that is inaccurate, incomplete or irrelevant for legitimate purposes, or are being processed in a way which infringes any applicable legal requirement. If you would like to review, change or delete personal information we have collected from you or permanently delete your account, please contact the ET Money's Grievance Officer.
We ask Users to identify themselves and the information requested to be accessed, corrected or removed before processing such requests. We may decline to process requests that are unreasonably repetitive, excessive, require disproportionate technical effort, jeopardize the privacy of others, appears to be suspicious of the request being received from person other the User or would be extremely impractical (for instance, requests concerning information residing on backup tapes) or if the information / material you request relates to existing or anticipated legal proceedings between you and us or providing access to you would prejudice negotiations between us or an investigation of possible unlawful activity. In any case, where we provide information access and correction, we perform this service free of charge, except if doing so would require a disproportionate effort. After you delete your information, residual copies may take a period of time before they are deleted from our active servers. However, the information may remain in our backup systems for a longer period.
Your right to review, update, correct and delete your information is subject to our records retention policies and applicable law, including any statutory retention requirements.
Retention of Data
We shall retain the records as per the PMLA (Prevention of Money Laundering Act 2002) guidelines and such other statutory / regulatory requirements from time to time. In the event any legal / regulatory proceeding is pending, we can retain records for a longer period as may be required by regulatory / statutory authorities.
We may keep our records of the electronic instructions/transactions in any form as permitted under applicable Laws. In this regard, all records, whether in electronic form, magnetic form, documents or any other form with respect to electronic instructions/online transactions shall be conclusive evidence of such instructions/transactions and shall be binding on the User. The User shall not request/demand any evidence or proof for the transactions undertaken through the internet and the audit trail of the log-in would be conclusive proof to establish the bona fides of the transactions. In the event of any dispute, records kept by us/ business partners/ third party shall be binding as conclusive evidence of the transactions carried out through the electronic mode.
How we protect your personal information?
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. These include internal reviews of our data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures, including appropriate encryption and physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data. All information gathered on the Company's Website / App is securely stored within controlled database. The database is stored on servers secured behind a firewall; access to the servers is password-protected and is strictly limited. However, as effective as our security measures are, no security system is impenetrable. We can neither guarantee the security of our database nor can we guarantee that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the internet. And, of course, any information you include in a posting to the discussion areas is available to anyone with internet access. We assume no liability or responsibility for disclosure of your information due to errors in transmission, unauthorized third-party access, or other causes beyond our control. You play an important role in keeping your personal information secure.
