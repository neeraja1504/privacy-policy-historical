| Privacy Policy
When you access this site, it is necessary for you to provide JAT Software with certain confidential information in order for you to use this site. That information will be recorded on JAT Software's servers. This information will be used only to provide you with the requested services and will not be sold, rented or otherwise shared with any third party other than your company or operating companies, subsidiaries, related entities and affiliates, without your prior written approval. Your company has entered into a confidentiality agreement with JAT Software that also requires that your information be held confidential by JAT Software and will not be seen by any employee or consultant who is not bound by the terms of the Confidentiality Agreement.
