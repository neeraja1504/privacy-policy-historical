English Grammar 101 - Privacy Policy
by Kitty Nash
When visiting English Grammar 101 , you can rest assured that we will never compromise your personal privacy or share your personal information without first gaining your permission. We understand that our visitors are our number one asset, and we would never knowingly do anything to break the trust you have bestowed upon us.
Any information you provide is used only to help maintain your account or enhance your experience at our website. We will never sell any of your information to a third party.
For further information, contact us at support@cingletree.com.
