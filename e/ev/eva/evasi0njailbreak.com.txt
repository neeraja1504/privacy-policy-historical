Privacy Policy
At Jailbreak Evasi0n Info, we employ the use of third-party advertising companies to serve adverts to you when visiting us.
The aforementioned companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to both Evasi0n Info and other sites in order to adequately provide adverts pertaining to goods and services that genuinely interest you.
The Bottom Line: Privacy is Important
If you would like additional information concerning this typical practice, or to learn how to opt out of the anonymous collection of your visits to improve the relevancy of ads, navigate to http://networkadvertising.org/ inside your browser of choice.
As a final note, and for additional peace of mind, all information sent from your browser to our server is fully encrypted through our SSL certificate and none of the information used in the comment section of Jailbreak Evasi0n Info’s articles is saved.
