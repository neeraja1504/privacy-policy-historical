About EveryDiet.org
We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy practices. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.
Cookies
A temporary (browser-based) cookie is used to prevent any advertising from being presented twice. This cookie is not persisted on your computer.
Other information
IP addresses are held temporarily in a log in order to track user traffic patterns to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web site and to gather broad demographic information (examples include which pages are more popular, referring urls, browser versions and plugins) so that we may continue to bring you content that best fits your interests.
Email addresses
We allow users to add their emails to our monthly newsletter. This is a double opt-in process (i.e. the user must confirm), and in accordance with all laws, the user can unsubscribe at any time.
User Reviews
Comments may be entered on diet reviews and these are stored in our database along with the users name. Information is not passed on to outside parties. We suggest useing a nickname or handle whenever entering any information on-line
Outside Links
This site contains links to other sites. Every Diet is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please feel free to contact us.
Third Party Advertising
Advertising is served by Google.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
