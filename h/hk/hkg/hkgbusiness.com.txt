Our Privacy Policy - Hong Kong Business Directory
Hello, we are HKG Business. Hong Kong Leading Business Directory, this is our privacy policy, and we appreicate you spending time reviewing it.
If you have any questions or require more information, please feel free to contact us at our email address, hello@hkgbusiness.com.
HKG Business values the privacy of our visitors and your privacy is of extreme importance to us. This page will outline the types of personal information that are received and collected by hkgbusiness.com and how it's used.
Server Logging
HKGBusiness.com is hosted on our own dedicated webserver and like most website on this internet, HKGBusiness.com will log your Internet Protocol Address, Browser Type (User-Agent), Date and Time of Visit, Referrer Page information, the pages you visited and other relevant information for us to analyse trends and to administer our website.
Cookies
Our site use cookies to store session information to help identify our visitors public available information. We may use this information to customise content according to your visit pattern.
Third Party
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website.
Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our websites and/or other sites on the Internet.
You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
Alternatively, you may also choose to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
We have no access to or control over these cookies used by our Third party vendors or advertisers.
If you have more questions, you should consult the respective privacy policy of our third party vendors on thier website on their practices, and how you may opt out from these cookies.
