Privacy Statement
Any personal information requested or gathered via this site will be used only for purposes necessary to conduct library business with you. It will not be traded, sold, or given to any other party except as specifically allowed or required by law.
Regarding e-mail
Under Florida law, e-mail addresses are public records. If you do not want your e-mail address released in response to a public-records request, do not send electronic mail to this entity. Instead, contact this office by phone or in writing.
