Privacy Policy | The Humane Society of the United States
The Humane Society of the United States (The HSUS) is committed to your right to privacy and to keeping your personal information private. You can visit the Humane Society of the United States website without telling us who you are or revealing any identifying information about yourself. However, because our website includes areas in which visitors can provide personally identifying information, we have developed this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and practices regarding information we collect about you.
