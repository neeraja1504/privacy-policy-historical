Privacy Policy – HULKPOP
To visitor:
Your privacy is important to us. This website does not collect any personally identifiable information for regular website users. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
Third Party Advertisements:
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use
information (including your cookies and device id) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. Cookies
