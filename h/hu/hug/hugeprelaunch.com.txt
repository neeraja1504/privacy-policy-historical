Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
HugePreLaunch.com is committed to customer service and we intend to uphold this by providing a private and secure browsing experience at HugePreLaunch.com. The following policy will help you to understand how we will treat your personal information. As we continue to improve our site and as we take advantage of developments in technology, this policy may change from time to time so please check this policy on an ongoing basis for revisions and updates.
Information Collection and Use
HugePreLaunch.com collects information from our web site in several ways. During registration, HugePreLaunch.com asks for information such as your name, email address, and phone. Once you have submitted this information and chosen a valid password, you will be able to take full advantage of everything HugePreLaunch.com has to offer. You may also be asked for personal information when you report a problem with our site, or services; or, if you otherwise contact HugePreLaunch.com.
HugePreLaunch.com is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others. Information is collected with the sole purpose of providing HugePreLaunch.com services.
HugePreLaunch.com will occasionally send you e-mail updates leading to the official pre-launch announcement. To ensure you receive these updates add HugePreLaunch.com to your address book.
You may unsubscribe from the information updates without any future correspondence or questions at anytime chosen.
