Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process and disclose your information including personal information with regard to your access to and use of the Website. We process personal data only in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Personal Information
We collect your personal information that we receive from you when you use the Website, when you register to use Website and/or use any of our services and when you communicate with us via e-mail.
Such information may include: username, email address, password.
We use, store and process this information for example to allow your access to the restricted sections and use the Website.
We do not share information with third parties and do not publish it anywhere.
We use cookies to collect information about the use of the Website.
Cookies are text files that the browser places to the user's device memory when they visit our Website.
We use cookies to identify a registered user, save search preferences and maintain browsing statistics.
