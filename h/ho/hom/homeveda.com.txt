Privacy Policy - Homeveda
HomeVeda understands the importance of maintaining privacy of personal information of its users. This summary of our privacy policy is intended to give you an overview of:
What information we collect about you
How we use your information
How and with who we may share information we have about you
How to contact us with questions or concerns
Information Collected About You We may collect “Personal Information” about you – such as your name, address, telephone number, email address or health information – in the following ways:
When you register on the HomeVeda website or mobile apps
When you sign-up for newsletters or other communications from us
When you participate in an online survey
When you provide personal information in comments or contact us section
We may collect “Non-Personal Information” – information that cannot be used by us to identify you – via Cookies, Web Beacons, mobile applications and from external sources, even if you have not registered with or provided any Personal Information to HomeVeda.
How we use your information The broad idea is to understand you as user better and personalize your experience on HomeVeda properties. Specifically, we may use your information for the following:
Administer your account
Provide your personalized content recommendations
Respond to your inquiries
Obtain your feedback
Statistically analyze user behavior and activity on HomeVeda
Send you newsletters, personalized emails or any other relevant information
How and with who we may share information we have about you We will not disclose any ‘Personal Information’ about you unless you specifically give us a permission to do so or in case needed for:
To comply with any legal requirements
Situations which can be considered as a threat to you or others
How to contact us with questions or concerns Please send us an email using the Contact Us page.
