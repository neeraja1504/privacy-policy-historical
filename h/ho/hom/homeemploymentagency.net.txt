Privacy « Home Employment Agency
Collection of your Personal Information
www.homeemploymentagency.net collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Profit System Online also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.
There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by homeemploymentagency.net. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by homeemploymentagency.net for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of homeemploymentagency.net Web site.
Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through homeemploymentagency.net public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: homeemploymentagency.net does not read any of your private online communications.
homeemploymentagency.net encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from homeemploymentagency.net so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. homeemploymentagency.net is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of homeemploymentagency.net and homeemploymentagency.net family of Web sites.
Use of your Personal Information
homeemploymentagency.net collects and uses your personal information to operate homeemploymentagency.net Web site and deliver the services you have requested. homeemploymentagency.net also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from homeemploymentagency.net and its affiliates. homeemploymentagency.net may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.
homeemploymentagency.net does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. homeemploymentagency.net may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, homeemploymentagency.net may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to homeemploymentagency.net, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
homeemploymentagency.net does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.
homeemploymentagency.net keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within homeemploymentagency.net, in order to determine what homeemploymentagency.net services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within homeemploymentagency.net to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.
homeemploymentagency.net Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on homeemploymentagency.net or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of homeemploymentagency.net; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of homeemploymentagency.net, or the public.
Use of Cookies
homeemploymentagency.net Web site uses “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize homeemploymentagency.net pages, or register with homeemploymentagency.net site or services, a cookie helps homeemploymentagency.net to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same homeemploymentagency.net Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use homeemploymentagency.net features that you customized.
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of homeemploymentagency.net services or Web sites you visit.
Security of your Personal Information
homeemploymentagency.net secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. homeemploymentagency.net secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
By viewing this Web site, you agree that you have waived any and all claims of any kind or nature that could be brought against this website concerning the loss, alteration or misuse of information. You must be aware that it is possible for your personal data to potentially be obtained by unauthorized users, such as “hackers” and used in an inappropriate manner that may result in harm to you. In the event that your personal information is subject to unauthorized access, you acknowledge and agree that homeemploymentagency.net is not responsible for the actions of the unauthorized users, and you waive your right to file any claims or causes of action against homeemploymentagency.net arising out of the unauthorized use of your personal information.
Changes to this Statement
homeemploymentagency.net will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. homeemploymentagency.net encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how homeemploymentagency.net is protecting your information.
