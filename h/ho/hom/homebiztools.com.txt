HomeBizTools Privacy Statement
HomeBizTools, owned and operated by B-K Publications, has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.
First and foremost, we strongly state that at no time and under no circumstances is any collected information disclosed to third parties or unauthorized persons.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor.
This site gives users the option of using our Contact Form for the purpose of removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service.
A users request to opt-out of receiving further communications is honored immediately. Any and all information pertaining to the user is removed and completely purged from our records without delay.
Outbound Links
This site contains links to other sites. HomeBizTools is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Acceptable Use
At no time and under no circumstances is any collected information disclosed to third parties or unauthorized persons.
Our site uses an order form, online forms, and autoresponders for customers to request information, products, and services. We may collect visitor’s contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers). Contact information is used to send orders and information about our company to our customers. The customer’s contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section above. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. At no time and under no circumstances is any collected information disclosed to third parties or unauthorized persons.
Correct/Update/Change/Modify
This site gives users the following options for correcting, updating, changing and modifying information previously provided through the site’s Contact Form.
