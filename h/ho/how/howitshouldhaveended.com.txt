Capture of Your Personal Information
As with many other websites, HISHE may gather information from you in two ways: actively and passively.
Active Information Collection
We actively collect information from you through your communication with us by email, feedback forms or registration information given to us by you.  Some of the information requested will be personally identifiable, such as name, address, phone, email, etc.  In order to register to use our website, you must provide your name, address, phone number, and email.   **   **While it is possible for users to access and view Content without creating an account, you can create an account for your convenience by entering a user name and password, which is then stored for future use.  It is your responsibility to protect your user name and password.  Use a variety of numbers, letters (both uppercase and lowercase), and symbols to create a secure password.  We may use the information provided to contact you for customer service reasons _.  _Regardless of whether you join the Website, we may use your email address, if you provide it to us, to respond to you or for other business purposes.  We reserve the right to retain any personal information that you provide.
Some areas of the Website may require you to submit additional information in order for you to benefit from the specified features (such as newsletter subscriptions or promotional offers). You will be informed at each information collection point what information is required and what information is optional _.  _You always have the option of opting not to use the additional features if you do not wish to supply the requested information.
Passive Information Collection
As you navigate through a website, certain information can be passively collected (meaning it is gathered without you actively providing the information) using various technologies and means, such as navigational data collection.  This Website will track IP addresses, assign internet tags and use both session and persistent cookies.  Cookies are small pieces of information (name-value pairs) stored on your computer’s hard drive by a website.  The website stores the data and later receives it back.  Our Website can only receive back the information it has stored on your hard drive and cannot look at any other material on your computer.  We use the cookies for personalization, to save member ids and passwords, gather information about areas you visit on our Website, or they may be used to deliver material to you that is specific to your interests.  Cookies help us to provide you with a better and more useful website.  You can adjust your web browser to either accept or deny cookies.  However, not accepting cookies can limit your use of our Website and possibly make some features unavailable.
Use of Your Information
The information that you provide to HISHE is used by us to complete your registration for a User Account, deliver content or services to you, provide information or for other purposes explained at the time we collect the information.  We may use any of your personal information given to contact you regarding future events, new features or new products that we think you may be interested in using or purchasing.  We may also provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, and traffic patterns on our Website, or other additional information.  Aggregate statistics are generated by combining data from any given or related category or categories.  We will also share your personal information in order to (1) comply with a court order or other legal process, (2) protect our rights or privacy, or (3) enforce our terms of use policy.
HISHE may provide your personal information to any successor or to any person or entity that acquires ownership or control of an HISHE site, whether as part of a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all of a Website’s assets and/or services or otherwise. You agree and consent to the disclosure to, and use by, any such successor or subsequent owner or operator, and your continued use of the Website signifies your agreement to be bound by such successor’s or subsequent owner or operator’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
Other than the aforementioned reasons, we will not sell, lease, or distribute your personal information to any third parties.

This Website is not intended for use by children under the age of thirteen.  We will not knowingly collect information from or accept user account registrations from anyone under thirteen years of age.  It is your responsibility to provide accurate and honest information when registering.  HISHE and howitshouldhaveended.com cannot be held responsible for users who provide inaccurate or incorrect data when registering for an account.  We only use the data you provide and have no way of checking the accuracy of the information given.  If you are a parent or guardian who has discovered that your child under the age of thirteen has submitted his or her personally identifiable information without your permission or consent, upon notice from you, HISHE will make reasonable efforts to remove the information.

We encourage our users to be aware of this when they leave howitshouldhaveended.com and to read the privacy statements of each website that collects personal information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by HISHE from the Website.
Security
HISHE have ended understands and is aware of your privacy concerns.  We strive to only collect personal information that is necessary or required to make you use of the Website a satisfying and efficient experience.  We will take all reasonable efforts to ensure that whatever information you provide to use will be maintained in a secure environment.  However, unfortunately, no data transmissions over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.  As a result, we cannot guarantee the security of any information that you transmit to us, and you provide such information at your own risk.

You should always keep your user id and password confidential.  If you ever feel like your user id or password has been compromised, you should notify us and change them immediately.  You should also periodically check your personal information that has been stored to your account.  If you see any discrepancy, you should immediately contact us to request that the error be corrected.
