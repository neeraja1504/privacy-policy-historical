Privacy Policy of the Free HoPaLi Homepage Service
Privacy Policy of HoPaLi
HoPaLi does not sell, share or trade user e-mail addresses or any other user account information.
HoPaLi account information is submitted and communicated through a secure and encrypted SSL connection.
The HoPaLi account system uses "Sessions", also known as "Cookies", to keep track of signed in users within the secure HoPaLi website and domain. All session data is transferred over HoPaLi's secure, encrypted connection.
HoPaLi does not collect any personal information from account users other than e-mail address, and HoPaLi is not interested in gathering personal or private information from its users.
HoPaLi does not track the sites that users link to and visit, nor does HoPaLi track or follow the users themselves.
