Privacy Policy Statement
Our physical address is:
House Industries
1145 Yorklyn Road
Yorklyn, DE 19736-0166
We can be reached via e-mail from our contact page or you can reach us by telephone at 302-234-2356.
We do not share, sell, trade or otherwise distribute personal information of any kind to any other organization, company or other entity.
We collect information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by visitors, such as physical addresses for mailing catalogs. We send periodic email updates to those people who have given us their email and given us permission to do so. We only use cookies to keep track of items added to the shopping cart. Any personal information offered by site visitors is passed through a secure connection.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future or if you want us to delete any of your personal information, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address, calling us at the above telephone number, writing to us at the above address.
