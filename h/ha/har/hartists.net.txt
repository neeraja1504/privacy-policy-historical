Privacy Policy - hArtists
This is the website of hArtists. You may E-Mail us at webmaster@hartists.net. We strongly believe in your right to privacy. This document outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our website, as well as some of the steps we take to make sure this information is not abused by third parties. We hope this document will aid you in making an informed decision about sharing your personal information with us.
For each visitor to our web page, our website software automatically recognizes the visitor's IP address or domain name, browser type, operating system, and preferred content language. This information may be used to customize the content and/or layout of our website for each individual visitor, or for internal research purposes and statistics generation.
We use cookies to record session information such as username and password hash for automatic log in. We also use cookies to customize the content on our website based on the information we have on file for the visitor. Cookies are small text files stored in your web browser's cache and are only accessible by the website that set them.
The "Profile" page allows registered users to provide their real name, gender, country of residence, and date of birth if they opt to do so. If the information is provided, it will be displayed publically on the users' "User Page." This information can be removed at any time by blanking the fields where it was entered.
From time to time, we may use the information that you provided for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our Privacy Policy. If this happens at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our website to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should periodically review this privacy policy.
Upon request we grant registered users access to all information (including proprietary information) that we maintain about them. Visitors can access this information and have it corrected by sending an E-Mail to us at the above address.
With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. However, information you provide may be disclosed when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our website and applicable laws, or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of hArtists.
If you have any concerns regarding how the information you provide is used, please E-Mail us and we will provide any necessary clarification.
