We cannot guarantee its absolutely safe.
Information Collection:
All the information and tips given on our site is collected from internet and some other useful sources.
Cookies:
Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored in your computer’s hard drive. Like many sites we use “cookies” to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site.

Our site may contain links to other sites that are not operate by us. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to the third party site. So, we strongly advice you to review our privacy policy of every site you visit.
Hairlosstreatmentstip has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for the content, privacy policy or practices of any third person sites or services.

Hair loss treatments tip site will update policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this site. You are advice to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
