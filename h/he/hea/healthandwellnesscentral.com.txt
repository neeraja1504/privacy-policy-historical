Healthandwellnesscentral.com Privacy Policy
We are glad to welcome you to the Healthandwellnesscentral.com. Please read this privacy statement to ensure that we are committed to keeping secure the privacy of our authors' details.
What information do we collect?
Since Healthandwellnesscentral.com is providing you the facility and support to submit and read health related articles, we may require from you some information that may be considered as personally identifiable.
Please provide us with the following information about yourself:
username
real name
password
e-mail address
postal address
Author Anonymity
Healthandwellnesscentral.com does not provide or guarantee author anonymity. Authors who wish to remain anonymous should either NOT complete the Author Profile in their member accounts OR complete it using a Pen Name. Although discovering an Author's real identity is difficult if a Pen Name is used in submitting articles, there are several ways for that information to be found if the Author Profile is completed with your real name and identity details.
Information Use
If you are Healthandwellnesscentral.com author member, your use of our service constitutes a business relationship wherein we provide a promotion service for your article(s). In exchange, Healthandwellnesscentral.com reserves the right to use the contact information collected during member registration to deliver notices regarding changes or updates to our service, to provide a free newsletter, or to send announcements and carefully screened offers and information on behalf of our partners or advertisers.
You may remove yourself from any or all of our mailings at anytime using the unsubscribe methods provided in those mailings. Any mailing received from our company is clearly identified and includes a valid email address and unsubscribe link which you can use to remove yourself from future mailings. Should you encounter problems in this regard, contact editor[at]healthandwellnesscentral.com.
Spam complaints will result in membership termination.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
Healthandwellnesscentral.com maintains your email address and other pertinent information in a secure database. Your contact information is never released or sold to any vendor, partner or outside agency. Healthandwellnesscentral.com, however, reserves the right to contact Healthandwellnesscentral.com members via email as deemed necessary. Such mailings will always include detailed unsubscribe information, providing members the option of removing themselves from future Healthandwellnesscentral.com mailings at any time.
Security
Your email address information is stored in a protected directory. Access is restricted to Healthandwellnesscentral.com staff.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
Healthandwellnesscentral.com may amend this policy from time to time. If significant changes are made in the way your personal information is used, we will post a prominent announcement on our site notifying you of the change.
