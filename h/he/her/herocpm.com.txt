Privacy Policy
HeroCPM is a trusty network in the field of online advertising.
We have our own privacy policies to guide the visitors how we use their personal information when they visit or joining our service.
1. When registering, you need to fill in registation form correctly: your name, your e-mail/ mailing address, your phone number, password. We use this information to contact you whenever we have new notifications...
2. We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
3. In case we contact to the third-party, we make sure that your personal information is carefully protected and not revealed by many security measures. Only Hero CPM staff can access your information.
4. However, your personal information may be disclosed without your agreement if we have enough reasons to believe that it is important to investigate, or complaint, bring legal actions against users.
5. HeroCPM uses cookies to provide online services and information for website owners. The cookies have many functions: enable webstise to work, remember user preferences, let users navigate between pages, improve the users’ experience. We make sure that cookies are very safe for your personal account or any files.
6. We always try hard to provide the users with the best services, especially secure all information you submit online. But, please remmeber that the information you give out online may be collected by people we can not find out.
Before joining our systems, you also need to consider our terms and conditions. If you do not agree, we politely suggest you to have a look at other networks.
