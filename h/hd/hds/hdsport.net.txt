Privacy Policy – HDSport.net
HDSport.net is not collecting any personal information. We will never ask you for your name, email, address, phone or anything other information which might be considered a personal information. We do use cookies to improve our website functionality. Our cookies are not used for collection personal information. We collect information about your IP address, OS and web browser through our web server logs. This information is not shared with third parties, unless required by law.
We might show 3rd party banners (Ad Server JavaScript tags) which could collect personal information according to their privacy policy. We do use Google Analytics and Google DoubleClick Demographics reports. If you don’t want to be track by these web analytics tools, please visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en .
