Privacy Policy | HIOX Softwares Private Limited
Information collected by Hiox.com from users:
Hiox.com provides services in software/application development, offshore IT services, business solutions, web content development, web hosting, domain registration, internet solutions and web design. You can view information, updates, etc of Hiox.com without any registration or providing any personal details. Personal, identifiable details are collected by the site only in the “Careers” section when you look for a career with Hiox.com and update your details. Apart from that, Hiox.com gathers the following information when you use the site: the full Uniform Resource Locator (URL) of the web page from which you visited Hiox.com, your Internet Protocol (IP) address which may include a domain name, server name, content type from the page headers.
For what purpose the information is used by Hiox.com:
Hiox.com gathers information from you only to provide you with a better service. It is collected only for internal records and to prepare reports based on figures. Hiox.com uses cookies for verifying that you are a Hiox.com user and it also uses for counting and tracking your number of visits to Hiox.com. In addition, it also uses cookies to determine the number of visitors that came to the Hiox.com website through a particular advertisement. Hiox.com uses IP address to gather broad demographic information such as your general geographic location and Internet Service Provider, server type, content type etc. This demographic information may also be used by Hiox.com to analyze aggregate web usage behavior.
Security of your information in Hiox.com:
Hiox.com makes sure that the information of the users is protected from hackers and abusive users. It uses highly advanced computer security safeguards to protect its databases and servers against risks of loss, unauthorized access, destruction, misuse, modification, or inadvertent or improper disclosure of data. These risks, however, cannot be completely eliminated and Hiox.com therefore cannot guarantee complete security in exceptional situations. Hiox.com segregates certain personally identifiable information provided by you.
Restricted Access to the information:
The access to the above mentioned information gathered from the users’ computer is restricted only to the admin of Hiox.com. The data are used only internally and stored in the Hiox.com database. Hiox.com does not provide the information to any third-party site or customers and hence the security of the details is ensured.
Links to other sites:
Hiox.com website may have links to various other sites and Hiox.com is not responsible for the privacy and security of the personal information collected by those sites from you.
Changes in the policy:
Privacy policy is subjected to change periodically as the contents of the site get updated and new features are introduced dynamically. Though Hiox.com informs users about the changes, it is your responsibility too to check the policy frequently for the recent changes.
Glossary:
Uniform Resource Locator
A URL (Uniform Resource Locator) is the complete web address of a file or document you view in the address bar when using the Internet. Address bar is the one where you type the name of the website and it is located at the top of the page. An example of a URL is:
https://hiox.com/index.php
https is the protocol
hiox.com is the server
index.php is the filename
Internet Protocol Address
An Internet Protocol (IP) address is a set of numbers that is automatically assigned to your computer each time you connect to the Internet. When you use your web browser to request a web page from another computer on the Internet, your web browser automatically gives that computer your IP address so that the data your request can be sent to your computer.
Domain Name
A domain name is a part of the URL which identifies a particular web page. For example, in the URL https://hiox.com/index.php, the domain name is hiox.com.
Cookies
A cookie is a small data file consisting of numbers, letters, or other characters that the Hiox.com server sends to your browser and stores on your computer's hard drive when you visit the Hiox.com website. The Hiox.com servers can access this cookie when you return to the website or use the Hiox.com.
The cookie automatically identifies your computer, but not your identity, to our servers while you use the Hiox.com website.
Browser
A browser, short for web browser, is a software application used to locate and display web pages when you search for a website. Two popular browsers are Netscape Navigator and Microsoft Internet Explorer. Contact us for any queries or concerns in private policy.
