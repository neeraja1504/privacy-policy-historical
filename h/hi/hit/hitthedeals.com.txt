Terms & Conditions

Upon registration we collect your personal information as entered &
confirmed by you to be true. Your personal information is stored by HTD for the

subscribers, and also to confirm/verify the process of payments for the

any subscriber/user information with any Third Party that is not affiliated or

Your personal information will also be used to better understand the

Requirement & Information
Age
Requirement – HTD Users/Subscribers are only eligible to
register/subscribe/transact at HTD if they are 18 years of age & above.
Personal
Information, Email & Privacy Policy - Upon registration we collect
your personal information as entered & confirmed by you to be true. Your
personal information is stored by HTD for the purpose of sending email about
the daily deals that HTD brings to its subscribers, and also to confirm/verify
the process of payments for the vouchers purchased by the subscriber/user at
HTD. Upon registration you will receive emails from HTD about HTD Daily Deals,
Newsletters, marketing emails on behalf of HTD merchant promotions &
events. Your personal information is safe with HTD, and we will not disclose
any subscriber/user information with any Third Party that is not affiliated or
in business with HTD. Your personal information will also be used to better
understand the requirement of HTD & its’ subscribers/users in order to
bring you the best deals in town. The user/subscriber can unsubscribe from such
emails at any time from the links provided within the email, to stop receiving
any further emails which may take up to 2-3 working days to be in effect.

Disclaimer – We reserve the right to disclose your personal information

This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by HTD.

– HTD is a secured website using an SSL (Secured Socket Layer) & a secure

Refunds
– We always want our customers to be happy. But HTD is not responsible for any
quality of product or service from the merchant. There is no refund on any
vouchers purchased, unless the merchant refuses to accept any valid voucher.
Invalid/Expired vouchers are non-refundable, and no voucher is valid for
exchange of cash.
Credit/Debit
Card Usage – Please ensure that the purchase of vouchers through
Credit/Debit Card is with your own Credit/Debit Card using your own HTD
account, and not a Third Party Credit/Debit Card and/or a Third Party HTD
account. Any purchase that is done through a Third Party Credit/Debit Card
and/or a Third Party HTD account, will entail to invalidation of a voucher
purchased through that Account/Card at any time, and no refunds will be offered
by HTD.
Cash
On Delivery – Cash on Delivery is not available at the time, but will
be introduced soon.
