Celtx - Privacy
This privacy policy is designed to assist you in understanding how Celtx uses and safeguards the information and resources that you provide to us.
Use of Information
Celtx does not sell, rent, loan, trade or otherwise transfer personally identifying information collected through the Celtx software or services to any third party.
Celtx may collect information which is required in order for the Services to verify your identity. This includes your username and password. You are personally responsible for safeguarding your username and password.
The Celtx website contain places where feedback and other comments can be reported directly to Celtx. Information which is voluntarily submitted is used to improve the Celtx Services. Celtx reserves the right to utilize this information for marketing purposes, for instance by displaying selected comments on the Celtx website or in other communications. If Celtx chooses to display a user-submitted comment it will either display it anonymously, stripping away all personally identifying information, or request permission directly from you before displaying any such personally identifying information.
No System Information Collected
No information concerning your computing facilities is collected that is not related to providing Celtx Services. No information about your computing facilities will be provided to any third party without your express permission.
Delete/Deactivation Policy
If you wish to modify or delete any personal information you submitted to Celtx, you may do so by either modifying your profile or contacting us at billing@celtx.com. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to delete all such information within fifteen working days from the date of the receipt of your request. Please note that it may be impossible to delete a user's entry without some residual information remaining in our system due to the presence of Celtx maintained backups and records of deletions. Celtx reserves the right not to delete information in order to comply with applicable local laws or regulations.
