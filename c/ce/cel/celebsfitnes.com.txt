Privacy Policy
CelebsFitness.com  to collect visitor data and analyze traffic on our site. This information helps us understand visitors interests and helps us improve our website. When you visit our site, the pages that you look at, and a short text file called a cookie, are downloaded to your computer/Mobile. A cookie is used to store small amounts of information. Security Factor :-
We scan our server regularly.
We use only verified and certified plug-in such as Jetpack, Yoast, Tablepress etc.
We do not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes.

We use spam filtering Third-party service “Akismet”, which checked the visitors comments and filtered the spam comments.
