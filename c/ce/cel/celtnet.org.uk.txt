Celtnet eZine
eMail Privacy Notice
CeltNet will not sell, exchange or otherwise give away your email address to anyone for any reason. When visiting the CeltNet site we do not collect any personal information about you (including eMail addresses) unless you specifically chose to fill-out a response form. By submitting an eMail address your are specifically signing-up to receive the Celtnet WebInfo eZine as well as information on updates to the Celt Net Blog and the occasional eMail detailing tips, tricks and services that may be of interest.
Site Ads Privacy
Like many internet sites that use advertisements to generate revenue we use third-party advertising companies ot serve advertisemsents on thi site. These companies may use information (but excluding personally identifiable information such as your name, address, email address and telephone number) about visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
If you would like more information about this practice and to know more choices about your choices in not having this information used by these companies, please click here.
In particular, we use th DoubleClick DART cookie, delivered by Google. You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick DART cookie by visiting Google ad and content network privacy policy.
