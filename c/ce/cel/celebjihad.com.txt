The only personal information we collect is what you give us. Frankly you are not that interesting to us.

Cookies
CelebJihad.com does not use cookies except for when you log in to your user account.

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads on CelebJihad.com. Those companies may use non-personal information about your visit to our site to display ads for goods and services which may be of interest to you. Deal with it!
