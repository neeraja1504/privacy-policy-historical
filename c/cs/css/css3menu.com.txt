EMAIL NEWSLETTER
By opting to receive this communication, CSS3MENU.COM will send you regular emails containing relevant product information, product upgrade information, and any special offers that we think may be relevant to you.
If at any time you wish to unsubscribe from these communications, please follow the unsubscribe information contained in the emails.
SOFTWARE DOWNLOAD AND ORDER
By downloading or purchasing any software from this website, CSS3MENU.COM reserves the right to request information from you.
If you do NOT wish to be contacted by CSS3MENU.COM after the download/order, then you can opt-out of any subsequent communications on the download/order page or by sending a request to our support email. In this case CSS3MENU.COM will keep your information on record but will not contact you unless you have another interaction with us where you change your communication preference.
If you do wish to be contacted by CSS3MENU.COM after the download/order, then CSS3MENU.COM will send you emails relating to upgrade information about that product, and any other information that is relevant to that download/order. If at any time you wish to unsubscribe from these communications, please follow the unsubscribe information contained in the emails or send a request to our support email.
ENCRYPTED CREDIT CARD PURCHASES
If you use a credit card to purchase a CSS3MENU.COM product, the credit card number is encrypted as it passes over the internet. Your credit card number is not kept or recorded by CSS3MENU.COM or our computers - it is simply passed through to the credit card company to purchase the course. In this respect, buying a course from CSS3MENU.COM is similar to a credit card purchase at any store. Neither CSS3MENU.COM nor the store keeps your credit card number on file.
PERSONAL INFORMATION
CSS3MENU.COM collects basic contact information when you subscribe to newsletter or fill in a contact form. This information will be used for administrative and identification purposes only and will not be shared with our partners or third parties. Personal information is stored on CSS3MENU.COM computers and is only accessed by authorized CSS3MENU.COM personnel.
COOKIES
CSS3MENU.COM uses a cookie to store your visiting and referrer information. A cookie is a small text file, stored on your computer's hard drive, that can be read by our system when you return to our site.
GENERAL INFORMATION
CSS3MENU.COM also collects general demographic information about visitors to our site. The information we collect creates a broad picture of our population rather than focusing on any one individual. This information is used to continually refine our products and to identify the most popular areas of our site.
