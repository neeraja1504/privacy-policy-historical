PRIVACY POLICY
PRIVACY
POLICY
Your
privacy
is
important
to
us.
Our
privacy
policy
(“Privacy
Policy”)
covers
how
we
collect,
use,
disclose,
transfer,
and
store
any
your
specific
information
that
we
may
collect.
If
you
have
any
questions,
please
let
us
know
at
support@crystalmedianetworks.com.
Collection
and
Use
of
Personal
Information
Personal
information
is
data
that
may
be
used
to
uniquely
identify
or
contact
a
single
person.
You
may
be
asked
to
provide
your
personal
information
to
us
and
we
may
use
this
information
to
improve
our
products,
services,
content,
and
advertising.
Here
are
some
examples
of
the
types
of
personal
information
We
may
collect
and
how
we
may
use
it.
How
we
may
use
your
personal
information
▪ The
personal
information
we
collect
may
allow
us
to
keep
you
posted
on
our
company’s
news,
certain
product
announcements,
software
updates,
and
upcoming
events.
It
also
helps
us
to
improve
our
services,
content,
and
advertising.
If
we
do
send
you
information,
you
will
be
able
to
opt
out
of
this
information
at
anytime.
▪ The
personal
information
we
collect
may
allow
us
to
send
important
notices,
such
as
communications
about
product
changes,
new
or
modified
terms,
conditions,
and
policies.
▪ We
may
also
use
personal
information
for
internal
purposes
such
as
data
analysis
and
research
to
help
us
improve
our
products,
services,
and
customer
communications.
▪ If
you
enter
into
a
sweepstake,
contest,
or
similar
promotion
–
whether
through
our
websites
or
applications,
through
our
partners
websites
or
applications,
or
through
any
mobile
campaign,
we
or
our
partners
may
use
the
information
you
provide
in
order
to
administer
those
programs.
Collection
and
Use
of
Non-­‐Personal
Information
We
also
collect
information
that
is
not
personal
or
data
that
cannot
be
associated
with
a
specific
individual.
We
may
collect,
use,
transfer,
and
disclose
non-­‐personal
information
for
any
purpose.
The
following
are
some
examples
of
non-­‐personal
information
that
we
collect
and
how
we
may
use
it:
▪ We
may
collect
certain
non-­‐personal
information
such
as
zip
code,
area
code,
unique
device
identifier,
location,
and
the
time
zone
where
any
of
our
products
are
being
used
so
that
we
may
better
understand
customer
behavior
and
improve
our
products,
services,
and
advertising.
▪ We
also
may
collect
information
regarding
activities
on
our
website
and
mobile
application
and
mobile
browser
services.
Typically,
this
information
is
aggregated
and
used
to
help
us
provide
more
useful
information
to
our
customers
and
to
understand
which
parts
of
our
website,
products,
and
services
are
of
most
interest.
Aggregated
data
is
considered
non-­‐
personal
information
for
the
purposes
of
this
Privacy
Policy.
Please
note:
If
we
do
combine
non-­‐personal
information
with
personal
information
the
combined
information
will
be
treated
as
personal
information
for
as
long
as
it
remains
combined.
Cookies
and
Other
Technologies
Our
website,
services,
applications,
email
messages,
and
advertisements
may
use
“cookies”
or
other
technologies
such
as
pixel
tags
and
web
beacons.
These
technologies
help
us
better
understand
user
behavior,
tell
us
which
parts
of
our
website
people
have
visited,
and
facilitate
and
measure
the
effectiveness
of
advertisements
and
web
searches.
We
treat
information
collected
by
cookies
and
other
technologies
as
non-­‐personal
information.
However,
to
the
extent
that
Internet
Protocol
(IP)
addresses
or
similar
identifiers
are
considered
personal
information
by
local
law,
we
also
treat
these
identifiers
as
personal
information.
Similarly,
to
the
extent
that
non-­‐personal
information
is
combined
with
personal
information,
we
treat
the
combined
information
as
personal
information
for
the
purposes
of
this
Privacy
Policy.
We
may
gather
non-­‐personal
information
automatically
and
store
it
in
log
files.
This
information
includes
Internet
Protocol
(IP)
addresses,
browser
type
and
language,
Internet
service
provider
(ISP),
referring
and
exit
pages,
operating
system,
date/time
stamp,
and
other
data
that
allows
to
understand
what
you
may
like
on
our
websites,
applications,
or
devices.
We
also
use
email
messages
that
contain
“click-­‐through”
links
that
may
take
you
to
certain
content
on
our
websites.
We
may
track
this
click-­‐through
action
to
help
us
determine
interest
in
particular
topics
and
measure
the
effectiveness
of
our
email
messages.
If
you
prefer
not
to
be
tracked
in
this
way,
you
should
not
click
text
or
graphic
links
in
the
email
messages.
We
or
our
partners
may
use
cookies
and
other
technologies
in
advertising
services
to
control
the
number
of
times
you
see
a
given
ad,
deliver
ads
that
relate
to
your
interests,
and
measure
the
effectiveness
of
ad
campaigns.
We
and
our
partners
may
also
use
cookies
and
other
technologies
to
remember
personal
information
when
you
use
our
website,
online
services,
and
applications.
Our
goal
in
these
cases
is
to
make
your
experience
more
convenient
and
personal.
If
you
would
like
to
disable
cookies,
please
refer
to
the
specific
web
or
mobile-­‐browser
your
are
using
to
find
out
how
to
disable
cookies.
