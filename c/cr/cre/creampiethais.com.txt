Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. Customer Support
Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personal information about our customers. As such, this Privacy Policy applies to collection, use, storage and disclosure of information by Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc..
In order to provide Internet billing services, Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. must collect, use, and share certain information about you. This policy explains what we collect, who we share it with, and how you can contact Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. in order to ensure the accuracy of the data we collect, use, and share. The information Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. collects from you helps Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. better serve you and the online community by continually updating the electronic payment services Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. provides. The data that Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. collects and maintains is as follows:
Information That You Provide to Us
Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. receives and stores all information that you enter on our sign-up pages except for security items such as Visa's CVV2. This includes:
Credit Card Sign-Up Page for Digital Products. The Web site you are purchasing an e-ticket for, your e-mail address, language preference, credit card number, expiration date, first name, last name, address, city, state, zip, country, your username and password, and your agreement to be bound by Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc.'s Terms and Conditions.
Check Sign-Up Page. The Web site you are purchasing an e-ticket for, your e-mail address, language preference, first name, last name, address, city, state, zip, country, your bank's ABA routing number, your account number, the type of account, your username and password, and your agreement to be bound by Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc.'s Terms and Conditions.
Information That We Automatically Receive
Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. receives and stores certain information whenever you download Web pages, purchase products or send us e-mail or forms. For example:
We identify the numerical IP address assigned to your computer by your Internet Service Provider.
E-mail Correspondence. Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc. often requests a confirmation when you receive and open an e-mail communication delivered by Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc.. We archive all e-mail sent in and out of our network, and we identify the origination IP address of all mail sent into our network. We require that you refrain from "forging headers" or sending "SPAM".
Please be aware that, as part of the Services, Tea Money Media / Ghost Pro Inc.. provides links to Web sites operated by third parties, and is not responsible for the information collection or privacy practices or the content of those third party Web sites or applications.
