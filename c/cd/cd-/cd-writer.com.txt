Cd-writer.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site:  CD-writer.com
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site.
Cookies
Our Site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart.
Advertisers
CD-writer.com Ltd prsently uses NO outside advertisers.
Order Forms
Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect contact information (like their email address), and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers).
Contact information from the order forms is used to ship purchases and information about our company and products. The customer's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary.
Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings. Instructions for opting out are included with our sales and promotional material.
Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services.
Contests
We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like email address).
Contact information from the contests is used to provide information about our company, products and services.
Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites. CD-writer.com Ltd is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Public Forums
This site makes chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All credit card transactions take place either on our own secure servers or via a third party such as WorldPay.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional/marketing information from us.
You can visit the following URL: http://www.cd-writer.com/cdflyer.html
