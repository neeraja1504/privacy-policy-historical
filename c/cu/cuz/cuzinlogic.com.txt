Privacy
Your privacy is very important to us and we are committed to protecting it. We ask for your email address when you make a comment on our site, but we will never sell or share that information unless you are the winner of a giveaway administered by Cuzinlogic. Then we will only share it with the sponsor of that giveaway so that they can send you your prize. When you give us your shipping address after you have won a giveaway administered by Cuzinlogic ****, we will only share that information with the sponsor to ship your prize. All shipping addresses will be forwarded to the sponsor within  10 days and deleted immediately after.
You will also find links to third party websites on Cuzinlogic. We are not responsible for those privacy policies or the information provided, as a result of visiting and consulting those websites or entering those giveaways. Please check the site for all questions, concerns, and privacy policy info, should you have any questions.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: //www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
Although we have made every effort to provide information that is accurate and up to date, Cuzinlogic can under no circumstances be held liable for any errors, omissions or results that may be obtained by using such information or links on this site.
If we decide to change our privacy policy, those changes will appear on this page, and we will update the date below.
This policy was last modified on 11/1/2017.
We hope this helps you better understand our privacy policy. If you have any questions, please use the contact form or email us directly at cuzinlogic@gmail.com to discuss your interest in working with us.
~Cuzinlogic
