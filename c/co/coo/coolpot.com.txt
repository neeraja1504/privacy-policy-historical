Privacy Policy
Personally Identifiable Information
We do not share personally identifiable information with third parties. That means any registered user data is not shared with third parties.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
Aggregate Information (non-personally identifiable)
We may collect aggregate information about our user base. For example, we may keep track of the number of users that have registered or the number of links that have been created on coolpot.com. We may display this information publicly or provide it to others.
