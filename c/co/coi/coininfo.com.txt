Data collection
We will sometime collect your email address, for purposes such as saving your account information, sending confirmation emails, authenticating accounts, accessing our forum, etc.
Our web servers record standard information such as the IP address, referrer, browser, ISP of each visitor to this site. This information tells us which domain you visited from (for example, google.com), but not your email address or other private and personal information. We use the information we record to help diagnose problems with our servers, to administer this website more effectively, and to gather broad demographic information about what countries and domains our visitors hail from and their behavior on our site.
