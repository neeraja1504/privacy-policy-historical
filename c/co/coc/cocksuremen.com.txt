Cocksure Men
Legal Privacy Policy Statement
This is the web site of CockSureMen.com.
We can be reached via our online form by clicking here.
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name and IP address, but not the e-mail address.
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who buy memberships from our sites, buy DVDs from our online store, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, the e-mail addresses of those who make postings to our chat areas, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
The information we collect is used to improve the content of my Web page, used to customize the content and/or layout of my page for each individual visitor, used to notify consumers about updates to my Web site, not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
With respect to cookies: We may use cookies to store visitors preferences, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends.
We never make the e-mail addresses of those who access my site available to other organizations. We do not share with any outside organization.
If you supply us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address. We will always contact you first via email and only contact you discreetly via the postal service as a last resort.
With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies.
Upon request we provide site visitors with access to unique identifier information (e.g., customer number or password) that we maintain about them, a description of information that we maintain about them. This information is provided solely via the original registrant's e-mail.
If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number, The DMA's Committee on Ethical Business Practices at mgoldberger@the-dma.org, state or local chapters of the Better Business Bureau, state or local consumer protection office, The Federal Trade Commission by phone at 202.FTC-HELP (202.382.4357) or electronically at http://www.ftc.gov/ftc/complaint.htm.
