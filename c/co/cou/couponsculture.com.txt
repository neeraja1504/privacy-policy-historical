Privacy policy Coupons Culture
CouponsCulture aims to protect the user privacy and the data provided by the users to CouponsCulture. Whether you are an existing registered user or a former user or just a visitor to our website, CouponsCulture intends to protect your privacy thereby protecting and securing all your personal and other information.
We collect information from you when you subscribe to our newsletter. When ordering or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your: e-mail address. You may, however, visit our site anonymously.CouponsCulture is the sole owner of the information collected on the site. The information provided by you to the website will not be shared, rented or sold to other parties in any way different from the ones disclosed here.
We may collect and process the information provided by you when filling forms on our site  www.couponsculture.com such as information provided on registering for our newsletters, posting discount.
While contacting us, CouponsCulture may keep a record of correspondence.
CouponsCulture may collect the information from the web logs of your visit to our site including traffic data, location data, other communication data and the resources that you access.
CouponsCulture may also keep a record of information provided in surveys, participating in such surveys is solely your choice.
The information regarding your usage on the websitemay also be obtained by us by using a cookie file. A cookie file is stored on the hard drive of your computer. These cookies help us to deliver you better and more personalized service and also to customize our site as per your individual interests, speed up your searches and recognize you when you return to our site.
