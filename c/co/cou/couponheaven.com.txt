Privacy Policy - Coupon Heaven
General
You privacy is very important to us! CouponHeaven.com does not require a user name or password at any time, and you are free to use our site anonymously.
Newsletter
Our newsletter registration form requires you to submit your e-mail address. We use the double opt-in method to make sure that legitimate requests for this newsletter occur. That means that you submit your e-mail address, and then you must click on a link in the confirmation e-mail we send in order to confirm your interest in our newsletter. You may opt out at any time. Your information will never be shared with anyone – period.
Tell-A-Friend
If you elect to use our “Tell-a-Friend” service to refer a friend to our site, you will be asked for the friend’s name and email address (as well as your own). Your friend will receive a one-time email inviting them to visit the site. CouponHeaven.com stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email. Your information and your friend’s information will never be shared with anyone – period.
Opt-out
Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter may opt-out at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the newsletter.
Links
This site contains links to other Web sites. CouponHeaven.com is not responsible for the privacy practices, use of cookies, or the content of such Web sites (please see our Terms and Conditions for more information). This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.
Traffic Data
Data about traffic and usage is not personally identifying or identifiable. Our web server logs provide us with details such as your IP address, the geographic location of your ISP, the type of browser you are using, and what site “referred” you to CouponHeaven.com. We may use some of this data to improve our site, so that we may provide the best experience for our users.
Cookies
CouponHeaven.com does not use cookies. However, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser when you click on a link that takes you to their site. We are not responsible in any way for the privacy policies of other web sites, or for their tracking methods. If you choose to set your browser software to reject cookies, you may not be able to use all of our features, including redeeming coupons.
Security
CouponHeaven.com has procedures in place to safeguard and secure the information it collects online. Security breaches do occur, however, and CouponHeaven.com will not be liable or responsible if information that belongs to you is intercepted and used by an unintended recipient.
