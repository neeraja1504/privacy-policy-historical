However, Coupon Sense is not responsible for the privacy practices of our Service Partners. As you visit their Websites and /or use their services, we encourage you to read their privacy statements, as they may differ from ours in some particulars.
Personal Information
Coupon Sense operates email services via the Coupon Sense Website for organizations ranging from small businesses to major enterprise customers and ISPs, Coupon Sense understands its customers’ need to protect personal and account information. In providing Coupon Sense and Coupon Sense Service Partner services to you, personal information may be gathered, tracked or used, to provide you with services and to suggest ways in which you can enhance the use of the Coupon Sense Websites.
In most cases, you will be asked to provide personal and account information for each service as appropriate for Coupon Sense to provide the service in a secure environment to you. This may include name, address, business, e-mail, US Mail, telephone, facsimile, or other information necessary to provide effective services, maintenance and support to your account and end users.
Requested Information
We request and use personal and customer information when you create an account, authorize end users, request or operate a Coupon Sense service, create and publish a data report, correspond with us or engage in other activities on our Websites. For example, if you request technical support online or via telephone, we will ask you for information necessary to authenticate you as a customer or end user, and to provide the requested support, such as your name, address and information about your computer hardware, software and the nature of the problem you are experiencing.
We also maintain logs to track usage of the Coupon Sense Websites by end users and to track maintenance and support dialogues with end users and customers. Providing such information is completely voluntary, and while individuals are under no obligation to provide such information to Coupon Sense, our customers may not get the full benefit of our products, services and support without providing such information.
How We Use the Information We Gather and Track
In many cases, you decide whether to authorize any activity that requests personal information. If you do not wish to provide the requested information, however, you may not be able to complete the activity. This includes operating Coupon Sense services online, as well as support and maintenance with you.
We use personal and account information in the following ways:
To provide you with information about the Coupon Sense Website, newsletters, informative emails, and alerts to future product ideas or improvements;
To assist us in providing end user support and account maintenance information;
To provide you access to Service Partner services and information that you have requested;
To assist us in creating better products and services to meet your future needs;
To provide you with access to services or otherwise engage in activities, such as protect testing or evaluation, that you select on the Coupon Sense Websites

From time to time, unless we have stated otherwise, we may provide certain information to selected Service Partners in order to effectively provide the product or service requested. For example, in providing ISP mediation services for our customers, we may need to share information about the email campaign practices of your end users with support personnel at Internet abuse desks. They may be used to provide online maintenance and technical support or perform other services for us. We require that these contractors keep the personal information of customers secure and confidential. Cookies
Coupon Sense may use cookies from time to time. A cookie is a small amount of data stored on the hard drive of the individual’s computer that allows us to identify the individual with his or her corresponding data that resides in our databases. You may read more about cookies at http://cookiecentral.com. Coupon Sense may use cookies to personalize your experience on our online services or portal pages, or to track end user usage of Coupon Sense services for customers. If you do not want Coupon Sense to deploy cookies in your browser, you can set your browser to reject cookies or to notify you when a Website tries to put a cookie in your browser software. Rejecting cookies may affect your ability to use of some of the products and/or services at the Coupon Sense Websites.
Coupon Sense may also use cookies to track your visit to our Websites. While our Web servers may automatically log your IP address, such basic Internet traffic statistics will not be tied back to other personal information.
Web Site Traffic
We may track domain names, IP addresses, email addresses and browser types from people who visit our Websites. Such information is not correlated with any personal information.
