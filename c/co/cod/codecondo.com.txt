Do we use cookies?
Yes (Cookies are small files that a website or its service provider transfers to your computer’s via your Web browser (if you allow) which permits the websites or service providers systems to identify your web browser and remember and record information.
We use cookies to perceive and save your likings whenever you visit our website again, tracking advertisements and compile mass data about site interaction and website traffic so that we can offer better website tools and experience in the future.
Do we share your information to third parties?
We do not trade or sell your personal information to anyone. This doesn’t involve trusted third parties who help us in, conducting our business, operating our website or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information private. We may also provide your information if we believe that it is worthfull and is fitting in law, protect others or ours right, safety. However, information which is non-personally identifiable may be provided to other parties for advertising and marketing purpose.

Sometimes, at our diplomacy, we may include or provide third party products or services on our site. These third party websites have independent privacy policies. We therefore have no accountability or control for the activities and contents of these linked websites. Nevertheless, we work towards safeguarding the integrity of our website and welcome any feedback about these websites.
