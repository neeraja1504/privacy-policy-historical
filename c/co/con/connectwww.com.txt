Privacy | CONNECTwww.com
At connectwww.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us . We ensure that the information that we collect is collected to improvise the quality of service we provide to our users and to enhance the quality of the content that is posted on our website.This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by connectwww.com and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, connectwww.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.This website contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.In case if you subscribe to our website, you provide us with your email ID. On the other hand, if you contact us with some query via email, we make it a point to retain your email address, and your email along with our response to your email.While commenting on any of the articles that were posted on the website, we ask you to provide your name, your email address and the website you are affiliated with.
Cookies and Web Beacons
connectwww.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on connectwww.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
connectwww.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. connectwww.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
