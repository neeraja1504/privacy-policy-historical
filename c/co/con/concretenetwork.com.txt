Privacy Policy - The Concrete Network
Information Collected
All of our form data and email marketing is permission-based. If you receive communications from us, it is because you have submitted your information to us via an online form for the purpose of receiving information either electronically, by phone, or through the mail.
In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page, and time of visit. Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website.
Your Options for Receiving Information from ConcreteNetwork.com
If you believe you have received unwanted, unsolicited information from ConcreteNetwork.com, please forward a copy of the information with your comments to info@concretenetwork.com.
To stop receiving information from ConcreteNetwork.com, you can easily unsubscribe from any email newsletters by following the instructions including at the end of any email you receive.
To remove your name from our mailing database, please send your contact information to khara@concretenetwork.com.
How We Protect Your Privacy
We use appropriate security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.
We will never share, sell, or rent your personal information with a third party.
