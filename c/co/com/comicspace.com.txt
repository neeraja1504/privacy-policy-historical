ComicSpace - Privacy Policy
Home / Privacy Policy
Visitor Information
ComicSpace.com does NOT collect your personal information without your consent. However, each time you access a page on ComicSpace.com, the following aggregate data is recorded...
Your IP address
The Web page that referred you
The date and time of your visit
The name and version of your Web browser
The information collected by ComicSpace.com is used to...
Determine the popularity of individual pages and sections
Improve site quality and services
The information collected by ComicSpace.com is NOT shared with other organizations.
ComicSpace Account
When you sign up for a free ComicSpace account, your email address will be recorded and stored in the ComicSpace.com database. Sign up is completely optional, and does not happen automatically. Your email address will be recorded ONLY if you fill out and submit the Sign Up form.
After creating a ComicSpace account, you can easily delete your account by logging in and going to Manage Account > Delete Account.
Your email address will NOT be shared with other organizations.
Cookies
ComicSpace.com uses cookies to...
Identify return visitors
Preserve session data
Ad Servers:
ComicSpace.com allows other reputable companies to place ads on its pages. As a result of your visit to ComicSpace.com, ad server companies may collect information about you such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
