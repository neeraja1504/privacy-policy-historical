COLLAPSENET PRIVACY POLICY
Registration Data and certain other information about you is subject to our Privacy Policy. You understand that through your use of the Site you consent to the collection and use (as set forth in this Privacy Policy) of this information, including the transfer of this information to the United States and/or other countries for storage, processing and use by CollapseNet and its affiliates.
Member Information We Collect
This refers to the information you give us such as your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, skills and interests, and credit card information if placing an order for subscription services or products through CollapseNet. All information regarding our online customers is stored on a highly secured server that is not accessible through the Internet.
Information Security
Only employees who need access to member information to perform their jobs are authorized to access member information. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural security measures to safeguard information.
E-mail Offers
Occasionally, we may send you e-mail offers for our products and services. We don't share e-mail addresses with other companies. Included with each e-mail are directions you can follow to discontinue any further e-mail offers from us.
Former Members
CollapseNet provides the same privacy services for member information provided by applicants and former members as it provides for existing members.
