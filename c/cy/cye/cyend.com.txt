Privacy Policy
SP CYEND uses the information that it collects about users or technical support lawfully and will not sell, share, pass on, distribute, issue, or rent this information to others.
In order to process your order efficiently you will be asked to provide personal information (name, postal address,email address,e.t.c).
SP CYEND may disclose your personal information to third parties to complete a transaction on your behalf or provide you with a service that you requested.For example, payments on the Website are processed by a third party contractor.
With your permission, cyend.com might use part of the information to send you electronically newsletters and/or other notifications.
