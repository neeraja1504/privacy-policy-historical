Check4D Privacy Policy
Do we collect your personal information?
No information is collected that will allow us or any party to positively identify you individually. This app merely provides live result feed without any input for user side.
What information is collected?
Information that may be collected is strictly general information such as geographical location, browser agent, operating system and length of time accessing this app etc. These information would not allow us to identified you as an individual but help us to understand general user's behavior for system improvement.
Google Advertisement
We utilize Google advertisement programme to provide user a positive user experience. Google, as a third party provider, uses cookies to serve advertisement that deem relevant to users based on user's previous surfing history.
