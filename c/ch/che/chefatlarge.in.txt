Privacy Policy - Chef at Large
This is the web site of Chef at Large. We can be reached via e-mail at sid [-dot-] khullar [-at-] chefatlarge [dot] in.
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes cookies to check for first time visitors.
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our monthly e-magazine, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit.The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page, used to notify consumers about updates to our web site and the availability of new issues. With respect to cookies: We use cookies to alert visitors to news that we think might be of interest to them when they return to our site.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please opt out using the unsubscribe link present in every email sent by us. With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies. We may do so in the future. In case we do, these will be restricted to the display of ads. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our web site periodically.
Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information [including proprietary information] that we maintain about them. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above email addresses.
