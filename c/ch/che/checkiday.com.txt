Privacy Policy | Checkiday.com
We take privacy seriously. Any information sent between you and our servers is encrypted.
What information do we collect?
We do not collect any unnecessary information from our users. Services that we use such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and Quantcast may collect user information. Additionally, we explicitly collect some information if you contact us or sign up for emails.
How do we use that information?
We use data from Google Analytics and Quantcast to better understand how our site is being used so we may improve the user experience. We use Google AdSense to serve ads so that we can keep this site free. We use the information you provide to respond to any inquiries.
What information do we share?
We do not and will not sell or share your information.
