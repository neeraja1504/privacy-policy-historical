Changing Earth
This Privacy & Data Policy is valid as of 24 May 2006.
Data Collected : We collect only the domain name or IP address (but not the e-mail address) of visitors to our website, aggregate information on what pages visitors access or visit, and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations. This information is used to improve the content of our website. For those who communicate with us via e-mail we will use your details in relation to that query only and not for any other purpose.
Cookies: We may use cookies to ensure that visitors are not repeatedly sent the same ads.
Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
