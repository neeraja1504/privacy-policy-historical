Free Chat Rooms Online
Chat Society (A wholy owned website of Wired 2000 Corporation), its partners and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and concerns our Internet users may have. We have outlined below information and our guidelines that we observe for responsible management of our client and customer information. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for this Web site: Chat Society
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. Additionally, we use your IP address to determine network traffic based per country of origin from aggreggated data. We do not archive individual IP addresses, only volume and percentage breakdown per country.
Information Collected From Our Tools
Our server automatically logs your date of activity and IP address to help diagnose problems with our site or network activity. Additionally, we use this information to compose aggreggated statistics of usage. Your personal information will not be released or divulged to any other parties.
Advertisers
We use an outside ad company to display advertisements on our site. These ads may contain cookies and the associated information is collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control (IP, date and website addresses).
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: webmaster[at]wired2000[dot] com
