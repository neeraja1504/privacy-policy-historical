Privacy Statement
Thank you for visiting The Community College of Baltimore County Web site. CCBC will not collect any personally identifiable information as a result of your visiting this site. We will only gather personal information that you submit via an online form or by registering to receive information from the college. CCBC's Web servers automatically gather non-personal information regarding site use and access, including the Internet domains and IP addresses from which users access our site. Information submitted to or gathered by CCBC will never be sold to a third party.
