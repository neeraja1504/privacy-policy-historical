March 22, 2009
Your personal data will be kept confidential to the extent required by law and, unless you provide ClipMoon with explicit consent, it will only be used in furtherance of your relationship with ClipMoon. Data collection, usage, and processing shall be carried out electronically. In accordance with applicable laws and ordinances, ClipMoon may submit information to law enforcement agencies and courts for the purpose of prosecution. Upon request, ClipMoon will immediately and without charge, provide you with information about saved data concerning you and your pseudonyms. You may request that this information be sent to you electronically. ClipMoon may transfer billing information to other service providers and third parties, so long as such transfer is necessary for the calculation of fees and/or for billing you.
