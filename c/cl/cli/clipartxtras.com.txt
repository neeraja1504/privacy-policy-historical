Privacy Statement for the WebXtras Network
The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for these web sites: WebXtras.com, ScriptXtras.com, JavaScriptXtras.com, CGIXtras.com, AdXtras.com, ArtXtras.com, PhotoXtras.com, ClipArtXtras.com, SubmitXtras.com, HostXtras.com, StatXtras.com, NetXtras.com, MailXtras.com, and SiteXtras.com.
IP Addresses: We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.
Cookies: Our site uses cookies to keep member preferences and to save your UserName so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.
Membership: Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (like their name and email address) and optionally asks for demographic information (like their job, age, and education). The customer's contact information is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Some demographic data that is collected at our site is shared with advertisers on an aggregate basis.
Links: This site contains links to other sites. WebXtras Network is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Advertisers and Sponsors: We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by our ad company, and we do not have access to this information.
Security: This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. However, WebXtras Inc., its partners, service providers, and affiliates are not responsible for security breaches that are the result of software, hardware, and or services that are not provided by, maintained by, under the direct control of or exclusively developed for WebXtras Inc.
Choice/Opt-Out: Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. You can send email to: WEBMASTER@WEBXTRAS.COM
Correct/Update: This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. You can send email to: WEBMASTER@WEBXTRAS.COM
Contacting the Web Site: If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: Customer Relations, WebXtras Inc., WebXtras Network, 18 Emerson Gardens, Boston, MA 02420, WEBMASTER@WEBXTRAS.COM.
