OUR PRIVACY POLICY
Information and Use
We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share personal information to any third parties for any reason. We respect the privacy of our users to the highest degree possible, and we are proud to say that our privacy policy is among the best of all sites on the Internet. We know that users are here because they're looking for our information, and that's the only purpose of our site.
Log Files
Like most websites we use basic log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring and exit pages and platform type. This is not personal information and cannot be linked to any individual person. This general information is used to compile help us understand more about the users of this website, to help us learn which pages users like best, and other information that help us make the site better tailored to the needs of our users in the future. THESE LOGS DO NOT REVEAL ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION ABOUT YOU!
