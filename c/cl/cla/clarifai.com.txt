The purpose of this section is to describe what data we collect, how we collect it and what we use it for. Simply put, we collect data that is publicly available on the internet, from our website, our SDKs, our APIs and our partners or customers to train our algorithms to be faster, smarter and more accurate. You are free to explore our websites without providing any information about yourself. However, if you sign up for the API or any other service, then we will require collection of account creation data, such as your name, email address, password, location and employer and we may collect other personal data as described herein. In addition, if you visit our website and allow tracking mechanisms (such as cookies), we may also collect IP address, browser, geographic information, length of visit and pageviews, referral source, adID and the like.
