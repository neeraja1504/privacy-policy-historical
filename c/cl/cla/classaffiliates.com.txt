Privacy Policy - Class Media Productions
All personal information (e-mail address, home address et cetera) that we obtain from visitors and customers will not be sold or shared with any third party. All information gathered is used for internal purposes only.
What kind of information do you collect?
When you purchase a membership with one of the Class Media websites you are asked to provide your name, address, e-mail, select a username and password, credit card number and its expiration date. When users request pages from our server, our Web server automatically collects some information about the users, including their IP addresses.
Who sees my information and how is it used?
We do not share your information with our members, vendors, sponsors or others.
How secure is my information?
We take every reasonable precautions to maintain the security of any information obtained from and about you. We provide you with a secure web page through which to submit your billing information when you join the membership portion of our web site. We maintain your personally identifiable information in a secure manner including storing such information behind firewalls.
We recognize the importance of protecting your privacy. If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, the practices of this web site or your dealings with us, please contact us at the customer service page.
Please also note we are not responsible for the content or the privacy polices of web sites to which we may provide links or the websites of its advertisers.
