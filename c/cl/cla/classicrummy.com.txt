Emails:

You also agree and acknowledge that when You use Our referral program, We will send emails to that person on Your behalf and will Your email address will be shown as the address from which such emails are sent to Your referral.
Cookies:
When You visit the Website, cookies may be left in Your computer. A cookie is a small text file that uniquely identifies Your browser and which is assigned by the servers of ClassicRummy. It may be used to personalize Your experience on www.classicrummy.com. Additionally, it may also be used for authentication, game management and security purposes. A cookie in no way gives Us access to Your computer or any information about You, other than the data You choose to share with Us.
Cookies may also be assigned by the advertisers of ClassicRummy when You click on any of the advertisements which may be displayed on www.classicrummy.com. In such an event, the cookies are controlled by these advertisers and not by Us.
You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but You can usually modify Your browser setting to decline cookies if You prefer. This may prevent You from taking full advantage of the Website.
In addition, ClassicRummy may collect IP addresses for the purposes of system administration, security, troubleshooting, and compiling aggregate information (such as how many visitors logged on to the Website). An IP address is a number that uniquely identifies Your computer on the internet. When You visit a particular web page on the Website, Our servers may log Your IP address.
Software Applications:
ClassicRummy may also use software applications for traffic analysis for the Website in order to gain information on statistics, for advertising and for determining the efficiency and popularity of ClassicRummy among others.
Information gathered is stored in a secured manner using several information security applications including firewalls. However, security is always relative and ClassicRummy cannot guarantee that its security measures are absolute and cannot be breached.
Also, data which is transmitted over the Internet is inherently exposed to security risks or threats. For instance, information transferred via chat or email can be compromised and used by others. Therefore, there is no security for such information.
When You register with ClassicRummy, Your account is protected by means of login information which includes a user name and a password that is known only to You. Therefore, it is Your responsibility to maintain the security of Your login information including Your usernames and password. If You come across any suspected breach of security, including compromise of Your login information, it is Your responsibility to immediately ensure the same is brought to the notice of ClassicRummy.

It is Your responsibility to read and understand the privacy policy of such websites when You follow a link outside www.classicrummy.com

Periodic analysis and survey of the traffic are conducted by ClassicRummy for market research and advertising purposes. ClassicRummy has a right to share Your registration information with the appointed market research and advertising companies or firms from time to time for the said purposes and it may also use cumulative non-personal information for auditing and analysis purposes with the aim of improving its services.
ClassicRummy confirms that this Privacy Policy is only a description of its operation regarding user information. This Policy is not intended to and does not create any legal rights in Your favor or in the favor of any other person.
