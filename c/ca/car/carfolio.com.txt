All the following information and specifications are preliminary, as Chevrolet have not yet released complete and final specifications. If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at webmaster@carfolio.com.
At Carfolio.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by Carfolio.com and how it is used.
Log Files
Like most standard Web site servers, we may make use of log files. This could include internet protocol (IP) address, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track movement on the site, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. This information is not linked to personally identifiable information in any way.
Cookies and Web Beacons
The only time Carfolio.com will set or query users' browser cookies is for the functionality of the "Your Stable" section of the site, where the Carfolio.com ID numbers of the models the user chooses to add to that section of the site are stored via cookies. No personal information is stored or retrieved by Carfolio.com at any time.
Carfolio.com partners with Google AdSense and Amazon.co.uk for serving advertisements to users, and these 3rd party services may make use of cookies to track user clicks.
These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to send, directly to your browser, the advertisements and links that appear on Carfolio.com. They automatically receive your IP address when this happens. They may also use other technologies (such as cookies or JavaScript) to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize the advertising content you see.
Carfolio.com has no access to or control over these cookies, once we have given permission for Google AdSense and Amazon.co.uk to set cookies for advertising.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers or ad networks for more information on their practices and for instructions on how to opt-out of certain practices. Carfolio.com's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such 3rd party advertisers and web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
