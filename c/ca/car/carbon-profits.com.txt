Carbon-Profits.com values your privacy, and is committed to protecting the information you share with us.
At present, Carbon-Profits.com is not collecting any personally identifiable information like name, company name, company address, e-mail address, phone number on our web site.
Our webserver logs keeps tracks of your IP address and browsing activity just like any other webservers. We analyze the server log for errors, site usage information.
If you contact our site via e-mail, we store those information in our e-mail box and address book. Internal reviews are conducted periodically on an as-needed basis to verify compliance with this policy.
Please contact our support at support@carbon-profits.com for additional information.
