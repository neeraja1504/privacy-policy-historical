Privacy Policy | Carbon Tesla
Your privacy is of extreme importance to us. This page outlines the types of personal information received and collected by CarbonTesla.com and how its usage
Log Files
Akin to many other websites, CarbonTesla.com makes use of log files. The log files usually include information like internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, ISP, time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to help analyze trends and track user’s movement around the site. IP addresses and other such info are not linked to any information that can lead to personal identification.
Cookies and Web Beacons
CarbonTesla.com does not make use of cookies.
DoubleClick DART Cookie
» DART cookie enables Google to serve ads based on your visit to CarbonTesla.com and other sites on the Internet.
» You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at google.com/policies/technologies/ads/
Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and to personalize the advertising content that you see.
We have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of 3rd party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your browser’s settings. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us via this form.
