Privacy Policy | Cary Audio
Introduction
Cary Audio is committed to protecting your privacy. The following privacy statement describes our practice with respect to the collection and use of your personal information on this website. By using this website, you consent to the terms of our privacy statement and to Cary Audio’s processing of personal information for the purposes stated below.
Contact Information
All requests for further information or for permission to reproduce any portions of the materials should be directed to:
Cary Audio

Collection
Cary Audio may ask you to submit personal information at various places on our website, including pages for requesting information, joining our newsletter subscription database, or submitting a service warranty registration. The information you voluntarily submit may include, but not be limited to: name, address, phone and email address. We strive to limit our information requests to what we believe is minimally necessary to provide the service you requested.
We use this information to contact you regarding information you have requested or information we feel will be of interest to you. The materials or services may include newsletters, event information, alerts of product updates, special offers, updated information, and other new services from Cary Audio. We may also contact you in response to sign-up forms such as ‘Contact Us’ or ‘Warranty Registration.’
Cary Audio offers you the opportunity to provide suggestions and contributions by completing a ‘Contact Us’ form. Please note that any comments or materials sent to us, including feedback data, such as questions, comments, suggestions and any other responses, are deemed to be non-confidential. We have no obligation with respect to such responses, and Cary Audio shall be free to use, reproduce, distribute and publicly display such comments and materials without reference to you personally. Cary Audio is also free to use any ideas, concepts, know-how or techniques contained in such responses for any purpose whatsoever.
Use of Non-Personal Information Collected
Cary Audio uses non-personal information such as demographic data to analyze and develop marketing strategy and to determine how you found out about us, your interests regarding our products and services, and how to further improve this website based upon user data. We may share such non-personal data with third parties.
Opt-Out
Cary Audio provides you the opportunity to exercise an opt-out choice if you do not want to receive emails from us or updates regarding new services and products offered on this website. You may opt out of our email program at anytime by choosing ‘Unsubscribe’ at the bottom of the last email correspondence you received. You may also opt out by contacting us as info@caryaudio.com. We will process your unsubscribe request as soon as possible, but be aware that this method will take longer to process.
Third-Party Website Links
This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we have no control over and are not responsible for the privacy or security practices of other websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of third-party websites. Cary Audio makes no recommendations or endorsements about any third-party products or services.
Changes to the Privacy Statement
Cary Audio reserves the right to change this privacy statement from time to time. If you continue to use the website, you are agreeing to the amended version of the privacy statement.
Security
We take reasonable and appropriate steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your privacy is very important to us. However, we do not represent that your personal information is 100 percent safe from security risks associated with personal information.
Enforcement of this Privacy Statement
If you have comments or questions with respect to this statement, please contact us.
