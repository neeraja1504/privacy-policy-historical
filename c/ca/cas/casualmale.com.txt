INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Automatic Information:
When you visit DestinationXL.com, our server attaches a small text file, known as a "cookie", to your computer's hard drive which will automatically collect and store information such as the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet, the Internet Protocol (IP) address of the computer you are using, the browser software you use and your operating system, the date and time you access our website, and the Internet address of the website from which you linked directly to our website, if any. This information collected by using a cookie is sometimes called "clickstream". This is the only information collected unless you voluntarily provide information to us.
We use clickstream information, which we may (subject to the relevant permissions) share with our affiliates, licensees, trusted vendors and companies who assist us in analyzing our site data and ways to improve user experience, only as anonymous aggregate data to determine the number of visitors to our website, to understand how visitors navigate our website, to determine common traffic patterns, including what site the visitor came from, to ensure that the website is working properly and to improve the design and content of our website. This aggregate information does not include individual names. For further information regarding cookies and how to disable them, refer to "Our Use of Cookies" below.
Wireless Service:
When you use our wireless service, we automatically collect information about your use of the wireless service, including:
utilization data, e.g. # of users of service by store;
session data, e.g. date and time device entered store, length of session;
store data, e.g. address of store where device is used;
use of DXL services, e.g. mobile coupons redeemed; and
websites and pages visited, e.g. DestinationXL.com, google.com

SECURING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION AND FINANCIAL DATA
We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to try to protect your Personal Information and financial data. An encryption methodology called TLS (Transport Layer Security) is used to help protect personal information that is transported via the Internet. For payment related data, DXL utilizes what we consider to be appropriate physical and technical access controls, and other information security methodologies and safeguards designed to improve data protection. For further information, click the "Back" button on your web browser and review our Security Statement.
While we strive to protect your Personal Information, no data transmission over the Internet, including e-mail and wireless service, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure against interception. Once we receive your transmission, we use our best effort to ensure its security on our systems, however, DXL cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us or from our website, and therefore you use our website and wireless service at your own risk. If your proposed e-mail or wireless communication includes sensitive information such as your Social Security number or your account, credit or debit card number, we strongly encourage you not to send to it to us electronically, but to contact us by mail or telephone instead so that one of our customer service associates may gladly assist you.
OUR USE OF COOKIES
DestinationXL.com uses cookies to collect website navigational information so that our Site, Blog, and Services can remember you and provide you with the information you're most likely to need. Site navigational information includes standard information from your web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your IP address, and the actions you take on our Site (such as pages viewed and links clicked). Please note that some cookies last only for your current browser session, while others persist for longer periods of time. For instance, when you return to our Site, cookies identify you and prompt the site to provide your username (not your password), so you can sign in more quickly. We also use information gained through cookies to compile statistical information about use of our Site, such as the time users spend at the site and the pages they visit most often. Those statistics do not include Personal Information. You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies. You do this through your browser settings. Each browser is a little different, so look at your browser's "Help" menu to learn the correct way to modify your cookies. Please note that if you turn cookies off, some features will be disabled which may make your site experience less efficient, and some of our Services and features may not function properly. To manage the use of targeting or advertising cookies on our Site or Blog, consult your individual browser settings for cookies. You can still shop and make purchases from DestinationXL.com even if you set your browser to reject cookies, however, we may not be able to provide you with as personalized a shopping experience.
