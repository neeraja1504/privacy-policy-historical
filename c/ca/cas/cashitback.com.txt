Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.
What kind of information do we collect?
We collect your personal information only when you complete your registration with us such as Email, name, city and interests etc. Visitors have the rights to visit our website by not disclosing their email address. Our site will never disclose any personal details to third party without your Permission.
Third Party links
Some of our sites contain links to other sites whose information practices may be different than ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy notices as we have no control over information that is submitted to, or collected by, these third parties. We will never willfully disclose any personally identifiable information about our online audience to any third party without first receiving the user's permission.
The utilization of your Details/Email
When you use our services we track your activity on our website. Your personal information is used to identify you in our system, allowing cashitback.com to contact you about the status of your order or respond to your questions via e-mail or telephone, and to allow you to store your basic information for repeat checkouts. We also use the information gathered from tracking user activities to improve our service.The email address provided by you would be used to send periodic emails on latest cashback offers and promotions. We occasionally send you email to keep you updated about new activities on cashitback.com and we send you emails regarding your transactions, payment confirmations and latest schemes. We also send newsletter which contains our best offers to assist you to save more. We send periodic emails on latest offers and coupons. It will be used to constantly communicate about our latest offerings and changes. We have provided the flexibility to opt out of our mailing list by unsubscribing from our mailing list. The unsubscribe link would be provided at the bottom of our emails.
Acceptance of this www.cashitback.com Web Site Privacy Policy
By using this site, you signify your agreement to the terms and conditions of this cashitback.com Web Site Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms and conditions, please do not use the site. We reserve the right, at our sole discretion, to change, modify, add, or remove portions of this policy at any time.
Contact
If you have any queries or questions about this privacy policy statement you can contact us on Support@cash-it-back.com so we can take suitable action.
