Electronic Cigarette, E-Cigarette Wholesale - China leading ecig distributor Cacuq
Thank you for visiting our website. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it and what we do with it. This is important; we hope you will take time to read it carefully.
Information that we collect
We collect information to provide better services to all of our customers - from figuring out basic stuff like where you are from, to more complex things like what products you are more likely to purchase.
We collect information in the following ways
Information you give us. When you sign up for a Cacuq Account, we'll ask for personal information, like your name, email address, telephone number, Skype account and business profile to store with your account.
Information we get from your use of our services. We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, like when you browse products of a certain brand, or search for products with certain specifications.
Cookies and similar technologies. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track user's movement around the website, serve targeted advertisements, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.
How we use the information that we collect
We use the information that we collect only for the following purposes:
To promote use of our services to you and others. If you are a registered customer on our website, we may send you informational and promotional content via email (e.g. new products and price updates). You can stop receiving our promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in every email we send.
To improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services
To send you System Alert messages. We may inform you of temporary or permanent changes to our services.
To communicate with our members about their account and provide customer support.
To meet legal requirements and protect the rights and safety of our members and third parties, as well as our own.
