Confidentiality of information
CACTUS considers any and all information related to its clients to be strictly confidential.
The following measures prevent information disclosure:
All our data security processes are ISO 9001:2008 certified and our IT security management systems are ISO/IEC 27000:2005-certified.
All our employees and contractors have signed confidentiality agreements and will not make any client information available to anyone outside CACTUS, unless authorized in writing by the client.
We respect the privacy of our clients; therefore, their personal information (name, address, email address, contact number, etc.) will never be made available to any outside party.
All documents submitted are managed through a secure online job management system; the documents that are sent to us as raw data for editing or publication support assignments can only be viewed by editors/publication experts working on your assignment and your customer service manager.
Your personal information is kept strictly confidential and CACTUS will never share it with external parties without your consent.
On request, we will also sign a confidentiality agreement with you before you submit your data to us.
Website Security
The CACTUS website uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption—the best online security available. This ensures that your personal information and files are secure. Our website is verified by VeriSign, the largest and most trusted certification authority online. For further details on security and encryption, please visit the VeriSign website.
Payment Security
In order to protect your credit card information, all online transactions are conducted using a secure 128-bit SSL encryption signed by VeriSign. Encryption ensures that no information is accessible to unknown parties, so you need not worry about information theft. CACTUS will never store your credit card information, so there is no possibility of fraudulent charges. Please visit VeriSign’s website for more information about payment security.
We have also partnered with PayPal to provide you with the most secure and easy-to-use online payment system available. For information about how PayPal can protect you from unauthorized payments, visit PayPal’s website.
