Additionally, when the terms “Site” or “Website” are used, these terms refer to Camdolls, any predecessor or successor domain or URL, along with any website published by Us, or website we may choose to operate in the future, unless a site is specifically exempt from this Agreement.
1.1.2 You, the User - As the User of this Site and/or Services, this Policy will refer to the User as “You” or through any second-person pronouns, such as “Yours,” etc. Hereinafter, the User of the Site and/or Services shall be referred to in applicable second-person pronouns. You certify that You are over eighteen (18) years of age.
1.1.3 User vs. Member - For the purposes of this Policy, all Members are Users, but not all Users are Members. This Policy applies to all Users whether they are Members or not. You become a User by accessing this Site or the Services in any way, therefore You need not become a Member of the Site to make this Policy apply to You. You are not considered a Member as defined by this Policy until such time as You have submitted Your online account Registration (hereinafter “Registration”). You become a Member by registering on the Site with id.net for a User ID and password as required by the Registration, as discussed below.
1.2 What this Policy is – This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy practices of Camdolls regarding any personal data You may provide to Us or that We receive from You. We remind You that by accessing the Site in any manner, You are affirmatively agreeing to being bound by all of the terms contained in this Policy.

4.2.1 Any information we receive regarding You is to enable Us to offer better services to You. We use Your information to complete transactions, respond to Your requests, answer Your questions, and notify You of promotions updates, or special offers that We think may interest You.
4.2.2 We also use Your information for optimizing the operation of the Site, enhancing Our promotional and marketing activities, improvement of Our products and services, troubleshooting, performing administrative tasks, handling of disputes, enforcing Our agreements, complying with laws and regulations, cooperation with law enforcement activities, and for other purposes necessary to provide You with the services You requested.

4.3.1 Every time You visit the Site, We receive and record information on Our server logs from Your browser, Your IP address, cookie information, and reference site domain name.

4.3.3 We may install both persistent and session cookies when You visit Our Site. Cookies allow Us to recognize users and avoid repetitive requests for the same information. If You choose to reject all cookies, there are areas of the Site that may not function properly.
5 DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION
5.1 Privacy Settings for Your profile – You can control the information of Your profile, You want to be accessible to Members or Users. There are three levels of information disclosure, which are the following:
5.1.1 Private – this means that You are on a one on one stream, and only the Member You are interacting with can see some of Your information that is displayed on Our Website.
5.1.2 Friends Only – this means that You can socially interact and stream with Members You have defined as “Friends”, and only those Members can view Your information that is displayed on Our Website and contact You.
5.1.3 Public – this means that all Your information displayed on Your profile and Your stream, can be seen by Users. In other words, this information can be associated with You, can show up when someone does a research on Our Website or a public search engine and will be accessible to everyone who uses Our APIs.
Take notice that by setting Your streams to “Public”, all Your streams will be accessible and viewed by Users.
5.2 Except in the event that you have willingly chosen to do so, We do not share Your information with other companies, organizations and individuals outside of the Company unless one of the following circumstances apply:
5.2.1 With Your consent – We will share Your information to other companies, organizations and individuals outside of the Company when We have Your consent to do so. We require opt-in consent for the sharing of any personal information.
5.2.2 For external processing – We provide Your information to Our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for Us, based on Our instructions and in compliance with Our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidential and security measures.
5.2.3 For legal reasons – We will share Your information with other companies, organizations or individuals, outside the Company, if We have a good-faith belief that access, use , preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:
meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request (including legal requests from jurisdictions outside of Hong Kong)
enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations
detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues
protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of the Website, Our Users or the public as required or permitted by law.
5.2.4 If We are involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, We will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected Users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
