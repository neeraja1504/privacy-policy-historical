Security and Privacy
We are committed to ensuring that the personal information you share with us remains secure and private. To demonstrate our commitment, we have developed the privacy policy outlined below. If you have any questions about this policy, or the content or practices of our Web site, we encourage you to contact us.
Collection of Personal Information and Use
We only collect personal information when you list a property, place an order, request a service, ask for information or complete a survey. Depending on the service you use, the type and amount of personal information collected will vary. The manner in which we use this data is explained later in this policy.
However, we may disclose personally identifiable information if required to do so by law or upon any corporate reorganization. Although our site may contain links to other sites, Media That Deelivers, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites.
Placing Subscriptions and Orders
When placing an order, you will be asked to provide your name, billing address, phone number, e-mail address and your credit card number and its expiration date. We use this information only to process, confirm and ship your order. We may also request your birth date, your driver's license number and state of issuance to verify your identity and to complete your order.
Using Another Service
When you request information, submit comment or ask questions via e-mail, we may request your name and e-mail address. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries, unless you specifically request to be added to our mailing list.
When you enter an online contest or other promotion, we may ask for your name, address, or e-mail address so we can administer the contest and notify winners. Also, as part of a promotion or contest, we may ask you to complete a customer survey to learn more about your shopping preferences. Media That Deelivers, Inc. will not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information.
If you request to be on our mailing list, you may receive communications via e-mail or postal mail about our products, services, or contest and promotions that may be of interest to you. Also, from time to time, we may request information from you via e-mail or postal mail.
You may receive information from us if you participate in a Media That Deelivers, Inc. contest or promotion even if you requested not to be part of our mailing list. If you do not want to receive or share information, please do not participate in these promotions or contests.
