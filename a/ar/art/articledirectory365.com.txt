Article Directory 365 will never sell, barter, or rent your email address to any unauthorized third party.
The information given to use to register at Article Directory 365 is only used for you to sign up and log in for future submissions. Your details will not be shared with anyone or made available for public viewing.
However, we can not protect the information you submit in the body of your article, although we do ask for visitors to respect the author of the text by crediting them should some or all of their submission by republished.
Article Directory 365 reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Please check this page periodically for changes. Your continued use of our site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes. Information collected prior to the time any change is posted will be used according to the rules and laws that applied at the time the information was collected.
