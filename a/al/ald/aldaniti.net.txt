Privacy Policy | Aldaniti Network
Privacy Statement
Aldaniti International Network Limited (‘Aldaniti’) is the proprietor of the site http://www.aldaniti.net and responsible for all the sweepstakes promoted by Aldaniti. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy statement, you should first contact us using the contact details at Contact Information. Aldaniti recognizes that your privacy and the protection of your personal information is important to you. This privacy statement discloses how Aldaniti helps to protect your personal information while using the Aldaniti web site http://www.aldaniti.net or the sweepstakes promoted by Aldaniti.
Aldaniti is registered under the Data Protection Act 1998 under registration number Z1688527. The personal information that you provide to Aldaniti is covered by the safeguards set out in that Act. To find out more information about the Act please visit http://www.informationcommissioner.gov.uk.
This privacy statement, operation of the site http://www.aldaniti.net and each of the sweepstakes promoted by Aldaniti are regulated by English law.
Collection of your personal information:
In order to use this site, you must first register with Aldaniti by completing the registration form. You will be asked to confirm your acceptance of the following statement before you can start the registration process:
“This present sweepstake/competition is carried out with the objective to incorporate your personal information in the marketing database, property of Aldaniti International Network, Ltd. Should you not wish that your personal information forms part of this mentioned database, we kindly ask you not to participate in this competition/sweepstake. Furthermore, if you do not inform us otherwise, your participation in this sweepstake is automatically treated as your permission to include your personal information in our database. Should you object to your personal information forming part of these databases mentioned above, please click here.”
You also agree to the terms and conditions of the privacy statement that govern how your information will be processed.”
Please note that you may withdraw any consent that you have given in connection with this site by logging on and amending your details at the site.
During registration you will also be asked to give contact information (such as name, surname, postal address, telephone and email address) and some demographic information (such as age and gender). Once you have registered you will automatically receive a welcome email, and a newsletter packed with great prizes for you to win or offers that may be of interest to you.
You will have additional options at different points in the site to provide more information about yourself (such as details of the mobile network that you use, number of children that you have or car owned by you). We encourage you to take advantage of these options, so we can provide you with a more personalized experience on our site.
Use of your personal information:
Aldaniti will not use or share your personal information in a manner that differs from what is described within this Privacy Statement or the registration opt-in statement without your prior written consent.
General disclosure of personal information
The information that you provide will be used:
by Aldaniti and carefully selected third parties to provide you with information about goods and services which may be of interest to you and we or they may contact you about these by post or telephone where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes.;
by carefully selected third parties to provide you with information about goods and services which may be of interest to you and they may contact you by post, telephone or electronic means (e-mail or SMS) only if you have consented to be contacted for such purposes.
by Aldaniti to determine the order of the questionnaires and prizes that are displayed to you at the site.
by Aldaniti to provide you (provided you are an existing customer) with information about goods and services similar to those which were the subject of a previous transaction with you and we may contact you by post, telephone or by electronic means (e-mail or SMS) for such purposes.
by Aldaniti to allow you to participate in interactive features of the site, when you choose to do so.
by Aldaniti to notify you about changes to the site.
by Aldaniti to carry out its obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and Aldaniti.
If you do not want Aldaniti to use your data in this way, or to pass your details on to third parties for marketing purposes, please tick the relevant box situated on the registration form.
Sponsored sweepstakes and Co-branded sites:
The site hosts a number of questionnaires. By completing a questionnaire and consenting to be contacted by Aldaniti or a third party, you consent to Aldaniti and the third party sending you information about products and/or services of interest to you based on the answers that you gave in completing the questionnaire and where you have consented to receive such information. The information that you provide may in addition be used, by Aldaniti and carefully selected third parties to provide you with information and offers by email, sms, voice, post or other appropriate means of communications about products and/or services that may be of interest to you where you have consented to receive such information. If you would prefer not to receive such information or offers please indicate your preference by ticking the box provided, located on the “Amend Details” page and/or the questionnaire.
Managing your personal information:
The data you provide to Aldaniti will be held as you have provided it. You may at any time amend or update your data by sending us an E-mail with your updated information to info@aldaniti.net.
You are entitled to see the information held about you and you may ask us to make any necessary changes to ensure that it is accurate and kept up to date. If you wish to do this, please contact us. Any access request may be subject to a fee of £10 to meet our costs in providing you with details of the information we hold about you.
Deregistration and un-subscribing from our database:
You may at any time deregister from Aldaniti by contacting us by sending us an E-mail to info@aldaniti.net. You may also decline to receive any further E-mails that Aldaniti or carefully selected third parties send out by contacting Aldaniti at the address above.
If you no longer wish to receive our newsletter and promotional communications, you may opt-out of receiving them by emailing us or in writing sent to Aldaniti International Network, 12 Brookend Street Ross-on-Wye, HerefordShire HR9 7EG, United Kingdom.
Security and storage of your personal information:
Any data that you provide that is retained by Aldaniti will be held in Aldaniti’s central database, to be accessed by reference to interest or demographic categories in order to provide you with information about those interests you have selected. The process of supplying you with this information is at all times under Aldaniti’s control although specialist third party suppliers who may be sub-contracted to assist in the process. Such third parties will hold your personal data and it may also be transferred, stored, and processed by them or a sub-processor on their behalf at a destination outside the European Economic Area (“EEA”) . Both parties will only process your data in accordance with our instructions and for no other purposes but they may contact you post or telephone for the purposes of informing you of similar goods or services to which you may have already transacted for via Aldaniti or where you have expressly stated that you would like to be contacted on the registration form. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer, storing and processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your personal data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.
The Internet is not a 100% secure medium for communication and, accordingly, Aldaniti cannot guarantee the security of any information you send to Aldaniti via the Internet. Aldaniti is not responsible for any loss or damage you or others may suffer as a result of the loss of confidentiality of such information.
Changes to this Privacy Statement:
Aldaniti may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. Aldaniti will also obtain your opt-in consent for any updates to this Privacy Statement that materially expand the sharing or use of your personal information in ways not disclosed in this Privacy Statement at the time of collection.
