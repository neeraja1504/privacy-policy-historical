Privacy Statement for algebra.help
Privacy Statement
Algebra.Help has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: algebra.help (algebrahelp.com).
IP addresses from users and cookies issued to them are used to create a "user session" which helps us follow trends in the way people use this website. The user sessions are also being used as we develop more advanced services that store user preferences, and worksheet scores.
Demographic data is collected from users on a volunteer basis by a 3rd party company on behalf of algebrahelp.com and Burst! Media. (Burst! Media is an advertising network to which algebrahelp.com belongs). The information collected in the survey is used in aggregate to plan updates and additions to the website, and shared with advertisers who may be potential sponsors or media who may potentially publicize the website..
This site contains links to other sites. algebra.help (algebrahelp.com) is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our feedback form asks visitors for contact information (including e-mail address), and optional demographic information. The e-mail address is only used to respond to questions or comments. The address of the referring web page (the URL of the page that linked a given user to the feedback form) is included with the e-mail to better increase the quality of our responses.
Some advertisements are displayed in conjunction with outside advertising networks such as google adsense and Burst! Media. Privacy policies of these networks change from time to time, so information on them is best obtained from the respective websites.
Contacting Us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact Algebra.Help jrmski @ algebrahelp.com
You may also contact us through our feedback form.
