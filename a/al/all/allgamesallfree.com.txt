Privacy Policy - AllGameAllFree.com
Privacy Policy
Here at www.allgamesallfree.com , www.allgamesallfree.net , www.allgamesallfree.org and all other sites owned by Agaf Inc. you can expect your privacy to be upheld completely. Below is a list of all the areas of our website where some private information is taken, and what it is neccesary for:
Third party advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
Content Submission
When you submit content to this website we ask for your email address, this is neccesary for us to be able to email you if there was a problem with your submission for whatever reason. Your email address will never be sold or shared with any third parties.
Sharing games and animations by email
When you share a game or animation with email the form asks you to put in your name, this is only so the message it automatically creates seems more personal to the person your sending it to, so they know who it came from. It also asks for your email as well, this is simply so the person you sent it to can reply to the email if they wish, this information isn't sold or shared with any third parties.
If you have any questions, comments or concerns regarding this privacy policy feel free to email me from the address: max[at]allgamesallfree.com
