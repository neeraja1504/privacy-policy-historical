Privacy Statement & Terms of Use - Anadea
By using this website or any other websites and apps owned and operated by Anadea, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Statement.
Introduction
Anadea values the privacy and security of the information you share with us. The following Privacy Statement is addressed to both companies and individuals who are on this website or interacting with all Anadea services, websites, or apps. This Privacy Statement applies to information collected through anadea.info ("the website"), and information collected through our App cost calculator and Business name generator. This Statement describes how Anadea collects, uses, shares, protects, or otherwise processes your personal information.
IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT, YOU MUST IMMEDIATELY STOP USING THIS WEBSITE AND ALL ANADEA SERVICES.
Collection and use of information
Anadea collects personal information as required to provide quotes for the provision of services to Clients, and occasionally for marketing and sales campaigns. You may need to provide Anadea with personal information such as:
Name;
Email address;
Mailing address (usually your company’s address);
Phone number;
Payment details (bank or credit/debit card details, or third-party service details, such as PayPal);
Social media account details.
Anadea can use such information for:
potentially and/or starting to work together;
creating agreements before we start work on a Client project;
processing payments and settlement;
measuring, improving, and customizing our services;
so we can contact you about our work together;
fulfilling other technical, logistical, financial, tax, legal, compliance, administrative, or back office functions.
If personal information is only used for a short period (e.g. for specific marketing campaigns) we may delete it.
Cookies and website usage records
Anadea collects information related to the use of this website using cookies and analytics. Anadea uses cookies to authenticate users when they visit the website, track their session, deliver content specific to their preferences and interests, and provide other functions.
Website log and usage information may include web requests, Internet Protocol address, operating system, browser type, browser language, date and time of the request, and cookies that may uniquely identify the browser and/or device you are using. Anadea uses this information to enhance the functionality of the website and to make access more convenient, secure, and efficient.
Anadea also uses information gathered from logs and cookies to monitor website performance, improve website design, functionality, and security, assemble web analytics and activity trends. Anadea retains such information for as long as reasonably required for business purposes or as reasonably required to comply with our legal obligations.
It is likely that the default setting for your browser is set to automatically accept cookies. You can, however, choose not to accept cookies through your browser controls, by going to the "Tools" or "Preferences" menu. If you would like to learn a little more about changing your browser settings and specifically what cookies are and how they are used look at your browser's help section, FAQ's or click on the following link: www.allaboutcookies.org
Data protection
Anadea is committed to taking commercially reasonable steps to protect all of the data we collect, process and use for customers and web visitors. Data is protected in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation ((EU) 2016/679 2018), which came into effect on 25 May 2018. Its purpose is to provide a better understanding to all EU citizens of how their data is being used and to protect the rights and privacy of individuals.
Anadea will not sell, trade or share information to outside third parties. This includes trusted partners or suppliers that we collaborate with, and have a commercial relationship with, and we will assure that they will take measures to protect and keep the data confidential in accordance with GDPR.
Disclosure of information
Anadea may disclose personal information where:
required by law or by an order or requirement of a court, administrative agency, or other government entity, or by court rules concerning the production of records;
Anadea has reasonable grounds to believe that use or disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, privacy, property, or safety of other web visitors, other clients, social media users, or any other persons or organisations connected to Anadea;
Anadea has reasonable grounds to believe that the information relates to the breach of an agreement or violation of the law that has been, is being, or is about to be committed;
it is necessary for fraud protection, risk reduction, or the establishment or collection of funds owed to us;
it is necessary to enforce or apply our legal agreements, to pursue remedies, or to limit damages to Anadea;
the information is public, which means it is not protected under GDPR and no right to privacy exists;
a user has provided consent or with user's direction;
other reasons allow it or other laws require it.
When we are required or permitted to disclose information, we will not disclose more information than is necessary to fulfil the disclosure purpose.
Anadea may disclose aggregate or statistical information to our business partners, for public relations purposes, or other purposes.
Security
Anadea combines technical and physical safeguards with employee policies and procedures to protect user information. We work to protect personal information during transmission by using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) software, which encrypts the information that is provided online at our Platform. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to protect the information. We will also continue to explore new technology for protecting information and upgrade our information security systems when appropriate.
Further, we permit only authorized Anadea employees to access any personal information. If an employee misuses personal information, we may take disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. If any third party individual or organization misuses personal information, we will take action, up to and including termination of any agreement between Anadea and that individual or organization.
Data integrity
Anadea processes personal information only for the purposes it was originally collected and in accordance with this Privacy Statement. Anadea takes commercially reasonable steps to ensure that the personal data is relevant, reliable for its intended use, accurate, complete, and current for the purposes for which it is used. Anadea retains information for as long as reasonably required for business purposes or as reasonably required to comply with our legal obligations.
Changes to our Privacy Statement
In the future, if we make any changes to our Privacy Statement we will update this page on our website, so please make sure to check our website and this Privacy Statement periodically.
In the event of a data breach
We care about our customers and your personal information is our responsibility. To protect your data, our policy will detect any attacks and we will take a responsive action as soon as possible, and as part of that, we will contact you. In compliance with GDPR, if there is a breach we will notify the United Kingdom's Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO), or another EU data protection regulatory body - depending on the jurisdiction where the data breach occurred, or the EU countries where clients or web visitors were affected, within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.
Contact information
If you have questions or concerns related to this Privacy Statement, please contact us.
If you would like to know more about the data we have, you have the right to require a copy of the data we hold about you. To check this information, please contact us.
If you no longer want to appear in our records, we will make sure we to remove you from our database.
For any complaints or concerns about how we manage your personal data, you can contact us.
