Privacy Policy for agma.io
Welcome to agma.io. If you continue to browse and use this website you consent to our privacy policy.
Third Parties:
- Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information as a result of ad serving on this site
For More information on Third Parties Go here: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/
Certain analytics and advertising tools on our site may also collect information about:
- Your General Location (Country, City)
- The amount of time you spend on this site
- The website that you came from
- IP Address
- Battery information
- Connection information
- Browser information
What data do we collect from you?
- If you register with us, we store the data you enter into our website registration page, such as username, password and e-mail address.
- Other data/information you enter through using the service, for example, but not limited to, competitions, surveys, scores, ranking.
- Information you provide by completing any in game purchase such as buying game currency or other items/products.
- We also store data when you use our services, independant on whether you are registered with us or have any registered account. This data includes your copmuter's browser version, IP address, referred suorce, operating system version, page loading time, network, generated device id information, and hashed MAC address.
- Game play history, or time played/used the service.
What do we use the Information for?
-To provide you the service.
- The information is used to protect your account and make it possible for you to recover it in case you lose the details. Some information is also collected to personalize your experience.
- Information is also collected to analyze user data and enhance the user experience.
-To improve the Services, for analysis or reporting purposes and to offer you technical support or help with your questions.
Do we discuss or trade your information with outside parties?
- No. All of the information you provide us will be kept private.
- No information is sold to any outside parties.
Disclaimer about the Ad technology providers We may use third
-party Service Providers to show advertisements to you to help support and maintain our website. Depending on the ads that are served, your data may be shared with any of the ad technology provider listed on the link below. Ad technology providers (including Google, Ad Exchanges, SSPs, DSPs, ad networks, bidders and vendors) use your data for example, to show you personalised ads or to report on conversions. In the overview in the link below you can find references to information on how the technology providers use your data. Link: http://agma.io/docs/Disclaimer-ad-tech-providers.pdf
Do we use cookies or local storage?
- Yes. Cookies/local storage are used to the save certain information, such as (but not limited to) settings, game options, nickname, account info.
How long do we keep the information for? We can keep the data for as long as it is relevant and useful for the purpose of which it was originally collected for.
Close or erase or delete account If you do not wish to have an account with agma any more, you can close it by contacting us. You will need to provide proof you are the original account creator, or the owner of the account, and/or the data belongs to you, in which we will be able to remove it. You can send us a message through social media, email, and more: http://agma.io/support.php
Advertising
We use third party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here (http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp)
Your consent settings/Opt out
Learn more about your consent settings and customize your consent choices by clicking on the blue button on the bottom left corner of this screen (Privacy settings).
Your acceptance of these terms
- By using this Site, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our Site. Your continued use of the Site following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
Changes to this Policy
- Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.
