Advplace.com - Privacy Policy
Advplace.com acts in accordance with current privacy legislation and aims to meet current Internet best practice. We will never disclose any information you may submit to us to third parties. All information you submit will only be used to receive or use services on our website.
We do not use cookies to identify or track individual visitors. Information stored in the server log files (IP addresses, for example) is used to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our web site, and to gather broad traffic/usage information.
Advplace.com may revise this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
We will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
