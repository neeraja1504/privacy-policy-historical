KÜHL Clothing Privacy Policy | Born in the Mountains
This is the web site of KÜHL® USA. We can be reached via email at customerservice@kuhl-usa.com. For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our newsletter and order our products. Information collected from customers is used for order notification and to send product information only. It is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
We are privately using anonymous data collected by the Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting.
All emails contain an unsubscribe link which can be used to opt-out. Alternatively, you can email customerservice@kuhl-usa.com to be removed from any mailing list. With respect to cookies: We do use cookies on the KÜHL® USA web site to store certain login details to make future visits quicker and easier for you.
