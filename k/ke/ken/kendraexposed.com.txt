KendraExposed.com PRIVACY STATEMENT
KendraExposed.com HAS CREATED THIS PRIVACY STATEMENT IN ORDER TO DEMONSTRATE OUR COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY. THE FOLLOWING DISCLOSES OUR INFORMATION GATHERING AND DISSEMINATION PRACTICES FOR KendraExposed.com. No personal information will be shared with any third parties without the customer's permission.
1 .ORDER FORMS.
KendraExposed.com is operated by Stardust Media Ltd (the "Company"). Our site uses an order form for customers to register and pay for site memberships or other products and services. We collect visitors' contact information (like their email address).
(a) Contact Data.
This information is used to send order confirmations, information about our company, and promotional material to our customers. The customer's contact information may also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. The customer's contact information is never shared with any third parties without the customer's permission. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings (see the Opt-Out and Delete/Deactivate sections below).
2. "COOKIE" TECHNOLOGY.
KendraExposed.com occasionally uses an industry-wide technology known as "cookies" which store certain information on your computer and which would allow us to customize your visit to our Site or simply facilitate your signing in to use the services. We do not extract information about individual users or their computers during this process, across time, or other websites. Users can disable cookies within their browser but some features may not work properly in our sites. For more information on "cookie" technology, please go to http://www.cookiecentral.com/.
3. COMPANY NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR THIRD PARTY OFFERS.
KendraExposed.com contains links to other related Internet sites, resources and sponsors of the Company, who, from time to time, will offer products and services that may be of value to you. Selection of one of these ad banners or links redirects the User off of KendraExposed.com to a third party. Transactions that occur between the User and the third party are strictly between the User and the third party and are not the responsibility of the Company. Because the Company is not responsible for these outside resources or their privacy policy or content, the User should direct any concerns regarding any external link to its site administrator or Webmaster.
4. MONITORING USE OF SERVICE.
We may monitor your use of this service and use or disclose any information so gathered to both satisfy any law, regulation or other government request and to operate the service properly and efficiently.
5. OPT-OUT.
Users may opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners by choosing such an option at the point where we request information about the visitor.
