Your privacy is important to Keenzo.com !
How we protect your privacy
We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system. Pages which require you to login or enter personal and financial information are secured by Verisign, the best in the industry.
Collected Information and Usage
Keenzo will not share, sell, or rent any personal information entered on our website unless ordered by a court of law.  Information submitted to us is only available to selected employees managing this information, our credit processor, and fraud checking institutions for purposes of order fraud checking, payment processing, contacting you, or sending you information on your request.
Keenzo also logs your IP address and browsing patterns while on our website.  This information is used to analyze visitor trends and demographics as well as detecting and preventing fraudulent activity.
Security
Keenzo takes precautions to ensure our customer data is secure both on and off line. When entering sensitive information on Keenzo.com,  that information is protected by SSL (Secure Socket Layer), the best encryption in the industry.   Keenzo employs encryption by VeriSign, the most advanced form of SSL available.  To learn more about Verisign, please visit their website at http://www.VeriSign.com.
The only data pertaining to your credit card that we keep on our servers will be the last four digits to be used as a reference for you and our processing staff.  All other credit card data is purged once we receive authorization on the card immediately after you enter your order..
