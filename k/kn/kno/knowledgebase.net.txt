nGenera Privacy Statement
nGenera Corporation Privacy Statement
nGenera Corporation, with its principal offices located at 3015 112th Avenue NE, Suite 100, Bellevue, WA 98004, together with its subsidiaries (hereinafter "nGenera") are committed to protecting the privacy of their clients. nGenera will not distribute, sell, or rent names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information to any third party.
nGenera may share non-personal, non-individual information collectively with its marketing partners strictly for its own research purposes. For example, nGenera will not provide specifics around who purchased a specific product, but rather how many customers purchased that product.
Member profile information (e.g. name, title, address, items purchased) may be used to create personalized content, offers, services, and advertising to be used only by nGenera.
Third Party Content
nGenera Web sites may contain content from third-party content providers, however the content provider(s) will not receive nGenera user or customer information from nGenera.
Cookies
nGenera and/or third parties may use cookies to compile statistical data in order to analyze how visitors use the nGenera Web site. The process may involve setting cookies on local systems in order to gather the necessary information to perform the statistical analysis. The information collected is designed to measure the performance of various marketing initiatives and overall Web site performance. This information can include the link and referral Web site, as well as activity on the nGenera Web site, including dates and information submitted on nGenera's site.
Log Files
nGenera uses IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement, and to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
Surveys
nGenera's site occasionally requests information from users via surveys. Participation in these surveys is completely voluntary. Information requested may include contact and demographic information. Survey data may be used to monitor or improve the use and satisfaction of nGenera's Web site, products, or services. nGenera will retain ownership to all data provided via voluntary surveys.
Links
nGenera's Web site contains links to other sites. Such other sites are not under the control of nGenera. Therefore, nGenera makes no representations as to the accuracy or integrity of the content included on such other sites.
When you access a non-nGenera Web site, you do so at your own risk and nGenera is not responsible for the accuracy or reliability of any information, data, opinions, advice, or statements made on these sites. nGenera provides these links merely as a convenience and the inclusion of such links does not imply that nGenera endorses or accepts any responsibility for the content or uses of such Web sites. nGenera encourages all of its visitors to read the individual privacy statements of such Web sites. The nGenera Privacy Statement applies solely to information collected by the nGenera Web site.
Additionally, while the nGenera Web site may be accessed from countries around the world and contains references to nGenera products, services, and programs such references do not obligate nGenera to provide such products, services, or programs in every country.
Opt-Out Policy
Please email unsubscribe@nGenera.com if you no longer wish to receive information from nGenera.
Changes to this Privacy Statement:
nGenera reserves the right to modify this nGenera Privacy Statement, and will publish notification on this Web site no less than 30 days before the revised statement takes effect.
