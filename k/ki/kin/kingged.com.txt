Privacy Policy | Kingged.com
Respect for your privacy is very important to us, so we want to explain our online information practices regarding how your information is collected and used.
The Information We Collect and How Used:
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on Kingged.com web site. On some pages, you can make requests and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected on these pages may include, but is not limited to, name, address, e-mail address, phone number. We use the information you provide to answer your requests and to send you materials.
On some of the pages within this website, you may find advertising.  The third parties who provide the advertising may collect information about Kingged.com website users, as well as, information about users who click advertisements.
We believe in honesty of relationship, opinion and identity. Kingged.com accepts forms of cash advertising, sponsorship, paid insertions or other forms of compensation. The compensation received may influence the advertising content, topics or posts made in Kingged.com. That content, advertising space or post will be clearly identified as paid or sponsored content.
The owner(s) of Kingged.com is compensated to provide opinion on products, services, websites and various other topics. Even though the owner(s) of Kingged.com receives compensation for our posts or advertisements, we always give our honest opinions, findings, beliefs, or experiences on those topics or products. The views and opinions expressed on Kingged.com are purely the writers’ own. Any product claim, statistic, quote or other representation about a product or service should be verified with the manufacturer, provider or party in question.
Kingged.com does not contain any content which might present a conflict of interest.
How To Contact Us:
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy and disclosure policies, please send us an email at info @ kingged.com
