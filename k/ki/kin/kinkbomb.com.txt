Privacy Statement
Your privacy and security is our top priority.   As you can understand with the nature of our site, we take your privacy very seriously.  You can be assured of our discretion on what information is visible and not visible.  Certain information such as credit cards, private information, addresses, real names are always kept strictly confidential.  We highly suggest utilizing email aliases that do NOT contain any identifying information such as first or last names or locations.  Utilizing discreet handles and emails will further help your privacy.
Due to the social nature of our site, there is information that you may wish to share or populate.  You are in full control over what other members can see or cannot see regarding this information.  For more information or greater detail, please visit our informational pages regarding your privacy, your accounts, and web security.
