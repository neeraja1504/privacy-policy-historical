Kirin Hobby - Toys, Action Figures, and Collectibles
Online ordering is secure.
Any personal details you submit to us using this website, including your address and credit card information, are encrypted and kept secure via industry standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software. 128-bit SSL is the standard for large-scale online merchants, banks, brokerages, health care organizations, and insurance companies worldwide.
We value your privacy.
Any and all the information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential. We will not sell, reuse, rent, disclose, or loan out this information without your permission.
We use the information we collect to process your orders and/or to notify you of updates you requested. We also collect information to maintain your user account and to provide you with a better shopping experience (ie. shopping cart, order history).
Please contact us if you have any questions.
