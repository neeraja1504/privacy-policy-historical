Privacy Policy
The following online Privacy Policy describes the information we collect from you and how we use it. By accessing or using this Website, you consent to our Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Website constitutes your acceptance to this Privacy Policy and any updates. Please review this Privacy Policy regularly so that you understand our current Privacy Policy and any modifications that have been made. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into, and is subject to, the Terms of Use.
Collecting Information
When you visit our Website, you provide us with personal information you knowingly disclose to us and Website interaction information collected by us. We request information from you (which may include name, age, address, telephone number, email address, city location, zip code, and when necessary, credit card information) when you register for the Website, make purchases, request to receive communications, or participate in a promotion or other Website feature.
Using Information
We may use the information that we collect to communicate with you about your purchase, account information, and customer service inquiries. We may also use the information to improve our products and customer service to provide you with a better shopping experience. In addition, we may send you information promoting our products, special events, and other offers that we feel you may be interested in. If you purchase a product from the Website, your name and address will be added to the Killer Dana email promotions and mailing lists. If you do not wish to receive email promotions, please call 1-949-489-8380.
Your California Rights
Killer Dana collects various types of personal information about you both online and offline, as described in this privacy policy. Under California law, customers of Killer Dana who are residents of California may request certain information about our disclosure of personal information during the prior calendar year to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please write to us at the following address: Killer Dana Customer Relations 24621 Del Prado Dana Point, Ca 92629. Accessing our web site from outside the US If you are visiting kdsurfshop.com from a location outside of the United States, you will be connected through and to servers located within the United States. All online orders will be accepted and fully processed in and shipped from the United States and all information you provide will be securely maintained on web servers and our internal systems located within the United States. By providing your information to us, you agree that all personal information will be used in accordance with this privacy policy.
Sharing Information
We do not rent, sell or otherwise share your information with outside companies.
Cookies
We use cookies to collect information from your browser when you use the Website. A cookie is a small removable data file that is stored by your web browser on your computer that allows you to place an order on our Website as well as allows us to enhance and personalize your online experience. If you choose to disable the cookies in your browser, you can still use all parts of the Website with the exception of the shopping cart function.
