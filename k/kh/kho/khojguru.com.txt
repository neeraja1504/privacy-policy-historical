Privacy Policy | Khojguru
PRIVACY POLICY
This privacy policy applies to any transactions and activities in which you engage, and data gathered, on KHOJGURU app and does not apply to any other web site or offline point of contact between KHOJGURU.COM or any other company, and consumers.
CUSTOMER'S INFORMATION PRIVACY
KHOJGURU respects & takes steps to protect & safeguard the complete privacy of our customers. We strive to take all care possible of the information that is received from you.
WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT & USE IT
We don't collect personal information from you without your consent. We ask for access to some information at the time of download and use only those you share access to. However, we get non-personal information pertaining to your app usage patterns, device details, location and content preference. This is ONLY to deliver tailored coupon experience for you. We DO NOT sell your information to any clients or partners, unless you wish to submit details to any merchant at the time of availing a deal.
When you sign up / register with us through your social networking profile, we collect information like, username, email id, phone number, postal address, gender and other public information on your social networking site. Since we do not support any financial / payment transactions on khojguru app and website, we do not save any passwords and card details of your purchases through us, and on the merchant site. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other Web sites.
COOKIES
We may use cookies to tailor and improve the content we deliver to you while you are on our sites. You can configure your computer's browser to alert you when a site is attempting to send you a cookie and allow you to accept or refuse the cookie.
TELL-A-FRIEND
If you elect to use our referral service for informing a friend about any of our services/site, we ask you for your friend's name and email address. KHOJGURU.COM will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit the KHOJGURU.COM Site.
LEGAL NOTICE
KHOJGURU.COM makes no representations or warranties that any information or content provided herein is accurate, complete, or authentic; and KHOJGURU.COM shall have no liability whatsoever for information or content provided by third parties. KHOJGURU.COM reserves the right to modify any information, content or services at any time without notice.
