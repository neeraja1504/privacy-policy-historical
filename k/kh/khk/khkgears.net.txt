Privacy policy | KHK Gears
– We understand how important personal information is and we comply with the laws related to protection and handling of personal information.
– We will use customer information for the following purposes.
Sending direct mail, catalogue, and E-mail information about our products and gear manufacturing services
Identifying customer needs and trends in order to improve our services
We may entrust sending direct mail and catalogue information via companies who are counterparties to our business contract.
– We appropriately manage customer’s personal information, and never disclose your personal information to third parties without notification unless there are statutory justifications.
However, we may disclose your information to a company under a confidentiality agreement, but only to the extent necessary.
Kohara Gear Industry Co.,Ltd.
