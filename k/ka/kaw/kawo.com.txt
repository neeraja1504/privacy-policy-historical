Privacy Policy – KAWO
About this privacy policy
KAWO provides the Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from users of our website kawo.com, hereby referred to as the “KAWO website” and app.kawo.com, hereby referred to as the “KAWO app”.
This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.
What information do we collect?
We collect personally identifiable information from you when you register on the KAWO website.
When registering on the KAWO website and KAWO app, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address or phone number. However, you may visit the KAWO website anonymously.
What do we use your information for?
Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:
To improve customer service
(your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs)
Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested
To send periodic emails
How do we protect your information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information.
KAWO is conscientious about safeguarding the confidentiality of your personally identifiable information. Access to your KAWO account information is restricted to authorized admins only. Our team will access the account only under these circumstances:
There is a technical bug that needs to be fixed
We are legally required to disclose your personally identifiable information to law authorities
Do we use cookies?
Yes, the KAWO app requires cookies to be enabled as they are used to maintain your login status.
Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information.
This does not include trusted third parties (Weibo.com, SendGrid.com & Nexmo.com) who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.
Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance
We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
Your Consent
By using the KAWO website and KAWO app, you consent to our privacy policy.
Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page, and/or update the Privacy Policy modification date below.
This policy was last modified on Monday 4th May 2015.
