Privacy Policy ~ Karen's Whimsy
If you email Karen’s Whimsy, we may save your email address in order to contact you in the future. If you do not want to receive email in the future, please let us know by sending email through our contact page.
The information collected is aggregate information on what pages visitors view. The information is used to improve the content of our web pages and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
We do not set any cookies.
If you supply us with a postal address or phone number, we will only contact you with information you requested or with questions regarding orders you have placed online. This information will not be shared with anyone else.
With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
