K12 Systems - Privacy Policy
K12 Systems, Inc. collects information only within the support structure of this website.  It is used to better serve our customer support needs.  Personal information collected will never be divulged to third party companies unless ordered by a court of law.  The information collected by K12 Systems includes, but is not limited to:
User ID associated with a purchase of a Sapphire Software product.
"Was this information helpful” responses associated with knowledge base requests
IP address of knowledge base requests
Email address of support request (if supplied by user)
For question or concerns with this Privacy Policy or the information collected by K12 Systems, Inc. Sapphire Software contact us at 866-366-9540.
