Privacy Policy
We value our relationship with you and your privacy is important to us. We may collect certain personal information about you when you visit our website, which may include information that can be used to personally identify you, including but not limited to your name, address, e-mail address, telephone number. However, Kolossal Leather safeguards the privacy of all customers as all details about personal information and transactions are maintained with strict confidentiality. We do not share your personal information or transactions with any third party vendors.
