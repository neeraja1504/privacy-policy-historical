Can you tell me how you deal with privacy issues?
www.SoccerInbox.com (hereinafter SoccerInbox) values the privacy of our visitors/customers and takes every known precaution to safe guard the information we collect from unauthorized use. The following is a detailed explanation of the privacy policy for SoccerInbox.
At SoccerInbox.com, we take security and privacy issues seriously. Incorporating 128 byte encryption technology, we ensure your privacy is protected. User ID with passwords are transmitted and verified on secure pages, ensuring that no unauthorized parties have access to your communications with SoccerInbox.com. This same level of security is applied to your shopping basket details, checkout and delivery information, in addition to all information pertaining to your account with BoxToDoor.com.
Is it possible other people could obtain my password from your Site?
Your User Password is only stored in an encrypted form, so that the password you enter will only be known to you. Because your password is encrypted by a one-way process, SoccerInbox.com has no means of decrypting your password.
Will other people be able to obtain my credit info after I use your Site?
Credit card transactions with SoccerInbox.com are authorized by professional merchant providers, who employ the highest standards of internet security. You can rest assured that your credit card details will never be exposed to any unauthorized person or entity. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our security policies, please feel free to contact the SoccerInbox.com Service Department.
Will you call me from times to times to 'Recommend' best deals?
Your Phone numbers will only be used at delivery, emergency of your orders, or other occasions under your permission. SoccerInbox.com do not use it to contact our you for sales.
Can I correct or remove my message from SoccerInbox.com?
You always have the option to view your account to make sure the information we have collected from you is indeed correct. You also have the option of removing any information from your account that you wish to keep private. However, please note that removing certain information such as your email address, contact information, and the like may greatly hinder our ability to effectively provide the service or products you have requested. We recommend, that you contact us first to discuss which information you may remove without interrupting the quality of service we intend to provide for you.
