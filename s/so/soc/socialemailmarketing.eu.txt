Privacy policy - Social Email Marketing
We care about your privacy. In this privacy statement you will find information about the personal data this blog collects, and how we use that information.
Social Email Marketing may collect and use information that you provide for the purpose of registering to the newsletter.
Social Email Marketing may use your personal information to enable your access to and use of the newsletter and send you updates on events or whitepapers that we might organize in the future and that are related to the topics this blog covers.
Your personal information will never be sold, rented or shown to third parties. You can at all times unsubscribe from the newsletter and change your personal data using the built-in tools of the newsletter.
All data you submit for the newsletter are hosted by our Email Marketing Service provider, MailChimp.
Please check this page occasionally because we may update this policy.
Please also note that we are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of websites we link to in our posts and that you should read the privacy policies of these blogs, firms or web sites.
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please send a mail to info@socialemailmarketing.eu
