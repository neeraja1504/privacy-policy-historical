SOLARIS IMAGES :: Privacy
SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. respects your right to privacy. This privacy policy enumerates our practices in regard to any information we collect online.
Personal information.
In order to provide services to our customers we may require you to provide us with certain information such as your name, address, phone number and e-mail address etc. This information will be kept in a confidential file for which SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. is responsible to be used for internal purposes only and more particularly to sell you products, audits on our latest promotions and new images.
By providing this information to SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. you are agreeing to allow us to share this information with SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS’s PVT. LTD. partners, subsidiaries as well, with special benefits, offers or promotions. You may at any time access, from our mailing list by sending us an e-mail at contact@solarisimages.com
SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. does not sell or lease information about its customers to third parties. SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. shall not grow wide any of your personal information to other companies or individuals without your permission. However, you may need to provide your name and delivery address to third parties that SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. uses for delivering purposes or when we are required by law.
SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. will take all reasonable care and steps to protect the confidentiality of your personal information, however be aware that perfect security on the Internet is highly impossible.
Use of Cookies
When you visit SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. website, you place a “cookies” in the hard drive of your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that a website can store on your web browser and latter retrieve. Cookies allow SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. to make its website more useful by storing information about your preferences. For example, cookies enable this website to recognise the information you have consented to give us, such as the light boxes you have created and determine what areas of the website are most of your interest. You can set your browser's option not to accept cookies, though as a result you may not then be able to access all of the features of SOLARIS MULTIMEDIA SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. site.
