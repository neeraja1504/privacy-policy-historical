Privacy Policy - Seenive
Welcome to Seenive. This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for Seenive's website and domain.
What personal information we collect
We track user activity (from they come, how long they stay on site, ...) with GA (Google Analytics). These information are used only for better user experience. We do not store this information on our side, we also do not provide them to third party companies.
How we use collected information
Collected information allows us to keep make Seenive better.
Content
This site display public content created by users of mobile application Vine. There are two types of content: multimedia content (videos and thumbnail pictures of posts and pictures of users) and text content (description and location of user and description of post).
Multimedia content is saved on Vine servers and is managed by Vine Labs, Inc..
Text content is saved on Seenive servers and is managed by Seenive. You can request Seenive to delete your text content from Seenive.com, please use this form and send us URL (eg. http://seenive.com/u/934268079968509952) of your profile page or post on Seenive. Every deleted profile will be marked as private to make sure that all your future posts will not be possible to view on Seenive.
Contact us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement the practices of Seenive, you may contact us.
