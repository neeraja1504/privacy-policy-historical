Keeping in mind the importance of privacy of personal information of visitors to this site, the following details the kinds of information that is collected as part of the process of serving Advertisements on this site, and how this information is safeguarded. By visiting seekavideo.net, you are accepting the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy covers seekavideo.net’s treatment of personal information that My seekavideo.net gathers when you are on theseekavideo.net website and when you use seekavideo.net services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that seekavideo.net does not own or control, or to individuals that seekavideo.net does not employ or manage. Information collected by seekavideo.net: seekavideo.net only collects personal information that is relevant to the purpose of the website. This information allows seekavideo.net to provide you with a customized and efficient experience. seekavideo.net does not process this information in a way that is incompatible with this objective. seekavideo.net collects the following types of information from its users:
1. Information You provide through the comments section: The information provided is entirely up to you and you may choose not to provide certain information.
2. Log Files: As is the norm with most websites, data located in log files is collected and used by seekavideo.net and its authorized agents. The information in the log files includes a visitor’s IP address, ISP, browser used at the time of visit to the site, the time you visited and the distribution of pages navigated on this site. seekavideo.net uses this traffic data to help diagnose problems with its servers, analyze trends and administer the website. Many companies offer programs that help you to visit websites anonymously. While seekavideo.net will not be able to provide you with a personalized experience if you cannot be recognized, seekavideo.net wants you to be aware that these programs are available.
3. E-mail Communications seekavideo.net is very concerned about your privacy and will never provide your email address to a third party without your explicit permission, as detailed in the “Sharing Your Information” section below. You will receive an email from seekavideo.net only if you have specifically subscribed to email reminders about new postings on the site.
3. Cookies: Third party advertisements are made use of on this site to support maintenance of the site. The advertisers use technology including cookies as part of providing advertisements on this site, which send the third-party vendor anonymous non-personally identifiable information including your IP address, ISP, browser used at the time of visit to this site, and in some cases, info on whether you have Flash installed. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are transferred to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser that provide information which is used for geo-targeting purposes in order to display advertisements appropriate to your location and your browsing-patterns while visiting this site. seekavideo.net cookies do not collect personal information, and seekavideo.net does not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to find out who you are or what your screen name or e-mail address is. You may choose to disable or selectively turn off cookies or third-party cookies through the settings provided in your browser, or by controlling the preferences in programs like Norton Internet Security. However, this may affect some of your interaction with this site as well as other websites. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You can specifically opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting this page.
Please Note: Neither do these ad-serving technologies in any way hinder or cause you any problems while using the internet to visit this or other sites nor do they spy on you in any way to collect information other than those mentioned. Their sole purpose is to display ads most appropriate to you based on your location.
