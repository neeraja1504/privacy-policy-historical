Privacy Policy | Segou456.info
What information may be collected by Segou456.info , and how is it collected?
Type of Information We Collect.
We may collect any information you enter on the Site or give us in any other way, such as through an email, user-generated content, survey, promotion, or letter using different types of technology, including, but not limited to “web beacons” or “clear gifs”.
We use Facebook login so you can use your existing Facebook account to access the features of the Site. You also must explicitly authorize Facebook to share your information with us when you firs login to the Site with Your Facebook credentials.
Collection of Information by Third Parties:
Our Site may also include third-party advertising, including online behavioral advertising, links to other website, and other content from third party businesses. These third-party websites and advertisers, or advertising companies working on their behalf, sometimes use technology to deliver the advertisements that appear on the Site directly to your browser.
Protect of Children:
It is very important to protect children on the internet. Guardians and Parents should educate their children about protecting themselves online. We don’t intend to and will not knowingly collect information from children under thirteen online.
How Secure Is My Information?
It is your obligations that you must protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer or device. Your emails to us are not secure against interception so do not include sensitive information in them. And you must do what you reasonably can to ensure the security of your personal information. We have electronic and physical procedures to help safeguard, maintain data security and correctly use your information. We may post a notice through the Site if a security breach occurs.
