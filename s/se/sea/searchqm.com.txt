PRIVACY POLICY
Our business name is HTTO Group, Ltd (“Company”). We are the owner of our web site, www.FBDownloader.com. All information provided in this Privacy Policy pertains to HTTO Group, Ltd and www.FBDownloader.com.
I.        General
A.       FBDownloader takes User privacy seriously and takes reasonable efforts to protect your privacy. We have created this Privacy Policy (“Policies”) in order to share with you our information collection and use practices. AS MORE CLEARLY DEFINED HEREIN, WE WILL NEVER KNOWINGLY SELL OR ASSIGN YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WITHOUT YOUR KNOWLEDGE AND OR APPROVAL. Please read these Policies carefully before sharing any personally identifiable information described below.
B.       These Policies apply solely to the web site located at www.FBDownloader.com (“web site”). Since Internet technologies are constantly evolving, these Policies are subject to change at any time and any changes will be posted on this page.
C.      These Policies work hand-in-hand with the Terms of Use , found elsewhere on the FBDownloader web site as well as the End-User License Agreement (EULA) for the FBDownloader Software Application.
II.     Definitions
The following words used in these Policies shall have the following meanings:
1.       " Personal Information " shall mean all data and/or information provided by and about User.
2.       "HTTO web site" shall mean all web sites on which FBDownloader is available for download.
3.       "HTTO User" shall mean all Users of the FBDownloader web site.
4.       "HTTO Products and Services" shall mean the FBDownloader Software Application.
**      *5.* **       "Registration Information" shall mean the Personal Information Users are required to give to HTTO in the registration process, in order to access our services.
_III.    What Personally Identifiable Information Does HTTO Collect and How Is It Used?_
A.       In order to download and use the Software Application, you will be asked for certain personally identifiable information such as name and email address. The information you provide may be used by us to communicate with you in the future.
B.       FBDownloader will not collect personally identifiable information from Users while they use the web site or the FBDownloader Software Application, unless you voluntarily provide it to us or authorize us to collect such information.
C.      FBDownloader may use or disclose User’s personally identifiable information to:
1.       operate and manage the web site;
2.       provide products/services that have been requested by User; or
3.       respond to other inquires or requests by User.
D.      We may disclose User’s personally identifiable information in the establishment or exercise of legal rights or defenses against legal claims, to comply with applicable laws or regulations, to comply with a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena or court order or to investigate suspected fraud or a violation of our Terms of Use. We will never sell User Information to any third party not affiliated with HTTO, without consent, except in connection with the sale or merger of HTTO or the division responsible for such services provided.
E.       HTTO may also use such information for marketing their products and services to you ONLY IF you have not ‘opted out’ to receive such marketing information from us.
F.       HTTO may, from time to time, send or post surveys or requests for User feedback in order to help us make our web site and services better. Any responses we receive from Users will be used only to evaluate our web site and services and no personally identifiable information from Users will be shared with any non-employees of HTTO.
G.      FBDownloader may, from time to time, send notices about special offers, promotions or contests. If you sign up to participant in any of these, we will collect your email address. If you are chosen for a promotion or as a winner in a contest, you give FBDownloader permission to use your name, image and other information about you, for marketing purposes.
IV. _     What Non-Personally Identifiable Information Do We Collect and How Is It Used?_
A.       HTTO may collect or track certain non-personally identifiable information, including, but not limited to, IP numbers and browser type, access times, domain names, usage habits and other information which does not specifically identify any individual.
B.       HTTO may use and disclose non-personally-identifiable information for any number of reasons, including but not limited to, working with business partners and improving our web site and services.
V.      Does HTTO Offer Forums, Chatrooms and Groups?
HTTO does not offer Forums or User Feedback at this time. If Users wish to provide Feedback to HTTO regarding the FBDownloader Software Application, User may send a message to Support@FBDownloader.com.
VI.     Can I Invite Friends & Family?
All FBDownloader Users are encouraged to distribute the FBDownloader Software Application and may invite new Users to discover it.
VII.   Does HTTO Use Cookies?
A.       HTTO uses cookies and other technologies, such as pixel tags, to track traffic flow and patterns of travel within, and through, our web site. The Applications we distribute also use cookies to provide us with retention and usage information and to store user preferences.
B.       Our server automatically asks a new User's browser whether it will accept our cookie file. If the cookie is accepted, our server will record the cookie's unique ID for our use in internal reporting. Every time the User returns to the web site or uses our Applications, we will be able to read that cookie's ID number and, among other things, can customize the services we deliver, based on the User's past preferences.
C.      If a User accepts our cookie, but later wants to remove it, it can be deleted through User’s web browser.
D.      HTTO does not use cookies to store information you provide. To maintain your privacy, you can disable your browser's ability to accept cookies, but doing so may affect your ability to access and navigate our web site.
E.       This Policy covers the use of cookies & web beacons by HTTO. It does not cover the use of _cookies & web beacons _by any other third-party advertiser.
VIII.   ** What About Data Security?**
A.       To ensure the security of your data, including address information, HTTO always uses industry-standard encryption technologies (256kb Secure Socket Layer – SSL) when transferring and receiving User data on our web site.
B.       HTTO has instituted appropriate security measures that protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from Users.
C.      HTTO requires that our third-party advertisers ensure the security of the data that Users provide to them, however, in the event of a breach of their security measures, User agrees that HTTO will not be liable for any loss or damage to you.
D.      HTTO limits access to personal information about Users to employees for whom we reasonably believe it is necessary, in order to provide products or services.
E.       HTTO has physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that comply with international regulations to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our web site.
F.       United States Electronic Communications Privacy Act Notice (18USC 2701-2711):  HTTO makes no guaranty of confidentiality or privacy of any communication or information transmitted on the FBDownloader web site or any web site linked to the FBDownloader web site. FBDownloader will not be liable for the privacy of email addresses, registration and identification information, disk space, communications, confidential or trade-secret information, or any other Content stored on HTTO equipment, transmitted over networks accessed by the FBDownloader web site or otherwise connected with User’s use of our service.
IX. _     Can I Opt - out?_
A.       HTTO gives you the option to remove your personally identifiable information from our list of active Users by completely uninstalling the FBDownloader Software Application from your computer or electronic device and deleting it from your Facebook applications.
B.       You may also choose to opt-out of certain communications from HTTO, such as software updates.
C.      All requests for unsubscribing or opting-out of future communication, should be made by sending an email to Customer Support, at Support@FBDownloader.com. We will respond within 24 hours.
D.      HTTO is not responsible for removing your personally identifiable information from the lists of any third-party advertiser who has previously been provided your information in accordance with this Policy.
X. _      Where Is The Web Site Located?_
The FBDownloader web site is maintained from within Israel. If you are located outside of Israel, by using our web site, you authorize the export of personally identifiable information to Israel, and its storage and use as specified in these Policies. XI.     What Is The Minimum Age To Use The FBDownloader Web Site?
A.       Use of the FBDownloader web site and HTTO products/services are intended for persons who are thirteen (13) years or older.
B.       If proof comes to the attention of HTTO that a registered User is under the age of thirteen (13) years, and is accessing our services and/or products without the consent, approval or authorization of their parents or legal guardian, HTTO will immediately terminate such account

B.       HTTO will not personally notify you of any amendments or modifications to these Policies. Users are urged to check these Policies frequently in order to determine whether any changes have been made. Users understand and agree that continued use of the FBDownloader Software Application and the web site assumes that they have read and accepted these Policies, as may be amended or modified from time to time.

These Policies are accessible from the Home Page of the FBDownloader web site, at www.FBDownloader.com, under the link “Privacy Policy”.
