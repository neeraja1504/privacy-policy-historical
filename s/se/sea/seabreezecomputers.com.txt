Privacy Policy
This privacy statement applies to all Sea Breeze Computers pages including "Which Superhero Are You? Quiz". Sea Breeze Computers does not collect any personally identifiable information from visitors to our web pages. This means that we do not know or collect your e-mail address, name or other personal information when you browse our pages.
Since we do not collect personal information from our visitors we therefore do not sell any personal information.
Advertisements and stat services may use Internet Cookies to monitor and keep track of the amount of clicks on an ad or the amount of visitors to certain pages, but these cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.
