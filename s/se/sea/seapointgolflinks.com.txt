Welcome To Seapoint Golf Club
Seapoint Golf Club is committed to protecting your privacy and has created this privacy guarantee in order to demonstrate our Clubs commitment to privacy (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 ).
We use your information to assist in providing you with the best possible service. The information collected will be used to keep in touch with you from time to time to let you know of any special offers, sales or promotions. Your consent for this service will be requested at the time of registration. If at any time you wish to withdraw your consent you may do so by sending an email with the subject line reading "remove" to privacy@seapointgolfclub.com and we will remove you from our list.
Your personal details will be kept secure and will not be shared with any third party orgainisation.
Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be sent to you by email to the address entered during registration.
