Welcome To Searchwiz.com
SearchWiz.com respects and protects your right to privacy. We understand the sensitive nature of privacy issues and we take have taken measures to safe guard your privacy. By using SearchWiz.com, you consent to the following privacy policy. This policy is subject to change and updates will be posted our 'privacy policy' page.
Coverage
This policy applies to our URL, www.searchwiz.com and any URL ending with www.searchwiz.com as part of the url.
Third-Party Links
As a search engine, and as an advertising publisher, you will find links our site, including but not limited to: search results and sponsor links. The web sites we link to are owned and operated by outside, third party companies. SearchWiz.com has no control over these third party web sites. The web sites we link to are not covered by this privacy policy. SearchWiz.com is not responsible for the content of these sites, or their collection of information and its use. If you have any concerns about your privacy at one of these sites, please review the privacy policies of these websites or contact them directly.
SearchWiz advertiser account registration forms require our customers to provide us with contact information (EX: their name and email address) so that we may open their accounts. We may use this customer contact information from the account registration forms to send the customer information about our company. Customers may opt-out of receiving future mailings by follow instructions on the emailing piece.
Information about our customers is not shared with any outside agencies or third parties. Our customer's information is not resold or given away for any purpose at any time.
Contact Information
Searchwiz.com only has access to your personal (identifiable) contact information in the following circumstances:
When you send comments, questions, suggestions, or feedback to SearchWiz.com via email, and include an email address and/or contact information. In these cases, SearchWiz.com only uses this information to respond to you.
Signing up for SearchWiz.com services, including but not limited to sponsor advertising and any other partnership programs which require registration, and the submission of certain information, which often includes contact information. Some of our services require payment information in addition to contact details. To ensure your security in these circumstances, SearchWiz.com uses a third-party credit card processing site for the entrance and (possible) storage of your credit card information. If you are not willing to provide this information to SearchWiz.com you may choose not to participate in these programs.
Choice/Opt-Out
SearchWiz.com provides users and customers the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us at any time. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database, to not receive future communications, or to no longer receive our service.
