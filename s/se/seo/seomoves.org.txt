Privacy Policy - Search Engine Optimization
Our Commitment To Privacy
Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. In order to better defend your privacy we offer this notification explaining our online info routines and the selections you can make about the way your information is collected as well as used. To enable this notice simply found, we make it obtainable on our homepage and at every spot where personally identifiable information can be requested.
The Information We Collect
This notice applies to all info compiled or submitted on the SEO Moves website. On certain pages, you can purchase items, ask questions, and register to receive materials. The sorts of personal info collected at these pages are:
Name
Address
Email address
Phone number
Credit/Debit Card Information ONLY if you are subscribing / purchasing online
Additionalinformation appropriate to projects being managed or serviced by SEO Moves.
The Way We Use Information
We use the data you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. We don’t share this info with outside parties but to the necessary extent to complete that order. We make use of data as long as it is descriptive of a web site as project info unique to that web site. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive and to provide reasonable notification of our project, service, or product news and updates. Many email addresses are used to provide a free subscription to our SEO Newsletter. These addresses are not shared with outside parties.
Registering with our website may also happen if you want to receive our newsletter as well as updates on our products and services.
We use non-identifying and aggregate data to better design our website plus to share with advertisers. For instance, we could relay information to an advertiser that a particular number of individuals visited a particular page on our website, or that a particular amount of men and a particular number of women filled out our registration form, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those people.
Lastly, we don’t utilize or share the personally identifiable data given to us online in methods unrelated to the ones previously mentioned without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or decline such unrelated uses.
Our Commitment To Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, preserve data accuracy, and ensure the appropriate use of info, we have placed appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the info we gather online.
Our Commitment To Children's Privacy
Guarding the privacy ofminors is particularly imperative. For that reason, we never collect or keep info at our website from people we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is built to attract anyone under 13.
How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information
You can retrieve your personally identifiable info that we collect online and keep up by logging into our tools and visiting the update page appropriate to that information - NOTE: you must log in. We utilize this method to better safeguard your information.
You can correct mistakes in your personally identifiable data by sending us a request that plausibly denotes error.
To protect your privacy and security, we will also take practical steps to authenticate your identity before allowing access or making corrections.
