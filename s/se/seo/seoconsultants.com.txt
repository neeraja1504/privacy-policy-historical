Our site uses multiple online forms for consumers and consultants to request various information about our services. We do collect visitor's contact information. The contact information is used to communicate with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving any future communication from us; see the choice/opt-out section below.
WhosOn Web Analytics
WhosOn is used to analyze traffic to this web site.
Internet Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Such measures include password protected directories and other server related security implementation.
Choice/Opt-Out
This web site gives users the following option for removing their information from our database and to not receive future communications from us.
Please use our contact form for opt-out requests.
Note: We do maintain a contact database from those who have submitted requests through our web sites. We do not send any unsolicited email for advertisements. We may decide in the future to send out an SEO Consultants Newsletter and will respectfully contact you once for your opt-in or opt-out, prior to including you on our list.
