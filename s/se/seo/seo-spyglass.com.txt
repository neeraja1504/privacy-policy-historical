SEO SpyGlass Privacy Policies
Your privacy is important to us. If you provide us with personal information on this website, we do not share that information with third parties, except to the extent necessary to provide you with the information or services you request from us or to comply with court order, law enforcement or to defend our rights against claims.
If you provide your email address to us, please be assured that we have a "no spam" policy at SEO SpyGlass. Your email address will not be sold or given to another party without your prior consent.
1. Anonymous Information
Our website analytics programs will gather certain anonymous information about your visit to help us track and improve our website services.
This data cannot be connected with you personally. The information collected records such things as the geographical location of our visitors, the length of time each visitor spends on our website, pages visited on our website, etc.
2. Cookies
An HTTP cookie may be used by us, our advertisers or service provides. This is not a software program, but a small piece of data sent to the browser you are using. You can choose to block cookies from your browser by using the Internet tools available to you from your Internet browser program. Blocking cookies may, however, prevent you from using certain services and features of our website. To insure that you will be able to use all of the features of this website, you may choose to allow cookies during your time on our site and delete the cookies after you have left our site. Full instructions for doing this are available from your browser service provider.
3. Data Security
SEO-SpyGlass.com maintains high standards of data security but we cannot be responsible for malicious attacks to our website, hosting service or data storage facilities or any other breaches of our security measures.
Any information collected on our website may be transferred internationally to any country in which we do business, and may be subject to the laws of different countries.
4. Links to Other Websites
You will find links from SEO-SpyGlass.com to other websites, advertisers, and service and product suppliers. SEO SpyGlass is not responsible for the privacy policies of other websites and we encourage you to view the privacy policies of any website you reach by following links from our site.
5. Children's Privacy
We do not collect information from children under the age of 18 years and our site is not designed to appeal to children.
6. Privacy Policy Changes
Our Privacy Policies are subject to change or modification at any time. New conditions will be posted on this page and you should view this page anytime you visit our website.
7. Your Implied Consent
By visiting SEO-SpyGlass.com, you have given your implied consent to the Privacy Policies on this webpage.
