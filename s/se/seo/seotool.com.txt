Privacy Policy SEO Tool - SEO Tool - SEOtool
Triangle Direct Media is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies. We use the information we collect about you during registration to provide a more personalized user experience. Triangle Direct Media will not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information.
It may be helpful for you to know how we use the information we collect:
When you register, we request your name and email address. This allows us to process your registration and allow you to login to the site as a registered user.
We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes at SEOtool.
We may collect certain technical information, which does not allow us to identify you personally, but pertains to your use of the Internet to access this site. This information includes which web browser software you are using, and which computer operating system is running on your computer. This information enables us to enhance the content and user experience you receive from SEOtool and specifically aids us in processing support requests.
By using this web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Triangle Direct Media. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we do or do not disclose it.
