SereniGy: Privacy & Security
SereniGy has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and security. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for SereniGy.
Also, SereniGy recognizes that your personal information must be carefully used and protected. We truly appreciate your trust that we will protect your information carefully and sensibly. This notice describes SereniGy's data privacy and security policy and describes the use of information collected from persons using SereniGy's Internet websites. By visiting SereniGy websites, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Notice.
The information we learn from customers helps us personalize and continually improve our ability for you to make purchases at SereniGy's websites.
Connection Data
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Website. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. We also use cookies to track aggregate user data about usage patterns, which allows us to continually update the site in order to improve your shopping experience.
Account Information
Our website's account registration form requires users to give us contact information (like your name and email address), unique identifiers (such as your social security number), and financial information (like your account or credit card numbers). We may use your contact information from the registration form to send information about our company or to contact you if necessary. You are always free to opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill you for products and services. Unique identifiers (such as social security numbers/tax id numbers) are collected to verify your identity.
Order Form
Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect your contact information (like your email address) and financial information (such as account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers. Your contact information is used to get in touch with you when necessary. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is used to bill you for products and services.
Online Surveys & Contests
From time to time we utilize online surveys and ask visitors for contact information (like email addresses) and demographic information (like zip code, age, etc). Data collected from our surveys will help us to send you customized information about our company, products and/or services. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
Traffic Data
Demographic and profile data may also collected at our site. We use this data to tailor our visitors experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use the industry-recognized leaders in secure server and encryption technology to protect the financial transactions of our customers. We also take precautions to protect against credit card fraud, including the verification of customer information with each transaction.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. You can send an email requesting that your name be removed from our mailing list to:  info@serenigyinc.com
