Privacy Policy – Stratechery by Ben Thompson
Your privacy is important to Stratechery LLC. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Stratechery LLC collects, and the ways in which Stratechery LLC uses that personal information.
Personal Information Collection
Stratechery LLC may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:
At Stratechery.com, if you sign up to receive either the free Weekly Article or the Daily Update, you provide your name and email address. That is the only information we have for you and all we need to operate the Stratechery.com (“Website”) or the Stratechery Newsletter (“Newsletter”); we do not collect any information on other visitors to the website;
We use third third-party vendors, identified below, to help with delivery of the newsletter and processing of your payment. We never see your full credit card number, which is handled through Stripe;
If you participate in the Stratechery Forum, you will create additional user-generated content, which is used for the Stratechery Forum only.
Legal Basis for Collection of Information
Stratechery asserts the following legal grounds for the collection of data:
Explicit Consent
When you sign up for the newsletter (either free or paid) or any other account at Stratechery.com, you provide explicit consent for Stratechery LLC to collect the above listed personal information.
Contract
If you enter an agreement with Stratechery.com for the provision of services in exchange for payment, Stratechery.com will be required to collect the above listed personal information.
Legitimate Interests
Finally, Stratechery.com may have additional legitimate interests, including account maintenance and administrative purposes, for the collection of the above listed personal information.
Using Personal Information
Stratechery LLC may use your personal information to:
administer Stratechery.com and/or the Stratechery newsletter;
personalize the website for you by displaying your name or changing the appearance of the website according to your settings if you log in;
enable your access to and use of the website services;
send to you the newsletter and account-related email;
send to you statements and invoices;
collect payments from you; and
send you marketing communications.
Where Stratechery LLC discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement. Except for the third-party vendors that collect your data separately when you sign up and pay, Stratechery has never shared any of its user data with any third parties.
In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified in this privacy policy, Stratechery LLC may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.
Data Processors
Stratechery LLC uses the following data processors with respect to the personal information collected:
ChartMogul: ChartMogul provides analytics on website membership. ChartMogul Privacy Policy.
Discourse: Discourse manages the Stratechery Forum and all related data, including user-created content. Discourse Privacy Policy.
MailChimp: MailChimp manages the creation and delivery of all newsletters sent from Stratechery LLC, and all related data, including open rates collected via an invisible pixel. MailChimp Privacy Policy.
Memberful: Memberful manages all website membership details including your sign up and account maintenance. Memberful Privacy Policy.
Stripe: Stripe manages all payment processing. Your credit card payment details are managed by Stripe and not released to Stratechery except the date and amount of each payment, the last four digits of your credit card, expiration date, and zip code. Stripe Privacy Policy.
WPEngine.com: WPEngine.com manages the hosting of Stratechery.com and all related user login data and analytics. WPEngine.com Privacy Policy.
3rd-party Service Providers
In addition, Stratechery LLC uses the following 3rd-party service providers for certain content served on Stratechery.com. By using Stratechery.com you are accessing these services. Stratechery LLC does not provide or receive any personal information to or from these services.
Algolia: Algolia provides search for Stratechery.com. Algolia Privacy Policy.
Cloudfront: Cloudfront is a content delivery network provided by Amazon Web Services. Amazon Web Services Privacy Policy.
Google Analytics: Google Analytics provides data concerning site visitors and activity at the website. Google Analytics Privacy Policy.
Jetpack: Jetpack provides security and backup services for Stratechery.com, and recommends additional articles to read. Jetpack Privacy Policy.
New Relic: New Relic provides analytics about website performance on Stratechery.com. New Relic Privacy Policy.
Typekit: Typekit, which is owned by Adobe, provides Stratechery.com’s font. Adobe Fonts Privacy Policy.
In addition, by using Stratechery.com you are accessing Memberful, Stripe, and WPEngine.com, but Stratechery LLC does not provide or receive any personal information to or from these services unless you are a subscriber.
Data Retention Policy
Data will only be retained until you request us to delete it pursuant to this privacy policy.
Securing Your Data
Stratechery LLC will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information. If Stratechery LLC stores any of your data, it will do so on secure servers.
Forum Posts
Be advised that Stratechery does not monitor Stratechery Forum posts for disclosure of any personal information you may post in any user-generated content. The forum software (Discourse) includes tools allowing you to edit or delete your posts. If you need assistance, you may contact Stratechery LLC with the contact information below.
Cross-border Data Transfers
Information that Stratechery LLC collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Stratechery LLC operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.
In addition, personal information that you post to the Stratechery Forum will be published on the internet and may be available around the world.
Children’s Privacy
Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important. For that reason, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18 or knowingly allow such persons to register with the Website or Newsletter. If you are under 18, please do not send any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, or email address. No one under age 18 is allowed to provide any personal information to or on Stratechery LLC. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us.
California Online Privacy Protection Act Notice
(1) Stratechery LLC does not track users, who interact with its website across the web, and therefore does not use “do not track” signals.
(2) Stratechery LLC does not authorize the collection of personally identifiable information from users on any Stratechery LLC website for third party use through advertising technologies.
GDPR Compliance
Stratechery seeks to fully comply with the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Pursuant to this regulation, you have the following rights on this website:
Right to be forgotten: You may cancel your account at any time. If you wish your data to be permanently deleted, you may contact us at admin@stratechery.com and it will be deleted within 72 hours.
Right to object: We have never performed any data science projects on user data and do not anticipate doing so in the future. If, at some point in the future we determine a use for any sort of data science project on our user data, we will notify you in writing before doing so. In that event you may remove your contact data from any such project by emailing us at admin@stratechery.com with the subject line “Opt Out of Data Science”.
Right to access and rectification: You can contact us at admin@stratechery.com at any time to access copies of all user data we retain for you. We will remove, update, or change any personal information about you from our servers immediately upon your request.
Right of portability: We only retain your name and email address. Nevertheless, we will provide that information to you upon your request.
Right of restriction: You can make a request for restriction or suppression of your personal data through the contact information at the end of this privacy policy.
Without prejudice to any other administrative or judicial remedy, if located in the European Union, you shall have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, place of work or place of the alleged infringement if you consider that the processing of personal data relating to you infringes the GDPR.
In addition to the above, an “unsubscribe” option is automatically included in the footer of every newsletter sent from the Stratechery. This allows you to easily unsubscribe from the newsletter. In the event you unsubscribe, we will immediately stop sending you the newsletter. Please note that unsubscribing from the newsletter with the newsletter’s “unsubscribe” button does not cancel your account with Stratechery LLC. In order to do so, please log in at Stratechery.com and cancel your account.
Updating This Statement
Stratechery LLC may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.
Other Websites
Stratechery.com and the newsletter contains links to other websites and embedded links to other content on the Internet, such as tweets and YouTube videos. Stratechery LLC is not responsible for the contents of those third party websites or responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.
