Our Privacy Policy
Cookie Privacy Policy:
﻿﻿StreamPoll is committed to protecting your personal information. We want our services to be safe and enjoyable environments for everyone. This page aims to help you to understand what information we might collect about you and how we use it.
What are cookies?
Cookies are small files which are stored on a user's computer. They are designed to hold a modest amount of data specific to a particular client and website, and can be accessed either by the web server or the client computer. This allows the server to deliver a page tailored to a particular user, or the page itself can contain some script which is aware of the data in the cookie and so is able to carry information from one visit to the website (or related site) to the next.
Information we collect about you:
When you interact with Streampoll we sometimes receive or collect personal information about you.
For example: Google Analytics. We use this service to determine the amount of visitors and if the visitor is a returning visitor or not. We also place other site cookies, these cookies will tell us who you are. For example; whether you signed in before and wanted to stay signed in on the website.
