3.1 The protection of your privacy in the processing of your personal information is important to us. We process any personal information we collect during your visits to this Site according to applicable data privacy and data protection legislation.

4.1 We collect certain personal information about you when you input information when using any of the functions available on this Site (for example, uploading a property to advertise it, registering your details to receive notifications about properties, or viewing usage statistics).

5. Use and storage of your personal information
5.1 Before you are able to use some of the functionality of this Site you may need to provide your name, address, a valid email address, telephone number or any other mandatory personal information we request. There is also optional personal information you can fill in which will help to enhance your experience, however this is not mandatory. We may also require additional personal information depending on the purposes for which you use this Site.
5.2 We may use personal information we collect from you only for the purposes of:
5.2.1 hosting and administering your profile with this Site;
5.2.2 providing you with the information you request from us (such as information about properties in which you have registered an interest) and keeping a record of the information we have provided to you, including a record of ratings and comments you have provided;
5.2.3 identifying whether you have complied with the Website Terms or Service Terms, as applicable, and otherwise for the administration and operation of this Site;
5.2.4 asking you to participate in surveys and feedback relating to this Site and for processing and analysing the results of surveys or feedback internally;
5.2.5 providing you with information about any products or services we provide that we believe may be of interest to you, but only where you have indicated your acceptance.
6. Other sharing of your personal information
6.1 In addition, we may share your personal information with third parties in the following ways:
6.1.1 we may use third parties to process personal information on our behalf (for example, we may use a third party to provide Site hosting services or to facilitate the provision of information to you or to provide products and/or services on our behalf) provided that where we do we will ensure that they have adequate security measures in place and are governed by our privacy policies in respect of the use of your personal information;
6.1.2 we will release your personal information when we are required to do so for legal or regulatory purposes or as part of legal proceedings or where it is reasonably necessary to do so to protect our legitimate commercial interests or other users;
6.1.3 we will release your personal information to others when you have given your consent to that release (including to third parties who wish to provide marketing or other information to you);
6.1.4 we may give personal information we hold about you to third parties as part of the process of selling our business.
