This Privacy and Cookie Policy ("Policy") explains how we use any personal information that you provide to us or we collect about you.
We are Starladder Limited, Unit F, 11 Floor, CNT Tower, 338 Hennessy Road, Wan Chai, Hong Kong ("Starladder", "we", "our" and "us").
Starladder provides the online platform for amateur, semi-pro and professional eSports tournaments.
Our registered users (“members”) participate in tournaments, create teams, engage with other members, post and view relevant content. Content and data on some of our Services are viewable to non-members (“visitors”).

We also have other domain names which link to the Internet sites listed above, in which case this Policy will apply to these Internet sites. Furthermore, we may add Internet sites which may not be listed, but which provide a link to this Policy and respect the terms within it.
We collect and use as less of personal data that is needed. We may collect personal data as well as other information when you use the following services:
- using our Sites,
- creating and using an account on any of the Sites,
- using of any of our other services or products (including support services),  which may be accessible via a third party platform, web application or social networking service (together with the "ancillary services").
In this Policy, we refer to the Sites, the account, and the ancillary services together as the "Services"
1. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT
1.1. Registration data
To create an account you can choose direct registration or social network authentication. Depending on what is described below, third parties may provide registration and authentication services. In this case, we will be able to access some data, stored by these third-party services, for registration or identification purposes.
Direct registration (on auth.starladder.com)
The user registers by filling out the registration form and providing the Personal Data directly to us.
Data collected: email address, username, and password.
Place of processing: Germany.
Facebook Authentication (Facebook, Inc.)
Facebook Authentication is a registration and authentication service provided by Facebook, Inc. and is connected to the Facebook social network.
Personal Data collected: email and public profile info (as specified in the privacy policy of the service). Place of processing: US – Privacy Policy.
VK Client Authorization (VKontakte Ltd)
VK Client Authorization is a registration and authentication service provided by VKontakte Ltd and is connected to the VK social network.
Personal Data collected: email and public profile info (as specified in the privacy policy of the service). Place of processing: Russia – Privacy Policy.
1.2. Account data
You have choices about the information on your profile, such as your first & last name, birthday, country, city, gender, photo (avatar), team membership, links to social media, battle.net id, steam id, skype, etc.
You do not have to provide additional information on your profile ; however, if you want to use special services via our Sites (to enter competitions, to find teammates, etc.) obtaining of some personal data could become necessary. It is your choice whether to include sensitive information on your profile and to make that sensitive information public.
Please do not post or add personal data to your profile that you would not want to be publicly available. We may also collect information related to personal information, ratings, results statistics, and team information in order to follow the career and achievements of our competitors.
We also collect information related to tournaments and store it for up to 2 years for administrative purposes. Examples: method of communication, experience, rating history, ratings, statistics of results, information about the team and the display name of the player, information about participation in tournaments.
Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department and Support department. We can also collect information provided by players who participate in our e-sports events to provide personalized service, support and assistance during the competition. Examples: dietary restrictions, food allergies and clothing sizes. Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department and Support department.
1.3. Tracking data
As further described in chapter 4, we use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels, ad tags and device identifiers) for the collection and processing of Tracking Data on the Sites. You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools.
When you use the Services, we may collect and process the following "Tracking Data": Location information associated with your IP address when you visit our Sites or sign-in into your account. Information relating to your use of our Services including, but not limited to, information about when and how you use the Services and data traffic and recordings of the parts of the screen you click on while using our Services (so-called "clickstreams").
Log files and statistics about actions taken in our Services. Technical information about any device that you use when you visit the Sites, including your Media Access Control (MAC), Unique Device Identifier (UDID) or equivalent device identifier, your IP address and the browser you are using.
1.4. Content data
We may collect personal data from you when you provide, post or upload it to our Sites or services: when you fill out a form (contact form, subscribe to newsletters, support request, bug report, respond to a survey, etc.), information you sent or post in any of the forums or chat, data you provide to us when participating in tournaments and other events, information relating to your profiles on social networking sites to allow integration with such social networking sites.
You do not have to post or upload personal data; though if you do not, it may limit your ability to use our Sites or service.
1.5. Transaction data
We collect information about transactions related to the payment of prize winners of cyber tournaments. Transaction data can be stored for up to 7 years (mainly for audit purposes). Examples: refund payments, refunds, payment information, delivery information, information about prizes and accreditations received in competitions. Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department, Support department, Security, Financial department.
1.6. Data on user activity on resources
When a user interacts with our services, we collect server logs. In most cases, server logs are kept for a period from 90 days to 1 year. Examples: IP addresses, access dates and times, region and activity logs. Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department, Support department and Security.
1.7. Device Information
We may collect information about devices used to access our services. Examples: hardware models, operating systems and versions, unique device identifiers. Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department, Support department and Security. Storage time: from 90 days to 1 year.
1.8. Communications
Starladder may use the information we collect from players and partners to send messages to our players about our current and future products, services, promotions, research, polls, news, updates, and events. Starladder may also use this information to advertise and hold contests and prize draw, to provide relevant awards and to show you relevant advertisements and content related to our services. Data collected about specific events, such as surveys, are stored for no longer than 90 days. Examples: name, email address, mail, survey information, purchase/license history, activity history, email usage data mail, e.g. display names of Facebook and Twitter users and other social networks and any web-platforms.
1.9. Media Information
We may collect contact information of journalists and players who have a significant impact on our community. Examples: the display name of the user is Discord, the display name of the Steam user, support for Twitch cheer, the display name of the user Twitch, the display name of the Twitter user. Who has access to the specified data types? Operational and publishing departments, e-sports department. Our Services are dynamic, and we often introduce new features, which may require the collection of new information. If we collect materially different personal data or materially change how we use your data, we will notify you and may also modify this Privacy Policy and Cookies Policy.

We may collect and process Account Data, Tracking Data, and Content Data to provide you with the services that you request from us and to perform the respective Terms of Use accepted by you.
Data collected for that purpose will only be kept as long as necessary to provide the requested Services and to comply with applicable laws. The Account data is optional - you do not have to provide additional information on your profile. However, if you want to use some special services via our website, it will be necessary to add some data to your profile.

Such requirements are specified in the rules of the tournament and published on the relevant site. Account Data is retained as long as your account exists.

We use your data if we think it is necessary for security purposes or to investigate possible fraud or other violations of our Terms of Use or this Policy and attempts to harm our visitors or users.
2.4. Marketing and advertising
We may promote our Services to you and others. We target (and measure the performance of) ads to Members, Visitors and others both on and off our Services directly or through a variety of partners, using the following data, whether separately or combined, including, but not limited to, using publishing newsletters to email addresses and marketing companies:
Data from advertising technologies on and off our Services, like web beacons, pixels, ad tags, cookies, and device identifiers, Member-provided information (e.g., profile, contact information), Data from your use of our Services (e.g., content you read, page visits, videos you watch, clicking on an ad, search history, feed, tournaments participation, etc.), including as described in Section 1.4.
We do not share your personal data with any third-party advertisers or ad networks for their advertising except for (i) with your separate permission (e.g., lead generation form) or (ii) data already visible to any users of the Services (e.g. profile).

2.5. Developing Services and Research
We use data to conduct research and development for the further development of our Services to provide you and others with a better, more intuitive and personalized experience, drive membership growth and engagement on our Services.
2.6. Customer Support
We use the data (which can include your communications) to investigate, respond to and resolve complaints and Service issues (e.g., bugs).
2.7. Aggregate Insights
We use your data to produce and share aggregated insights that do not identify you. For example, we may use your data to generate statistics about our members, their geographic and interests, to calculate ad impressions served or clicked on, etc.
3. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION
We will not share your personal data with anyone else unless in the following limited circumstances: To the extent that we are required to do so by applicable law, by a governmental body or by a law enforcement agency, or for crime prevention purposes. Where necessary to enforce the Terms of Use or the rules of the competitions you participate, to provide you with Services you request from us, to protect the safety of our users and third parties, to protect our rights and property and the rights and property of other users and third parties, or in other cases if we believe in good faith that disclosure is required by law.
We may disclose and allow access to information collected through our Services to facilitate the targeting, delivery, and measurement of advertising through and in connection with the Service: to facilitate the targeting, delivery, and measurement of online advertising on third-party services on our behalf; and otherwise to facilitate transmittal of information that may be useful, relevant, valuable or otherwise of interest to our users.
Where necessary to provide you with Services you request from us, we may share your personal data with third parties who provide services to us, including to countries outside the European Economic Area. We reserve the right to transfer personal data of participants who expressed a desire to take part in tournaments to other processors for the purpose of (not limited to): buying tickets for various types of transport for the Participant in order to organize his transfer to the venue of the tournaments (both LAN, and Online); submission of documents to visa centers, consulates, embassies for the purpose of documenting the procedure for crossing the border by the Participant in the event of such crossing during the transfer to the venue of the tournaments (both LAN and Online); rent / order apartments of any type (e.g. hotel rooms, flats, etc.) to accommodate the Participant of the tournament; conducting cash transactions. Information about third parties to whom such personal data was transmitted, the Participant can receive after a corresponding written request to the email address specified in clause 7.3. of these rules.
Nevertheless, Starladder guarantees that with each of the information processors, who are third-party data processors, an appropriate personal data protection agreement has been concluded. In any case, the nature of the transfer of user data by third parties is a one-off character and strictly in the cases mentioned above. We may share anonymous information, i.e., information which does not directly or indirectly disclose your identity, and aggregated information (meaning information about groups and categories of users, including game statistics and behavior, but which does not identify and that cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual user) with our advertising partners. We may also allow our advertisers to collect anonymous information and aggregated information within the Services and they may then share that information with us. Our advertisers may collect this information through the use of tracking technologies like cookies and web beacons.
This enables our advertisers to develop and deliver targeted advertising in the Services and on the websites of third parties so that they can try to serve you with advertisements for products and services that are most likely to be of interest to you. Advertisers will also use this information to monitor, improve, or modify their operations.
4. COOKIES
4.1. What are Cookies?
As described in Section 1.3 of our Privacy & Cookies Policy, we use cookies and other technologies to ensure everyone who uses our services has the best possible experience. Cookies also help us keep your account safe. By continuing to visit or use our services, you agree to the use of cookies and similar technologies for the purposes we describe in this policy.
A cookie is a small file placed on your device that enables features and functionality. For example, cookies enable us to identify your device, secure your access to our sites generally, and even help us know if someone attempts to access your account from a different device. Cookies also help us serve relevant ads to you.
4.2. Why do we use cookies?
Cookies can be used to recognize you when you visit our sites, remember your preferences, and give you a personalized experience that's in line with your settings. Cookies also make your interactions with our services faster and more secure. Additionally, cookies allow us to bring you advertising both on and off the Starladder sites and bring customized features to you through social media widget such as our "Share" button.

All users who are minors in the jurisdiction in which they reside must have the permission of, and be directly supervised by, their parent or guardian to use the Services. If you are a minor, you must have your parent or guardian read and agree to this Policy prior to you using the Services.
By signing up for an Account, you represent and warrant that you are a "natural person" who is over the age of eighteen (18) or whose legal guardian has accepted and agreed to these Privacy & Cookies Policy.
6. YOUR RIGHTS
6.1. Data retention
We retain your personal data while your account is in existence or as needed to provide you Services. This includes data you or others provided to us and data generated or inferred from your use of our Services. In any case, Starladder is not intended to keep your personal data longer than the circumstances and the purpose of processing such personal data. The storage period of personal data that is not used to the extent necessary to provide services to the user does not exceed 3 years, with the exception of certain data categories, for example, we keep an archive of documentation to support any existing or expected legal cases and audit for up to 7 years. Examples: professional player contracts, personal affairs, transaction information. Who has access to the specified data types? Tournament Operations department, Legal department and security service, Financial department.
6.2. Rights to Access and Control Your Personal Data
For personal data that we have about you have the right to:
- Delete Data : You can ask us to erase or delete all or some of your personal data (e.g., if it is no longer necessary to provide Services to you).
- Change or Correct Data : You can edit some of your personal data through your account. You can also ask us to change, update or fix your data in certain cases, particularly if it’s inaccurate.
- Object to, or Limit or Restrict, Use of Data : You can ask us to stop using all or some of your personal data (e.g., if we have no legal right to keep using it) or to limit our use of it (e.g., if your personal data is inaccurate or unlawfully held).
- Access and/or Take Your Data : You can ask us for a copy of your personal data and can ask for a copy of the personal data you provided in the machine-readable form.
- To Transfer data to the resource indicated by you : you can request us to transfer your personal data in favor of the resource indicated by you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, then please contact us at [email protected]
