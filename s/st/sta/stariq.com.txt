Horoscopes, Astrology and Charts - StarIQ.com
StarIQ.com supports your privacy and has created this statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
Disclosure
Under no circumstances will any data collected at StarIQ be made available to any individual, company, or agency other than for the specific purposes of delivering goods or services requested by the registrant.
We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. We may use cookies to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site and for other purposes.
StarIQ.com site registration may require users to give us contact information, unique identifiers, and demographic information. The contact information is used to contact the visitor as described on the registration page. Users may opt out of receiving future mailings at any time. Unique identifiers may be collected to verify the user's identity and to be used as account numbers in our record system. Demographic and profile data may also be collected at our site. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site, showing them content that we think they might be interested in, and displaying the content according to their preferences. Also, birth data may be collected for the purposes of astrological calculations and the delivery of personalized astrology information based on these calculations.
StarIQ.com contains links to other sites. StarIQ.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
StarIQ.com has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
Children's Guidelines
With respect to registrants under the age of 18 years:
StarIQ does not collect online contact information without prior parental consent or parental notification, which will include an opportunity for the parent to prevent use of the information and participation in the activity. Without prior parental consent, online information will only be used to respond directly to the child's request and will not be used for other purposes without prior parental consent.
StarIQ.com does not collect personally identifiable offline contact information without prior parental consent.
StarIQ.com does not distribute to third parties any personally identifiable information with out prior parental consent.
StarIQ.com does not give the ability to publicly post or otherwise distribute personally identifiable contact information without prior parental consent.
StarIQ.com does not attempt to entice children by the prospect of a special game, prize or other activity, to divulge more information than is needed to participate in the activities of the site.
Participation is Your Choice
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt out of receiving communications from StarIQ.com at any time. This site allows users the option of sending an email to remove@stariq.com at any time for removing their information from our database, and to not receive future communications, and to no longer receive services of StarIQ.com.
Correct/Update
StarIQ.com allows users the option for changing and modifying information previously provided at any time by sending an email to change_info@stariq.com
