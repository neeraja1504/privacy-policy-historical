None
This page describes how we use and protect any information provided by you to us.
Cookies
Like many websites, starplugins.com uses website cookies to keep track of your logged in status and various other aspects of your visit to the site. These cookies are not used for purposes other than the correct functioning of the website and providing us with anonymous usage data that we use to improve the website. The cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information, and are not used for advertising purposes.
Advertising
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website.
Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
You may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Ads Settings. (Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.)
Email Addresses
Any email addresses you supply to us are held in strict confidence and are never sold or otherwise disseminated to any third parties, except where applicable laws demand us to do so.
We may occasionally send newsletters to your email address, but you can opt-out of any such newsletters at any time. Instructions on how to do so will be included within the newsletters and instructions are also available on this site.
Payment Security
Payments on this website are handled by external third-party payment providers such as PayPal. These providers operate industry leading security and fraud prevention measures.
We never see, store or otherwise have access to any of your credit card details, but merely receive notification from the payment provider that your payment has been correctly authorised on our behalf.
Your Account With Us
In order to download products, you need to have an account with us.
The account stores basic information about you, such as your email address and purchase history. This enables us to authorise and configure your downloads.
All information stored about you is visible in the account section of the website after you have logged in.
If you wish to close your account and erase all account information, please contact us, and your request will be fulfilled within 24 hours. Please make sure you have downloaded your products before requesting account closure.
