Privacy Policy | Standard Fiber
Standard Fiber is committed to respecting your privacy. Any information we collect about you is used to process orders and to provide a more focused customer experience. We will not sell or disclose any information that identifies you to a third party if requested. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.
What types of information does Standard Fiber collect?
The only information Standard Fiber collects is the information you knowingly provide on our contact form â€“ name, company, e-mail address, phone number, mailing address, etc. You are under no obligation to provide us any of this information; however, we may not be able respond to your request without it.
We use cookies, a common feature of your browser used to assign identification to your computer. The unique user ID contained within your cookie automatically identifies your computer - but not you - to our servers each time you visit. Cookies by themselves cannot be used to find out the identity of any user, but may be correlated with identity information in our log files. You can decline cookies through your browser preference options.
We also collect the IP address of visitors to our Site, and collect aggregate information about the use of our Site.
How does Standard Fiber use this data?
Standard Fiber uses the information collected from you primarily to respond to and fulfill your requests. Currently we do not use it for any additional marketing purposes.
Standard Fiber may contact you by e-mail to respond to customer service questions you have submitted, or to address a problem affecting your use of the Site, or to send you a confirmation message upon completion of the account registration process.
With whom does Standard Fiber share information?
As part of our commitment to your privacy, Standard Fiber does not share your information with any other third parties.
Links to other sites
Standard Fiber links to other sites not affiliated with Standard Fiber. These sites have their own policies and practices with respect to online privacy. We cannot be responsible for the privacy practices of these unaffiliated web sites.
Contact Information
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about Standard Fiber's information collection practices, please contact us at info@standardfiber.com.
