Privacy Policy - Stevesie
General Information
We collect information from both public visitors and registered account holders including, but not limited to access times, IP addresses, referral traffic patterns & repeat visits.
All information is collected and held privately; we never share, sell or solicit any of our collected information to other parties. We may however turn over our information if involving threats, safety of others, or if required by law.
Information Gathering
Whenever you access this site, your IP address is recorded and we store activity using Google Analytics.
Cookies
We do not directly use cookies in any parts of our web application, however we use third party services such as Google Analytics, which makes use of cookies.
Remarketing
We may make use of Google AdWord's Remarketing Advertising, displaying advertisements throughout Google's advertisement partners across the world wide web after initially visiting our site. Third-party vendors, such as Google and members of their advertisement distribution network are able to display advertisements tailored to visitors of stevesie.com based on a cookie that is placed within your browser.
If you would like to opt out of remarketing, please follow Google's Ad Settings or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
Security
All interactions with our servers are performed via SSL level encryption, preventing any "man-in-the-middle" attacks to intercept your personal information.
Changes
We may periodically update this policy with small changes from time to time without notice - it is the reader's responsibility to keep up-to-date with this policy. In cases where we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify all users via email.
Questions
If you have any questions regarding our policies, please contact stevesie@stevesie.com.
