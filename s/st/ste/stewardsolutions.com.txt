SAMMS Adsorbents by Steward - Privacy Statement
Steward has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for http://www.sammsadsorbents.com
Steward will not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information.
Our site uses an order form for visitors to request information and services. We collect users' contact information including but not limited to addresses, emails and phone numbers. Contact information from the forms are used to distribute information about our company, and from time to time promotional materials. When partners will receive the customer's information, it will be disclosed as such. The user's contact information is also used to contact customers directly when necessary.
This site contains links to other sites. Steward is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such web sites.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you.
