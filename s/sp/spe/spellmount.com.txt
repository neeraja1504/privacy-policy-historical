Tempus Publishing - Your Privacy
We will collect and record your name, address and other contact details, together with details of any purchases you make. We will use that information for two purposes only:
For processing orders, deliveries, refunds etc.
For mailing and emailing (but not telephoning) occasional promotional information. However, we will not send you such information if you have indicated (on the checkout page or elsewhere) that you do not wish to receive it.
We will never allow your information to be used by anyone other than the Tempus Publishing Group.
