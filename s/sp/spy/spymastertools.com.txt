Privacy Policy
Spy Master Tools is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. Your credit card information is secure; it is not stored online and will not be shared with another company. Information collected from our website is anonymous. Your account information is contained in private files and we will not sell or release your email address to any other parties.
Spy Master Tools uses a secure server (SSL) to encrypt all of your personal information. We use strong security measures to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of your information once it is in our records.
We use "cookies" to keep track of your current shopping session to personalize your experience and so that you may retrieve your shopping cart at any time.
