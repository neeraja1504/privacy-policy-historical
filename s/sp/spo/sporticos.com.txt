Any of the information Sporticos.com collects from you may be used in one of the following ways:

To improve our website
To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature
To send periodic emails
How does Sporticos.com protect your information?
Sporticos.com implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.
Cookies
Cookies are small text files that a site can place on your computer or mobile device to store certain information. We use cookies to keep track of how you use our website, make our website work efficiently, and deliver relevant content including advertisement.
You can set up your browser not to allow cookies, however some parts our site would not function properly. You can get more information about cookies here: http://www.allaboutcookies.org/
Third party links
Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third party products or services on our website, such as Google Analytics and Facebook plugin. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. Sporticos.com have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.
