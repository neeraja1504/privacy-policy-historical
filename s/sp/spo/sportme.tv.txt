SportMe - where sport fans connect and share
Privacy Policy
SportMe is committed to ensuring your privacy is protected. Any information that you provide to SportMe by which you can be identified will be used only in accordance with the privacy policy stated below.
Cookies:
The viewer may receive a cookie. A cookie is a small data structure sent from a web server to the internet browser and saved on your hard drive as a text file. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies. If you do not wish to receive cookies you can disable this function in your web browser although doing so may restrict access to some web pages.
Cookies store non-personal information such as your server address, your top level domain name, the date and the time of your visit to the site, the pages you accessed and downloaded, the address of the last site you visited, and the type of browser you are using. We use this information for monitoring our web site use to facilitate web site management, development and planning. We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyze data about webpage traffic and improve our website in order to tailor it to customer needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes.
The only self identifying information involved in the cookies shows the general location where you come from, helping us to track which countries use the website to the greatest extent. We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
Security:
While SportMe takes measures to ensure privacy of all information transmitted on the website, SportMe is unable to guarantee secure transmission of information over the internet. Viewers must be aware of the risks they are taking in any information that they choose to submit to any website or in electronic mail.
Links:
SportMe is in no way responsible for websites that are linked from SportMe, once you leave the SportMe jurisdiction we are unable to ensure your privacy. The protection policies of the other website in question may vary greatly from that of SportMe.
Changes:
Changes may be made at any time according to our privacy policy. Changes will become effective immediately upon changes to this website. Viewers may check this website at anytime to be up to date on the privacy policies of SportMe.
