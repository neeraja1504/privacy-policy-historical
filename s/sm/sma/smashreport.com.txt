This Privacy Policy lists the manner in which your data is collected and used by the site/service, and you are requested to read it carefully. Certain features of SmahsReport are accessible only upon registering with us.
How and Why we collect user information
When you register with us, we ask you certain personally identifiable information such as your name, email id, Date of Birth etc. This information is used to identify you as a special individual and to respond to your requests. We take extreme care to ensure safety of this information.
We also ask your personalized preference such as your favorite player, favorite team etc. This information is used to customize our product and services to suite your preferences. Other users can see your personalized information such as your favorite player, favorite team.
We may mail you latest update and happening on the website. You may choose to unsubscribe from the mail service by following instructions provided in the mail. We may also gather other information provided by browser. This information remains safe with us and is used to serve you even better.
We will automatically receive and collect certain anonymous information in standard usage logs through our Web server, including computer-identification information obtained from "cookies" sent to your browser from:
1. Web server cookie stored on your hard drive of the device you are using
2. The domain server through which you access our service
3. The type of web browser you're using
4. An IP address, assigned to the device which you use
5. The type of device you're using to access website
Third party vendors (including Google) use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Their use of the DoubleClick cookie enables them and their partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting Ads Settings.
You could also opt out of a third-party vendors' use of cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info .
Other websites
SmashReport content may have links to other websites. We are not responsible for privacy practices of such websites. You are advised to read privacy policy of those websites.
Your Content
SmashReport gives you freedom to voice your opinion. However all our users are advised to ensure that they do not violate copyright laws meant to promote original work.
You are requested to notify us at contact@smashreport.com if you come across content that violates any of the guidelines mentioned here. We take appropriate action to ensure that no activity on our website violates guidelines mentioned.
