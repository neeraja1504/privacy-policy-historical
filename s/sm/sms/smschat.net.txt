Privacy and Security Policy
Your right to Privacy and data Security is our primary concern that is why we are committed to protecting your privacy when visiting and accessing our Site by developing specific policies and practices. We use the information we collect about You in order to provide brokerage services in compliance with regulatory requirements. Below are the guidelines we use for protecting the information You provide us when opening an account with ASAIG or when utilizing the products and services available through ASAIG. We will endeavor to protect the trust You have placed in us by respecting the privacy of all Users, especially as its related to maintaining the confidentiality of any information we may obtain.
We have prepared this notice to explain what types of information we may require, how we use it and under what circumstances we may share it.
** 1-    ** Specific types of information for specific purposes
You can visit our Site without giving us any information about yourself.
We have tried our best to minimize the amount of information requested from You. The information we request in the form is required for us to perform the delivery of the Service You ordered such as your name, e-mail address, telephone number, birth date, social security number, investment objectives.
We do not sell or license lists of our customers or the personal, non-public information that you provide to us.
We restrict access to the personal, non-public information that you have shared with us to those ASAIG employees, agents, and affiliates who need to know such information in connection with the services that ASAIG provides to you.
We maintain strict employment policies that prohibit employees who have access to your personal, non-public information from using or disclosing such information except for business purposes.
** 2-    **  ** Security Measures**
We use the latest encryption and security technologies and fraud detection techniques to keep personal and account information safe and secure and to maintain such information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration or destruction Although, we will use all reasonable efforts to safeguards the confidentiality of your personal or account information, transmissions made by means of internet cannot be secure. We will not be liable for disclosures of your information due to errors in transmissions or unauthorized acts of third parties.
** 3-    ** Use of Cookies
When You view the Site, we might store some information on your computer. This information will be in the form of a “cookie” or similar file. Cookies are small pieces of information stored on your hard drive, not on our Site. Cookies do not spy on You or otherwise invade your privacy, and they can not invade your hard drive and steal information. Rather they help you navigate a website as easily as possible. We use Cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to prevent You from reentering all your registration data at each connection. Most browsers are set to accept cookies, however if your prefer, You can set yours to refuse them.

In addition, ASAIG also records your IP address, which the Internet address of your computer, and information such as your browser type and operating system. If any time, You would like to change, amend, or update any personal information in your account, you can do so by sending us an e-mail through the “Contac Us” link available in this Site. Here you can change or update your information.
