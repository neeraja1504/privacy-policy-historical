SMS Receive Free | Privacy Policy
It is our policy to respect your privacy regarding the use of this service. No personally identifying information is kept, and all message contents are temporary. Your privacy and trust is very important to us.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, SMSReceiveFree collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. SMSReceiveFree's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how SMSReceiveFree's visitors use its website. From time to time, SMSReceiveFree may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website. SMSReceiveFree may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. SMSReceiveFree may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, SMSReceiveFree does not collect or disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. Google is used as an advertisement vendor and employs cookies to serve ads on this site. SMSReceiveFree has no access to the cookies used by Google or other advertising vendors for the serving of these advertisements.
This policy last updated September 20, 2014.
