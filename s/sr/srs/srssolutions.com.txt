Privacy Policy | SRS Solutions
We are committed to protect your privacy. As such, we will try our best to explain to you what information we collect at what time and how we use that information. Please carefully read our privacy policy to better understand how we use information.
We may collect and use information about how our customers use our website and services. If we collect such information, we will use it only on an aggregate basis, i.e. not tracked to you, unless we have first notified you and obtained your consent.
Submitting Forms
When you submit any of the forms available on this website, you agree that any information including the contact information you enter and submit will be used by our representatives to identify and contact you for the purpose of your inquiry and serve you better. The information will under no circumstances be sold, distributed, rented or otherwise shared with third parties unless we have first notified you and obtained your consent.
You understand that there is no obligation on you to share personal information with us.
Links
Our website may have links to external resources such as partners, affiliates, and other helpful websites. We are not responsible for the content of these websites.
Cookies
We may send a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie does only store textual data and cannot be executed or run. Therefore, it cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. SRS uses cookies to track which sections of the SRS Network you have visited and how you used them. You can choose whether to accept cookies or if you want to be notified when a cookie is sent by changing the settings of your browser. If you choose not to accept cookies, your experience at our website and other websites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.
