Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 1, 2014.
Sli.do s.r.o. provides the Privacy Policy to inform users about policies and processes regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from users of sli.do on www.sli.do and other domains runned by sli.do s.r.o. (“Sites”).
What information do we collect?
We collect and process information from you when you create an event, interact with an event or register on our sites. You may be asked to enter details about you and your feedback in order to provide you better insights, however you may use our sites without it.
Why do we need your information and how we use it?
We collect information from you in order to enhance your user experience, namely following:
Personalize your experience and make using our sites easier and faster for you.
Provide you better insights
Improve our website and services based on your feedback.
How do we keep your information protected?
We store your data on our servers located within the European Union and United States. We implement a large palette of security measures in order to maintain the safety of your personal data when we collect and process them.
Your private financial information (including the credit cards, social security numbers, financials, etc.) are transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) and encrypted to our database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. Such information will be stored for the time of your subscription and 60 days after the expiration.
How do we use cookies?
We use cookies on our sites for two main reasons:
In order to save your settings for future visits.
To store your interaction with our sites
We use the information collected using cookies to improve our sites and services. We may also contract with third party service providers in order to help us improve our business and services.
Do we disclose information to third parties?
We do not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We will only provide information about your interactions on our site with your specific approval. We may provide the information about feedbacks and results to trusted third parties with your consent, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. This information is used mainly for improving of our services and for research purposes.
We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. Non-personally identifiable information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.
Other important notes
At our discretion, we may occasionally include or offer third party products or services on our sites. As these have separate and independent privacy policies, we have no responsibility and liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. However, we do our best to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites and services.
As we are in the compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our sites are directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
This online privacy policy applies to information collected through our website only, therefore not to information collected offline.
Your consent
By using our site, you consent to our web site privacy policy. The consent to provide the information about feedbacks and results to trusted third parties is given separately and you are able to switch it off in your profile settings.
Changes to our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
