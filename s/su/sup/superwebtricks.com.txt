Showeblogin Privacy Policy
SuperWebTricks.com knows that you care how information about you is collected, used and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly. We are committed to protecting your information and respecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.
We collect information like name and email address from you while you sign up for our Email Newsletter, subscribe the RSS Feed and register yourself for any quiz contests on our website or submitting any forms or giving your opinion through commenting on our site. Moreover, we are using Google Analytics to see you live and to collect your location, browser data and device information etc.
The collected information may be used to communicate with you and provide better personalized services and user experience. Your information is fully secure with us and we never either share or sell your any data to anyone in any manner.
However, we are using Google AdSense, Affiliate Ads and Direct advertisements and therefore your web browser automatically sends certain information to them. You may read in details that how Google uses your data when you visit our site. Showing ads helps us offer many of our services for free to our users and live visitors.
Hence, we use cookies to personalise content and advertisements, to provide social media features and to analyse our traffic. We also share information about your use of our site with our social media, advertising and analytics partners.
