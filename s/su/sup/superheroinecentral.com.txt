Super Heroine Central
Our Commitment To Privacy: Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage.
The Information We Collect: This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on (www.SuperheroineCentral.com) website. On some pages, you can order products/subscriptions or make requests. The types of personal information collected at these pages may include your name, address, email address and, in some case your phone number (for tangible goods sales only).
The Way We Use Information: This information is used solely to process subscription and order fulfillment and such information is not shared with any other entity. We will not contact you in any way unless you request it. This includes email, telephone and physical mail.
Our Commitment To Data Security: To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
How To Contact Us: Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send an email to sales@superheroinecentral.com.
