Subbmitt.com - Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Information Collected
We collect no personal information unless you provide it. The only personal information requested is your name and email address and both of these are optional.
Our website automatically collects information about your computer including your ip address, browser type, operating system, & referring page. This information is for our internal use only and is not shared with any third parties.
Cookies
We may use cookies at our discretion to record user activities & display ad information. Ads from third parties shown on our website may also use cookies at their discretion. If you don't want to use cookies see your web browser on how to disable them, however some portions of our website may be affected.
Parents & Kids
This website is not intended for use by children, specifically those under 18 years of age. Kids 18 years and younger need their parents permission to use this website.
