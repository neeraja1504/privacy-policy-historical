Privacy Policy of Sulit.PH | Buy and Sell Philippines
Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes your privacy rights regarding Sulit.ph 's collection, use, storage, sharing, and protection of your personal information. It applies to the Sulit.ph website and all related websites, applications, services, and tools where this policy is referenced, regardless of how you access or use them. By using our Sites and Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and consent to the processing of your personal information as described here.
The term “personal information” shall describe information that can be associated with a specific person and can be used to identify that person, whether from that data, or from other information that Sulit.ph has or is likely to have access. Sulit.ph does not consider personal information to include information that has been made anonymous or aggregated so that it cannot be used, whether in combination with other information or otherwise, to identify a specific user.
Information We Collect
A person can browse the Sulit.ph 's website, or related sites, without telling Sulit.ph who the user is or revealing any personal information about the user. Oncee a user registers for Sulit.ph account, the user gives Sulit.ph his/her personal information. If the user chooses to provide Sulit.ph with personal information, the user consents to the transfer and storage of that information on Sulit.ph servers.
Sulit.ph collects the following types of personal information in order to provide the user with the use of and access to Sulit.ph 's sites, applications, services and tools, and to help Sulit.ph personalize and improve the user's experience:
Information Collected Automatically
Each visit of a user to the Sulit.ph sites, use its applications, services and tools, or interact with the advertising or content, Sulit.ph automatically collects information sent to it by the user's computer, mobile device, or other access device. This information is associated with the user personally only when the user signs in as a registered user, and includes but not limited to:
device ID or unique identifier, device type, geo-location information, computer and connection information, statistics on page views, traffic to and from the sites, referral URL, ad data, IP address and standard web log information; and
anonymous information collected through Sulit.ph 'suse of cookies and web beacons
Information Provided by Users
Sulit.ph collects and stores any information the user enters on Sulit.ph sites or provides in the context of using Sulit.ph 's sites, applications, services or tools. This may include, but is not limited to:
information the user provides Sulit.ph when registering for an account, including email address, phone number, mobile telephone number and physical contact information;
additional information the user may provide via social media sites or services;
information provided in the context of community discussions, chats, dispute resolution, correspondence through Sulit.ph 's sites, or correspondence sent to Sulit.ph ; and
additional personal information Sulit.ph may ask the user to submit to authenticate the user or if Sulit.ph believes the user is violating site policies
Information Collected From Mobile Applications or Devices
When a user downloads or uses the mobile applications, or access one of the mobile optimized sites, Sulit.ph may collect information about the user and the user's mobile device. This may include location information as authorized by the user and his/her device. Sulit.ph may use this information to provide the user with location based services, such as advertising, search results, and other personalized content.
Sulit.ph will obtain the user's explicit consent before it collects any sensitive personal information from the user as a result of the use of Sulit.ph 's mobile applications or access of its sites using a mobile device.
Information Collection from with Social Media Sharing and Registration
Sulit.ph offers sign-on services that allow the user to use third party login credentials to access Sulit.ph and related sites. Sulit.ph also offers services that allow the user to share information with third party social media websites such as Facebook, Google Plus, Twitter, and others.
The user may choose to provide Sulit.ph with access to certain personal information stored by such third party websites. The personal information that Sulit.ph has access to varies by website and is controlled by the user's privacy settings on that website and the user's consent. By associating an account managed by a third party with the user's Sulit.ph a account and authorizing Sulit.ph to have access to this information, the user agrees that Sulit.ph may collect, use and store information from these websites in accordance with this User Privacy Policy.
How We Use Information
The information we collect are used to provide you with a safe, efficient and customized experience. We may use the information we collect to:
provide the Services and customer support you request
resolve disputes, collect fees and troubleshoot problems
prevent, detect and investigate potentially prohibited or illegal activities, and enforce our User Agreement
customize, measure and improve our Site, Services and advertising
contact you tell you about our Services and those of our corporate family, service updates, targeted marketing and promotional offers based on your communication preferences
contact you to resolve disputes, collect fees, and troubleshoot problems with your account or our sites, services, applications or tools, or for other purposes authorized by law
contact you at any telephone number using auto-dialed or pre-recorded message calls or SMS or other text messages (if applicable) for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or our User Agreement
compare information for accuracy and verify it with third parties; and/or comply with applicable laws
Transfer to Third Parties
Except as otherwise expressly included in this Privacy Policy, this document addresses only the use and disclosure of information we collect from you. If you disclose your information to others, whether they are buyers or sellers on our Site or other sites throughout the internet (including the ones we link to), different rules may apply to their use or disclosure of the information you disclose to them. Sulit.ph does not control the privacy policies of third parties, and you are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties where applicable. We encourage you to ask questions before you disclose your personal information to others.
In addition, subject to applicable law, we may share, transfer, and disclose all the information we collect, to the following:
Companies that form part of the our group of companies (the "Group Companies"), to enable the Group Companies to offer their respective services/products;
Service Providers that help us provide our services, to the extent needed to perform their duties and their functions;
Government Authorities, at their request and only when legally required to do so; and
Purchasers or potential purchasers of our rights and obligations relating to the services we provide. In this case, all information collected are deemed assets that may be transferred to such third parties.
In addition to the foregoing, the information we collect may also be shared, transferred and disclosed, when disclosure is necessary:
To protect the rights and interests of Sulit.ph , its companies, business partners, customers or third parties, as may be required and permitted by law; and
To pursue legitimate interests, as may be required or permitted by law.
Closing of Account and Retention of Personal Information
Upon the user's request, and subject to the provisions in this section, Sulit.ph will close the user's account and, as soon as reasonably possible, remove the user's personal information so that it cannot be viewed by other users. Removal will be based on the user's account activity and in accordance with applicable laws. Sulit.ph may also close or deactivate inactive accounts, or accounts that are used in violation of Sulit.ph 's User Agreement.
In general, personal information from closed or inactive accounts will be deleted or rendered anonymous as soon as reasonably possible after the account is closed or deactivated. Under certain circumstances, Sulit.ph may retain personal information from closed or inactive accounts for longer. Grounds for doing so would include; prevention of fraud, resolution of disputes, to enforce Sulit.ph 's User Agreement, or to otherwise take actions permitted by law. Under such circumstances this information will be kept as long as it is necessary and relevant for Sulit.ph 's operations, and to comply with any applicable law.
Security
Your information is stored on our servers located in the United States/Singapore . We treat data as an asset that must be protected and use tools (encryption, passwords, physical security, etc.) to protect your personal information against unauthorized access and disclosure. However, as you probably know, third parties may unlawfully intercept or access transmissions or private communications, and other users may abuse or misuse your personal information that they collect from the site. Therefore, although we work very hard to protect your privacy, we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your personal information or private communications will always remain private.
Privacy Policy Updates
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this site. Changes take effect 30 days after they are posted to the site. This policy was last updated effective February 20, 2015.
