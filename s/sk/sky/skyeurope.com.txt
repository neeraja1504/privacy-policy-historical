Skyeurope - Privacy Policy
Our server automatically and indirectly collects information in relation to the activities of users during their visit to the web site. The information gathered relates to the pages viewed, the date and time of access, the IP address, and the internet browser and operating system used. This data is used with the sole aim of acquiring anonymous statistical information regarding the use of the site and/or for purposes strictly related to the functioning of the site itself.
Viewing of the site and use of its services do not require any form of personal identification.
The user can voluntarily provide his email address and other personal details to indicate an error on the site, to send feedback of various kinds, or to receive information from our staff.
The data collected will be processed by Azulsen s.r.l., the proprietor of the skyeurope.com website, with full respect for the current law. All necessary security measures will be adopted to prohibit access to non-authorised parties.
SkyEurope makes no direct use of cookies.
