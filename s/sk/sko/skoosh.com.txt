Skoosh Privacy Policy
Skoosh conforms to the Data Protection Act of 1998, and any person providing information to Skoosh. is protected by the laws therein.
Any or all Personally Identifiable Information collected from this website is used by Skoosh for contact purposes only. We contact our customers on an infrequent basis to let them know about special offers and new features available on our site. You always have the option to unsubscribe from our mailing list. Information collected is kept on record by Skoosh for future reference. Any information collected is not viewed by/given/sold/rented to any third party, and remains strictly confidential.
The Personally Identifiable Information that Skoosh collects is:
» full name
» home address
» telephone number
» e-mail addresses
These details are used only to send requested/important information to customers, and for our own marketing research. Credit card payments over the internet are secure and are managed using secure server technology (SSL encryption). Our card acquirer is Barclays Merchant Services, the information is sent using 128bit SSL encryption. N.B. We do not store your credit card details.
Any concerns that you may have with regards to security can be addressed to the IT Manager on thecontact page.
