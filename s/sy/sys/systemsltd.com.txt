Privacy Policy of Systems Limited | Systems Limited
Systems Limited has always been fully committed to respect your privacy. We aim to maintain the confidentiality and appropriate protection of your any personally identifiable information, i.e. name, address, and telephone number.
In general, you can visit our website without telling us who you are or revealing any other personal information. We track the Internet address of the domains from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the individual user remains anonymous. Systems Limited would like to assure you that we do not :
Collect personal information from you unless you provide it to us
Sell the names and addresses of our customers (site visitors) to outside vendors
Support & encourage SPAM
