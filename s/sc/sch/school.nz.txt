Privacy Policy for www.school.nz
This privacy policy applies exclusively to the website at www.school.nz (i.e. not to sites like something.school.nz) Any of the school websites linked to from this website are not covered by this policy - separate privacy policies should be maintained by each of the schools and other sites linked to.
In order to operate a secure website we make use of an internet technology called "cookies". Cookies may be stored on your computer to identify your preferences and login information for www.school.nz (if any). We also collect certain other web usage information such as your IP address, details of which pages you visit and the date and time of such visits.
Advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number - unless you explicitly supply such details) to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
DoubleClick DART cookies
We also may use DART cookies for ad serving through Google's DoubleClick, which places a cookie on your computer when you are browsing the web and visit a site using DoubleClick advertising (including some Google AdSense advertisements). This cookie is used to serve ads specific to you and your interests ("interest based targeting"). The ads served will be targeted based on your previous browsing history. DART uses "non personally identifiable information". It does NOT track personal information about you, such as your name, email address, physical address, telephone number, social security numbers, bank account numbers or credit card numbers. You can opt-out of this ad serving on all sites using this advertising by visiting http://www.doubleclick.com/privacy/dart_adserving.aspx
