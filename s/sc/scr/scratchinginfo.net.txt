Privacy Policy
At http://www.scratchinginfo.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by http://www.scratchinginfo.net and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, http://www.scratchinginfo.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies and Web Beacons
http://www.scratchinginfo.net does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on users browser type or other data that the visitor sends through their browser. Some of our advertising partners may use web beacons and cookies on our site.
These ad networks or third-party ad servers  use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on http://www.scratchinginfo.net send directly to your browsers. They may automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as JavaScript, Web Beacons or cookies ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to analyze the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
http://www.scratchinginfo.net has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. http://www.scratchinginfo.net’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
