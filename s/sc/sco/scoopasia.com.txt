Scoopasia
Privacy Policy
Scoopasia LLP recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all information provided by users of Scoopasia.com. We created the following policy guidelines with a fundamental respect for our users' right to privacy to guide our relationships with them.
Information Collected by Scoopasia.com
Scoopasia.com collects information about users during their registration for our services. Their participation in activities on our Web site, including content posting, contests, polls, and through the use of cookies. When users request pages from our server, our Web server automatically collects some information about the users, including their IP addresses. These addresses are used by computers on the network to send the requested pages to users.
Use of Information
Scoopasia LLP will not reveal any personal information that users provide to us to any third party without their permission.
No submitted email address will be sold, rented or given to third parties for marketing purposes. Only Scoopasia related updates, announcements, and automatically generated server messages will be sent to our registered members.
Scoopasia LLP utilizes users' IP addresses to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site. We also use it to gather broad demographic information about our users in general. This information does not contain anything that can identify users personally. We may combine demographic information supplied by a subscriber at registration with site usage data to provide profiles, in aggregate form, about our users and their preferences in the content of the site and advertising. We may share this composite information with our advertisers to help them better understand our services, but Scoopasia LLP does not give advertisers information about individual users of Scoopasia.com.
Our site uses cookies to deliver content specific to users' interests and to honor their preferences. This information is used to assist us in creating a site that will serve the needs of our users.
General
Scoopasia LLP is not responsible for the content or the privacy polices of Web sites to which it may provide links to or the Web sites of its advertisers.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your dealings with Scoopasia LLP, you can contact us here.
