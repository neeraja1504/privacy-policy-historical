Directory of Science - Privacy Statement
**Privacy Policy for DirectoryOfScience.com
**
We respect the privacy of all visitors to our website.
You can browse our site without registration or disclosing your any personal information.
We do not sell, disclose, lease, rent, or share any personal information including e-mail addresses (if you email us), and we do not use cookies on this website.
In order to serve advertisements to this site, a third-party advertiser may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
If we are utilizing an Advertising Network that sets cookies, we would not receive or collect any of the information.
Our Web server automatically receives anonymous "general usage data" which could include a record of which pages at our website visitors frequent the most. This information helps us determine which sections and resources are most popular with our viewers and allows us to serve them better.
This website provides external links to other websites. We are not responsible and cannot control the content and security of other sites you visit once you leave our website.
