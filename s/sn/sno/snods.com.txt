SNODS - The twitter game where you capture the big and baddest twitter users for glory and profit!
|  Last Updated: June 8th, 2009
Personal information and other data we collect:
Using the twitter API we will grab your name, avatar image, and other twitter profile information you have set as publicly available on twitter.
We use cookies and other technologies to maintain your session on the site and to record your actions within the site.
We log session information when you visit any of our sites or games, including the URL, IP address, browser type and language, and the date and time of your requests.
Use of information:
We don't share your personally identifying information with third parties, unless as part of a criminal investigation.
We may share aggregated non-personal information with third parties.
We use tracking information to monitor for abuse, provide custom content, and to improve user experiences.
Your choices and profile information:
There will be optional information that you can provide within our games and sites that will show on your public profile. We urge great care in not providing any personally identifying information in any of these areas.
*** We reserve the right to update our privacy policy at our sole discretion. Any changes will be prompty updated on this page.**
