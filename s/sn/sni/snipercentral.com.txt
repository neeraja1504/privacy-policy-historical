Sniper Central Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to Sniper Central will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:
SniperCentral does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, period.
Browser information collected on the website:
SniperCentral analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Use of cookies:
We do not use cookies on this site.
Privacy of email sent to us:
Any email sent to SniperCentral is kept confidential. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses of emails sent to us to anyone.
