SnazzySpace.com - Privacy Policy!
Below is our privacy policy
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies you can opt-out by visiting the NAI opt out URL located here Opt Out of Behavioral Advertising.
Terms of Use
All content and services provided by SnazzySpace are for personal use only. You may not distribute any content or services on this site without our written permission. Layouts and graphics may not be modified and links back to SnazzySpace may not be removed. You may not use our content for anything prohibited by these terms and conditions.
User Privacy
This privacy policy has been made to provide information about our commitment to our visitors privacy while browsing our website.
Information Automatically Logged
The only thing we log from our visitors are traffic statistics information and their IP address. The statistic information helps us see where our visitors are coming from and providing overall traffic analysis.
Third Party Sites
There are links to third party sites mainly from advertising companies bringing in traffic revenue. SnazzySpace is not responsible for the content provided by these sites nor their privacy policy practices.
Personal Information
SnazzySpace does not collect any personal information. This site will never ask visitors for their contact information (including email address or MySpace details).
