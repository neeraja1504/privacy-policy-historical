Privacy Policy | SnapStream
SnapStream is Committed to Your Online Security
Online privacy is a serious concern. At SnapStream, we uphold the same level of privacy standards that we demand from other websites.
We will never sell, trade, or otherwise make available any personally identifiable information to any 3rd party organization or company not affiliated with SnapStream.
What information do we collect?
We collect information such as name, e-mail, mailing address, and/or phone number from you when you respond to a survey or fill out a form.
What do we use your information for?
Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:
(a) Traffic Analysis - SnapStream uses software programs to analyze traffic to this website. These programs collect aggregate data, not personal information, and are critical to improving the user experience within the website.
(b) Periodic Emails - The email address you provide may be used to send you occasional information such as company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. We will never sell or share your information with anyone else not affiliated with SnapStream. Note: If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.
(c) Customer Service - Where applicable, your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.
(d) Forms - Inquiry forms, such as information requests or site registration, collect information that is securely stored and will only be used to direct you to the resources you seek. This may include follow up by one of our associates to answer questions you may have.
Links to Other Sites
The SnapStream website may contain links to other website and internet resources. SnapStream is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these other website and internet resources.
Do we use cookies?
Yes. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.
Online Privacy Policy Only
This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline. By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
Questions and Concerns
If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy you may contact us at marketing@snapstream.com.
