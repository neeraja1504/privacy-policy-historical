Secure Billing Worldwide
If you need assistance with any questions you may have regarding your account, contact us at support@sbw.com or call 1-877-282-9057.
SBW Privacy Information
Your privacy is very important to us. SBW adheres to the following privacy policy regarding its collection, use and dissemination of personal and non-personal information collected through its website. SBW reserves the right to modify its privacy policy at any time.
Collection of Non-Personal Information
Whenever you visit a web page on the internet, your browser automatically identifies the IP address from where your page request was initiated. It also volunteers certain basic information like browser type, the operating system you are running, and in some cases the page from which you referred to the Site. The SBW servers automatically collect this information and tracks it on a page by page and on a statistical basis.
Use of Non-Personal Information
SBW uses browser type, operating system, and page visits for statistical analysis of site usage and navigation trends.
Collection of Personal Information
When you sign up as a customer via our secure form, we may ask for your name, your e-mail address, contact information, credit card, bank routing and bank account numbers. SBW also uses "Cookies" and session data to collect personal information about our visitors.
Use and Disclosure of Personal Information
SBW will not give or sell this information to third parties. SBW will also respond to legal requests for information where required by law to do so.
Security
Access to your personal information is limited to the SBW employees who we believe have a justified business reason to access your information. All personal information is protected through password access, encryption, restricted network access and only available to identified corporate users. Your information is protected under strict PCI guidelines.
