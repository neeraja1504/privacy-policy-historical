Xythos Privacy Policy
Xythos Software is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. The following information explains our privacy practices.
Collection, Use and Disclosure of Information
Xythos Software collects the domain name and/or IP address (i.e., the location of your computer on the Internet) of each user that visits the Site for system administration and troubleshooting purposes. We do not link this information to any personally identifiable information, which means that your session on the Site will be logged, but you will remain anonymous to Xythos Software. In addition, some areas of the Site may permit you to submit contact and other information to Xythos Software. Your contact information may be used to provide you with information such as press releases and other news concerning Xythos Software and its products and services, changes to the Terms and Conditions, and other information Xythos Software believes you may find valuable. By electing to submit contact or other information to Xythos Software, you are consenting to such communications by Xythos Software. You may opt-out of receiving such communications from Xythos Software (see "Opt-Out" section below). Xythos Software does not share your contact information with any third party without your consent, except a court or governmental authority if reasonably required by law.
Choice / Opt-Out
It is up to you whether or not you provide your contact information to Xythos Software. Once you have provided Xythos Software with your contact information, it may send communications regarding its products and services, the Site or otherwise, as noted above. Xythos Software provides you with the opportunity, at any time, to opt-out of receiving such communications, and/or to request the removal or your contact or other information from Xythos Software's database, by indicating your desire to opt-out:
In an e-mail sent to info@xythos.com; or
