Privacy Policy | Sharetec Credit Union Management Systems
Sharetec Systems, Inc. respects your privacy and we are committed to protecting it. Sharetec Systems, Inc. provides this Privacy Statement to inform you of our Privacy Policy and practices and about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used. We make this notice readily available on our home page and at the bottom of every Sharetec.com web page. What we collect:
If you inquire through our contact form or site analysis form, any information your submit will be used to answer your inquiry ONLY.
We do track IP's and user sessions on our site and NEVER match that information with any user profile.
How we use this information:
All information you submit to us, will be used internally at Sharetec Systems, Inc. to help answer your inquiring or meet your needs.
In the case of client information, we use the contact information of existing clients to inform them of changes and offers at Sharetec Systems, Inc. through direct e-mail.
What we do not do:
We will NEVER give your contact information or any related information you submit to Sharetec Systems, Inc. to a third party.
We cannot control the practices of sites linked to and from Sharetec.com.
Please contact us if you have any questions or concers regarding this privacy policy.
