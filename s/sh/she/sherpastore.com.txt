Privacy Policy | MECLABS Training Group
MECLABS, MarketingSherpa and MarketingExperiments are committed to respecting and protecting your right to privacy. We won't rent, sell or barter your email address or other personal information to anybody and will never do so. We will also not make your email address available to third parties unless they are serving as a contracted suppression or broadcast vendor on our behalf to send you the email you explicitly requested, and they have strict privacy and security precautions in place.
Information Collection and Use
MECLABS is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to third parties in ways other than those disclosed in this policy.
Purchases on MECLABS sites
We require information from the user on orders forms to properly complete the transaction. This includes contact information (like name, telephone number, email address) and financial information (like credit card, expiration date).
We request information from the user on our order forms. Here a user must provide contact information (like name and shipping address) and financial information (like credit card number, expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill customer's orders. If we have trouble processing an order, this contact information is used to get in touch with the user. All online transactions are processed using secure sites.
Contests
Participation in contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose contact information such as name, email and shipping address. Contact information will be used to notify the winners and award prizes.
Choice/Opt-out
Users who no longer wish to receive a newsletter or what to decline any promotional communication from MECLABS may opt-out of receiving these communications by replying to the unsubscribe link in the e-mail or online.
Notification of Changes
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will seek to advise all users of these changes.
