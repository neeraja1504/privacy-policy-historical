Shopit, Inc. ("Shopit.com") values the users of our website (the "Website") and has created this privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") to demonstrate our commitment to customer privacy. Your privacy and trust are very important to us. We recognize that you may be concerned about our collection, use and disclosure of the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") that we collect when you use the Website and the services offered on the Website ("Shopit.com Services"). The terms "user", "seller", "profile", "user profile", "account" and "store" can all be used when referring to your Personal Information and where it may be used within this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes the information that we collect from you when you use the Website, how we collect this information, and what we do with it after we collect it. By using the Website you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or comments regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@shopit.com.

What Types of Information Does Shopit.com Collect From Me?
Personal Information :
Shopit.com provides Web-based service permitting users, among other things, to list, browse, buy, sell and trade products, and to communicate with other users via email, instant messages and chat. In almost all cases, we do not obtain any Personal Information about you unless you choose to reveal it. You are not required to disclose any personally identifiable information to us, although certain parts of the Website and certain services may not be available to you unless you do so.
We collect Personal Information, such as your name, address, phone number, email address, credit card number, and financial information that you submit to us in the user account registration process. You will also have the option to fill out an online profile with additional Personal Information about yourself. You may update your Personal Information at any time by logging into the Website and editing your online profile. You can view your updated profile to confirm your edits have been made.
Shopit.com also collects the information you submit or upload to your account to list items in your "store" or "widget" - the areas of your account where you advertise products you want to make available for sale to other users. Such information includes products descriptions, images, pricing and video commercials. As further provided in the Shopit.com Terms of Use, we are the sole owner of all information we collect relating to item listings.
When you use Website email, chat, instant messaging or other similar features of the Website to communicate with other users, we collect the content of those communications. We will also collect the content of any messages you send to us, including emails sent in response to Shopit.com surveys or announcements regarding special offers, promotions or sweepstakes. If you sign up to receive a newsletter, we will collect your email address.
Non-Personal Information :
Like the operators of most websites, we also collect and log non-personal information that your browser makes available whenever you visit the Website, such as your browser type, browser language, the URL of the previous website you visited, Internet domain and host names, your ISP, operating system, clickstream patterns, your Internet protocol (IP) Address, and the dates and times that the Website and the Shopit.com services are accessed ("Non-Personal Information"). We also track and compile aggregate statistics regarding use of the Website ("Aggregate Data"). In addition, we contract with several online partners who may track Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data to help us manage, monitor and optimize our Website and the Shopit.com services and to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising, communications and visitor use of the Website. To do this, we may use web beacons and cookies. See "What About Cookies and Web Beacons?" below.
Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data cannot be easily used to identify you personally. Although new techniques exist to obtain personally identifiable information using IP addresses, Shopit.com does not use IP addresses for that purpose; provided, however, that we may log the IP address of a user's computer and compare the online information provided in the Shopit.com profile to try to prevent, eliminate, and investigate user fraud.

How Does Shopit.com Use My Information?
Personal Information :
We use Personal Information primarily for our own internal purposes, such as providing, maintaining, evaluating, and improving the Shopit.com services and the Website, and providing customer support. If you create a Shopit.com account, we will use the information that you provide us to communicate with you regarding "store" sales or trade transactions, to provide you with periodic updates and notices regarding the Shopit.com services, and to provide you with promotional information regarding our company or partners . Similarly, if you sign up to receive a Shopit.com newsletter, we will use the email address you provide to send you the newsletters.
We use all information you submit relating to items for sale in your "store" to create listings for such items. We also transmit offers, counteroffers and similar user communications in the course of facilitating purchase, sale and trade transactions between users.
Shopit.com automatically displays a limited amount of personally identifiable user profile information, including users' pictures (if provided), age and location to other users and the public in order to facilitate interaction and foster a sense of community among users of the Shopit.com services. We encourage users not to reveal additional Personal Information on the public profile, such as addresses and telephone numbers, but users may provide any Personal Information they wish in private messages to other users. Users' email addresses are not available in the public profile; however, a user's Shopit.com email address is revealed to another user if the user sends a message to the other user through the service. The recipient's Shopit.com email address is not available to the sender unless the recipient chooses to reply. Users' non-Shopit.com email addresses are not displayed or revealed to other users when they communicate through the service. To facilitate searching and finding friends and acquaintances on the service, Shopit.com allows users to search for other members based on Personal Information provided in numerous categories in the public profiles.
We may periodically view or access individual records for the purpose of resolving a problem or dispute, a support issue, a suspected violation of the Shopit Terms of Service or as may be required by law. Shopit.com uses age information to limit access to Shopit.com services to users 13 and older. For more information, see "What About Children's Privacy?" below and the section relating to children in the Shopit.com Terms of Service.
Non-Personal Information :
We use Non-Personal Information and Aggregate Data to track the use of the Website and the Shopit.com Services and for other internal purposes, such as improving Website security, diagnosing and resolving technical problems, providing, maintaining, evaluating, and improving Shopit.com services and the Website, and monitoring, preventing and investigating user fraud and abuse. This information helps us constantly improve the quality of the Website and the Shopit.com service.

When Will Shopit.com Disclose My Information to Third Parties?
Except as expressly stated in this Privacy Policy, in the Shopit.com Terms of Service, or at the time we request information, we do not disclose customer email addresses or any other Personal Information to any third parties.

We may disclose your Personal Information to any successor-in-interest of ours, such as a company that acquires us. In the event Shopit.com goes through a business transition, such as a merger or acquisition by or with another company, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your Personal Information will likely be among the assets transferred. You will be notified via email or by a notice on our Website of any such change in ownership or control of your Personal Information. You may not be able to receive notices from us if your browser is not set to accept cookies.

We may disclose your Personal Information to prevent an emergency; to prevent harm to others; to respond to legal requirements; to investigate or prevent illegal activities, suspected infringement or fraud, or violations of the Shopit.com Terms of Service; to protect or enforce our rights and policies; to protect or enforce the rights of a third party; or as required or permitted by law (including, without limitation, to comply with a subpoena, search warrant or court order).

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Website. Shopit.com does not provide any Personal Information to the advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad. Advertising partners may also use web beacons to deliver targeted advertising to your browser. See also "What About Cookies and Web Beacons?" below.

We cannot guarantee all sellers will comply with this obligation. If you believe a Shopit.com seller is using your Personal Information in a way that does not comply with our rules, please contact privacy@shopit.com and include your user name, the seller's user name and a description of your concerns.

What About Cookies and Web Beacons?
A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. Most websites use cookies. We use cookies to track your use of the Website and the Shopit.com services, provide you with a more personalized user experience, and to allow you to login and begin use of the Shopit.com services automatically when you visit the Website. Users who disable their web browsers' ability to accept cookies will be able to browse the Website but will not be able to use certain services and features.
A web beacon (also known as a clear GIF or pixel tag) is an often-transparent graphic image, usually no larger than a 1x1 pixel that is placed on a web page or in an e-mail that is used to monitor the behavior of the user visiting the Website or receiving the e-mail. In all such instances, the web beacon collects only anonymous information about your visit to a particular website or receipt of email and sends that information to servers for processing. Shopit.com and/or its third-party service providers use pixel tags to better understand the traffic patterns of visitors.
Cookies and web beacons used by Shopit.com and our online partners are not linked to Personal Information. Some of our users may use cookies or web beacons on our Website in connection with their stores. We have no access to or control over these cookies and web beacons. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies and web beacons by Shopit.com and our service providers only and does not cover the use of cookies or web beacons by any other third party, Shopit.com user, advertiser or marketer. For more information about how our third party advertising and marketing partners use cookies and web beacons, see "Disclosure to Third-Party Advertisers and Marketers" above.

Security
The security of your Personal Information is important to us. We use commercially reasonable security measures to protect user information stored within our database from loss, misuse, theft of alteration, both during transmission and once we receive it. Shopit.com hosts the Website and the Shopit.com service in a secure server environment that uses a firewall and other advanced technology to prevent interference or access from outside intruders. When you enter sensitive information such as a credit card number and/or social security number on our registration or order forms, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). We restrict access to user information to those employees and subcontractors who need access to perform their job functions, such as our customer service personnel, technical staff and third-party service providers. Moreover, Shopit.com user accounts are secured by user-created passwords to prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure appropriate use of user information.
We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the Personal Information submitted to us. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Of course, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of your username and password. For any additional information about the security measures we use on Shopit.com, please contact us at privacy@shopit.com. Please note that, as further described above, Shopit.com provides several third-party service providers, advertisers and marketers with access to various types of user Personal Information solely for the purpose of permitting such parties to furnish services to users or the Website. To our knowledge, such parties employ reasonable security measures to protect user information against unauthorized access or use. We cannot monitor or control handling of user information by these parties, however, and their use of such information is accordingly governed by the privacy policies and terms of service of those parties.

What About Children's Privacy?
Protecting the privacy of children is VERY important to us. For that reason, the Website is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not structure any part of our subscriber or affiliate services to attract anyone under that age. If parents believe their child (any age under 18) has accessed our service without their permission, Shopit.com encourages them to install parental control software on their computers permitting them to block their child's access our Website. For more information on Shopit.com's policies and procedures regarding children, please see the section entitled "Children" in the Shopit.com Terms of Service. You may also visit http://www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy for information from the Federal Trade Commission about protecting children's privacy online.
Under Thirteen
The Shopit.com Services are intended for users ages 13 and older only, and our system does not allow anyone who indicates they are under the age of 13 to register as a user. We will not knowingly collect or use any Personal Information from children that we know to be under the age of 13. In addition, we will delete any information in our database that we know originates from a child under the age of 13. Although advertisements promoting Shopit.com on third-party websites may unintentionally reach children under 13, Swapwedo.com does not target advertising to children in that age range.
Thirteen to Seventeen
Prospective users between the ages of 13 and 17 can only use the Shopit.com Services subject to their parents or legal guardian's supervision and consent, as further described in the policies and procedures outlined in the section of the Shopit.com Terms of Service entitled "Children". If you are between the ages of 13 and 17, you, your parent, or your legal guardian may request that we modify, remove or deactivate any of your Personal Information in our database and/or opt out from receiving communications from us. If you wish to do so, please see "How Can I Review and Make Changes to My Personal Information?" above and "Can I Opt Outï¿½?" below, or contact us at privacy@shopit.com. Shopit.com may insert a prominent notice of your age on your profile or store, if you are under 18, to notify other users of your age. Alternatively, if you are under 18 and you enter into a sale or trade transaction with another user, we may generate and send an automated email or other similar communication to the other user notifying him or her of your age.
