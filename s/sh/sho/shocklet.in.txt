Privacy Policy - Shocklet
About :
‘Shocklet.in’ has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and disseminating practices. If you have any questions or comment about our policy, please feel free to contact us or leave your comments.
Personal information :
The personal information you enter on our website is not for sale. Using our website Shocklet.in will not cause you to receive any unsolicited email. The information send to us by the visitors via contact forms and comments will be just used for communication purpose and no such information shall be sold to a third party.
Links :
Shocklet.in may contain links to others website. The Shocklet Team will not be responsible for the privacy practices of others website. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and we advise our users to read the privacy statement of each and every website that collects your personal information.
This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by Shocklet.in.
We are not responsible for any information or offers given by other websites.
