SimBrief.com - Virtual Dispatching Solutions
This statement sets out our policies with regard to personal information. Changes to the content or services found on This Site may be changed without notice. You agree to visit this page regularly and your continued use of This Site will constitute acceptance of any changes made to this policy.
What We Collect
Visitor activity is tracked using Google Analytics. Google Analytics is subject to the privacy policy of Google, Inc., which you can find on Google's website. Information collected and processed by Google Analytics includes the user's IP address, network location, and geographic location. Google Analytics acquires all its information directly from the user, by installing a "cookie" on the user's computer. We do not share any information we collect with Google, and Google Analytics does not collect any personal identifying information.
In addition, This Site may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on some pages. Third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's visits to This Site. Google may also use the DoubleClick DART cookie to enable it and its partners to serve ads based on a user's visits to other sites on the Internet. Users can choose to disable the Doubleclick Cookie at any time by visiting the following webpage: http://www.google.com/settings/ads/onweb/optout?hl=en. For more information about Google's Advertising Policy, please visit Google's Privacy Page.
Upon registration users may be required to provide additional information, such as their desired username, password, and E-mail address. The E-mail address provided may be used to send you information regarding your account.
Third Parties
User information stored on this website will never be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any third parties, other than for the express purpose of delivering the service requested or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.
Use of Cookies
This Site may use cookies to store login and session information. A cookie is a small text file that is stored by your web browser on your computer or mobile device. While cookies can be disabled or blocked in your browser settings, doing so will adversely affect the functionality of This Site.
External Links
This Site may contain links to other websites which are hosted by and maintained by third parties. Proceeding to such websites is at your own risk and we make no representations regarding the content of any such website. We cannot be liable for any loss or damage which may arise from the use of third party websites.
