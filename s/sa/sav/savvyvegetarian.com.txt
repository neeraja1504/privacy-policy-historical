Savvy Vegetarian Privacy Policy
Savvy Vegetarian respects and protects your right to privacy.
We will not sell, rent, or give your name, address, phone numbers, email address or other personal information, to anyone, for any reason. We don't publish live email addresses on Savvy Vegetarian
Our email list is compiled, and our newsletters are sent through MadMimi, which is a secure 100% permission based email broadcast service.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. Companies such as Google may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Google, go to Google ad and content network privacy policy.
