PRIVACY AND TERMS
Commitment to Privacy
We are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. We never sell, rent, lend or share any personal information about you to any third party other than those (such as courier company) who require such information to complete the delivery of your order.
This website is operated by Revecoco Limited (collectively "we", the "Company"), a Hong Kong registered limited company, established and governed by the laws of Hong Kong.
Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Data
We endeavour to ensure that all our collection, transmission, storage and usage of personal data shall be carried out in compliance with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, Cap 486 of the laws of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region
You need not supply any personal data in order to access our website or purchase in our shops. You may decline to provide us with the requested personal data, but in such case we may not be able to complete your orders. By submitting your personal data you consent to the use of that data as set out in this Statement.
We collect your name, contact email, mobile phone number, delivery address and other personal data necessary to delivery your orders to you. We may transfer these personal data to courier company to complete the delivery to you.
When you register as a member with us, we collect your contact email, mobile phone number to provide better services to you and send you important notice concerning your orders or your products.
When you choose to receive our newsletter, we will send you direct marketing emails from our company.
Your personal data are stored in our secured database for customer services and product warranty.
You can request to remove your data from our database or view your personal data with us by contacting us at hello@sallystoy.com
When you purchase online, we do not collect your credit card information. Your payment will be processed by a third party payment gateway PayPal.
When you purchase in our shops, we do not collect or use your credit card or other payment card information. Your payment receipt will be securely stored and only accessible by authorised staff of our company.
Cookies
Cookies are small computer files that stores your browsing histories and data your input on websites. As with most professional websites our website use Cookies to improve user experiences and complete your orders. You can disable cookies from your browser but this may affect the functionality of our website.
We use cookies to store your locale and language preferences, maintain your items in your shopping bag, keep you logged in etc.
This site uses Google Analytics which is one of the most widespread and trusted analytics solution on the web for helping us to understand how you use the site and ways that we can improve your experience. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content.
For more information on Google Analytics cookies, see the official Google Analytics page.
Links to Other Sites
This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.
Minimum Age to Purchase
You must be 18 or above to purchase from us.
Secure Transactions
Security and privacy of your personal information including username and password, shopping bag details etc. are our biggest concern. They are protected by the strongest 256-bit Secure Socket Layering (SSL) technology provided by RapidSSL, a company of GeoTrust. That means no one can extract or steal your data over the internet. Not even when you are using WIFI from a public hotspot. Only your internet device and our server can access these data.
Online transaction processing are made using PayPal. We will NOT store or process your credit card information. You will be asked to enter your credit card information on PayPal.
The charges on your card statement will appear as our holding company "PAYPAL*REVECOCOLTD".
Genuine Products
We guarantees all products sold from us are genuine products. Product quality is our top concern. We make sure all products are all new.
Overseas Shipping Policy
Depending on your delivery address, we will ship with DHL, FedEx, UPS or Hong Kong Post SpeedPost (EMS). A tracking number will be provided to you when we shipped the package. For some countries, duty tax may be required. The recipient will be responsible to pay for any duty tax needed. We are not responsible for any delay, custody caused by custom authorities of your country.
