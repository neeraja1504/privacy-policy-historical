www.samsontech.com | Privacy Policy
At Samson, our customers privacy is important to us. When you supply personal information to us, you can be sure that is for our use only. We do not sell or rent our customers private information. When you send an e-mail or submit information in an online form, you can be assured that the information will not be used outside of Samson.
