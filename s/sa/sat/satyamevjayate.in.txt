Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy covers the information Star India Private Limited ("STAR" and/or "We") collects from the user(s) ("User(s)" and/or "You") of www.satyamevjayate.in (" Website ") and how We handle and/or deal in the Personal Information provided by You in the course of the use of the Website by You . This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Service available on http://www.satyamevjayate.in/termsofuse.aspx This Privacy Policy is applicable throughout the world and is deemed to be effective as of January 26th.
By registering on the Website, You agree to allow us use of your Personal Information, as defined below, in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy determines, inter alia , how We deal in and/or handle the Personal Information provided by You, the purposes for which We may use such Personal Information, correction of inaccuracies in the Personal Information by You, withdrawal of consent to use Personal Information by You.
The Website is owned by Aamir Khan Productions Private Limited (" Production House "), and is licensed to STAR for the purpose of the management, operation and development of the Website and the provision of services to You.
Here are our privacy principles, which have been dealt with in greater detail below:
Providing information to us is your choice.
You can choose to review, correct and delete the Personal Information (defined later) You have provided to us.
You always have the ability to opt-out of receiving communications from us.
The Personal Information provided by You is used by STAR to increase the quality of the Website and the services provided by STAR to You. In the event that You do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and/or the collection of the Personal Information by STAR as stipulated in this Privacy Policy, please do not access and/or use the Website and/or avail the services provided by STAR. If you refuse to accept the terms of this Privacy Policy, We may choose not to offer You the services for which the Personal Information was being collected.
Information Collection and Use
The information which You may provide to us or which We may collect is or could be the following:
Your registration details which may include details such as your name, age, gender, location/pin code, email address, mobile number and the password provided by You ("Personal Information"). You may note that We adopt reasonable security measures to protect Your Personal Information from being exposed or disclosed to anyone including being disclosed on the Website;
Information that you post/upload on the Website which may include but is not limited to messages, board posts, reviews, blogs, ratings, data, notes and testimonials, poll votes, images, audio or video files and any software (" User Content ");
Your usage of the Website;
Details of the computer system or computer network which You use to visit the Website and undertake any activity on the Website; and
Any other information which whether individually or in combination with any other information is capable of identifying You.
For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, the term " Personal Information " shall not include any information which is not capable of identifying You either individually and/or in combination with any other information.
Our primary purpose in collecting information from You is to allow You to use the Website along with the various services and products available on the Website. We may also use the information for transactional emails or to provide You with information, direct marketing, online and offline advertising and other materials regarding products, services and other offers from time to time in connection with STAR or its parent, subsidiary and affiliate companies (" STAR Entities ") and its joint ventures. We may also collect information to track User behavior and preferences for internal analytics from all Users of the Website. This information is collected through the use of server log files and tracking technologies to collect and analyze certain types of technical information and may include cookies and web beacons.
We may combine your Personal Information and information collected from tracking technologies and aggregate it with information collected from other Users using our Website to attempt to provide You with a better experience on our Website. You understand, agree and acknowledge that our collection, storage and processing of your Personal Information is for a lawful purpose connected with a function or activity of the STAR Entities and its joint ventures.
If You do not consent to the use of cookies by STAR, You may at any time adjust Your browser settings and/or delete and/or disable the cookies. However, please note that should You choose to delete/ disable the use of cookies on Your computer, certain sections of the Website and/or services provided by STAR may not be available to You.
Consent to Personal Information -
You hereby consent to the collection, storage, use, disclosure, transfer, of the Personal Information by STAR for the purposes of this Privacy Policy. You hereby agree and acknowledge that the collection, storage, use, disclosure, transfer, of the Personal Information by STAR under the terms of this Privacy Policy is for a lawful purpose and is necessary for the provision of services to You on the Website.
You further understand, agree and acknowledge that your Personal Information may be classified as sensitive personal information such as Your password, age, physiological details, sexual orientation, bank account and credit card details, under applicable laws as is considered necessary by STAR to provide access to various services on its Website to You and for your usage of the Website.

You may at any time withdraw Your consent to the collection, storage, use, disclosure, transfer, of the Personal Information and/or the sensitive personal data or information by STAR, by addressing a written communication to this effect to the contact address of the Grievance Officer specified below. However, You agree and acknowledge that in the event that You choose to so withdraw Your consent, STAR shall be entitled to not provide any or all of the services on the Website to You.

We may enable access of the Users' information to the STAR Entities or its joint ventures, agents or third parties in relation to the Website or for any other marketing related activity undertaken by or on behalf of the STAR Entities and/or its joint ventures and/or for the purposes of enhancing the quality of the services provided to You on the Website. Email Policies:
STAR may use the e-mail ID provided by You for the purposes mentioned in this Privacy Policy. You have full control regarding which of these emails You want to receive. Security:
STAR uses ordinary industry standard technology designed to help keep your Personal Information including sensitive personal data or information safe. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information You put in before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of the customer data We collect is protected against unauthorized access. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure correct use of information, We will employ commercially reasonable and practicable security practices and procedures and security methods and technologies. We will also ensure on reasonable commercial efforts basis that any agent or third party that We employ complies with the same security standards as us for the protection of your Personal Information including any sensitive personal information or data.
Your information may be transferred to or be maintained on computers, computer systems and computer resources located outside of your state or country where the privacy laws may not be as protective as those where you live. If You are located outside India and choose to provide information to us, please be aware that STAR keeps or transfers information to India and processes it there. Your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.
Unfortunately, the transmission of information over the Internet is not completely secure. Although We strive to protect your personal data, We cannot guarantee the security of your data while it is being transmitted to our site; any transmission is at your own risk. Once We have received your information, We have commercially reasonable procedures and security features in place to reasonably endeavor to prevent unauthorized access.
