Privacy Policy
At 144hzmonitors.com , the privacy of our visitors is of high importance to us. The privacy policy outlines the types of personal information that is received by us and how it is used.
If you require any more information or have any other questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us here.
Log files
We use log files from Google Analytics (it’s common practice) for various purposes, including marketing strategies. The information in the log files includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, Internet Service Provider (ISP), browser type, date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user flow throughout the site, and gather demographic and geographical information. These information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. We only use the log files for ourselves and do not pass on information to any 3rd parties. If you do not like your usage to be tracked, it is possible to opt out of Google Analytics and prevent tracking cookies being placed on your computer.
Google AdSense and the DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party advertisement vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. The use of DART cookies by Google enables them to serve adverts to visitors that are based on their visits to this website as well as other sites on the internet.
To opt our of the DART cookies you may visit the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following url: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.  Tracking of users through DART cookie mechanisms are subject to Google’s own privacy policies.
Other Third Party ad servers or ad networks may be also use cookies to track users activities on this website to measure advertisement effectiveness and other reasons that will be provided in their own privacy policies, rappagestv.com has no access or control over these cookies that may be used by third party advertisers.
Other cookies
In addition to the Google Analytics tracking cookies,  _144hzmonitors.com  _provides link to Amazon products in select posts and guides. We only place them where, in our opinion, it makes sense for the user. These cookies automatically expire after 24 hours, and no personal information is gathered. The purpose of these cookies is to track any purchases you make within the given timeframe, and if so, ensure that we are given a small percentage advertising fee for that product. We have no access to or control over these 3rd party cookies.
All links that start with http://geni.us/ will redirect you to Amazon.com (or .co.uk, .ca, .de, .es, .it, .fr depending on where you live).
You can read more about Amazon’s cookies on their website.
