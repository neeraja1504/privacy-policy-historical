Our Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. Anyone under 13 may not provide any personal information on the Site. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are under the age of 13, please avoid using the Site and do not provide any information to the Site.

We may also collect information about your computer or device, internet connection, IP address, operating system and browser type.
The technology we use to automatically collect your data and keep you logged in may include cookies, flash cookies or web beacons.
Data Collected from Social Media Pages and Lead Ads
All data collected from Lead Ads on Facebook, Twitter and other social media pages owned and managed by 10KHits may be used to create a new account for the user and also subscribe the user to 10KHits newsletters. We may occasionally send out promotional e-mails to our users.
