Usage guide

Personal numbers are free and can be used for a variety of uses including keeping yourself protected whilst interacting with online strangers (dating lines, chat rooms, online auctions, classifieds) offline through voice communication . By using a personal number you no longer have to give your real telephone number to people online whom you may not know well and which can expose you to safety issues. Our personal numbers shall ONLY be used for Personal Numbering Services and PERSONAL COMMUNICATIONS. A Personal Number is defined as a Telephone Number which allow users to receive calls or other communications and have these communications forwarded to other mobile or landline numbers. Note we agree to forward your personal number to your mobile or landline in most countries. If our system does not accept your network to forward calls to please contact support@1to1chat.net.

Or any other commercial activity that is not in line with the original use.
