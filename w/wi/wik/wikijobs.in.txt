Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd. is committed to respecting your online privacy and recognizes your need for suitable protection and management of any personally identifiable information (Personal Information) you share with us
Personal Information contains any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home.For further information, you can visit Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd.’s web pages with without revealing any personal information about yourself.
Technologies Used For Tracking
Some of our web pages utilize ‘cookies’ and other tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file used to collect information about website activity. Some cookies and some other technologies may serve to remind personal information previously indicated by a web user. Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them and how to remove them.
You may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies with your browser. But please note that if you choose to erase or block your cookies, you will need to re-enter your original user ID and password to gain access to certain parts of the web site.
Tracking technologies may record information such as internet domain and host names; internet protocol (IP) addresses; browser software and operating system types; click stream patterns; and dates and times of the accessing details of the user. Our use of cookies and other tracking technologies allows us to improve our website. We may also analyze the information that does not contain personal information for trends and statistical analysis.

Ltd. will take reasonable steps to ensure that these third-party service providers are obligated to protect personal information on Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd.'s behalf.
Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd. does not intend to transfer personal information without your consent to third parties, who are not bound to act on Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. By using this website, your consent to the terms of our online privacy policy and to Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd.'s processing of personal information for the purposes given above as well as those explained where Wisdom IT Services (India) Pvt. Ltd. collects personal information on the web.
