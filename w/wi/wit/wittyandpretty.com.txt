Privacy Policy | Witty + Pretty
****Witty+ Pretty takes your privacy seriously. The only personal information we collect is what you give us, and we do not share your information with third parties. All information obtained on this site is used solely to analyze performance.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit this site. These companies may use anonymous , aggregated information ( not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please see: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice visit the NAI at http://www.networkadvertising.org. To opt out of this type of advertising, you can visit http://www.aboutads.info/choices. We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. If you have questions, feel free to contact ashley@wittyandpretty.com.
