Information We Collect.
You do not have to provide any information to us in order to use our Site. The only information we collect from you is information you choose to submit to us. This includes comments, feedback forms, and requests for information.

As a general practice, we do not sell your personally identifiable information to third parties. Generally, personal information that you provide through our Site is information we may seek to enhance our customers experience in Which Wich restaurants or the Which Wich Site.
We use third-party suppliers and service providers to facilitate our Site and may take personal information available to them to the extent necessary for site hosting. Whenever we do this, we require that our third-party suppliers and service providers agree to maintain the confidentiality of the personal information we provide to them.
Which Wich is concerned about the safety and privacy of all its users, particularly children. Which Wich will not knowingly collect information from users under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not provide us with any personal information.
