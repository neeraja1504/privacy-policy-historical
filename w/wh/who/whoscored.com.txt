How does WhoScored.com protect your information?  WhoScored.com implements a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.  Cookies
Cookies are small text files that a site can place on your computer or mobile device to store certain information. We use cookies to keep track of how you use our website, remember if you are logged in or not, make our website work efficiently, and deliver relevant content including advertisement.
You can set up your browser not to allow cookies, however some parts our site would not function properly. You can get more information about cookies here: http://www.allaboutcookies.org/.
