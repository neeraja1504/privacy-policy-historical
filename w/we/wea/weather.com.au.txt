Weather.com.au - Privacy Policy
No personal identifiable information is collected by Weather.com.au Pty Ltd. The web server does however log low level information for each request for statistical purposes only. Information logged for each request includes -
Your IP address
Date and time
Page visited
Referring web page
Your Web Browser
We will not make an attempt to identify users or their browsing activities. However, in the unlikely event of an investigation, a law enforcement agency or other government agency may exercise its legal authority to inspect our web server logs.
We will only record your e-mail address if you send us a message. Your e-mail address will only be used or disclosed for the purpose for which you have provided it and it will not be added to a mailing list or used or disclosed for any other purpose without your consent.
Weather.com.au does not utilise browser cookies. However, third party sites such as our advertising partners may set cookies for the purpose of serving ads.
For further information regarding our privacy policy, please use the contact form.
