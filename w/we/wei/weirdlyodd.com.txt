Privacy Policy - WeirdlyOdd.com WeirdlyOdd.com
The privacy of our visitors to WeirdlyOdd.com is important to us. ****
At WeirdlyOdd.com , we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important. Whenever you visit our website, our web server will recognize who you are, your browser, operating system, IP address etc.
When you comment on any post you will need to give the user name and email id which will be stored in the database. Your personal information will  never be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside WeirdlyOdd.com.
We use third party advertisements on WeirdlyOdd.com to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site. Google, through their Adsense Program, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to WeirdlyOdd.com users based on their visit to this and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
For any further queries or questions you can contact us directly at:
contact [at] weirdlyodd [dot] com.
