Webpage Screenshot Terms Of Service
This is the terms of service for for webpage screenshot website AND the chrome extension "Webpage Screenshot"
We are working hard to improve Webpage Screenshot. For your offline and online daily use.
Example for offline use: We want to add a feature to open the your screenshot directly in Photoshop, and copy to clipboard
In the future you will have the option to create a gallery, with all your screenshots.
For doing that changes we must update our TOS.
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page. (Alternatively, you can direct users to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.)
When you wish to share a picture with your friend and click on the share button, the picture is sent to our server where it is secured. The picture is only accessible to those who you choose to send it to. You can also delete the image from our server. We do everything to insure your privacy and keep your images private. Of course you must protect your connection and insure that it is secure and that others cannot view it.
When you install the extension you receive a code which is totally random. Every picture that you send is the extension include this code. This number is for solving problems that may arise with the users, and to allow the user to view or erase previous pictures.
When the extension is installed a folder is created in your Google Chrome profile. We do not save any other files or data on your computer except on this folder. When you uninstall the extension this folder is erased.
When you take a screenshot we do not save the picture file, you must do so yourself by choosing "save" or share.
You acknowledge and agree that we may use data capture, syndication analysis and other similar tools to track, extract, compile, aggregate and analyze any data or information resulting from your use of the Plug-in and that we may share or make available this information with our business partners. If you object to any of these uses, your sole recourse is to not install the extensions within your browser, or stop using the extensions and remove the extensions from your browser.
Your pictures and images will never be in any public index.
Disclaimer
We beleive that maintaining and preserving the privacy of user data is an important value. We cannot guarantee that user information and uploaded screenshot will remain private. We acknowledge that, in spite of our committed effort to protect private user information, determined individuals may still develop data-mining and other methods to uncover such information and disclose it. For this reason, we can make no guarantee against unauthorized access to information provided by you.
If you have important data, make an offline copy. We do our best to keep your images for lifetime but we cannot guarantee that. We can decide one day to delete images without any notice.
If you have any questions about the TOS, please contact us at: extensions@bubbles.co.il
