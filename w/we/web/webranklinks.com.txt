Privacy Policy | Web Rank Links
Privacy Policy:
Web Rank Links (" Web Rank Links ") operates http://webranklinks.com and may operate other websites. It is Web Rank Links policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, Web Rank Links collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Web Rank Links purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Web Rank Links visitors use its website. From time to time, Web Rank Links may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
Web Rank Links also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http://webranklinks.com blogs/sites. Web Rank Links only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to Web Rank Links websites choose to interact with Web Rank Links in ways that require Web Rank Links to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Web Rank Links gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at http://webranklinks.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Web Rank Links are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, Web Rank Links collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with Web Rank Links. Web Rank Links does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
Web Rank Links may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. Web Rank Links may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Web Rank Links does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
Web Rank Links discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Web Rank Links behalf or to provide services available at Web Rank Links websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Web Rank Links websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Web Rank Links will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Web Rank Links discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Web Rank Links believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Web Rank Links, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an Web Rank Links website and have supplied your email address, Web Rank Links may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what's going on with Web Rank Links and our products. If you send us a request (for example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Web Rank Links takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.
Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Web Rank Links uses cookies to help Web Rank Links identify and track visitors, their usage of Web Rank Links website, and their website access preferences. Web Rank Links visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Web Rank Links websites, with the drawback that certain features of Web Rank Links websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
Business Transfers
If Web Rank Links, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Web Rank Links goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Web Rank Links may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.
Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Web Rank Links and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
Privacy Policy Changes
Although most changes are likely to be minor, Web Rank Links may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Web Rank Links sole discretion. Web Rank Links encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. If you have a http://webranklinks.com account, you might also receive an alert informing you of these changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
