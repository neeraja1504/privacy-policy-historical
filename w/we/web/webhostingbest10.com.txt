Web Hosting Best 10 – Privacy Policy
Our Website servers automatically recognize a visitors domain name, we don’t however store any visitors email addresses, (where possible).
We collect only the domain name, and NOT the e-mail address of visitors to our Web page, user-specific information on which pages consumers access or visit on our website, information submitted by the visitor such as contact us form or survey information and/or site registrations.
The information we collect is used for review and then removed and discarded. It is used to improve the content of our Web page/website and used to customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual visitor experience.
Cookies: To make sure our visitors are not shown the same the same information continuously we use cookies.
Please contact us via info@WebhostingBest10.com if you do not want to receive emails from us.
We may use customer information from time to time for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. We will contact you if our information practices change or we use your data for these new purposes, you retain the right to opt out of these changes.
We provide site visitors with access to all information that we maintain about them [including proprietary information] upon request. You the consumer have the right to obtain all of this information.
Security: WebhostingBest10.com always use industry standard encryption technology when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site.
If you for any reason come to believe that WebhostingBest10.com is not following its stated privacy policy, please contact us via our contact form, or if you prefer contact us directly info@WebhostingBest10.com
