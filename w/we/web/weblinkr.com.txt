Our Privacy Policy
We at Weblinkr take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.
We may collect personal information, including email addresses, when you register to use Weblinkr and its services, add a weblink to Weblinkr or submit questions, comments, bug reports or view the site. We may also collect our users' IP addresses and information regarding our users' use of our website and services. We may also collect information related to the referring URL, access times and browser type.
In order to offer and provide optimized and personalized services, we use cookies to store and sometimes track information about our users. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to a user's browser from a web server and is eventually stored on a user's computer hard drive.
Visit your Weblinkr Settings to edit your profile and other information. When you're logged in, the Weblinkr page headers will include a link to Settings.
Weblinkr reserves the right to modify all or some of this Privacy Policy at any time without notice.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this website or your dealings with Weblinkr, you can send an e-mail, in English, to support at weblinkr.com
