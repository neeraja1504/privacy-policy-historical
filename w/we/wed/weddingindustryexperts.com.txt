Privacy Statement « Wedding Industry Experts Awards
We respect the privacy of everyone who visits our site. We will not sell, rent, share or disclose personal information to third parties.
We do not collect personal information about our users without their knowledge and prior consent. No scripts or devices are used for this purpose. In order to use certain features of our website such as the members area and awards voting system we ask you to register by entering personal information. This information is given voluntarily and will be used only as stated for the purpose of setting up a profile page or delivering a service. This information will not be given, traded or sold to any third party without the user’s knowledge and prior consent.
Members can control the information they display publicly on their member profiles.
We do not collect or store credit card information.  We use Paypal’s PayFlow gateway to process all payments.
WeddingIndustryExperts.com will not knowingly accept submissions or correspondence from children under 18 years of age.
If you require any more information or have any questions about privacy, please feel free to contact us by email at info[at]weddingindustryexperts.com
