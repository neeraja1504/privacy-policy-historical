Privacy Policy | Rugby 7s 2016
Privacy Policy
Any personal information that you provide to us, for example information submitted via the enquiry form; or the IP address that you use to visit our site; will be treated in the strictest confidence, in accordance with the Data Protection Act, and will not be passed on to third parties without your consent.
How will we use your information
Any personal data that you supply to use via the enquiry form will be used to contact you about your enquiry, and may be used to send you generic marketing information about the Wales Sports Bureau. It may be used aggregated with other data to help us build up a picture of website usage and interest, for example by geographic spread or by sporting discipline. Your personal data will never be shared with third parties.
