Privacy Policy - WPSpeak
We strongly believe in utmost privacy for our viewers. Our privacy policy covers all the aspects you need to consider before using our website.
Personal Information
We respect the privacy of our users. At WPSpeak, the users are never asked to reveal their personal information such as race, gender, address, income etc.
We do not provide personal information to any third party unless required by authorities like law and order. Users should feel safe with us, take our word for it.
We don’t store personal information of our servers unless required for the something important for of our site.
Use of Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns.
WPSpeak also uses Cookies including:
DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
You can disable cookies through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
