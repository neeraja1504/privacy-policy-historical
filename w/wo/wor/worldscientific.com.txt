Your Personal Information obtained by this Website
When you use this Website, we may ask you to provide us with certain personal information, such as your name, organization, or employer, postal address, email address, phone number, credit card, or other payment information and a password (where a login has been provided) ("Personal Information"). We may also collect other information, including, but not limited to, the URL you came from, IP address, your browser type, country, and telephone area code, where your computer is located and the pages of our website that you viewed.
We collect this Personal Information in order to record and meet your requests for our products and services, and we use the Personal Information that you provide as part of our effort to keep you informed about our products and services where you have actively provided us with your consent to do so.
Your Personal Information will remain active until you request us to no longer send you communications.
Should you submit information through our online forms, the data may be captured using various 3rd parties, including but not limited to Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc and then copied to WSPC's data systems. An email is triggered to alert WSPC staff of new entries, so details of your enquiry may be stored by our employees in our email archives.
GDPR: Our compliance and your rights
Under the GDPR 2018, World Scientific is classified as both a Data Controller and Data Processor when offering our products or services to individuals within the EU. Where we act as the data controller we are responsible for how and why your Personal Information data is processed, and for keeping records of how and when you consented (agreed) that we could do so.
Where we act as the data processor, employees of World Scientific will be able to use and manage your Personal Information data e.g. for marketing or research purposes. We will also be able to update your Personal Information data or assist you with the enactment of any of your rights relating to Personal Information data you have provided to us, which you can do by using the contact details provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.
All Personal Information data which you provide to World Scientific will be processed in line with the GDPR; that is we:
1. impose confidentiality obligations on all personnel processing relevant data;
2. review our data security and integrity policies;
3. maintain records of Personal Information data and processing activities;
4. abide by the GDPR's rules regarding the appointment of sub-processors such as Mailchimp
5. implement procedures to assist in the compliance of the rights of data subjects (private individuals);
Your rights: As an EU-based citizen the following rights are conferred to you under the GDPR:
1. You have the right to request a copy of all Personal Information data that World Scientific holds on you as an individual and it will be honored within one month from receipt.
2. You have the right to request that any incorrect information held by World Scientific is updated without undue delay.
3. You have the right to request that all Personal Information data relating to you is erased by World Scientific.
4. You have the right to receive a copy of your Personal Information data in a structured, machine-readable format where appropriate (e.g. CSV files).
5. You have the right to withdraw your consent for further processing at any time, either by opting out of future emails or by contacting us directly at [dpo@wspc.com](mailto:dpo@wspc.com).

We may use the information that you provide or that we collect for the following purposes:
to register you with this Website, to set up and manage your World Scientific account, and to administer our products and services;
to provide any services or information you have requested;
to notify you about our products or special offers that may be of interest to you, customer services and profiling your purchasing preferences;
to ensure that content from this Website is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;
for detecting and protecting against error, fraud or other criminal activity;
to enforce our terms and conditions of use, including carrying out security reviews to validate your identity, contact details, and financial information; or
in any other way as described to you at the time of collection of your Personal Information.
When you sign up for our newsletter or marketing updates, we will notify you of essential service information and any alerts you have requested. You may also, from time to time, receive information on products, services and offers available to you from World Scientific.
If you do not want us to contact you with details of our products, services and special offers, that we think you might be interested in, please unsubscribe by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link on the email from World Scientific or by contacting us directly at unsubscribe@wspc.com

As a registered user, you can access your account information and make corrections or updates upon log in at any time. The accuracy of such information is solely the responsibility of the user. You may also request access to other Personal Information about you that may be held in the Service.
If you request to deactivate your account or delete your Personal Information, please email us at dpo@wspc.com.sg. We will endeavor to fulfill your request but some Personal Information may persist in backup copies for a certain period of time and may be retained as necessary for legitimate business purposes or to comply with our legal obligations.
Cookies
When we provide services, we want to make them easy, useful and reliable. Where services are delivered over the Internet, this sometimes involves placing small amounts of information on your devices. These include small files known as cookies. They cannot be used to identify you on a personal level.
This information is used to improve services to you through the following examples:
* keeping track of your current shopping session to personalize your experience and so that you may retrieve your shopping basket at any time
* enabling a service to recognize your device so you don't have to give the same information several times during one task
* recognizing that you may already have given a username and password so you don't need to do it for every web page requested
If you do not accept cookies you will be unable to use this Website.
