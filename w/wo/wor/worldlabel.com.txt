Worldlabel's Privacy Policy
Information We Collect
We collect and store various types of information depending upon how you use our site. For example, we collect information you provide when you place an order, register with us, or send us an e-mail or call us. The information provided to us will not be sold or distributed in any way. This information may include your:
Name
Phone #
E-mail Address
Mailing Address
Billing Address
Credit Card Information - protected by industry-leading 256-bit SSL encryption
Cookies - A cookie is a small piece of information sent by a website that is saved on your hard disk by your computer's browser. It holds information a site may need to interact with you and personalize your experience.
Our Disclosure of Your Information
Any customer information we gather will NEVER be sold or shared with our affiliates, partners, or any other third-parties.
Your Account
By using this site, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account and password and for restricting access to your computer. You agree to accept responsibility for all activities that occur under your account or password. Worldlabel.com reserves the right to refuse service, terminate accounts, remove or edit content, or cancel orders at our sole discretion.
Security Measures
Providing a secure shopping environment is one of Worldlabel.com's highest priorities. When our registration/order form asks you to enter personal information (such as your credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software currently available in the industry - up to 256-bit SSL high-grade encryption schemes depending on your browser settings (128-bit standard) provided by Thawte Consulting.
How to Contact Worldlabel.com
If you have any questions or concerns regarding purchasing online, please feel free to call us and speak with a customer service representative directly to place your order. We can be reached from 9am - 9pm EST at 1-877-955-2235 or by filling out a support request.
