MojoSupreme.com - Free Daily Videos, Free Contests, Top 10 Lists, Blogs and much more
English version:
Mojo Supreme owns and operates a number of web properties, including but not limited to MojoSupreme, BloggerMojo, StreetMojo, WatchMojo, TenMojo, GuideMojo, AxeMojo, WhyMojo, RawMojo.
At present time, the only time you will be asked any information is on StreetMojo, where you can a) create a profile to get access to thousands of contests and prizes and b) receive emails should you choose to receive emails from us. We will never sell that email address to anyone, ever.
On all other sites owned and operated by Mojo Supreme, neither are you asked any information nor do we maintain records of any surfing patterns.
In the future we might offer readers to sign up and create a profile so they can set their preferences, but this is not the case now.
Eventually, we might serve ads using third party ad servers and tags, when you see ads on the site, you can access the Privacy Policies below from our ad serving partners for more information. If you have any questions, just email us.
