Privacy Policy - Waleg.com
Waleg.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our website commitment to your privacy. The following statements disclose our information gathering and dissemination practices for Waleg.com.
We use your IP address to administer our Web site. IP addresses are not linked to anything personally identifiable. Visitors to Waleg.com are anonymous.
Waleg.com contains links to other web sites. We provide the addresses of these web sites as an avenue of information for our visitors and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these web sites.
We have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and would like to know your options in relation to not having this information used by these companies, click here.
To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit networkadvertising.org
If you have any questions about this privacy statement and/or our practices involving your privacy, feel free to contact us for any inquires.
