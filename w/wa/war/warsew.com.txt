Privacy Policy | WarSew
WarSew (aka www.warsew.com), a World Wide Web site owned and operated by WarSew ltd, recognizes its readers rights to privacy.
Storing information
WarSew doesn’t collect any information about its visitors. The only stored information is IP address of readers who submit a comment to particular article. These records are strictly confidential and will not be reproduced, sold or misused in any other way. Storing IP address is strictly for safety reasons, so we can determine eventual attacker or spammer.
This site does not sell, rent, lease or allow any third parties to view or use the information collected. WarSew does not maintain any mailing lists and does not send unsolicited emails.
Links
This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. When you leave our websites we encourage you to read the destination site’s privacy policy.
Cookies
Currently we assign cookies to our readers in order to save their preferences. This data is not shared with any third party. Accessing our websites is not dependent on accepting cookies and all major browsers allow you to disable cookies if you wish. Many of our advertisers use cookies in order to determine the number of times you have seen an advertisement. This is done to limit the number times you are shown the same advertisement. We do not have access to this data.
Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: cober@cober.si
