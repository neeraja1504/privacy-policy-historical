Privacy Statement
WAND, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy of our customers and users. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for WAND.com and all other applicable WAND Directory Network partner sites.
Your IP address is used to help identify and log your session information. We use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, to save passwords so no re-entry is required each time you visit our site, and for other Web browser related purposes.
Our sites' registration form asks users to give us contact information such as name, phone number and e-mail address. We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company, the services we offer and relevant messages from our partners. Customer's contact information is also used for contact with regards to technical issues or information maintenance when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving non-technical mailings; please see the choice/opt-out section below.
This site contains links to other sites and locations. WAND, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other such Web sites.
Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor's contact information such as name, phone number e-mail address. Contact information from the order form is used to send orders, information about our company and relevant messages from our partners. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
An outside company is used to display ads on our site(s). These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, our ad company may also collect cookies received with banner ads, and we do not control, nor do we have access to or usage of this information.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control and will make every effort to maintain the security and privacy of that information.
Supplementation Information
This site supplements the information that you provide with information that is received from third parties.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from our WAND Directory Network partners and us.
Removal/Discontinuance of Service
This site gives users the following options for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive services from our company.
Send e-mail to info@wandinc.com with your request
Call the following telephone number: 303-623-1200 x450
Correct/Update
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.
Send e-mail to info@wandinc.com with your request
Call the following telephone number: 303-623-1200 x450
