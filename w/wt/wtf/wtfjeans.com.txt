Privacy Policy
Our customers and web site visitors are our most valuable asset. Information submitted to WTFJeans will never be sold or shared!
In the name of WTFJEANS DOO, we commit ourselves that we’re going to protect the privacy of our buyers. We’re collecting only necessary basic data, necessary for our business development and for informing our customers in line with good business practices and in order to provide quality services.
We give customers an option including the decision of whether or not they want to be on the mailing lists used for marketing campaigns. All information about customers is strictly confidential and available only to employees to whom it is necessary to perform the job. All employees in WTFJEANS DOO are responsible for compliance with the principle of protection privacy.
We may need to disclose personal information when required by law, because we think that is necessary to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on our Web site.
A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user’s computer tied to information about the user. We use session ID cookies and persistent cookies. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie is terminated. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user’s hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser help file directions. By setting a cookie on our site, users would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If users reject the cookie, they may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user will be limited in some areas. Persistent cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance our website's user experience. Like most standard Web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information. IP addresses are tied to personally identifiable information to enable our Web-based service.

Payment
All payments are securely handled via Banka Intesa. Once you submit your payment, you will be redirected to secure website of Banca Intesa, where you can pay with Visa, Master Card and Maestro.
When entering your payment card details, confidential information is transmitted over a public network in a secure (encrypted) form by using SSL and PKI systems, as the most cryptographic technology.
Data security, when shopping, is guaranteeing by payment card processor, Banca Intesa at Belgrade, so because of that the complete process of collection is on the bank site. Not even for a moment your payment card data will be available to our system.
All payments will be effected in Serbian currency - dinar (RSD). The amount your credit card account will be charged for is obtained through the conversion of the price in Dollar into Serbian dinar according to the current exchange rate of the Serbian National Bank. When charging your credit card, the same amount is converted into your local currency according to the exchange rate of credit card associations. As result of this conversion there is a possibility of a slight difference from the original price stated in our web site.
