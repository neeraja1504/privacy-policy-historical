Cookies
PASSIONSFABRIKKEN APS may use cookies on the Websites it operates. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer's hard drive by our servers to identify your computer to our servers. No personally identifiable information stored in the LUUUX cookies will be shared with outside companies for any purpose without your permission.
Minors and Children
LUUUX is intended for audiences of 15 or over and you are not permitted to register on the LUUUX site if you are under the age of 15. If you between the age of 15 and 18 years and not an emancipated minor, you should always consult your legal parents or guardian and get their permission before submitting personal information.
