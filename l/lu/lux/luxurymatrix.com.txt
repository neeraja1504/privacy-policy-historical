| While visiting LuxuryMatrix.com, we may collect personal information that is needed to process on-line transactions and maintain status within product lists. We do not share or sell information collected while on our site to third parties outside of LuxuryMatrix.com who may want to offer their own products or services. LuxuryMatrix.com prohibits the use of web technology, which collects user-identifying information such as extensive lists of previously viewed sites, email addresses, or other information to identify or build profiles on individual visitors to LuxuryMatrix.com. Personal information is gathered and maintained while visiting our site and is stored within a temporary “cookie.” We employ the use of “cookies” to store information needed for the shopping cart process. This is a temporary “cookie” that is destroyed once the browser is closed. We reserve the right to change this privacy statement, as we deem necessary. |
