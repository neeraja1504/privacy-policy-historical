Privacy policy | Lynx Edicions
In keeping with Spanish Law 15/1.1999 dated December 13th, in relation to the Protection of Private Information, we hereby inform you that when you give your personal information to Lynx Edicions, these details are stored in an electronic database. The Data Protection Agency knows of the existence of our client database and Lynx Edicions furthermore guarantees that all reasonable measures have been implemented as permitted by current technology to guarantee protection against loss, inappropriate use, alteration, illegitimate intrusion, and deletion of the private information in our possession.
Our only purpose for keeping your personal data in our database is to send you information regarding our publications. You may exercise your right to rectify or cancel any of the information in our files by contacting our Customer Service Department. Lynx Edicions does not sell or transfer private information to third parties.
