Look & Listen - Privacy Policy
Look and Listen is committed to protecting the user's privacy.
The privacy policy herewith applies to all web pages related to this website.
All user information gathered on the website is used to personally identify the user on the site for the subscribed service for which the forms are completed.
The information will not be sold or made available to anyone. The information will not be used for anything outside of the stated uses within the Terms and Conditions of this website.
Certain information such as pages visited, type of products accessed, type and quantity of purchased products may be gathered for market related research only.
