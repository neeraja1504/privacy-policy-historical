Privacy Policy
Welcome to Loopy Gadgets. Com, We are a dedicated online gadget magazine which showcase the latest consumer electronics, Gizmos and Gadgets. More on LoopyGadgets.com >>
Privacy Policy
Loopy Gadgets does not collect personal information from any of our visitors and members.
If you are a member, in the process of registration you may have been asked for your name, email address and possible a date of birth.
If you are a Guest, when leaving a comment you will be ask for a Name and Email Address.
You are not obligated to providing your full name and your email address is only collected because it is required by the WordPress Platform that runs the processes on our site.
We do not use the information collected here in any way, if you subscribe via email for updates, updates are sent out automatically by Feedburner.
We will continue to give our readers the latest consumer electronics and gadget news on a daily basis.
