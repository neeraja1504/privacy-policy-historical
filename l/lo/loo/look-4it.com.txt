LOOK-4IT.COM is committed to protecting your privacy online. The following describes our commitment to the privacy of your information, the information we gather, and how that information is used.

Information Collected on the Web Site
LOOK-4IT.COM does record and track IP addresses , referral website , and pages viewed for statistical purposes. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
