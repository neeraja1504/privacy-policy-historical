Logo Templates - create a logo with great logo designs
"logo-template.com" respects the privacy rights of our online visitors and customers, and is committed to protecting the personal information collected about you.
Any email that you send to "logo-template.com" will be considered confidential to within the company and its affiliates, unless you have expressly provided us with permission to forward the email to unaffiliated parties.
In order to fulfill the services ordered by you, "logo-template.com" only asks for the information necessary to complete your order.
Your personally identifiable information will never be sold or shared by "logo-template.com" with any affiliated or unaffiliated third parties, except in unusual circumstances as required by law.
This site provides links to other Web sites that are not controlled, maintained, or owned by "logo-template.com", and "logo-template.com" is not responsible for the content of those Web sites, and reliance on that information should only be undertaken after an independent review of its accuracy.
