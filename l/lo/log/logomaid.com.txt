What does this privacy policy cover
This privacy policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information collected through a website owned and operated by Global Solution Partners. Use of personal information
Global Solution Partners needs certain personal information if you wish to purchase our products, receive our newsletter, or use certain services we provide at this web site.This information may include your personal contact information or your company contact information. Global Solution Partners will use this information in order to reply your inquiries, provide you with requested products and services or contact you regarding new products and services.By accessing the services of Global Solution Partners and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.Global Solution Partners may disclose your personal information when:
• we need to respond to court orders or such other legal process
• we need to protect the personal safety of the users of our websites or defend the rights or property of Global Solution Partners
• we find that your actions on our web site violate terms of use or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services
• we sell all our business or if Global Solution Partners is acquired by, or merged with another entity
Global Solution Partners retains your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes identified above or as required by law.
