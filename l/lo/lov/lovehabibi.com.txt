Privacy Policy - LoveHabibi
We respect your privacy. We will never give any of your personal information to anyone without your express permission and we will never send you spam of any kind. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our terms of service.
Emails from LoveHabibi
We may occasionally email you about new LoveHabibi features or to tell you about what's going on at LoveHabibi. If you wish, you can access the privacy settings for your email address specifically through Options tab once you are logged in, and adjust your email settings to receive only those things that you want, or nothing at all.
Automated technical information & cookies
As virtually every website in the world does, we collect automated technical information about how users interact with the website or save "cookies" to enable certain website features. This includes but is not limited to Internet protocol (IP) address, computer and connection information such as browser type, version, and time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system, and platform. This information is not personally identifiable by us and we only use it to track and improve our service.
Member interactions
We also encourage our users to be mindful of their privacy and not to disclose their personal information to other members thoughtlessly on the website, including names, addresses, telephone numbers, email addresses or instant messenger nicknames. Please note that LoveHabibi does not monitor communications between members on the website and cannot be held liable for the content of such communications.
If you have any questions or concerns about privacy at LoveHabibi, please contact us.
