Website Privacy Policy | Love Fate Destiny
This is the web site of Love Fate Destiny.
We can be reached via e-mail at contact us
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes IP address.
We collect the domain name and e-mail address (where possible) of visitors to our Web page, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit.
The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web page.
All pictures, quotes and stories are under the ownership of their respective owners and if you are the owner of them and wil like them to be removed from the site or wish to have them credited to you, please contact us.
We use third-party advertising companies such as those from Google to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit www.networkadvertising.org
With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us.
