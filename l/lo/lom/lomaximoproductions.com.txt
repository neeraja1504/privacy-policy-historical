Any Content uploaded by you to Lomaximo Productions Corp. becomes published Content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy.
Lomaximo Productions Corp. uses IP addresses to measure, analyze, and identify usage and trends, administer the website and collect information for its databases.
A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. Cookies identify you for our database, but provide no information other than that which you have already provided Lomaximo Productions Corp.. You may reject or delete the cookie, however, you may not be able to access your uploaded files if you have not already created an account. Cookies also can enable Lomaximo Productions Corp. to track and target your interests to enhance your experience on our website.
You may review, alter or delete personal information or account information on your Account page.

These websites or services may collect personally or non-personally identifiable information about you and place a cookie on your browser.
