Privacy
Labome.Org provides free searching services. We do not collect personal information about you when you visit Labome.Org website unless you choose to provide that information to us.
Information Collected and Stored Automatically
Of the information we learn about you from your visit to Labome.Org website, we store only the following: the IP address from which you access the Internet, the date and time, the Internet address of the Website from which you linked directly to our site, the names of files or words you searched, and the browser used to access our site. This information is used to measure the number of visitors to various sections of our site and identify system performance or problem areas. We also use this information to help us expand the coverage of reagents and other information, and to make the site more useful. Occasionally, we provide this information to third party entities to generate better and more timely antibodies and other products, or provide better content.
Cookies
A "cookie" is a small file that a web site transfers to the hard disk of your computer allowing our server to "remember" specific information about your session. We use cookies in Labome.Org website to speed up your search. The only information in our cookies is an ID number if your browser is set to accept cookies.
Personally Provided Information
If you choose to provide us with personal information by sending an email, or by filling out a form with your personal information and submitting it through our Web site, we use that information to complete your request. Your contact information may be shared with third parties, if we consider such third parties may be able to provide products or services that fulfill your requests.
Links to Other Sites
We provide links to other Internet sites that provide various information. Once you link to another site, you are subject to the privacy policy and copyright of the new site.
