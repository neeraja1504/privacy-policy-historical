Privacy Policy
This privacy policy is about how we use and protect any information that you give to LazyNinja.in
We take atmost care to protect your information which you share with us. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.
This privacy policy is active from 1st Jan 2013. It may change time to time but our basic terms will not change.
What we collect
We may collect the following information:
name
contact information including email address
demographic information such as postcode or zipcode, city, state
other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers
What we do with the information collected?
The information is collected to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and for the following reasons:
For improving our products and services
For keeping track of all the users of website
We may periodically send promotional emails about new products, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting to you
From time to time, we may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. We may contact you by email, phone, fax or mail. We may use the information to customise the website according to your interests.
