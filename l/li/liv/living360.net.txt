Privacy Policy
Living360.net does not collect email addresses and therefore will never sell, rent or share your personal information.
Living360.net will use cookies, pixel trackers, server logs, and any Internet technology in order to measure the effectiveness of the ourservice and any Advertising campaigns as well as to target meaningful content and Ads to our users.
Living360.net uses DoubleClick, Google, and other third-party Ad Servers to serve and display Advertisements on Living360.net. Living360.net does not provide personally identifiable information to Ad Servers. These Ad Servers and Advertisers may use cookies, pixel trackers, or other internet technology to improve the effectiveness and targeting of Advertisements to you within Living360.net and any other place where that third-party is serving Advertisements. Living360.net does not have access to, nor control over, advertisers’ or ad service providers’ cookies or how they may be used or how they were collected. In many cases, this information could be used to show you ads based on your interests on other Web sites. ****
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us by info@living360.net.
