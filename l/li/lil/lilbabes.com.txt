Privacy Policy / Lil' Babes
Lil' babes website is a free service for surfers that does not require registration or the submission of any personal information to use the website. Some extra functionality of the web site requires a free registration.
Cookies
The web site utilizes a standard technology called "Cookies" to collect information about how our website is used.
Information gathered through cookies may include date and time of visit, pages viewed, time spent at the website, and other websites visited just before and just after our website. This information is not shared or sold to third party organizations for any purpose.
Registration
Some extra functionalities of the web site require a free registration. The website only collects member name, password and an e-mail address and stores it in the database, to uniquely identify a member. This information is not shared or sold to third party organizations for any purpose. E-mail address is never disclosed anywhere on the site.
Registered users may choose to post comments at different parts of a web site. These comments become a part of the web site's publicly viewable content. The information that is disclosed is the name of the member, the date and time posted and the content of his/her comment. All other information regarding a member's comment is not shared or sold to third party organizations for any purpose.
Registered users may also rank models. The information on members' votes is aggregated and shown on a website. The information on how a member ranked a model is not shared or sold to third party organizations for any purpose.
