Lipseys.com Privacy Policy
LIPSEY’S Privacy Policy
Lipsey’s wants to take this opportunity to inform you of our privacy policy. We know that you are concerned about how information about you is used and shared. We appreciate your trust in us and we take this matter seriously. By visiting www.lipseys.com, you are accepting the practices described in this policy.
What Information Does Lipsey’s Gather?
The information we learn from our customers helps us personalize and continually improve your experience with Lipsey’s. We retain information that you provide to us which includes information on a credit application, federal firearms license, and order history. Just like other websites, we use “cookies” to personalize your shopping experience on our site. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser. Cookies allow you to take full advantage of our personalized features; we recommend that you leave them turned on. Please take notice that Lipsey’s does NOT share this information with any third parties and you are fully protected by sharing it with us. Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others.
Does Lipsey’s Share E-mail Address Lists?
Lipsey’s does NOT share, sell, or make public the e-mail addresses that we receive. If at any time you wish to be removed from our e-mailing list, please notify us at firearms@lipseys.com and we will have you promptly removed.
How Secure Is My Information?
We work to protect the security of your orders during transmission by using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts information you input. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and to your computer. Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer.
Conditions of Use, Notices, and Revisions
Unless stated otherwise, our Privacy Policy applies to all information that we have about you and your account. We stand behind the promises we make, and will not change our policies and practices to make them less protective of customer information collected in the past without prior notification to you.
