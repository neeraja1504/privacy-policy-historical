Privacy Policy
LifesDream.org knows that your privacy is important to you and recognizes your right to maintain your privacy on the web and beyond.
We guarantee that no private information about you will be shared with any third party unless LifesDream.org has reason to believe you are causing interference or injury to LifesDream.org, its affiliates or agents, in which case information will only be used for legal purposes.
LifesDream.org may share aggregate demographic information about users to advertisers and others for the purposes of customizing ads for the site. For example, LifesDream.org might say that x% of users live in the Europe, but no personal information will be revealed.
LifesDream.org contains links to other sites. LifesDream.org is not responsible for the privacy, the practices, or the content of such web sites.
Your e-mail address is used exclusively for sending you messages or for contacting you. Forms you fill out to interact with our company may request that you give us sensitive information such as your e-mail address. LifesDream.org will not share your e-mail address with any third party.
Cookies are used on our site. It is necessary to detect traffic statistics and to provide automatic personalization, which makes the usage of our service much easier. We do not use cookies to receive other information. Informaiton is used by us for service working.
By using the LifesDream.org web site you are consenting to the privacy policy as described. LifesDream.org reserves the right to change its privacy policy at any time and in the event that a change has occurred changes it will be posted on this page.
