Privacy Policy
Personal information
1. All the information that Likestool.com gather from the users are private and secured. Likestool.com only collect personal information from you when you provide it to Likestool.com, such as when you: (1) register; (2) place an order to use Likestool.com services; (3) participate in one of Likestool.com promotions; (4) or (5) contact to Likestool.com. The personal information that Likestool.com collect from you may include your name, e-mail address. Data regarding your visit(s) to Likestool.com and any use of Likestool.com resources are collected, which includes location data, weblogs, traffic data, and any other communication information.
Use of your information
2. Your information gives Likestool.com an opportunity to create and offer services for you. Here are the list of how Likestool.com use your data.
a. Your information will not be sold or used on any other site but Likestool.com.
b. When you make any request to Likestool.com you let Likestool.com use your information provided Likestool.com with, regarding to the Likestool.com products or services. If you give a permission to Likestool.com may also send information on products or services you may be interested in.
c. We may use data or let third parties use information that is not related to goods or services you may be interested in. This can be done only in the case of your permission.If you don't want to give consent for our site or third party you can use the opportunity to decline. If you decline once we will remove your details from any third party communications or mailings.
d. All your data that we have will be stored on secure servers. Transaction data is encrypted for your safety. Note: data via online transmission is not entirely secure. We cannot warrant total protection and security data, we only take all reasonable action to protect information sent to us online. Transmission of any data by you is at your own risk. You take the responsibility for the passwords safety and confidentiality.

Use of cookies
4. In some cases Likestool.com use cookies to collect mathematical information regarding to Likestool.com services and advertisers. There is no personal information share that could identify you. Any information that collected is for statistical use only about Likestool.com visitors and how they have used Likestool.com services. Likestool.com may collect information about your common internet use with a cookie file. The required information by the cookie helps Likestool.com to improve the website and services suggested to you. You have an opportunity to decline cookies by setting your browser options to decline all cookies. Remember: if you decline the download of cookies some features of Likestool.com may not work.
Information exposure
5. Likestool.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people without your permission except under the following conditions.
Likestool.com respond to legal processes, subpoenas, court orders, or to establish or exercise Likestool.com legal rights or defend against legal claims.
In case of selling any or all of Likestool.com business to a third party your information may be shared.
Children's privacy
6. Any child should always ask a parent for permission before sending personal information to Likestool.com, or anyone online for that matter. Likestool.com is not intended for anyone under the age of 18 with the exception of those who are 14 years of age or older and have obtained permission from their parent or legal guardian.
