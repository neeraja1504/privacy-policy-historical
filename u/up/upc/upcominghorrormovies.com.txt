Privacy Policy | UHM
UpcomingHorrorMovies understands and respects the rights to a visitor's privacy and thus have created this Privacy Policy page to better detail how personal information is used on this site.
What Kind of Personal Information is Collected?
Occasionally the site may hold a giveaway, where a reader has the option to enter their personal information (name, email, address) in a form to enter the giveaway, but aside from that no personal information is stored by the site. The info received for the giveaway is used specifically for the giveaway and nothing more.
Third Parties
UpcomingHorrorMovies uses third-party advertising companies to serve ads on this site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, you may visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
Disclaimer
The information provided in this Privacy Policy should not be construed as giving business, legal, or other advice. This page was simply created to inform the reader of how their information may be used. UpcomingHorrorMovies reserves the right to update this privacy policy whenever necessary and will note when it was last updated at the bottom of this page.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy you may contact us via form here or direct email at webmaster@upcominghorrormovies.com.
