Privacy Policy - uscity.net the Internet Business Directory for the United States, a National Network of State and City Directories
The following statement has been developed to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy.
This statement discloses the privacy practices for the uscity.net web site.
USCity.net collects personally identifiable information (name, e-mail address, etc.) and/or demographic data (state, zip code,  etc.) that you submit when your register for our services or make a purchase from one of our online stores, or send us an email. We and/or our 3rd party partners may use this information to process and maintain your account. Credit card information will be provided to third parties only to provide products and services that you order. We may use the contact information you provide to contact you about your account and/or to send you necessary information directly related to the product(s) or service(s) for which you have registered. We will not provide your contact information to unaffiliated third parties without your express permission. Some of the programs in uscity.net uses Cookies (small text files transferred from our Web site to your hard drive) to recognize repeat users,  and facilitate your access to and use of our site. Cookies do not damage files or allow anyone to access your personally identifiable information. They also provide information to us about site usage to help us improve and update our site. The security of the information you provide to uscity.net is important to us. We use encryption software and other security measures to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of sensitive information under our control. However, the uscity.net site includes links to other web sites, and when you choose to visit those web sites, we are not responsible for their privacy policies or how they treat user information. In addition, we may permit third parties to offer registration-based services through our site. We are not responsible for the actions and policies of these third parties, and you should review the privacy policies of these parties when supplying personally identifiable information to them. USCity.net reserves the right to alter our Privacy Policy as business needs require. Any changes will be posted to our web site in a timely manner. This statement and the policies outlined here are not intended to and do not give you any contractual or other legal rights.
