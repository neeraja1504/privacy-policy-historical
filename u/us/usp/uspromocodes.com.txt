Privacy Policy - USPromoCodes.com
Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy has been developed by USPromoCodes.com in order to notify our customers of our policies and demonstrate to you our serious commitment to privacy. Every time you visit or use this website, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. USPromoCodes.com takes user privacy seriously and takes appropriate measures to protect your privacy. For each visitor to our Web site, our Web server automatically recognizes only the domain name of the consumer, but not the e-mail address.
No one under 13, please! No part of this website is designed to appeal to children, and no one under 13 years old must disclose personal information on this site.
Learn more about protecting children's privacy online by visiting www.ftc.gov/kidzprivacy.
Please be aware, that when you click on a link such as "Click To Show Coupon Code" or "Redeem Coupon" you will be directed to the website of the advertiser.
We inform you that we do collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, as well as aggregate information about what pages consumers frequent, plus user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
The information obtained is later used to improve the content of our Web page, to notify consumers about updates to our Web site, and to contact consumers for marketing purposes. We guarantee that this personal information is never shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
We do not set any cookies.
To provide you with the most recent information that may be of interest to you, we have established business relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server of those companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
From time to time we may need to use customer information for some new, unanticipated uses not previously mentioned in our privacy notice. We claim that in case our information practices change at some time in the future we will let you know of these changes by posting the policy changes to our Web site so that you could opt out of these new uses. If you have concerns about how your information is used, check back at our Web site regularly.
