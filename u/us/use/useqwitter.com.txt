Twitter Counter (“TWC”) Privacy Policy
We collect public data about Twitter accounts, the e-mail addresses of those who
communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or
visit, and information volunteered by the consumer (such as survey information and/or site
registrations). The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web pages
and the quality of our service, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for
commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we
have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
• It is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action
regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to
the physical safety of any person, violations of Terms of Service, or as otherwise
required by law.
•   We transfer information about you if Twitter Counter is acquired by or merged with
another company. In this event, Twitter Counter will notify you as it may become
subject to a diﬀerent privacy policy.
!
Information Gathering and Usage
•   When you register for a paid Twitter Counter service we ask for information such as
your name, company name, email address, billing address, credit card information.
•   Twitter Counter uses collected information for the following general purposes:
products and services provision, billing, identiﬁcation and authentication, services
improvement, contact, and research.
•   Credit card information will never be stored by Twitter Counter or on TwitterCounter
B.V. servers, but by a third party payment provider.
!
Cookies
Twitter Counter uses cookies to collect data about usage of the Service and to improve the
Service. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web site's
computers and stored on your computer's hard drive. Twitter Counter may use persistent
cookies to improve user interaction with the Service and to gather information on usage of
any part of the Service, including but not limited to The Visitor Widget.
!
Widgets
Twitter Counter oﬀers several Widget is a small piece of code that can be installed and used
on third-party websites not related to Twitter Counter. Twitter Counter uses cookies to
collect and display data about users that visit third-party websites with The Visitor Widget
installed.
!
Data Storage
TWC uses third party vendors and hosting partners to provide the necessary hardware,
software, networking, storage, and related technology required to run Twitter Counter. TWC
owns the code, databases, and all rights to the Twitter Counter application.
!
Disclosure
TWC may disclose personally identiﬁable information under special circumstances, such as
to comply with subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Service.
!
Changes
TWC may periodically update this policy. We will notify you about signiﬁcant changes in the
way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address
speciﬁed in your Twitter Counter primary account holder account or by placing a prominent
notice on our site.
!
