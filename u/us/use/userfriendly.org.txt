UF - Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy of userfriendly.org
We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to us will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.
userfriendly.org does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including survey forms or email lists.
userfriendly.org occasionally analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the content available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
userfriendly.org launched a voluntary registration system for users in May 2000. Individuals must affirmatively request to join our mailing lists by signing up through a form on our web site. We will not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease these addresses on our list to anyone, though we will use this mailing list to keep members informed of upcoming events and to market User Friendly services and products.
If you have any additional questions, please contact us at info@userfriendly.org
