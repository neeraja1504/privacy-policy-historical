URL4SHORT.INFO - Privacy Policy
URL4SHORT.INFO : Free url redirection and url masking service Privacy Policy
URL4SHORT.INFO policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. URL4SHORT.INFO will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.
URL4SHORT.INFO will not sell or give your email address to anyone and you will never be placed on any list. However, URL4SHORT.INFO may disclose your information when URL4SHORT.INFO believes in good faith that the law requires it.
URL4SHORT.INFO may use your email address to inform you about URL4SHORT.INFO major service changes, important updates, or special promotions.
IP addresses: URL4SHORT.INFO website may record your IP address but uses it only for internal purposes. It will never be given to any other person/company/website for any reason.
Cookies: URL4SHORT.INFO website uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site. A cookie is a small piece of information used to identify an individual when the person is accessing a site. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason.
URL4SHORT.INFO reserve the right to change this Policy anytime without prior notification. In this case the new Policy will be published at privacy.html
If you have any question, please do not hesitate to contact us.
