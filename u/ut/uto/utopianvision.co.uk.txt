Privacy Statement
This privacy statement intends to make clear how any information that is entered or collected about you is used.
Information Collected
Any information that is collected about is taken purely for the purpose of providing you with an improved service (e.g. allowing you to login, send emails and ecards, remembering preferences etc.). Utopian Vision is the sole owner of this information and will not sell, share or rent this information in any way that differs from this statement.
Tell A Friend
When you use our Tell A Friend service to let someone know about our site, the information entered is not stored and is used solely for sending a one time email to your friend.
Your Email Address
If you have provided your email address, you should expect it to be used only in a request from you e.g. if you ask a question using our contact us form, our reply would be sent to your email address. Also, if you forget your password, information about resetting it will be sent to your email address.
External Links
Throughout our site we may have external links that take you to sites that are not under our control. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites and therefore also encourage you to read the privacy statements of these sites.
Contacting Us
If you have any questions regarding this statement or anything else regarding this site please use our Contact Us Form
