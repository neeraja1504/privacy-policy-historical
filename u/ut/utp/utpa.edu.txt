The University of Texas-Pan American
Privacy Notice
The University of Texas-Pan American website(www.utpa.edu) is provided as a service to the students and employees of UT-Pan American, and to the general public. The following three items pertain to pages within the www.utpa.edu domain (those pages starting with www.utpa.edu).
1. Cookies are information stored on your computer at the request of various sites. If you have security setup to detect them before writing, you may be aware of them. Our use of cookies is limited to providing you with a distinct session within our site, and we do not collect or use this information in any way.
2. Online forms requesting personal information are used to respond to specific requests. Your request may be redirected to another office in order to provide you with the information that you seek.
3. Any log information collected is used for analysis and statistical purposes. This information will not be released to any outside parties unless legally required to do so.
