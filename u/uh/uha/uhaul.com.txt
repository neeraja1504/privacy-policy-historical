U-Haul: Privacy policy
Privacy Policy
When you use this Web Team Associates, Inc. website, you trust us with information about you. Specifically, you may provide us with information that is directly connected to your name or personally identifiable information (“information”). Your privacy is important to us, and we believe it is important to share with you how we handle your information. Please take the time to read this privacy notice carefully, and let us know if you have any questions. This notice addresses the following:

Contact Information
This notice applies solely to information collected by this website, except where stated otherwise. This notice does not apply to third parties. If we are required to contact you about your information, we reserve the right to do so by e-mail, telephone, or mail.
This site may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects information.

The particular information collected may depend on the nature of how you choose to interact with us and what services you use. The data we may collect includes:
Name

Device / Browser Information

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 and if we learn that we have received information from a visitor under the age of 13, we will delete this information in accordance with applicable law.
How We Use Information
We collect information for fulfilling orders, providing services, and/or improving products and services. Examples of how we use information we collect include:
Reserve equipment and/or storage space, services, and/or products
Purchase and/or use certain products or services
Compliance with any contract for the rental of equipment and/or storage space and/or the purchase of products and services
Communicate with you, provide services or information you request, respond to your comments, questions and requests, serve you content and/or promotions, and send you notices
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address and email address are used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information

We share information with our business partners and service providers. Examples of whom we may share information with include:
U-Haul International, Inc., its parent, affiliates, related corporations and specifically its wholly owned subsidiaries, the “U-Haul Co” Rental Companies and related corporations and their affiliates/business partners, U-Haul Co. Rental Companies dealers, U-Haul Self Storage Affiliates, and Moving Helpers
Vehicle repair and/or tow truck companies that may provide breakdown/road side assistance
Payment card issuers, banks, credit reporting and fraud checking agencies, debt collection agencies, and insurance companies
Transportation or driver licensing authorities, directly or through intermediary organizations
Government or private organizations responsible for the processing or handling of transportation, tolls, traffic/moving or parking related violations
Government, judicial, regulatory and law enforcement agencies where the disclosure is required or authorized by law
In the event Web Team Associates, Inc. or substantially all of its assets are acquired, customer information will be one of the transferred assets.
We may permit our agents, vendors, consultants, and other service providers to access information collected through our website to carry out work on our behalf. These third party service providers are prohibited from using information obtained in this manner for any purpose(s) other than to provide the services we have engaged them to perform.
We reserve the right to disclose your information if required or authorized to do so by law, or in the good faith belief that such action is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, respond to claims, or protect the rights, property or safety of our company, employees, customers, or the public.

How you can access or control your personal information will depend on which services you use. If you have created an online account, you can view or edit your information by accessing your account online. Otherwise, you may also contact us with questions or complaints about our use and/or disclosure of your information. A request that we not use or disclose your information may affect our ability to provide you with our products and/or services. Further, we reserve the right to retain data as required by applicable law; and for so long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which the data is retained except to the extent prohibited by applicable law. Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies and similar technologies like pixel tags to track customer visits through our site and improve your browsing experience. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device which is used to store information. If your browser accepts cookies, this information enables us to save you time when returning to the site by remembering who you are and identifying your preferences.
Cookies also provide a smoother shopping experience by helping us to uniquely identify shopping carts and nearby locations. Cookies cannot profile your system or collect information from your device. We do not use cookies to store payment card numbers or any other personal identification. You may reset your browser to clear cookies or choose to set your browser to block all cookies, including cookies associated with our site, but know that some of the site’s functionality may not function properly.
Online Tracking
Our website uses on-premises and third-party web analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to help us analyze how visitors use the website. To learn more about opting out of data collection through Google Analytics, click here.
Certain third parties may collect Personal Information about your online activities over time and across different websites when you use our website. Your browser settings may allow you to automatically transmit a “Do Not Track” signal to websites and online services you visit.  Like many websites and online services, we do not alter our practices when we receive a “Do Not Track” signal from a visitor’s browser.
Security
We maintain appropriate safeguards to help protect personal information collected through the website from theft, loss, misuse, or other unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Although we use reasonable efforts to safeguard information, transmission via the Internet is not completely secure and we cannot guarantee the security of your information collected through the website.
Your California Privacy Rights
Under California’s "Shine the Light" law, California residents who provide personal information in obtaining products or services are entitled to request and obtain from us once a calendar year information about the customer information we shared, if any, with other businesses for their own direct marketing uses. If applicable, this information would include the categories of customer information and the names and addresses of those businesses with which we shared customer information for the immediately prior calendar year.
