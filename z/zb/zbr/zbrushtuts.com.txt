Privacy Policy – zbrushtuts
This Privacy Policy describes the information collected by Zbrushtuts  (“zbrushtuts”  “we”, “us” or “our”) through our email subscription and how we use that information.
We Collect Limited Information With Our email subscription.
When you subscribe to zbrushtuts we require you to provide limited information ie email adress and we don’t collect any information except email adress of person. And subscripbed email are for those persons who wish to receive regular updates on tutorials, news or other zbrush content and also to receive our newsletter. With those email addresses used solely to send the newsletter.
When You Request Support, You May Send Us Email
We allow users to contact us if they have support questions about content of website. Or if they wanted their tutorials or other related content on our website.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
We use Support Information only for the support for the internal operations of our website. We don’t rent, sell or share your email address.
