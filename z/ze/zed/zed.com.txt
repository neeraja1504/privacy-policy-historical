According to the Organic Law 15/1999 of 13 December on the Protection of Personal Data and to the Act 34/2002 of July on Information Society Services and Electronic Commerce, Zed communicates to the USERS its Data Protection Policy: Zed does not collect any data from USERS unless they voluntarily request any of the services included in sections as ?Contact?, ?Careers?, or others. The personal data collected will be treated confidentiality and with the sole purpose of providing the requested Services.
The USER is hereby giving his/her consent to Zed for the processing of his/her personal data in a data file owned, managed and under the responsibility of Zed for the purposes of answering their questions, doubts or queries. The personal data file has been already communicated to the Spanish Data Protection Registry. USERS can exercise their rights of access, cancellation, rectification or opposition to their personal data by sending an e-mail to the following address: unsubscribe@zed.com to Zed including: (i) Name, surname and copy of its personal identification card and, if acting on behalf of another person, the appropriate authorization in this regard; (ii) Purpose of the request; (iii) Address for notification and (iv) Date and signature of the USER.
In addition, the USER has the responsibility of updating or modifying his/her personal data, following the instructions of the previous paragraph.
