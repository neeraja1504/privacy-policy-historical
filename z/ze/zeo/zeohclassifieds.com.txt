My Crazy Email: Privacy Policy
If you share a Crazy Email with MyCrazyEmail.net  you can feel confident that your personal information will never be shared or displayed. That means your name and email address will never be shared or displayed.
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact me by email at den@mycrazyemail.net.
Also, if you have an interesting topic that you would like to share with the world go ahead and forward it to me den@mycrazyemail.net .  And don't worry your privacy will always be protected.
Comments Always Welcome!
Oh and
At MyCrazyEmail.net, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by MyCrazyEmail.net and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, MyCrazyEmail.net makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies and Web Beacons
MyCrazyEmail.net does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
DoubleClick DART Cookie
.:: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on MyCrazyEmail.net.
.:: Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to MyCrazyEmail.net and other sites on the Internet.
.:: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on MyCrazyEmail.net send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
MyCrazyEmail.net has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. MyCrazyEmail.net’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
