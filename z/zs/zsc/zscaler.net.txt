Zscaler Cloud Security : SaaS Web Security - Privacy Policy
| Use of Information Zscaler Gathers from Visitors:
Zscaler has a solid commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of our corporate web site.
Common Uses that Zscaler Employs:
On our Web site, we may ask you for your name, address and email information when downloading a white paper or downloading free software. We gather this information in order to send you information that you request, such as more information on a specific product, training or service and to update you when we have new products and services that might be of interest to you. We do not sell or trade your name to any independent third party.
Responses and Updates:
Zscaler will typically respond to email and other inquiries that it receives. Zscaler may retain such correspondence in order to improve its products, services and Web site and for other disclosed purposes.
Opt-out Policy:
