Personal Information
When you choose to register an account, or make a donation, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, address, city, country, zip code and phone number.
What do we do with this information?
We use the name and email address to send you information and updates, and to provide you with correspondence when necessary. We use this information to verify registration and/or product offerings. We use some of the optional information to help us provide statistics to guide LBW (i.e. who is visiting us and from where) so that we can better reach people and invite them to the use this web site.
What do we not do with the information?
We don't sell your identity to any third parties. We don't sell your information you place on our sites to any third parties.
Cookies and IP Addresses
There is additional information that can be gathered by an operating web site. One is the IP address (random numbers assigned to individual computers) of the machine you use to reach these sites. This information is used to help diagnose problems with our web site. Your IP address may also be used to block your access to areas of the site if we determine that you are abusing the sites resources.
Another way to gather information is to use a cookie (small text file transferred from our Web Site to your hard drive). Our web site uses cookies to determine if you are logged in and also to save your login information to speed up your access to the sites. Cookies do not damage files or allow anyone to access your personally identifiable information - they simply allow us to tailor information and advertisements to make your visits more enjoyable and meaningful. They also provide information to us about site usage to help us improve and update our site. We may share aggregate (not individual) site usage statistics and demographic information with advertisers and other third parties, but this information will not include any personally identifiable information and this practice is not currently undertaken.
