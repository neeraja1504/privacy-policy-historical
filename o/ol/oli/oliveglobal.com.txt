Olive E-Business Privacy Policies
This statement discloses the privacy practices for OLIVE E-BUSINESS. Please direct questions regarding this statement to Olive's Customer Support staff - support@oliveglobal.com
Information Collection
OLIVE E-BUSINESS recognizes your right to confidentiality and is committed to protecting your privacy. Olive e-Business is the sole owner of the information collected on http://www.oliveglobal.com. We will not give, rent, loan, or sell any identifiable personal information to any other third party, unless legally required to do so. We may store and process that information to better understand your needs and how we can improve our products and services. In addition, we may use that information to contact you.
What we do with the Information we Gather and Track
The personally identifiable information gathered from the forms filled out on our web site is: Used to provide you with information about Industry news, special events, and/or special features and offers suited for you and your field Used to improve the Oliveglobal.com service Used to gather broad demographic information about user trends
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. Some Olive e-Business pages use cookies. By setting a cookie on our site, users would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Cookies are used to better personalize the customer support provided by Olive e-Business.
Olive e-Business contains links to other Web sites. However, Olive e-Business is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
