Privacy Policy — Kim Udell.com
PRIVACY POLICY
Knowing how your information is used and shared online is important in today’s digital age. Our website (KimUdell.com) protects and respects your privacy and any information we collect about you when using this site will only be used as necessary to conduct business.
Who Are We
Our site, www.kimudell.com, is owned and operated by Kim Udell.
This Privacy Policy applies only to your use of KimUdell.com. It does not apply to any websites that are linked to from our site even if we provide those links. We do not have any control over how your data is collected, stored or used by other websites. Please check the privacy policies of these sites for a clear understanding about how they handle your data.
Information We Collect
Data such as cookies (see section below) are collected automatically by our site. Other data is collected voluntarily by you, such as via an opt-in form. Depending upon your specific use of our site, we may collect some or all of the following data:
- Name
- Business/Company Name
- Job Title
- Email Address
- Demographics
- IP Addresses (automatic)
Why We Collect This Information
We use this data to:
Provide you with the content we produce.
Update you on our products and service offerings.
Responding to communications from you.
Conducting market research.
Supplying you with email newsletters that you have subscribed to.
Notify you about other relevant products or services that would be useful to you.
Gather feedback to improve your overall experience with our business and our site.
Your information will never be used or shared in any other way.
We only keep your data for as long as we need to in order to conduct business with you; and as long as we have your permission to keep it.
Links to Other Sites
Our site may contain links to 3rd Party sites in our industry that may be helpful to you. This includes articles, images, videos, etc. We are not responsible for how these external sites use your data, so they are not covered in this privacy polity. Please check each site’s individual policies to ensure you understand how they operate in terms of privacy.
We will never sell or distribute your personal data to 3rd parties without your consent.
How We Use Cookies
A cookie is a file containing a specific identifier of letters and numbers that are sent by web servers to web browsers and is stored by the browser. This information is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.
Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies will remain valid until they are deleted or reached their expiration date. A session cookie, on the other hand, will expire when the web browser is closed.
We may use cookies to track and analyze traffic browsing data for each user; this helps us understand your needs and identify which pages you find the most useful to enhance our services and website.
Cookies do not typically contain any information that personally identifies a user, but personal information that we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.
Most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies even though they automatically accept them. You can edit your browser settings to block them, but this will impact the usability of some websites.
How to Control Your Data
You can unsubscribe from our email communications at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of the email. If you would like information about the data we have on you – or want us to delete your data completely – email us a request at: support@kimudell.com.
KimUdell.com may change this policy at any time, so feel free to check this page periodically to ensure you understand our policy going forward.
