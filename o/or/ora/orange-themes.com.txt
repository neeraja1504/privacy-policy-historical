Privacy Policy - MOOZ Themes
If you would like more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
At MOOZThemes.com, the privacy of our visitors is of utmost importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by MOOZThemes.com and how it is used.
Log Files
The MOOZThemes.com website makes use of server logging technology to produce log files which document very basic information about each visit. This information includes IP addresses, time of visit, and the browser in use.
Cookies
MOOZThemes.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
Facebook & Google Adsense Cookies
Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense & Facebook.
These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on MOOZThemes.com send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
MOOZThemes.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
Google Adwords & Facebook Ads
Google Adwords & Facebook Ads is a marketing and advertising platforms built and offered to the public by Google & Facebook. MOOZThemes.com may use Adwords or Facebook to promote products or articles. This service operates with the Adwords advertising network and the Doubleclick or Facebook Cookie. You can learn about opting out here: https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922 and here: www.facebook.com/policies/cookies/.
Google AdSense
Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google. MOOZThemes.com uses the “Doubleclick” Cookie, which tracks use of MOOZThemes.com and user behavior concerning ads, products and the services we offer. You can learn about opting out here: https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922.
Changes
We reserve the right to change this policy at any time.
