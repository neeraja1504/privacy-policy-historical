Welcome to Oriental Trading
Your Privacy Rights
We occasionally rent or exchange customer information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. However, we will not disclose a customer's information to third parties for the third parties' direct marketing purposes if the customer has exercised the cost free option that we make available in order to prevent that information from being disclosed to third parties for those purposes. In order to exercise that option to prevent such disclosure of your customer information cost free or to request information concerning list rental or exchange you may email us at donotshare@oriental.com. Please include your name and address information.
