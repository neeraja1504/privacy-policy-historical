The following Privacy statement details the practices and policies of OBOads.com, owner and operator of OBOads.com Website:
OBOads.com may collect any or all of the following information:
Your name
Your e-mail address
Your address
Your IP address
Any other information is voluntary
OBOads.com will never sell any of the above information to a third party without prior consent. We use this data to tailor the visitor's experience at our site to content that most closely matches individual interests. OBOads.com may require the use of cookies to store login session information.
Financial Informtion
We do not collect any financial information and no personal financial information is stored on OBOads web servers.
Opting-Out
Any OBOads users may opt-out of receiving regular e-mails by one of the following methods:
Responding to the email using the unsubscribe option provided;
Responding to the email;
Unchecking the appropriate box on the "Prefs / Options" page in the members area;
Terminating your membership - Contact support
