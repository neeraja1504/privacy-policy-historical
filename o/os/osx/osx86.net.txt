Privacy Policy - HackintoshOSX.com
We recognize the importance of your privacy. This policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it and how we safeguard your information. HackintoshOSX.com may be required to revise this Privacy Policy in the future to comply with any applicable European regulations, in order to utilise new technologies or to improve your HackintoshOSX.com experience, so please check back periodically.
When you access HackintoshOSX.com
HackintoshOSX.com uses cookies to store and collect information about your usage of the site in order to enhance your online experience and to help us improve the quality of our services.
When you access HackintoshOSX.com, your computer automatically sends us information such as the URL on our site you have visited, your IP address, your browser type and language, and the date and time of your request.
If you perform a search on HackintoshOSX.com, your searches are logged and may be used internally, so as to better personalise our service to you.
We use Invision Power Board software for the forum section of HackintoshOSX.com. Cookies are used on the forum to store your login details which are encrypted within the cookie. You can also delete cookies set by the forum.
When you Contact Us
If you contact us via email, your details and a history of the communication will be stored by us in order for us to deal with enquiries adequately and improve our customer support services.
Links and Affiliated sites
HackintoshOSX.com contains hypertext links to other sites, advertisers and other third parties. By clicking on any of these links, you are leaving the HackintoshOSX.com website. HackintoshOSX.com has no control over the information collected by any third party after you have left the HackintoshOSX.com website. HackintoshOSX.com encourages you to read their privacy policies.
The data collection and usage practices of any third party linked to HackintoshOSX.com are not covered by this Privacy Policy. HackintoshOSX.com assumes no liability and is not responsible for any practices of such third party web sites, including their privacy practices or content.
Protecting your Information
We use a secure server cluster situated in a secure data centre to protect all the information you provide us with. We have invested heavily in equipment and highly qualified staff to ensure all information you enter is protected from access by any third party and only necessary and restricted access is available internally. We also protect all our connections to the Internet using various security measures including firewalls and regular security audits on all our servers.
Contacting HackintoshOSX.com
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, you may e-mail: info at hackintoshosx dot com
