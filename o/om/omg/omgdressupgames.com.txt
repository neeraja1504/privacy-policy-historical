Privacy
Our Privacy
Omgdressupgames.com is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Statement explains what information we collect when you interact with our website.
No information is sold or released to anyone about visitors to Omgdressupgames.com without prior consent or a court order. In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first.
By accessing Omgdressupgames.com, you acknowledge and fully understand omgdressupgames's Privacy Statement and freely consent to the information collection and use practices described in this Website Privacy Statement.
Omgdressupgames.com does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that Omgdressupgames.com cannot always determine that a user is a child. Omgdressupgames.com does not maintain databases of children or adults.
Omgdressupgames.com collects certain information from and about its users from web server logs.When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded. Omgdressupgames.com does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order. Omgdressupgames.com archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors on the web site, and for security reasons. The log files are eventually deleted.
IP reports for advertisement clicks are shared with the advertiser. However, they usually have this information already since it is captured by their server when you view or click an ad. This is done to verify billing for the ads. No additional information associated with any specific user is provided to the advertisers.
We may track information about visits to administer the site and analyze its usage. Examples of information we may track include:
Your Internet protocol address.
The kind of browser or computer you use.
Number of links you click within the site.
State or country from which you accessed the site.
Date and time of your visit.
Name of your Internet service provider.
Referring website.
Pages you viewed on the site.
Overview
Omgdressupgames.com provides users with free access to our own dress-up and related games, as well as other similar games, hosted on omgdressupgames.com.
Your privacy is important to us. Omgdressupgames.com's service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed.
Omgdressupgames.com will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All personal identifiable information Omgdressupgames.com collects are only gathered with your voluntary act. Omgdressupgames.com keeps all information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances:
- When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process.
- When such action is necessary to protect and defend the rights or property of Omgdressupgames.com.
Omgdressupgames.com uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.
Omgdressupgames.com uses subscription forms for individuals to subscribe to its ezine . Omgdressupgames.com will use your email address to provide you with the ezine, which is usually mailed daily. Omgdressupgames.com will never send you any unsolicited email outside of the ezine.
Omgdressupgames.com does not and will not share, sell or rent your email address to outside third parties. Neither does it supplement your information with any outside third parties. The contents of Omgdressupgames.com's mailing list will only be disclosed due to law or court demands.
Unsubscribing from Omgdressupgames.com's ezine is simple and can be done by following the instructions at the end of every email sent.
