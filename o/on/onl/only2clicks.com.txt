Only2Clicks.com Privacy Policy
We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website. We do not process this information in a way that is incompatible with the purpose for which it has been collected or subsequently authorized by you. We collect the following types of information from our users:
Your computer and browser profile
Your "IP" address and/or Internet domain access point
Your name
Your country
Your e-mail address
The URLs (website address), name of the links and categories you've created within the system.
We will not turn over your IP address or any of your details to any third party unless absolutely required by law. Additionally, rest assured your email address is safe.
We have full rights to remove users from the system if they don't provide a valid email address, created by a 'bot' (not human), or abusing the system.
'Remember Me' option (using cookies) is enabled by default allowing quick access to the web site without re- log-in. If you are using public computer you can simply sign-out by clicking "change user" link on the top right corner.
Only2Clicks reserves the right to alter this privacy statement without prior notice. Please refer to this page for the latest available information regarding the privacy practices of Only2Clicks Web site.
If Only2Clicks were to ever change ownership, you will be notified and have the choice having your account deleted prior to the transfer.
If any of this concerns you, feel free to drop us a line at support@only2clicks.com
