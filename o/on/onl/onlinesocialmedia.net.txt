Privacy Policy : Online Social Media - Online Social Media
Advertisers
Third-party companies serve our advertisements, and these companies may use information – not including your email address, name, or telephone number – about visits you make to this and other websites. This is used to provide advertisements about goods and services you may find interesting. Click here to find out more about this practice, and to learn about your choices in not having companies use this information.
External Site Links
Online Social Media links to other sites across the Internet. The privacy policies and content on these sites are the responsibility of their respective owners.
IP Addresses and Logging
Our server logs information when you visit onlinesocialmedia.net, this information shows us details about the visitors to our site. This includes IP addresses, referring website, length of stay, date/time you visited, etc. The information is for statistics only.
Cookies
Online Social Media may use cookies to store session information; this is used to help you with some ad information and forms.
