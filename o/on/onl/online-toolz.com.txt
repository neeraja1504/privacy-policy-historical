Privacy - Online Toolz
Last updated: 01 Feb, 2011
What types of information do we collect?
All information collected by OnlineToolz is Anonymous information, meaning that it does not lead to your personal information ( ex: your name or your address ) it is just enough information to differentiate between different users accessing the website
What information do we collect specifically ?
Whenever you visit OnlineToolz, we collect the following:
Visitor Information
1- IP (Internet Protocol) address
2- The address of the last URL you visited prior to clicking through OnlineToolz
3- Your browser information (ex: Firefox, Chrome ..etc)
4- Your OS (Operating System) name/version
Do we use Cookies ?
No, but Third Party Services may do

Changes to Our Privacy Policy
