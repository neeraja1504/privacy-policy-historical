Protecting Your Privacy

Collecting Personal Information
We collect personal information to: (i) provide the various services offered via this website, including orders/reservations for services to be provided by Member Airlines and others, (ii) better understand your needs, and (iii) aggregate information with our Member Airlines and others for analysis. This information is collected from a variety of sources, including: (i) when you provide us with information, (ii) when your internet browser connects to our website, (iii) one world Member Airlines, and (iv) third parties who provide other services to you. By providing your personal information via this website you consent to its collection. The personal information we collect through these various sources may include, but is not limited to: (i) name, birth date, addresses and information from state issued identifications, including passports, (ii) email addresses, telephone, fax and pager numbers, (iii) credit card and frequent flyer account information, (iv) travel information, including flight selections, travel dates, other information associated with such travel and obtained via this website, including for accommodations, vehicle rentals, food and other selections, (v) nationality and country of residence, (vi) use of self-service devices, flight status notifications and online check-in, (vii) information provided via survey, focus group or other market research activity, and (viii) information provided to travel agents and/or customer service representatives.
Personal information collected will be retained until services obtained via this website are completed, which for those with a continuing relationship, e.g., an account or membership, will be until that account is closed or membership is ended, plus the period of time for which they must be retained to comply with applicable law and/or are subject to audit.
Residents of the European Union have certain rights under EU law to request access to “Personal Data” we collect and/or process, as well as to request modification, deletion or limitations on use or transfer of such information. If you are an EU resident and would like to make such a request, please contact privacyofficer@oneworld.com. Proof of identity and/or authority may be required.
Cookies
A “cookie” is file that a website can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your system. Such cookies are needed to facilitate the provision of certain functions of this website. By using this website, you consent to the receipt of such cookies.
Do Not Track Signals
This website does not respond to do not track signals.
Aggregate Information
When you visit this website, we may use cookies, tags and associated technologies to collect non-personal information such as your browser type, your operating system, the name of your internet service provider and the web pages you have visited. This aggregate information does not identify you personally. Information that does not identify you personally, including from which personally identifiable information has been removed, is not subject to the limitations on disclosure in these Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
Links
This website contains links to other sites. Please note that these Terms of Use and Privacy Policy apply only to information collected by one world via this website. one world is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other sites or by third parties.
Who Do We Share Your Personal Information With?
To respond to inquiries that you may send via this website, for example, with regard to availability of services, we may share your information with third-party processors that provide such information. To provide you with services that are provided by third parties, including our Member Airlines, we may share your personal information with such third parties and/or thirty-party processors that receive such data on their behalf, for example, that accept and make applicable airline reservations and/or process payments. Such parties are based in the various countries in which our Member Airlines, such third-party service providers and/or their third-party processors are based, including, without limitation, Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Chile, China, Colombia, Ecuador, Finland, Germany, Japan, Jordan, Malaysia, Morocco, Peru, Qatar, Russia, Spain, Sri Lanka, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Such parties may share your personal information in furtherance of the provision of those services. Each such party receiving such personal information may also use such personal information as specified in their own privacy policies (if any). In those cases, in which you identify yourself as part of a group or as an employee of an employer in connection with ordering services via this site, for example, by those making reservations or obtaining training via this website, we may share information regarding such use with such group and/or employer.
Charges and Credit Card Information
When you order services via this website, we may collect and process credit card information from you that will be shared as provided above. To pay for certain services ordered, please follow instructions provided. Credit card information will be handled and used in accordance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).
