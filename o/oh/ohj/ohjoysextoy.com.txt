Bucko – A Comical Murder-Mystery - Privacy Policy
Bucko.com, Darcomic.com and Ohjoysextoy.com and all related content, do not collect personal information without your knowledge or consent. Nor do we sell, rent or give personal information onto other party’s.
These websites all utilize advertisement, which uses advertising cookies. We do not control these common place cookies. These cookies contain information relevant to the access of our sites for advertisement purposes. You may opt out of this third-party vendor’s use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page. Currently Bucko.com only utilizes Google Adsence. Darcomic.org uses Project Wonderful, Google Adsence and ContextWeb’s ADSDAQ. And Ohjoysextoy.com uses Hiveworks Ads. You may also be able to opt out of these cookies at those respective links/sites.
We also count and follow the visitors activity while surfing our sites. This is for analytical purposes only, used only to see which comics are the most popular, which helps us then build better comics. This data is of a superficial nature, and we will never be able to identify or contact you with it.
All these websites are intended for mature audiences.
