Privacy - Out of Aces
Privacy Policy Statement: This is the web site of OutofAces.com, part of Plunder Media. We can be reached via email at info [at] plundermedia [dot] com. We collect information on consumers who browse our Web page and leave comments on articles, this is limited to email addresses, names, and IP numbers, we do not sell this information to any third parties. With respect to cookies: We do not set any cookies. If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us email at the above address. With respect to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information. If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above email address.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, check out
http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
