TonysAuction.com - Americana, Antiques, Picks, Free Auctions
Privacy Policy
TonysAuction.com is committed to User Privacy
The privacy of TonysAuction.com's users is of the utmost importance. Members can be secure in knowing their information shall not be openly divulged to any third parties not related or affiliated with the site, or applications within the site. TonysAuction.com reserves the right to divulge information to law authorities, affiliates, members and other entities to enhance and sustain the integrity of the site.
At TonysAuction.com, your information is considered private. We do not not release your private sales information unless forced by a court order, subpena or unless we have your prior consent. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices of this web site.
Our User Registration requires Users to give us contact information, or personally identifiable information such as their name, phone number, mailing address, registered username / password and email address. Our site does use cookies to track session information only and not to monitor user usage. Users may edit or update their registered personally identifiable information at any time by visiting the User Info page.
Each individual auction page includes a convenient feature called Email This Auction to a Friend. This feature allows the sender to give a direct link back to an auction page, which may contain items or services of interest to the referral recipient. TonysAuction.com does not view, collect or maintain any auction referral information.
This site offers forums such as chat rooms, news groups or message boards available to our Users. Any information disclosed in these areas becomes public domain, openly displayed for viewing by all visitors to our site. Users should exercise caution when sharing certain personal information within these areas. We do not monitor or control information exchanged between Users completing transactions at TonysAuction.com.
This site features security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of the information under our control. TonysAuction.com uses firewall-protected servers and SSL encryption, providing a secure data transfer and storage environment. Sensitive User Information given to TonysAuction.com such as credit card data is protected and stored within this secure environment, allowing no unauthorized access. Sensitive User Information under our control is accessed only by authorized personnel within TonysAuction.com, as requested or authorized by the User. We will continue to integrate new technologies and equipment to stay above the industry standard. However, there are always potential security risks when doing business on the Internet.
Users may opt-out of receiving communications from TonysAuction.com, simply by contacting our Customer Support. Users may custom arrange their auction event email notices by visiting the Auction Manager "User Setup" file and then opening their "Email Msg" folder.
