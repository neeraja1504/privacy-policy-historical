Web Site Usage Consent
Your use of the Your Mobile Phone Reviews UK Limited Web Site signifies your acceptance of our privacy policy & legal disclaimer terms.
Submitted Product Reviews
You do not have to register to submit product reviews for usage within the Your Mobile Phone Reviews UK Limited Web Site. All information collected is in no way passed to any other parties & is used exclusively within the relevant section of the Your Mobile Phone Reviews UK Limited Web Site (if approved), it is never supplied to third parties, individuals or other companies.
Personal Information
Your personal information is not shared in any way with any other third parties, individuals or companies.
Cookies & User Tracking
Cookies are implemented & issued in order for data to be captured for use with Google Analytics. For more detailed information on cookie usage with Google Analytics please visit the following link - Cookies & Google Analytics
Advertising Cookie Usage
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit the following link - Google Advertising and Privacy
