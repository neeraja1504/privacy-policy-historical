Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System understands that privacy online is important to users of Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System, especially when conducting business. This statement governs Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System's privacy policies with respect to those users of Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System who visit without transacting business and Visitors who register to transact business on Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System and make use of the various services offered by Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System.com.
"Personally Identifiable Information" refers to any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains, including, but not limited to, name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, financial profiles, social security number, and credit card information. Personally Identifiable Information does not include information that is collected anonymously (that is, without identification of the individual user) or demographic information not connected to an identified individual.
What Personally Identifiable Information is collected?
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System may collect basic user profile information from all Visitors. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System collects the following additional information from Authorized Customers: the names and email addresses.
How does Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System use Personally Identifiable Information?
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System uses Personally Identifiable Information to customize the Site, to make appropriate service offerings, and to fulfill buying and selling requests on Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System may email Authorized Customers about research or purchase and selling opportunities on the Site or information related to the subject matter of Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System may also use Personally Identifiable Information to contact Visitors and Authorized Customers in response to specific inquiries, or to provide requested information.
With whom may the information may be shared?
Unless required by law, Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System does not share any information with anyone.
How is Personally Identifiable Information stored?
Personally Identifiable Information collected by Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System is securely stored and is not accessible to third parties or employees of Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System.
What choices are available regarding collection, use and distribution of the information?
Authorized Customers may opt out of receiving unsolicited information from or being contacted by Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System by deleting their membership.
Are Cookies Used on Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System?
Cookies are used for a variety of reasons. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System uses Cookies to obtain information about the preferences of Visitors and the services they select. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System also uses Cookies for security purposes to protect Authorized Customers. For example, if an Authorized Customer is logged on and the site is unused for more than 10 minutes, This website will automatically log the Authorized Customer off.
How does Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System use login information?
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System uses login information, including, but not limited to, IP addresses, ISPs, and browser types, to analyze trends, administer the site, track a user's movement and use, and gather broad demographic information.
How does Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System keep Personally Identifiable Information secure?
All of Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System's employees are familiar with Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System's security policy and practices. The Personally Identifiable Information of Visitors and Authorized Customers is only accessible to a limited number of qualified employees who are given a password in order to gain access to the information. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System audits its security systems and processes on a regular basis. While Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System takes commercially reasonable measures to maintain a secure site, electronic communications and databases are subject to errors, tampering and break-ins, and Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System cannot guarantee or warrant that such events will not take place. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System will not be liable to Visitors or Authorized Customers for any such occurrences.
What happens if the Privacy Policy Changes?
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System will let Visitors and Authorized Customers know about changes to the privacy policy by posting such changes on the site. However, if Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System is changing the privacy policy in a manner that might cause disclosure of Personally Identifiable Information that a Visitor or Authorized Customer has previously requested not be disclosed, Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System will contact such Visitor or Authorized Customer to allow such Visitor or Authorized Customer to prevent such disclosure.
Links:
Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System contains links to other web sites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are moving to another web site. Your Cash Group - Advertising Revenue Rotator System encourages you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policies may differ.
