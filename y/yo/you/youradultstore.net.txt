Your Privacy is Important.
We recognize the importance of protecting the privacy of information provided by our members, customers and visitors to our websites. We have created the following guidelines to demonstrate our commitment to the issue of privacy. We respect your right to privacy and feel it is important for you to know how we handle the information we receive from you via the Internet. Your continued use of our site signifies your acceptance of our privacy policy.
We have appropriate security measures in place to ensure information received from our online visitors is as secure as possible against unauthorized access and use. We do not disclose your personal information to unrelated companies who intend to sell their products or services to you.
We do not identify visitors to our website. You will remain anonymous to our system unless you choose to tell us who you are. For example, when you submit a question or request information via our e-mail submit form, this information is kept confidential and only disclosed to those who may be able to help address your questions.
We may contact you by telephone based on the information you provide to us to process and fulfill your request, even if you have opted into the Do-Not-Call List administered by the Federal Trade Commission, any state equivalent Do-Not-Call List, or the do-not-call list of this company. If one of our employees or agents contacts you on our behalf by telephone and you do not wish to be contacted again with respect to your inquiry, you should specifically make a request to such employee or agent not to be contacted.
Throughout our website you may be directed through links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content relative to other companies or organizations. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites as their information privacy practices may differ from ours.
We may periodically send e-mail communications to you related to our distributor program, Website updates, product updates and general topics. If you do not wish to receive such emails, simply opt out by using the opt out link at the bottom of the emails.
