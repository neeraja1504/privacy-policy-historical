Yeandi Directory
Privacy
Submittal Information
In order to update you about the status of your submittal or to ask questions we require your name and email address. We record this information along with the IP address and date and time of your submittal to prevent spamming of the directory.
We only use such information in connection with the yeandi directory, and do not use it for marketing purposes. We do not share your information with any outside companies.
Search Terms
In the case of a search, we log the specific search terms requested, and again the date and time information. We use this information to learn more about how the directory is used, and to improve the quality and usefulness of the search facility.
Feel free to make suggestions for improvement to the directory - you can email info@yeandi.com
