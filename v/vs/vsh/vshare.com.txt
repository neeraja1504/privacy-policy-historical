vShare
Vshare is serious to regard the privacy of Internet users, because our business success depends upon our ability to maintain the trust of our users.
If you have any concern about our products or website's privacy policies, please contact us. We read every message submitted and try to reply to every one promptly. We may also file your comments to improve the program and site, or review and discard the information.
When does vShare collect personally- identifiable information?
vShare will collect your information if the information is licensed to us, for example new released software subscription comes for users, software submission come from authors or order come from customers. We won't collect any personally- identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address from this web site without your knowledge, and will take measures to protect your privacy.
If you have any question or concern about this matter, please do not hesitate to contact.
