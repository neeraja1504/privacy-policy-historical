Your Privacy Matters to Us
This section outlines our policy relating to any personal information you might wish to provide us when you visit our site.
Our business has been built on trust between our shareholders and ourselves. We have a duty to safeguard and keep confidential any information relating to our shareholders or their investment affairs. Whether it is provided to us over the phone, through electronic media or while visiting this site, we will strive at all times to ensure that the information is kept confidential and secure.
If we do ask you to provide personal information for promotional purposes, we will specify the purpose for which such personal information is collected at the time of collection. If we do send such promotional mailings to you thereafter, you will always be given an opportunity to opt out, should you wish to.
In addition, the personal information collected from you may be transferred to, stored, processed or otherwise administered by third party agents, contractors or service providers who provide administrative, telecommunications, computer and other data mining and processing services. In such event, we will notify you of this possibility at the time of collection of personal information, and you will always have an option as to whether to provide personal information to us or not.
Internet Privacy Policy Statement and Personal Information Collection
Virgin Gold Mining Corporation (hereinafter referred to as ' _ ** _ VGMC** ', 'us', 'we' or 'our') is committed to maintaining the privacy of every current, former and prospective shareholder. We recognize that you entrust important personal information to us, and we wish to assure you that we take seriously our responsibilities in protecting and safeguarding this information. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes in detail how we deal with information that we collect on this website (the "Site").
This Policy may be updated at any time. Please ensure that you regularly read the latest version of this page.
1.0 Information We Collect
We will not collect any personal information that identifies a visitor to this site individually unless specified otherwise in these terms or VGMC expressly requests you to do so in which event VGMC will specify the purpose for which such information is collected and you will be given an opportunity to opt out, if you wish to do so.
Some of this information will be gathered through the use of "cookies". Cookies are small bits of information that are automatically stored on a person's web browser in their computer that can be retrieved by this site. Cookies can make the site more useful by storing information about your preferences on particular sites, thus enabling us to provide more useful features for you. The information collected by "cookies" is anonymous aggregated research data, and contain no name or address information or any information that will enable anyone to contact you via telephone, e-mail or any other means. It also contains no shareholders’ sensitive data, such as account username and password. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can set your browser to disable cookies or inform you when they are set. However, by disabling them, you may not be able to take full advantage of our website.
In addition to our duty of confidentiality to shareholders, we shall at all times fully observe the following principles, save otherwise appropriately agreed by the shareholders:
* collection of personal data from shareholders shall be for purposes relating to the provision of investment services or related products;
* all practical steps will be taken to ensure that personal data are accurate and will not be kept longer than necessary or will be destroyed in accordance with the internal retention period;
* personal data will not be used for any purposes other than the data that were to be used at the time of collection or purposes directly related thereto;
* personal data will be protected against unauthorized or accidental access, processing or erasure;
A shareholder's personal data is classified as confidential and can only be disclosed by us where permitted or otherwise legally compelled to do so.

7.0 Notice to Shareholders Relating to the Personal Data Policy
From time to time, it is necessary for shareholders to supply VGMC with data in connection with the opening or continuation of accounts and the establishment or continuation of account facilities or provision of online services or compliance with any laws or guidelines issued by regulatory or other authorities.
Failure to supply such data may result in VGMC being unable to activate or continue accounts or establish or continue account facilities or provide online services or comply with any laws or guidelines issued by regulatory or other authorities.
The purposes for which data relating to a shareholder may be used are as follows:
the daily operation of the services and credit facilities provided to shareholders;
designing online services or related products for shareholders' use;
marketing services or products of VGMC and/or selected companies;
Data held by VGMC relating to a shareholder will be kept confidential but VGMC may provide such information to the following parties (whether within or outside Panama) for the purposes set out in paragraph (d):
any agent, contractor or third party service provider who provides administrative, telecommunications, computer, payment or securities clearing or other services to VGMC in connection with the operation of its business;
any other person under a duty of confidentiality to VGMC including a group company of VGMC which has undertaken to keep such information confidential;
a shareholder making any transfer into another shareholder’s account (by providing the name of the receiver for verification);
any person to whom VGMC is under an obligation to make disclosure under the requirements of any law binding on VGMC or any of its branches or under and for the purposes of any guidelines issued by regulatory or other authorities with which VGMC or any of its branches are expected to comply;
