Privacy and Security
At VogaCloset we respect your privacy and preferences for the use of your information are our highest priority. We take great care to safeguard information in our possession.
We never share customer information (including e-mail addresses) unless it is absolutely necessary to provide you with products or services we offer. We may also disclose information when you tell us to do so, to identify or contact you, to protect your rights or the rights of VogaCloset or as required by law.
Any third-party companies we use to provide you with products or services are required to keep your information secure and confidential.
For more information about your privacy and security please read our Privacy Policy.
We do not store credit card details.
If you have any other questions or concerns regarding VogaCloset's access to or use of your personal information, please e-mail us at customercare@vogacloset.com.
VogaCloset is a U.K.-based online fashion store offering the latest women, men and kids trends from the runways directly to the closets of fashion enthusiasts in the Middle East.
