Vitale Srl-Privacy Online
By accessing the website www.vitaleboutique.it operated by Vitale Srl or using its content, you accept the terms of this Online Privacy policy, as detailed below. If you do not accept these terms, please do not access the site and not to use its contents.
Collection of personal information
As you perform activities such as ordering products, downloading software, or participate in contests, Vitale Srl may ask you to provide personal information by compiling and sending a specific form (e-form). You are totally free to accept or decline this request. If you decide to accept Vitale Srl may ask you to provide personal and/or corporate information and other data that identifies you.
Vitale Srl collects this information to register it and allow you to participate in activities chosen by you. If you order a product, for example, the information is used to send the goods or other benefits that may be offered to registered users.
Vitale Srl will use the information you provided to keep you updated on new initiatives, special offers and other new products and services of Vitale Srl.
Vitale Srl recognizes and attaches great value to a responsible use of such information.
Vitale Srl will adhere scrupulously to your specifications.
Use of cookies and the types of data processed
Visiting our. www.vitaleboutique.it site, you can move between pages anonymously and access important information without revealing your identity. In order to improve our site, we use "cookies" to keep track of your passage. A cookie is a reduced set of data transferred to your browser from a Web server and can only be read by the server that made the transfer.
This category of data includes:
•    the IP addresses of computers used by users who use the service;
•    the number of hits;
•    pages viewed;
•    the date and time that you access;
•    delia's life on the content displayed.
•    the page of origin before arriving at www.VostraDitta.com
•    the Web browser;
•    the operating system you are using.
•    the resolution of the system used.
•    The language
•    The destination
The data is used for the purpose of obtaining anonymous statistical information on site usage and to check its correct functioning. The data could be used to ascertain responsibility in case of hypothetical computer crimes against the site except for this possibility, the data on web contacts do not persist for more than seven days. No data is disclosed.
Almost all browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can have your browser notify you when you receive a cookie, allowing you to choose whether to accept it or not (for some Web pages that require an authorization, cookies are not optional. Customers who choose not to receive cookies probably cannot access those pages).
Vitale uses cookies to track your visit in ns. sitowww.vitaleboutique.it. Our Web servers automatically log the IP/Internet address of your computer. This information does not identify you personally. You will therefore remain anonymous unless you have provided Vitale srl personal information in other forms.
