Privacy Policy | Viunk.en
Information
Viunk.es collects and uses information from the Web to run your analytics tool. This information can include: your IP address, domain name, estimated traffic, analysis on-page/off-page SEO, usability, load times, popular web ...
This information is used by Viunk Intelligence for the operation of its service and to provide specific reports of domain names.
Cookies
Please note that when users browse through our website using a third company, Google, which includes the use of the DoubleClick DART cookie:
· Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
· The use of the DART cookie allows Google to serve ads to users who visit their sites and other Internet sites.
· Users can disable the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and privacy policy of the content network.
To learn more about this service visit http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html third
If you do not want to receive cookies can configure your browser to notify and reject the installation of cookies sent by the Site, without jeopardizing their access to contents.
