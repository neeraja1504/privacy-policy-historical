JimWorld Privacy Policy Statement
This is a web site owned and operated by Jim Wilson.
The postal address is:
28432 Via Alfonse
Laguna Niguel, CA 92677
Jim can be reached via e-mail at jim@jimworld.com
Or you can reach us by telephone at 949-360-9915
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.
We collect aggregate information on what pages visitors access or visit and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
The information we collect is used for internal review and is then discarded, used to improve the content of our Web site and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our organization.
When you supply your name and/or e-mail address when registering to access any of the interactive services on this site, we do not gather that data for any other use than to identify you when you access those services. However, we do not have any control over address harvesters that may invade our web site for the purpose of harvesting addresses for use by other parties.
When you register to be added to one or more of the e-mail lists maintained by us, we do not share that information with any other person or business.
As a general policy, we do not allow participation in our web sites by children under the age of 13. If you feel an exception should be made for your child, it will be necessary for you to call Jim Wilson at the above phone number.
