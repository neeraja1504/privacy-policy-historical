Privacy Policy for vertix.io

What information is collected:
> Your email Address (For account recovery or Service Related Notices)
> Your Username
> Your Password (Encrypted on Database)

> Third parties may be placing and reading cookies on your browser, or using web beacons to collect information as a result of ad serving on vertix.io
For More information on Third Parties Go here: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/

What do we use the Information for?

recover it in case you lose the details. Some information is also collected to personalize
your experience.

Do we discuss or trade your information with outside parties?
> No. All of the information you provide us will be kept private.
> No information is sold to any outside parties.
How is your information Protected?
> The information you provide is stored in a secure database. Any sensitive information; Password,
is hashed on the database.
Do we use cookies?
> Yes. Cookies are using the save certain information: settings, account info.
