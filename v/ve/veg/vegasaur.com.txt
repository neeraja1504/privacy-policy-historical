Vegasaur.com Privacy Statement
Collection of your personal information
The personal information we collect may include your name, company or e-mail. In addition, we collect non-personal data, such as the date and time pages are viewed, information regarding types of Internet browsers used by users and other statistics.
Use of your personal information
Your privacy is very important to us. We will not share any collected information with third parties and use it only for internal needs. This information is used to help us improve our level of service.
Cookies
A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer by a web server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a Web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
No Ad-Aware, Viruses or Trojans
We ensure you that no ad-aware, viruses or trojans included in our software provided that you download the installation packs from our web site.
Changes to this policy
If we change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on the Site so that users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we will disclose it. Any such posted changes will be effective upon the date indicated by the posting.
