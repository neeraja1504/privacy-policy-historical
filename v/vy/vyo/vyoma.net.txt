Your use of Vyoma.net indicates an acceptance of the terms of the Privacy Policy.
What do we do with the data?
We use it to make vyoma.net better for you and everyone.
Providing you with recommendations
Notifying you about new features, material, exams and content
Providing you with updates regarding any policy changes or service issues
We never share your data with any third parties
We do not store any payment details.

We will always explain clearly what data we’re collecting about you and why. We will only collect data we need to: give you a better experience improve our services

It’s up to you how you use our services. And you can completely manage your Vyoma account at any time independently.
Trust
Your trust is very important to us. So we’re committed to keeping your data safe and secure. We promise to never sell your personal details to anyone
Cookies
A cookie is a small amount of at a that is sent to your computer. We assume that you are happy to use cookies while using our website unless explicitly denied. Information supplied by cookies can help us to understand the profile of our visitors and help us to provide you with a better user experience. It also helps us recognise when you are signed in to your account and to provide a more personalised experience.
