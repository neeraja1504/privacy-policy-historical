Definitions
Affiliates                 Companies related by common ownership or control. They can
be financial and non-financial companies. Numerica Credit Union
does not share with our affiliates.
Non-affiliates              Companies not related by common ownership or control. They can
be financial and non-financial companies. Numerica Credit Union
does not share with non-affiliates so they can market to you.
Joint marketing             A formal agreement between non-affiliated financial companies
that together market financial products or services to you. Our
joint marketing partners include financial service providers.
