Nikken - The Official Site of Nikken Products
Nikken Inc. knows that you care how information about you is used and shared and we appreciate your trust in us to do that carefully and sensibly. This notice describes the privacy policy of Nikken Inc.
By visiting our website you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this Privacy Notice.
Controllers of Personal Information
Any personal information provided to or to be gathered by nikken.com is controlled by Nikken.
Collection and use of your personal information
The information we receive from customers helps us personalize and continually improve your shopping experience at nikken.com.
When you use this site you will be asked to provide information such as your contact details. We will store this data and hold it on computers or otherwise. We will use this data to fulfill our agreement with you.
We use the information to handle orders, deliver products and services, process payments, communicate with you about orders, products, services and promotional offers, update our records and generally maintain your accounts with us and recommend merchandise and services that might be of interest to you. We also use this information to improve our store and platform, prevent or detect fraud or abuses of our website and enable third parties to carry out technical, logistical or other functions on our behalf.
As part of our service to you we may keep you informed of such products and services including special offers, discounts, offers and competitions. If you do not wish to receive information of such products and services from Nikken, please check the opt-out box provided on the registration page of the website.
Fraud prevention
In order to protect our customers and us from fraud and theft, we may pass on information that we obtain from making identity checks and other information in our customer records, including how you conduct your account, to other Nikken subsidiaries and to financial and other organizations (including law enforcement agencies) involved in fraud prevention and detection, to use in the same way.
Disclosure of your information
We may give information about you to the following, who may use it for the same purposes as set out above:
to other companies in the Nikken Group
to employees and agents of the Nikken Group to administer any accounts, products and services provided to you by the Group now or in the future
to anyone to whom we transfer or may transfer our rights and duties under our agreement with you
if we have a duty to do so or if the law allows us to do so
We do not sell your information to third parties.
Security
We are committed to using the highest security standards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access.
For data transfer we use a security system together with bit encryption, offering an extremely high level of data safety. Those areas of the website protected by this system will display a closed padlock in your browser.
Payment by card is secure under the above system. We do not retain credit card details within the website database. These are only used to carry out the payment transaction. However, we cannot guarantee the security of any data you disclose online. You accept the inherent security risks of providing information and dealing online over the Internet and will not hold us responsible for any breach of security unless this is due to our negligence or wilful default.
