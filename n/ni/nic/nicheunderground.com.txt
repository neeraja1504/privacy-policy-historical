Your privacy is important to you and to us. To protect your privacy NicheUnderground.com/members and its owner follows different principles in accordance with worldwide practices for customer privacy and data protection.
NicheUnderground.com/members AND ITS OWNER WON’T SELL, RENT OR GIVE AWAY YOUR NAME, MAIL ADDRESS, PHONE NUMBERS, EMAIL ADDRESSES, OR ANY OTHER INFORMATION TO ANYONE.
NicheUnderground.com/members will use the stat-of-art security measures to protect your information from unauthorized users.
What Information NicheUnderground.com/members Collect from Users
For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer’s domain name and IP address. We collect only the domain name, IP address and aggregate information on what pages consumers’ access or visit, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our web page.
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail or information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web page, disclosed when legally required to do so, at the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our web site and applicable laws or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our web site.
With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record user-specific information on what pages user access or visit.
How We Communicate With NicheUnderground.com/members Users
NicheUnderground.com/members is communicating with users by email and phone in two cases:
if NicheUnderground.com/members user provide name, email and/or phone number by subscribing to our newsletter
if we receive direct support request from user.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future you can unsubscribe via the link provided with each email or simply by sending us a request to our support email.
If you supply us with your postal address on-line, you will only receive the information for which you provided us your address.
Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us for which you provided us your telephone number.
Security
With respect to security: we have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from user at our site.
