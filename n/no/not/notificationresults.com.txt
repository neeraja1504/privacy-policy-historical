Period of time using our Site, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally noticeable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally visible information may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, postal address and phone number.
Log Data
Like many site speculator, we collect more details that your browser sends whenever you visit our site log data. Cookies
Cookies are files with little amount of data, which may include an unspecified unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Like many sites, we use “cookies” to gather more details. You can require your browser to decline all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
