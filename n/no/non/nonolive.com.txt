Nonolive Privacy Policy
Scope of this Privacy Policy
This privacy policy is applicable to how the Nonolive community handles personally identifiable information that is left when users log in to the website and server, as well as other personally identifiable information shared with our business partners.
This privacy policy is not applicable to organizations that do not belong to the Nonolive community or non-Nonolive employees.
This privacy policy constitutes an inseparable part of the Nonolive User Agreement and must be read together with the Nonolive User Agreement including but not limited to Clause 6 (on Privacy Protection) of the Nonolive User Agreement.
Collection and Use of Information
The Nonolive community will collect your personally identifiable information when you register a Nonolive community account, use other Nonolive community products or services, access Nonolive community webpages, or participate in promotional or prize winning games. The Nonolive community also collects personally identifiable information from our business partners.
When you register a Nonolive community account, we will ask for your name, email address, interests, birthday, gender and personal webpage, in addition to a personal profile. Once you are successfully registered with the Nonolive community, we will be able to identify you when you log in to the server.
The Nonolive community automatically receives and records the information on your browser and server logs, including your IP address, information in the Nonolive community cookies and your browsing history.
The Nonolive community uses this information to enhance the quality of the video services that we provide and to improve the effectiveness to better tailor our services to your interests and needs.

The Nonolive community is not responsible for any collection, disclosure and/or use of your personal information provided by you to any third party's website which may be linked to or from our website.
Cookies
The Nonolive community will set or access Nonolive community cookies on your computer.
The Nonolive community allows those companies that publish advertisements on our webpages to set or access cookies on user computers. Other companies will use cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies, and cannot access cookies used by the Nonolive community.
In performing operations associated with our products and services, the Nonolive community uses web beacons to retrieve cookies from our website.
Web Beacons (if applicable)
Our webpages include electronic images (known as a "pixel GIF" or a "beacon") that allow the website to calculate the number of webpage users or users that have saved a certain cookie. The ways in which the Nonolive community uses beacons include:
Beacons used within the Nonolive community
The Nonolive community uses beacons on the website to calculate the total number of users and to identify registered users using saved Nonolive community cookies.
Web beacons are used outside of the Nonolive community domain
The Nonolive community uses website beacons to collect data for partner websites; relevant data is also used for statistical purposes.
The Nonolive community user data recorded by website beacons are compiled and made available to our partners as aggregate data for reference purposes. Aggregate data includes information such as user count and usage, but does not include any personally identifiable data.
When the Nonolive community places website beacons to collect data for our partners, we require our partners to inform users on their privacy policy page that the webpage uses website beacons.

HTML Emails
The Nonolive community typically adds a beacon when sending emails in HTML format (emails containing images) to count the number of opened messages.
Typically, electronic images are considered to be part of the webpage and, including banner ads, can all be used as beacons. Therefore, beacons can be added to all advertisements placed by the Nonolive community.
The Nonolive community tracks certain usage behavior. Information collected from web beacons are used to understand how customers use our products, provide more accurate reports and enhance the effectiveness of our marketing.
Editing Personal Account Information and Preferences
At any time, you have the right to edit your Nonolive community account information and preferences, including whether you would like to receive information on the latest products from the Nonolive community.
Security Safeguards
Your Nonolive community account is password protected to ensure the security of your privacy and information.
Under certain conditions, the Nonolive community uses the universally accepted SSL encryption system to ensure the security of information transfer.
Amendments to the Privacy Policy
The Nonolive community periodically makes changes to the privacy policy. If there are significant changes to the privacy policy, we will promptly notify users by posting the relevant terms in a prominent position on the webpage.
