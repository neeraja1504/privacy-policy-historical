Privacy Policy
You know... for private things.
The policies below are applicable to websites, email newsletters, or any other Web content owned or operated by Nebo Agency, unless any such site or email publication contains another, separate privacy policy. Nebo Agency sites are maintained by Nebo Agency, 1031 Marietta Street NW, Suite A, Atlanta, GA 30318.
Nebo Agency respects each individual user's right to privacy. Nebo Agency may collect personal identifying information when such information is specifically and knowingly provided from users during (i)request for information about our services or company information, (ii)request for Web presence analysis or search engine marketing analysis, (iii)contest, survey, sweepstakes or site registration promotions, and (iv) subscription registration for sites or services requiring such registration information. Nebo Agency may use this information for marketing and promotional purposes. Nebo Agency occasionally informs our users of articles, statistics, white papers, new services, and other third party product and service offers that we believe our users will find interesting. Users are given the option to choose whether they would like to receive such mailings upon registration. Individual users can request, at any time, that their information not be used for such purposes by opting out of our mailing list(s).
Nebo Agency will not allow companies and organizations to rent or purchase our lists.
