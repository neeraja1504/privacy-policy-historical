NetDania - Privacy Policy
COOKIES
The login and download sections require you to enable cookies in your browser. On this website we only use temporary cookies. This means that the cookies are automatically removed as soon as you end your browser session at our site. We do not save any kind of personal information from cookies. The temporary cookies are used to gather statistical information and to analyze the downloads from the site.
PERSONAL INFORMATION
We do not share any personal/contact information collected on this site with any third parties. None of the information that you give us is transferred, sold, loaned or rented to third parties without your prior consent. The contact information collected on this site may be used to inform you about the latest developments on the site.
EXTERNAL LINKS
This site contains links to other sites. NetDania is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
CONTACTING THE WEBSITE
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the site practices, or your dealings with this website, please do not hesitate to contact us at:  info@netdania.net .
