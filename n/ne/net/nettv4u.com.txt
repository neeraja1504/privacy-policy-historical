The Information That We Collect From Our Users:
Nettv4u is dedicated to protecting the user’s privacy and data information. The information that we collect on our users while using the Nettv4u website are the following:
Like any other websites, Nettv4u may place cookies to your computer’s hard drive. We may transfer cookies to your computer if your browser is established to accept cookies. This is a diminutive piece of data with a string of alphanumeric characters that distinctively identifies your web browser so you can easily load Nettv4u the next time you visit our site. Nettv4u will use session cookies and persistent cookies. The Session Cookie is provisional and it will disappear once you close your browser. You can set to refuse all cookies or denote when a cookie is being sent. But, some features of Nettv4u website may stop working correctly. The Persistent Cookie remains even when you already close your browser. Your browser may use this on your succeeding visits to the website. Nettv4u uses cookies to record the information so you never have to re-enter our site when you visit it again. The information that we stored are non-personally identifiable information. However, if you do not wish this information to be collected, you can normally opt out of providing this information by configuring your browser to disallow cookies. But please be reminded that some section of the Nettv4u website may not dispense reasonable user experience if you have turned off the use of cookies.
We will be using third-party advertising companies to provide ads while you browse Nettv4u. The third-party advertisers and ad servers will put and display their ads on our site and they may also gather user information via cookies, pixel tag, clear GIF or Web Beacon and other similar methods. Third-party advertisers and ad servers may utilize the information they gathered for presenting their advertisements, for assessing and evaluating the efficiency of their advertisements, or other uses. The use and the gathered user’s information by the third-party advertisers and ad servers is administered by the significant privacy policy of the third-party and therefore, it is not covered by the Nettv4u Privacy Policy Statement. The privacy policy of Nettv4u is certainly distinct from the privacy policy of the third-party advertisers and ad servers.
When using the Nettv4u website, our servers will automatically record specific information from your browser. Nettv4u collect server logs; including the Internet Protocol or IP address, web request, browser type and language, domain names, pages viewed, number of clicks, referring or exit pages, URLs, span of time that the user spent on particular pages, date and time of your request, platform type and cookies.

We utilize third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics to assess the traffic and usage of our website. The analytic tools will gather information from your browser such as a web page request, web pages that you visit, height, width and add-ons of a browser, and other information that helps us in improving our service. We gather and utilize the following information in a collective form so it could not be manipulated to recognize a particular or individual user.
Nettv4u does not use personally identifiable information to use our service. We do not require users to create an account to use our site.
