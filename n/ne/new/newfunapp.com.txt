Information Collected and Used :
The information we collect is used to improve the content and the quality of our Services, and is not shared with or sold to other organizations for any purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested.
When you access the Services we will ask for extended and special permissions from your Facebook account if it is necessary for the application. The permissions must be accepted in order to use the application. Please note that because third parties manage the purchases of virtual goods offered by the Services, we do not have access to your credit card details and billing information.
In short we only collect that information that requires for the social application that you want to use. And most important that we are not sharing user's information in any situation or condition.

Our Commitment To Children's Privacy :
Protecting the privacy of young children is especially important for us. If you are under 13 years of age, then please do not use or access our website at any time or in any manner. If we learns that personally identifiable information of persons under 13 years of age has been collected on the our Site then we will take the appropriate steps to delete this information immediately.
We recommend that minors over the age of 13 ask their parents or guardians for permission before sending any personal information to anyone over the Internet.
