The ordinary visitor means if you are not a registered dealer / user of the website www.NehruPlaceMarket.com, We are able to get the information about your internet browser details, ISP Â  and IP address details in case you are an ordinary user. This information is collected for traffic analysis only. The cookie does not contain personal details. Depending on the browser that you use, you can set your preferences to block/ refuse cookies, and/ or notify you before they are placed. StatCounter does not sell, give, or trade the statistics they store to any 3rd parties for data-mining or marketing purposes. Also you can see the privacy policies of these service providers by visitng their respective websites like for privacy ploicy of Statcounter visit statcounter.com, for google analytics visit google.com and so on.
