NFOrce Entertainment B.V. - Privacy disclaimer
Revision: 2013-10-17
Privacy
This policy covers how we use your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and will take all measures to protect your personal information.
Any personal information received will only be used to fill your order or to administrate your services with NFOrce Entertainment B.V. We will not sell or redistribute your information to anyone.
Only authorized law enforcement agencies are allowed to request such information. After 12 months of non activity, meaning you have no services with us anymore, your details can be removed from our system. Our administration (invoices) will be kept for the legal required period.
