QQ Solutions Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
QQ Solutions has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for the www.QQSolutions.com website.
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help administer our website. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information, specifically, information on the demographics of visitors to our website.
Cookies
Our site may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests. This can include visitor-specific information to enable our visitors to use certain custom features of this website.
Registration Forms
Our site's contact form and certain other forms require users to give us contact information (e.g., their name and email address). Contact information from the registration forms is used to send information about QQ Solutions and its products. The visitor's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor, when requested.
External Links
This site may contain links to other sites. QQ Solutions is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. These include a restricted, password-controlled database accessible only to our Webmaster.
Contacting the WebSite
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, refer to the contact page to get the complete contact information for QQ Solutions, Inc.
