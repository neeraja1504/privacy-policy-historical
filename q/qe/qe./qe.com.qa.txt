Privacy Policy
Qatar Stock Exchange (QSE) takes reasonable precautions to protect investors' personal information entered during online registration and account creation on its website to use available e- services. This Privacy Statement describes how your personal information is collected, used, and safeguarded by QSE.
QSE collects personal information from investors to provide requested services and enhance investors' experience.
QSE is the sole owner of the information collected on this website, and will not sell, share, rent or use your personal information in ways different from what is disclosed in this privacy statement, unless required by law enforcement agencies. For the provision of e-services that require account information, QSE exchanges information with the Qatar Central Securities Depository (QCSD). QSE also uses third party advertising companies to serve ads. QSE may give these companies statistical information about your visits to this web site during a specific period of time without prejudice to the investors' personal data.
