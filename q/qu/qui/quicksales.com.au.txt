quicksales Privacy Policy
About this Policy
The website  www.quicksales.com.au (the Site) is operated by carsales.com Limited (ACN 074 444 018) (quicksales).

How is personal information collected?
quicksales collects personal information in a number of ways, including:
directly from you when you provide information directly to quicksales in person, by phone or in writing (whether electronic or otherwise) for example when you create a listing of an item on the Site or when you enquire about an item on the Site;
directly from you when you communicate with quicksales or any other person using quicksales Communications Services;
from third parties such as:
quicksales related companies;
resellers of quicksales services and service providers to quicksales;
operators of linked or served web sites, applications and advertising on the Site;
social networks where you choose to share that information when creating an account;
from 3rd party data providers who have been directed by you to send us information on your behalf;
through use of quicksales proprietary software in which personal information is stored;
from organisations identified or described under the heading "How is personal information disclosed"
How is personal information used?
Your personal information may be used for the following purposes:
to maintain appropriate business records;
to research, develop, improve and use within quicksales products and services and by its outsourced service providers to provide and improve their services;
to pre-populate / customise / provide shortcuts on our websites;
to conduct surveys to determine use and satisfaction with quicksales services;
to generate statistics in relation to the Site;
for the purposes of feedback, reviews and ratings;
to send you newsletters, promote and market special offers and other services to you (if you do not want to receive these, please let us know by clicking here);
for purposes connected with the operation of the Site including to help ensure safe trading on the Site, verify account authenticity, send invoices, facilitate payments, resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems;
for staff training and quality assurance;
to personalise the site experience and / or the advertising you see when you visit the Site or elsewhere; and
to contact you.
How is personal information disclosed?
quicksales may disclose personal information to individuals and organisations other than quicksales. The organisations to which we may disclose personal information include:
quicksales staff and quicksales' related bodies corporate and their staff;
the public if you advertise with quicksales or use publicly available quicksales Communications Services;
individuals and organisations that advertise with quicksales if you submit an enquiry or seek certain services through quicksales
If you do not want quicksales to disclose your personal information to these individuals and organisations, you should not submit an enquiry.
trading partners (members of the Site with whom you have completed a transaction) Note: members you have completed a transaction with can request to exchange contact details with you (name, address and phone numbers);
Service providers who offer their services via the website including transport services, payment services, or others.
outsourced service providers who assist quicksales to provide its services including:
information technology providers;
marketing and market research advisers; and
professional advisers; and
payment platforms.
entities to whom you request we send your information;
resellers of quicksales services;
organisations involved in a sale/transfer of quicksales assets, business or shares;
to assist government or regulatory authorities or police;
a rights owner or their legal representatives where intellectual property infringement is alleged;
as we are required by law, for example pursuant to a subpoena or in accordance with unclaimed money legislation.
Where quicksales provides information to a third party at your request (for example where you submit an enquiry), you acknowledge that your information will be handled by that third party in accordance with its privacy policy and not in accordance with the quicksales privacy policy.
Maintaining personal information quality
quicksales strives to maintain accurate and up-to-date records. To facilitate this, please let us know if any of your details change. You may update certain personal information through your membership account (if you have one) or click here to contact us by email.
quicksales may destroy or de-identify personal information once the information is no longer deemed required by the business.
How to access your personal information
You generally have a right to access your personal information, subject to a few exceptions provided by law. If you wish to access your personal information please Click here or on the "contact us" link at the bottom of this page. Please remember to provide as much detail as you can as this will help us to find the information. There is no charge for this service, however you may be required to verify your identity before any information is released to you. Please note that depending on the volume or nature of the information required, it may take a number of days to fulfil the request.
About security and personal information and credit card information
quicksales takes reasonable steps to protect personal information collected from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modifications or disclosure. Your personal information may be stored in hard copy documents, but is generally stored electronically on quicksales' systems.
quicksales protects credit card information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts the information you input. Your full credit card details are not stored on quicksalesï¿½ servers. Credit card payments are processed by a third party Australian payment gateway company. Credit card information for customers on 'Auto-Bill' are stored by the payment gateway.
Transfer of personal information outside of Australia
Some of quicksales' service providers are located outside of Australia, including in the United States of America and Canada. It may be necessary to disclose your personal information to those service providers for some of the purposes listed above. By providing quicksales with your personal information, you consent to the disclosure / transfer of your personal information outside of Australia as set out in this privacy policy.
Dealing with quicksales anonymously
You may deal with quicksales anonymously by using a pseudonym, unless quicksales is required or authorised by law to deal only with an identified person, or it is impracticable for us to respond to you if you have not identified yourself.
What if you don't provide personal information?
If you choose not to provide personal information when requested, quicksales may not be able to provide you with the full range of quicksales services.
quicksales, Linked Sites and personal information
When you visit a website or use an application on the Site, quicksales records anonymous information such as IP address, time, date, referring url, and other referral information, device information and location, carrier, site interactions such as your access to website features, pages accessed, content, searching activities, lead submission, and files accessed and downloaded, type of browser and operating system.
Such information collected by quicksales may be linked to other information quicksales holds about a user, compiling a complete profile about quicksales users' browsing activities on the Site.
quicksales may use third party advertising companies to collect data and/or serve ads when you visit its website or another website on the carsales Network. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to websites in order to provide advertisements about good and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, visit http://networkadvertising.org/.
quicksales uses "cookies" and other methods to collect and store the information set out above. A cookie is a small file that stays on your computer until, depending on whether it is a sessional or persistent cookie, you turn your computer off or it expires (this may be as little as 30 days or up 2 years, or longer in some cases). Cookies may be used by quicksales or by service providers engaged by quicksales to provide you with the full range of services it offers, remember your preferences, remember the sites you visit on the Site, measure and analyse usage of the Site and for security. Persistent cookies may be used to identify the sites you visit on the Site and to personalise the advertising that you see when you make future visits to those Sites or other sites or platforms. You may adjust your Internet browser settings to disable cookies. Cookie data may be sold, licensed or otherwise provided to third parties for sale via advertising exchanges for this purpose. If cookies are disabled quicksales may not be able to provide you with the full range of quicksales services.
The Site may include links to third party web sites, co-branded web sites and applications and advertising served to the Site by third parties (Linked Sites). The operators of Linked Sites may collect personal information through the use of cookies and otherwise. quicksales is not responsible for Linked Sites and recommends you read the privacy policy of Linked Sites before disclosing your personal information.
This site uses Crazy Egg web analytics service. Crazy Egg may record visitor's activities on the Site, which may include mouse clicks, mouse movement, and scrolling activity performed on the Site, user screen size, browser versions and other user experience based metrics. Crazy Egg does not collect any personally identifiable information, including credit card information, that you enter in this website. For more information see: Privacy Policy for information collected by the Crazy Egg Web Analytics Service. We are using the information collected by Crazy Egg service for analysing usability of the Site and associated purposes. To opt out of this analytics service, please email contact@crazyegg.com.
quicksales uses Nielsen/NetRatings web analytics tools to measure and analyse internet usage across the Site. This service helps us measure and respond to our online consumers. The data collected does not identify a user personally.
What if I have a problem?
If you have a problem, complaint, want to change your personal information or just wish to enquire about privacy please contact us.
