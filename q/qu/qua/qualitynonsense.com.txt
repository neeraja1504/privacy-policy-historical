Quality Nonsense Ltd Privacy Policy
This privacy policy explains what Quality Nonsense Ltd does with any data collected.
Demographic and user data is collected in order to offer the best user experience possible. For example, your IP address is used to administer the web server, to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information using web analytics software, like Google Analytics, which use cookies.
This site collects demographic and profile data to tailor user experience. Based on this information, we may display content we feel you might interest you.
We may request data such as contact information, email address and demographic data. Any information you provide is used only to send you information you specifically request. You can opt out at of all communication at any time. We do not spam, rent, sell or otherwise share mailing lists.
This site contains links to other websites. Quality Nonsenes Ltd is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites.
Got questions about this privacy policy? Please get in touch.
