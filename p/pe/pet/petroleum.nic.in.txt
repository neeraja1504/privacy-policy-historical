Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas
Privacy Policy
As a general rule,this web site does not collect Personal Information about you when you visit the site , without revealing personal information ,unless you choose to provide such information . Any Personal information collected shall be used only for the stated purpose and shall NOT be shared with any other department organisation (public/private).
This site may contain links to non-Government sites whose data protection and privacy practices may differ from ours. We are not responsible for the content & privacy practices of these other Websites and encourage you to consult the privacy notices of those sites .
