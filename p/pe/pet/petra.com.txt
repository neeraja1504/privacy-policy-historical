Privacy Policy for Petra Industries
Petra Industries values your business and your confidence in our ability to service your needs. It is based on this trust that you should feel secure in your review of our website and of any data you may choose to share electronically.
This privacy statement is applicable to www.petra.com, a website owned by Petra Industries, LLC
We have developed our website with security measures to protect the integrity and security of your personal and professional information. Petra believes in protecting your private information. Petra may use your information for improving your buying experience for future purchases, improving our website, improving customer service, processing your transactions, sending periodic emails or to administer a contest, promotion, survey or other website feature. However, your information will be held in strictest confidence and stored for Petra’s use only. If at any time you would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions in each email. We will not give away, sell, transfer, rent or lease your personal information to any third party without your consent. We may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law or legal action when necessary, enforce our website policies, or protect our’s or others’ rights, property or safety.
Petra.com uses Google™ Analytics service. Google™ Analytics uses a first-party cookie and JavaScript code to collect information about visitors to the site and to track data. Google™ Analytics anonymously tracks how visitors interact with the website, including where they came from, what they did on the site, and whether they completed a conversion. For more information, see Google™’s Privacy Policy. We are using the information collected by Google™ Analytics to create an optimum experience for our customers.
Petra.com, at its discretion, may include or offer third party products or services on our website which contain links to those other websites. However, these third party websites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked websites and cannot be responsible for privacy practices on other website locations. Please be sure to review the privacy statements of those links for further information. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about those third party sites.
All information within this website, including images and text, is copyrighted and remains the exclusive property of Petra Industries, Inc. and may not be used without our consent. This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our website and not to information collected offline.
By using our website, you consent to this privacy policy. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. If you have questions, comments or concerns regarding this privacy statement, or feel that this website is not following its stated policy, you may contact us at feedback@petra.com.
