Privacy statement
Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the utmost importance to us, our reputation and integrity is directly related to how we serve our users. Please find below a description of how we strive to protect the privacy of Internet users, and maintain the Internet as a highly credible medium. The following discloses our information-gathering and dissemination practices.
Confidentiality
We regard all personal information collected from our users as completely confidential. This information is not shared, sold or rented in any way to third parties.
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our websites. Your IP address is also used to help identify you and to gather geographic and broad demographic information.
Contact Information
Our sites provide contact forms/links for visitors. We collect contact information (ie, names and email addresses) to get in touch with the user when necessary.
Opt Out
Users may request not to be contacted at any time by indicating so on their first or any subsequent communications with us.
External Links
Our sites contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
Advertisers
We allow third parties to perform data analytics on User Data and serve ads utilizing the User Data. Third-party vendors or ad networks, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings, and out of the use of third-party vendor's or ad networkâ€™s cookies for interest based advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
Contacting Us
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of our sites, or your dealings with our websites, you can contact us.
