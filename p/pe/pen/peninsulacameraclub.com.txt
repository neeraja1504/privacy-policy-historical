Peninsula Camera Club
Software Pursuits, Inc., the Visual Pursuits provider, is committed to protecting your personal and corporate privacy. The following specifies and outlines our privacy policy.
1. We do not disclose any personal or company information (name, telephone number(s), email address(es), credit card information, etc.) unless specifically authorized to do so by you or an authorized representative of your corporation.
2. We do not disclose any online communications between you and Software Pursuits. The single exception to this rule is when specifically required due to legal processes.
3. We do not disclose any information relating to your activities when accessing any Software Pursuits web site. This includes, but is not limited to, (1) the dissemination of information contained on the Software Pursuits web site, (2) data which may be downloaded from or uploaded to the Software Pursuits web site.
4. We use secure technology, privacy protection controls, and restrictions on employee access in order to safeguard your personal information. Employees who violate our privacy policies are subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of their employment and legal action.
5. We will not disclose any information relating to any user identification, passwords, serial numbers, etc. which may be required for the use of any Software Pursuits, Inc. product or service to any other party without your express consent. The single exception to this policy is the dissemination of serial numbers, product information and other information required by authorized resellers of Software Pursuits, Inc. products.
6. We may record a "cookie" on your machine when you access our web site. We use this cookie to remember who you are so that you do not need to fill out contact information each time you use our websites. If you prevent the saving of the cookie on your machine we will have to ask these questions more frequently. Your contact information is not saved in the cookie, just a value so we can identify you in our databases on our server. Cookies may also be used to pass information between different pages of our web site. This may be necessary, for example, to complete a purchase using our web site. Values saved in such cookies would be meaningless to others.
7. We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads which may appear on our websites. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to you based on your visits to our sites and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the  Google ad and content network privacy policy.
8. When you give us contact information we may e-mail you if we discover a critical problem in our software. Unless you object, we will also send e-mail related to our products and services. You can reply to any of these messages to prevent future e-mailings. We want to use your contact information to provide service, not to be a pest.
9. We retain information in our databases until it is no longer required to provide the service requested of us or your organization. We archive information as a standard backup procedure. Obsolete archives are generally purged after one year or when they are deemed to have no value to you or your organization.
If you have any comment on or have questions concerning our privacy policy, have a concern or policy violation you wish to report, or wish to change or delete the customer information you submitted to Software Pursuits, Inc., please contact us using any of the methods on our  contact us  page.
