Privacy policy
When you use Nphone, Nphone may access to the contact list, photos and documents stored in your mobile phone for the purpose to exchange it with your friends.
The data that Nphone use are anonymous, there are used for the proper functionning of Nphone.
By using Nphone, you grant to Nphone the right to have acces and display all media content (e.g. audio & video recordings and text communications) that you upload, send, or otherwise exchange with Nphone, for the only purposes of operating Nphone service.
Any of the information that Nphone collect from you when you create your Nphone account is used to make sure that you are a real person and to give you access to our services.
Contact list: in Nphone-iOS and Nphone-Android, Nphone automatically imports your contacts’ phone numbers from your phone’s address book.
Photos and other files : We only access to the files stored on your phone when you send it in text messages with Nphone. Such files are only temporary stored.
Audio & videos recordings and text messages : we access and temporary store audio and video packets when you make Nphone calls, but we never read or listen your private voice, video, or text content.
Your personal information are transmitted over internet using TLS encryption protocol. It warrants the security of your information.
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information, or all type of content that you may upload or send with Nphone (e.g. text conversations, calls, videos, photos; etc.).
We do not use cookies.
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
