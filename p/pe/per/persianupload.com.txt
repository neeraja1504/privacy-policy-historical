Welcome to PersianUpload, a free file upload solution. Simply browse, select, and upload!
PersianUpload policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. PersianUpload will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.
PersianUpload will not sell or give your email address to anyone and you will never be placed on any lists. However, PersianUpload may disclose your information when PersianUpload believes in good faith that the law requires it.
PersianUpload may use your email address to inform you about PersianUpload major service changes, important updates, or special promotions.
PersianUpload website record your IP address but uses it only for internal purposes. It will never be given to any other person/company/website for any reason.
PersianUpload website uses cookies only to maintain your information as you move throughout this site. A cookie is a small piece of information used to identify an individual when the person is accessing a site. At no time is any personal information stored in a cookie, nor is any tracking information made available to other sites for any reason.
PersianUpload reserve the right to change this Policy anytime without prior notification. In this case the new Policy will be published at http://www.persianupload.com/info.php?act=privacy_policy
If you have any question, please do not hesitate to Contact us.
