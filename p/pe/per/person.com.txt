Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy is to ensure that your privacy is protected while using our Site.
Please make sure to read the Site's Terms & Conditions as well.
- When you chose to join the site, we ask you to provide us with certain information such as your e-mail address.
- We also collect your IP address.
- Finally, we collect and keep your login and password (the information you need to use the site) as well as any publications you post on the site for others to view. For example: if you send us personal correspondence, such as emails or letters, or if other users or third parties send us correspondence about your activities or postings on the site, we may collect such information into a file which is only accessed by authorized personnel. We may also collect other users' comments about you in our complaints department.
You should know that your e-mail information can not be accessed or used by the public to send you direct messages. All users can send you e-mail only by using Person.com message system.
- We may internally use the personally identifiable information collected on our users to improve our marketing, to improve our content and products, and to customize our site to our users. This allows us to improve our site and meet your needs.
- We may also use the personally identifiable information about you to deliver information to you that, in some cases, is targeted to your interests.
- We may also use your information posted publicly here on other sites that belong to our company.
- We also send periodic informational updates and promos to our consenting users via e-mail.
- Our departments (for example our Customer Service Department) may use personally identifiable information to answer your questions, troubleshoot problems, and enforce our Terms and Conditions.
- We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
Obviously, in case we are required by law enforcement agencies and or in case we are required to enforce intellectual property or other rights, we may disclose your information.
You have the following choices to update or delete your information from our database:
You may log in with your password and update or delete your profile. Please note that the information is not deleted from the Company's database but will be saved under a deleted status and will not be presented. If your account is inactive for 1 year, it is automatically deleted.
To mark your account as deleted:
Go to 'account overview'. At the top of the page press 'Suspend My Account' link.
Person.com users may remove their information from our database if they do not wish to receive future communications or do not wish to be part of the site:
Go to 'account overview' to delete your account. At the top of the page press 'Suspend My Account' link
Your use of our website, including any dispute concerning privacy, is subject to this privacy statement and the Terms and Conditions. BY USING OUR WEBSITE, YOU ARE ACCEPTING THE PRACTICES SET OUT IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY AND OUR TERMS AND CONDITIONS. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time by posting the changes on this page. Your continued use of any portion of our website following posting of the updated privacy statement will constitute your acceptance of the changes.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
support@person.com
