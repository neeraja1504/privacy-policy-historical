Privacy Policy - People's Party of Canada
How we use your personal information
Personal information (name, address, e-mail address, telephone number and other contact information) is used only to communicate with you, or to facilitate your participation as a volunteer. We take great care in the way we store and use it. The People’s Party of Canada (PPC) is a national organization, with a riding-based membership system; due to this your personal information may also be used by our local riding associations. As a registered federal political party, the PPC (including its Electoral District Associations and Riding Associations) is subject to regulation under the Canada Elections Act.
Identification and unsubscribe information
If you received a message from us, you are either: a PPC member, a recent donor or volunteer, you have recently taken action with one of our online tools, or you have an existing relationship with us.
To update or correct the information you provide to us
, please send an email at info@peoplespartyofcanada.ca.
To unsubscribe from our electronic messages or Newsletter
, please click on the opt-out link at the bottom of the last email you received – or send an email at info@peoplespartyofcanada.ca.
Privacy statement
The security of your personal information is important to us. We use security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system. Security audits are periodically conducted to ensure the integrity of our systems.
Sharing and Usage
We do not share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone outside of the Party, without your permission or unless so ordered by a court of law. Information submitted is only available to PPC staff and EDA executives who manages this information for purposes of communicating with you on matters pertaining to PPC activities. These people received a series of guidelines on the use of data and are not allowed to share this information. If you have received unwanted, unsolicited email sent via this system, please forward a copy of that email with your comments at info@peoplespartyofcanada.ca.
Visiting our website
When visiting PeoplesPartyofCanada.ca, your privacy is respected and protected.
Any information collected while you are visiting our website, such as server log data, is managed in accordance with this privacy policy and is protected by applicable law. Server logs include statistical information, such as visitors' IP addresses, time and duration of visits, and pages visited. These statistics may be used to improve your experience on PeoplesPartyofCanada.ca.
In addition, non-personal information may be collected through the use of cookies. Cookies are small text files maintained by your browser in order to remember user settings as you navigate sites. PeoplesPartyofCanada.ca uses cookies, for example, to remind your browser whether you chose to view our website in English or in French. You can also adjust your browser’s settings to refuse cookies, or to accept them on a case-by-case basis.
Links to other websites
Our website contains a small number of links to other websites. The PPC is not responsible for the content or the privacy policies of these websites.
