Real PropertySold.ca - buy sell real estate toronto - Privacy Policy
PropertySold.ca Inc. is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy whether you are browsing for information or conducting business with the PropertySold.ca Inc. electronically.
When you visit PropertySold.ca, we will not collect your personal information unless you choose to use and receive online products and services that require it. For transactions with PropertySold.ca Inc. involving credit cards, PropertySold.ca Inc. uses up-to-date security protocols to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of information submitted by you.
If you choose not to use the Internet to provide personal information to PropertySold.ca, you can contact us by telephone or mail. Please find the appropriate contact details on the Contact page of this website.
Some areas of PropertySold.ca require the use of cookies. Cookies are generally used to make it more convenient for users to access information and navigate the Website. If you choose, they may be used to 'remember' your password and make 'logging in' faster. These types of cookies need to be stored on your computer's hard drive.
PropertySold.ca links to other sites created and maintained by other public and/or private sector organizations. We provide these links solely for your information and convenience. When you follow a link to another Website, other than PropertySold.ca, you are leaving PropertySold.ca and our policies do not necessarily apply.
