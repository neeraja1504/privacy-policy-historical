What is the Purpose of this Privacy Policy?
Promethean respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.  This Privacy Policy explains how Promethean collects, holds, uses and discloses any personal or other information that we collect from you and from third parties or that you otherwise provide to us.  Please read the following carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your personal and non-personal information and how we will treat it. This policy does not apply to third party Web sites or applications to which we provide links from our Service or from the Site.
“Personal Information” as used in this Privacy Policy means information or an opinion about an identified individual, whether true or not, and whether recorded in a material form or not. This includes your name, names of family members, your address, your telephone number, your e-mail address, your screen name or user name, a photograph, video or audio file, information about a child or his/her parents, or personal unique identifiers such as your social security number (US persons only) or date of birth. It also includes sensitive information such as race or ethnic origin, political opinions, membership in political associations, physical or mental health or condition, membership in government program, philosophical beliefs, membership in a professional or trade association or union, sexual orientation or practices, criminal record, genetic information, and biometric information (“Sensitive Personal Information”).
The information we collect and hold about you depends on who you are, including whether you are a teacher, student or parent.

We are not responsible for circumvention of any privacy settings or security measures contained on the Service or the Site.
In order to access any Personal Information, you must set up an account which is protected by a password for your privacy and security. We also use coding practices which take steps to prevent attack on our Service from web browsers and malicious scripts, by processing all actions through several permission verifications checks.
You can help protect against unauthorized access to your account and Personal Information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.
