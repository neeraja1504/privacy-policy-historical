Privacy Policy
We do not collect any personal information from our visitors.
We may register the URL from which you entered our site, your IP address, and your browser type and version, all of which are collected in an anonymous manner without being linked to any of your personal information.
We do not, and never will, make our visitors data available to any outside parties.
If you sign up for service with any of our partners they might collect personal information from you including your name, e-mail address, billing address, credit card or other payment information, and a password. We do however not get access to any of these informations.
If you have any questions about how your privacy is handled, please do not hesitate to contact us.
