Terms and Policies for Project Management Docs
Terms and Policies
Template Usage
All of our project management templates are free for you to use as you see fit. They are intended to be modified to be used for your projects and are provided as a starting point in the development of project management documents within your organization. We hope that you find our templates useful and welcome your comments. Public distribution of our project management templates is only permitted from this site; although you're permitted to redistribute these templates within your organization we do not permit public distribution (i.e. from websites accessible to the public).
