prHWY Privacy Policy
Created: January 1, 2010
Last Updated: January 1, 2010
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. This notice shall apply to all information prHWY.com collects or is submitted by the user on the prHWY.com web site. To make this notice easy to find, we have provided a link to it in the footer section throughout the web site.
INFORMATION COLLECTED AND USE
We are the sole owner of the information collected on the Site unless specifically stated otherwise. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this policy.
prHWY.com collects personally identifiable information during the registration process; email address, chosen screen name, password, etc. These are required for users to participate and create press releases on the web site.
EMAIL
Email addresses will not be provided to a third party without explicit permission. prHWY.com may send out emails concerning related web site news, offers, surveys, or system update notices. Registered members may also receive notification emails from prHWY.com, which informs them of actions (e.g. messages, press release postings, etc.) that have been performed on the web site.
COOKIES
Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are tranferred to your computer's hard drive through your web browser to enable our web site to recognize your browser. prHWY.com uses cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our site.
IP ADDRESS
prHWY.com uses IP addresses to analyze trends, diagnose problems, administer the site, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.
POLICY CHANGES
prHWY.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time without prior notification. Changes to this policy will be updated on this page, and the last updated date above will reflect the date of the change.
