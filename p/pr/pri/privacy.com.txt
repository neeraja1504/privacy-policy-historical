The Information We Collect
Brierley+Partners collects information such as name, email address, mailing address, and company information. We also collect unique identifies such as user name and password as necessary for the Loyalty Programs we support and host.
How We Use the Information we Collect
We use the information we collect to respond to your requests for information, administer your account and send you commercial communications.
Choice
We collect information for our clients, if you are a customer of one of our Clients and would no longer like to be contacted by one of our Clients that use our service, please contact the client that you interact with directly. If you are a Client and would like to update your account please contact us at privacy@brierley.com.
Corporate Anti-Spam Policy
Clients of Brierley+Partners have agreed to distribute commercial e-mail through Brierley+Partners systems for the purpose of their marketing efforts. Brierley+Partners and its Clients have agreed to adhere to the CAN-SPAM Act.
You may chose to stop receiving commercial emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in this emails or contact us at privacy@brierley.com.
If you feel that you have received unsolicited commercial email from Brierley+Partners please send an email to: abuse@brierley.com. Please include or attach the unsolicited e-mail referenced in the abuse e-mail.

We may provide your personal information to companies that provide services to help us with our business activities such as providing analytics or offering customer service. These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process

Website Activity
Brierley+Partners collects anonymous information about visitors to it's corporate web sites and web sites it hosts on behalf of its customers. The type of information collected includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses which is used to identify information like web pages visited, unique visits, types of browsers used, and web browser session-based activity. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.
Use of Cookies
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies on this site and the sites we host and support for our clients. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.
We may use both session ID cookies and/or persistent cookies. We may use session cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our site. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser's "help" file.

If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site, such as contests or surveys, will be limited.
Also, Brierley+Partners may use cookies on our customer web sites in an anonymous fashion to track overall website performance. Additionally, Brierley+Partners may use third party web analytics services to help us track and optimize our website performance. The service uses Scripts and Log Files to track non-personally identifiable information. These third parties are prohibited from using collected data for any purpose other than as a service provider to Brierley+Partners.
Website Security
Information collected through www.brierley.com and client hosted web sites is done via a secure (SSL) web interface and maintained within a secure operating environment. Our team of infrastructure and security professionals continually monitors our web interfaces and ensures technology updates are made regularly to deter any attempts for unwanted intrusion.
We follow generally accepted standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. No method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, however. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our Web site, you can contact us at privacy@brierley.com.
Access
If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may correct, update, amend, delete/remove, by making the change on our member information page or by contacting us by telephone, email, or postal mail at the contact information listed below. We will respond to your request to access within 30 days.
Data Retention:
Brierley+Partners will retain your personal data and data we process on behalf of our Clients for as long as needed provide you servicers or services to our Clients. Brierley+Partners will retain and use this personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
Appended Information:
The Brierley+Partners may supplement/append the data it collects for their Clients with additional data received from third party sources.
Links
The Brierley+Partners corporate web site may include links to other Internet sites that are not operated or controlled by Brierley+Partners. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. This may additionally be true for web sites hosted by Brierley+Partners on behalf of our customers.
Social Media (Features) and Widgets:
Our Web site includes Social Media Features, such as the Facebook and Twitter button [and Widgets, such as the Share this button or interactive mini-programs that run on our site]. These Features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our Site. Your interactions with these Features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement hosted at www.brierley.com. We may additionally post these changes in other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.
