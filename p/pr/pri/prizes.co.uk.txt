Privacy Policy | Prizes.co.uk
Last updated: 27th June 2017
This policy (together with this sites Prize Terms and Conditions and any other documents referred to therein) provides an explanation of what information this site collects, how it is collected and how it may be processed (and may be disclosed) by Quantance Media LTD, company no. 9014771, (“us”, “our”, “we”). By submitting your details to us via this website, Prizes.co.uk (the “Site”) you consent to our collection and use of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Applicability and third party websites and services
This Privacy Policy only applies to our website and does not apply to third party websites you may link to from this Site or third party web pages or other online services which may be displayed on this Site. If you click on a link to a third party website or service, or supply information to a third party website or service that is placed within our webpage, you are interacting with that third party and this Privacy Policy will not apply. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those third party websites and services.
We may collect personal information from you in the following ways:
When you provide it to us. We may collect your information when you submit respond to our surveys or competitions, when you request further information from an advertiser, or when you otherwise provide us with your personal information, including, but not limited to, your name, email address, home address, contact information or any other information you may provide;
From third parties. We may collect your information from third parties, including websites which publish our ads and offers, as well as other online and offline sources such as identity verification services and other service providers and vendors and we may combine such information with other information you have provided to us;
Automatically. We may collect your information automatically, based on interaction with our site, using cookies and other technology (as described below).
Types of information we may collect
Personally identifying information: We may collect personally identifying information when you provide it to us as described above. Examples of the types of personally identifying information you may provide include, but are not limited to, your name, email address, telephone number and postal address, and any other information you provide to use which can be used to identify you. You can choose not to provide us with such information, but some of our services and certain parts of our Site may not be accessible to you.
Other information we collect: We may automatically collect information about your interaction with our Site, our advertisements and other promotional content using technologies like cookies and web beacons, and via our web server's log files. Examples of information which may be collected by our various technologies include browser information, your device IP address, whether you clicked on one of our advertisements, pages you've visited on the Site, which website you came from, date and time stamp information, and other navigation and click-through information. Although information we collect through these tracking devices is not always personally identifiable when collected, we may combine such information with personally identifiable information like your name or email address or other information that could be used to identify you.
How we may use and share the information that we collect
The information that we collect about you may be used by us and shared with third parties in any manner and for any purpose permitted by law. This may include disclosing (with or without remuneration) the personally identifiable information that we collect to third parties for their marketing purposes. Once we provide your information to a third party for any reason, we do not control and are not responsible for that third party’s use or disclosure of such information.
Examples of the ways we may use the information that we collect include:
to contact you about products or services that you have requested or that you have requested further information on;
to contact you regarding products or services that we, our group of companies or our carefully selected third parties think may interest you;
to notify you about your account details if you register to use a secure part of the Site;
to send you information you have asked for and to respond to any requests, enquiries or complaints you may make to us;
to contact you to ask for feedback about our services and the Site.

Cookies and Other Technology
Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer or device by your web browser. We use cookies to store information so that you do not have to re-enter your information upon revisiting one of our Sites. Cookies may also enable us to provide personalized content, monitor our marketing campaigns, track your entries and submissions and gather information about your interactions with our Site or with one of our advertisements. Cookies may be set by us or our business partners and service providers when you visit our Site or when you interact with one of our advertisements or other content. We may use both session cookies, which terminate when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which may remain on your computer until you delete them.
We may also track your use of our Site, advertisements and other content through web beacons (also called pixel tags or clear gifs). Web beacons can be used for various purposes, including helping us improve our Site, services and marketing communications.

Personal Information Transferred to the U.S.
The information that we may collect from you may be transferred to, stored and processed at, a destination outside the European Economic Area (“EEA”) including the U.S. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this data transferring, storing and processing. We will take reasonable steps to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy. The transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure and we cannot guarantee the security of your data therefore any information you provide to us is at your own risk.
How We Protect Your Information
We use commercially reasonable safeguards and technologies to protect the information that we collect and use, such safeguards meet the adequacy requirements of the European Data Protection Directive. We also expect our business partners and service providers to do the same.
Your choices regarding collection of your information
Providing Information. You can choose not to provide us with your personally identifiable information. Note, however, that you may not have access to certain of our products and services if you decide not to provide us with your information.
Opting Out of Our Promotional Emails. You may opt-out of receiving marketing and promotional emails from us or our partners at any time by using the opt-out mechanism contained in such emails (also referred to as “unsubscribe”), or by submitting your request using our Contact Form Please allow 10 business days for us to process your opt-out request. Also, note that opting out of receiving promotional email communications will affect only future communications from us. You may opt-out from our further sharing of your information by submitting your request via our Contact Form. If we already provided your information to a third party before you changed your preferences or updated your information, or if you requested to receive offers from third party advertisers directly on our site, you may have to change your preferences directly with that third party as well.
Subject Access Requestsd: In accordance with the European Data Privacy Directive you have the right to access or correct the information held about you. You may request information we may hold about you by contacting us via our Contact Form.
Children's Privacy
This Site is intended for general audiences, and is not intended for use by persons under the age of 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will delete it. If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us using the information below.
Changes to our privacy policy
We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time by posting an updated Privacy Policy to our Site without advance notice to you. We encourage you to check this page often for updates and revisions. Any updated Privacy Policy will be effective immediately upon posting.
