Privacy Policy Pricedealsindia.com
Please read our privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. The privacy policy described here is for PriceDealsIndia.com only. Once you click external links from this site we advise you to review the privacy policies posted at the respective sites.
Collection of Information
We collect personally identifiable information, like names, Email ID, mobile phone numbers etc to fulfil you specific request. And, this information is used only for completing the specific request.
We will not share your information to any third parties. Your personally identifiable information is kept secure.
Only authorized employees, agents and contractors have access to this information.
Provision to unsubscribe from Emails and newsletters is also given.
We reserve the right to make changes to this policy. Any changes to this policy will be posted in this page..
