Privacy Policy - Free Online Games
Privacy Policy
This Web site is owned and maintained by PlayJolt.com. Your privacy is very important to us. Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should understand our policy and how we gather and use information that may be collected from you. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and its use.
We use a third party company to gather nonpersonally identifying tracking information by counting the number of page views for a given page on our site and to provide aggregate data across our site. The type of data collected is your IP (Internet protocol) address, your Internet Service Provider, the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer, Firefox, or Google Chrome), which pages you visit, and for how long, your operating system (cell phone vs windows, for example), and the country you are from. This allows us to tailor our content to your needs and to help our advertisers and sponsors better understand the demographics of our audience.
PlayJolt.com provides its service free to users and derives its revenue from third party
advertising companies. If you click on a 3rd party advertisement a cookie may track the origin of any subsequent purchases that originated from our site.
How Cookies Work
A cookie is collected when you visit a Web site. The cookie itself contains no personal identifying information, but it does enable us and advertisers to track user traffic patterns. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. If you've set your browser to notify you before accepting cookies, you will receive a warning message with each cookie. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use this site.
If you have any questions don't hesitate to contact us at webmaster@playjolt.com.
