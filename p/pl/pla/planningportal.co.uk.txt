Privacy Policy
Our Privacy Policy
The personal details you give us will be used to provide you with relevant information specifically for your job requirements. All information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, leased or rented to companies outside the Wolters Kluwer Group. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
1. Notice and Disclosure
Croner.CCH collects the following information regarding visitors to the web site: domain name, name, information regarding what pages are accessed, information volunteered by you (such as survey information), e-mail address and site registrations.
Croner.CCH will analyse the web site data to constantly improve the value of the materials available for our subscribers and registered users, where possible tailoring this material to individual requirements.
2. Use of cookies
A cookie is a small data file that certain web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited, but the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself.
We do not use cookies on this site, but if you subscribe to one of our LivePublish online information packages, you will be asked to receive cookies in order to access the LivePublish service.
3. Privacy of our e-mail lists
Croner.CCH maintains several e-mail lists to keep subscribers, registered users, the press, professional institutions and other interested parties informed about new products or services.
Registered users join our mailing lists by signing up through a form on the web site. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease e-mail addresses to any companies outside the Wolters Kluwer Group.
