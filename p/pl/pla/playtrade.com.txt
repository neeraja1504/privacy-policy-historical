Rakuten.co.uk Shopping : Privacy Policy
Dated: 2016/02/15
This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect and how we use it. It relates to your use of the following services (the "Services") in each case as applicable:
Marketplace
RMail
AdSales
SuperPoints
Payment Services (if you are a Merchant using Marketplace)
WHO CONTROLS MY INFORMATION?
Any information that you provide to us through your use of the Services is controlled jointly by Rakuten Europe S.à.r.l. (registered under number B136664 at the Luxembourg Trade and Companies Register, with the VAT number LU22375408), and its fellow group companies, Rakuten US Inc., Rakuten Asia Pte Ltd., Rakuten Deutschland GmbH, and Rakuten Inc. If you are a Merchant using Marketplace, then payment information collected in order to process Transactions and make and receive payments from Buyers is collected and controlled by Rakuten Europe Bank SA ("REB"). Rakuten Europe S.à.r.l., Rakuten US Inc., Rakuten Asia Pte Ltd., Rakuten Deutschland GmbH, Rakuten Inc and REB are together "Rakuten" or "we" for the purposes of this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about data we hold about you, you can contact us at:
Address: 2 Rue du Fosse, Luxembourg, L-1536 LUXEMBOURG
Email: privacy@rakuten.co.uk
THE DATA WE COLLECT
We collect and store certain information as a result of your interactions with our Services. The information we may collect includes your:
your name and chosen nickname
e-mail address, delivery address, billing address, telephone number, bank account details
credit or debit card number and expiry date or details of any other payment method you use
the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer or mobile device to the Internet
your login and password
your computer or mobile device and connection information such as your browser type and version, your operating system and platform
the full Uniform Resource Locators (URL) clickstream to, through and from the relevant Service (including date and time)
your cookie number
any products you viewed or searched for
your purchase/sale history, personal preferences
HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
We use the information we collect to:
provide you with the Services
provide you with your Rakuten ID account, which enables you to use other services provided by Rakuten group companies
process transactions and keep our business records
communicate with you
customise your experience when using the Services
analyse how you and other users use our Services
improve our Services and develop new services
detect and investigate fraud and other illegal activities and potential breaches of the terms and conditions that apply to the use of our Services
provided you have opted-in, notify you about offers of products, services or information that might be of particular interest to you.
if you have consented, contact you about surveys and user groups
offer you the opportunity to take part in competitions and prize promotions
enforce our legal rights
comply with any legal requirements to which we are subject
We may combine your information with information lawfully obtained from other third party sources and use it for the above purposes.
We will not sell or rent your personal information to any third party without your express consent.
WHO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH
We may share this information with carefully selected and trusted third parties, including other Rakuten group companies and technical and other service providers to us.
If you are a Buyer and engage in a transaction with a third party Merchant your email address, postal address and contact information may be shared with the Merchant for the purpose of completing the transaction. You will also receive transactional emails from the Merchant confirming the order, including dispatch or eventual refund, and follow-up invitations to leave feedback for the Merchant.
During any contact with Merchants, we strongly encourage Buyers to ask questions before you disclose personal information which the Merchant may not have obtained during the process of the order. Merchants are prohibited from asking a buyer for their payment card details, bank details or other payment method details. If Merchants and Buyers share information directly, that information is not governed by this Privacy Policy.
If lawfully requested, we may also share information with law enforcement agencies and/or third parties in respect of information requests for criminal investigations and alleged illegal activities.
Your information may also be transferred to another company in the event of sale of the whole or part of our business to a third party.
We ensure that all business/technical partners, Merchants and relevant third parties are bound by contractually binding promises to process information we share with them in accordance with our directions, this Privacy Policy and all applicable data protection laws.
TRANSFERS OUTSIDE OF THE EUROPEAN ECONOMIC AREA
Your personal information may be transferred outside of the European Economic Area ("EEA") to countries which may not have equivalent protections for your data as your country of residence. If we transfer data outside of the EEA, we will endeavour to ensure that your rights and freedoms in respect of the processing of your personal data are adequately and appropriately protected.
MARKETING PREFERENCES
When you set up your Account you will be given an opportunity to set your communication preferences. You can also click 'Unsubscribe' at the bottom of our marketing emails to stop receiving them. Each time you make a purchase from a Merchant you will also have the opportunity to select the communications you want to receive from that Merchant.
MOBILE PRIVACY
Some of our Services may be offered by us as mobile applications, and you may also be able to access some of our Services using a mobile device through a mobile optimized website, or otherwise. This Privacy Policy applies to all such mobile access and use of mobile devices. When you access some of our Services using a mobile device or download any of our mobile applications, with your consent, we may receive information such as geo-location information and information unique to your mobile device. Please be reminded that most mobile devices allow you to control or disable location services in the device’s settings menu. We recommend that you contact your mobile service carrier or the manufacturer of your mobile device if you have any questions about how to disable your device’s location services.
LINKS TO OTHER SERVICES
Please note that we do provide links to other services, which may not be governed by this Privacy Policy. You should view the respective privacy policies of those services for further information.
ACCESSING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
You have the right to access your personal information that we hold by accessing your Account and, if necessary, have it amended or deleted. Some information, such as that displayed in a customer review or within Merchant feedback cannot be removed by you once given.
CLOSURE OF ACCOUNTS
If you close your Account, we will retain personal information from closed Accounts in order to comply with our obligations under applicable laws, collect any fees owed and to assist with any investigations we may need to perform.
USE OF COOKIES
Please see our Cookies Notice for information on how we use cookies.
CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY
We may occasionally amend this Privacy Policy to account for changes to our Service and the way we handle your information. If this Privacy Policy is amended we will give you reasonable notice. Changes will apply from the point of time notified to you.
