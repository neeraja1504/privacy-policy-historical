Privacy policy - PlagTracker.com
This Policy Statement explains our policy regarding the use of all information collected about visitors to the Plagtracker.com website. The raw information that is collected is used to help us improve our website. This includes customization of content, formatting and layout. The information collected is never rented, shared or sold to any third party. Plagtracker.com web servers automatically collect information when you visit our website. This information includes your browser type, operating system, IP address, the duration your visit, the number of pages visited, and time spent on each page. We may employ cookies in order to customize web content based upon the aforementioned information.
Plagtracker.com's Use of Optional Information
For some of our services, you may be asked to provide your name, e-mail address, and other personal information. This information will only be used for its designated purpose and will never be rented, shared or sold to any third parties. Plagtracker.com reserves the right to make use of any demographic information for internal marketing research purposes.
Future Use of Information Previously Gathered
Plagtracker.com reserves the right to use any previously gathered information for new, unanticipated uses not covered in this statement. All changes to practice and policy will be noted on this page. Plagtracker.com reserves the right to use data collected from the time of the policy change for these new purposes. Please ensure that you check this web page on a regular basis. Plagtracker.com has installed and maintains the appropriate security measures to protect all data from loss, unwanted alteration or misuse. On Plagtracker.comyou will find links to a number of government and other external websites. None of these links should be construed as Plagtracker.com's endorsement of the accuracy, content, opinion, policy, procedures, products, services, viewpoints or accessibility of the linked website. Upon leaving Plagtracker.com, you are subject to all the terms, conditions and policies of the linked website.
Disclosures Prohibited
Information acquired in relation to this article is for the exclusive use of the commissioner to discharge their duties hereunder. All collected information is not for the public domain and cannot be used in a legal action unless the commissioner is a party to the action notwithstanding any provisions of law. Information shall be made available to all parties in making and determining a claim for benefits. At the commissioner's discretion, information may be made available to all parties affected in effecting placement. Plagtracker.com constantly undergoes change as we strive to improve our customers' browsing experience. As new technology and services come into use, our policy will be updated accordingly. Refer to this page regularly to learn of any changes.
Information Access
Private individuals have the right to request a copy of all the personal information held by Plagtracker.com to correct any inaccuracies after payment of a fee of $35.00 (USD). Upon receipt of such a request from our support team, we will grant you access to maintained information in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
