Plex Capital
When you visit our sites, we may obtain personal information about you when you register on our website or when you contact our support team.
When you register at Plex Capital Inc., we may collect some of your information such as name, country, and any other appropriate data. All this information may be needed to restore access to your account in case you lose or forget your password.
Plex Capital Inc. may create cookies to your computer and consequently use them. We will use session cookies to keep track of you whilst you navigate the website while the persistent cookies to enable our website to recognize you when you visit us. You can delete your cookies in the browser option / setting.
Plex Capital Inc.will never share your individual personal information with anyone without your authorization. Your information will be private and will not be released to the third parties.
If you have receive unnecessary or spontaneous email sent from Plex Capital Inc or claiming to be sent from Plex capital Inc, please do not hesitate to forward a copy of that email together with your comments to plexcapital@yahoo.com for review.
