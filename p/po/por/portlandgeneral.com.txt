Privacy Policy | PGE
PGE collects anonymous, aggregate information from all visitors to our website. This type of information is not linked to personally-identifiable information and is used to generate statistical reports about the general use of our website.
We store information that we collect through log files to create a profile of our users. Log files record internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks. We use this data to analyze trends, administer the website, track customer movement through the site and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. A profile is a set of stored information detailing how individual customers use the website. It is used to help us improve the experience of our customers and maintain a better website.
As a visitor to this website, your identity will remain anonymous unless you register for online account services, enter a sweepstakes or enroll in a program. In such cases you will be notified that personal information, such as your name, address, phone number and email address, is required. We may use this information, along with third party sources, to provide information to PGE based on your preferences. We may tie personally identifiable information to your profile to  statistically analyze website usage; to improve our content and product offerings; and to customize our website’s content, layout and services. We may also use such information to deliver content to you that, in some cases, is targeted to your interests, such as banners and messages concerning new programs, services, promotions and events. In addition, this information may be used to provide email messages suited to your needs and interests. See “email communications to customers” below for opt-out instructions.
If you are starting a new electric service account, we will ask for additional personal information for credit verification and security purposes only. This information will be used to provide a requested service, to facilitate purchases and to provide you with email communications. See “email communications to customers” below for opt-out instructions.
If you choose to use our service to share pages on our site with a friend, we will ask you to provide your friend’s email address. We will automatically send a one-time email to your friend with the requested information. PGE does not store this information.
PGE will not sell or rent the information collected at this site to any party or share it in ways different from those disclosed in this statement.
