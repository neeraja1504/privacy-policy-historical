Privacy Policy - Pornhub Toys®
If you have questions about this privacy policy you may contact support@pornhubtoys.com. This privacy policy explains how we collect, protect, use and share information gathered from and about you. We hope this disclosure will help increase your confidence in pornhubtoys.com. This privacy policy does not apply to information that you may submit to us offline, or to websites maintained by other companies or organizations to which we may link.
Type of information collected and use of information collected
In the event you subscribe and make use of any service provided via the website (either in connection with any other website and/or app or not), we shall collect and store personal information as provided by you, such as address, e-mail address, zip code, country, username, password and IP address. In the event you make use of any part of the website for which a fee is payable to Pornhub Toys, Pornhub Toys shall collect and store personal information necessary to be able to bill the fees payable.
Refusal to provide personal information to  Pornhub Toys
If you choose not to submit personally identifiable information when requested, you may not be able to access all or certain areas of our website and it may considerably limit the services we can provide you.
Use of cookies and other means to collect information
You will know when personally identifiable information is collected because you will be asked to fill out a form. However, we may collect some information about you by using data collection technology, so that it may not be readily apparent to you that it is being collected. For instance, when you come to our website, your IP address is collected so that we know where to send information you are requesting.
An IP address is a number that is used by computers on the network to identify your computer every time you log on to the Internet. A “cookie” is a small file or record-keeping device that websites often store on a user’s computer. You can disable cookies or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent to your computer. We do not use IP addresses or cookies to collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, or to link this information to personally identifiable information already collected about you. This information may be used to make our site more interesting and useful to you.
Additionally, we may combine this information with personally identifiable information to identify a visitor in order to protect our service, website or visitors to the website and/or apps.
Sharing personal information
We may share any non-personal information with third parties, as far as such sharing is necessary for the proper or optimal performance of any part of the website or app. In addition, we might share certain non-personal or anonymized information with third parties to enable us to track certain use of the websites and/or app and parts thereof. From time to time, we may employ and share your personal information with vendors who use it solely to provide support for the internal operations of our website and/or app (e.g., technical support and network delivery). Any such entities will not use your personally identifiable information for any other purpose and have agreed to maintain the confidentiality, security, and integrity of the personal information they obtain from us. It is our policy not to share, sell or rent your personal information to any company not directly related to the manufacturing or publishing of Pornhub Toys products.
Links to third party sites and/or apps
You should be aware that while you are on a website and/or in apps operated by Pornhub Toys, you may be directed to other sites and/or apps that are beyond our control and for which we are not responsible. Because we cannot guarantee that the privacy policies of these websites and/or apps meet our standards, we recommend that you read the privacy policy of the website to which you link before you submit any personal information.
Access and opt-out
If you wish to review, update, or delete any personal information that we may have collected about you, please contact us via email at support@pornhubtoys.com.
Safeguards
We maintain appropriate safeguards that ensure the security, integrity and privacy of the personally identifiable information we collect from all visitors. Personal information collected by us is stored in secure operating environments that are not available to the public and are only accessible by authorized employees. We also have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
Questions and complaints
If you have any questions, complaints or comments regarding our privacy policy or practices, please contact us via email at support@pornhubtoys.com. By using this site, you signify your assent to the Pornhub Toys online privacy policy.
If you do not agree to this privacy policy, please do not use this website.
We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. please check back from time to time to ensure you are aware of these changes. Your continued use of this website will signify your acceptance of these changes.
