Privacy Policy
This document details the usage of data collected by PornTubeSE. We do not collect any personal information from our unregistered users. Registered users may provide information on an opt-in basis. We do not require an email address in order to sign up.
Data collected
Registered and non-registered users can browse videos and images without giving up any of their personal information. An IP address is recorded on registration and in the server logs for security reasons. Registration is required in order to upload image galleries and favorite videos. Your email address and any other information will not be sold or used for any other purpose.
Cookies
Cookies are used only for the following purposes - keeping you logged into the site when you sign in with your PornTubeSE account and preventing more than one popunder from displaying during your session.
Third Party Cookies
Third party cookies are set by Google Analytics to track pageviews.
Log file information
Server logs are rotated daily regarding visitor IP addresses, web useragents, referring urls, and the time of visit.
Disclosure of information
PornTubeSE will not disclose or use any of your information unless required to do so by a law enforcement agency or in the event of a Terms of Use violation.
Security
PornTubeSE takes the following steps to maintain a secure website:
- Google safebrowsing tools
- Daily maintenance
You use PornTubeSE at your own risk, however we do everything in our power to make your experience a safe and secure one.
