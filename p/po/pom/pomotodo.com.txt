Privacy
Privacy
Information collection and usage
We would record your personal information when you sign up, modify account information and using Pomotodo. These information would be used to provide, maintain, protect and improve Pomotodo, or to develop new services.
Information publication and sharing
We would not release or sell your personal information to anyone. Except:
You permit a third party to share the information;
We cannot provide your required services without releasing your personal information.
We need to provide information to those companies who serve you on behalf of Pomotodo. (These companies do not have right to use your personal identify information, unless you are notified);
We are forced by legal processes;
We find that you have violated any of our service terms.
Cookies
Pomotodo uses Cookies to store user data and customize web page contents. So please enable Cookies on your web browser.
Advertisement
We do not cooperate with any advertising service.
Revision of privacy policy
We may revise the privacy policy. Whenever there is a big revision of privacy policy about personal information, we would make conspicuous notification at our website.
