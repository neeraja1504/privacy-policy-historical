Privacy Policy
PRIVACY
Power-level.net is committed to protecting your private information. No data will be transmitted to any party unless required to do so by law. Power-level.net gathers private identifiable information including your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number.
Please notice that if you directly reveal private identifiable information or private sensitive data through the Company's Chats, communities, or public online forums, this information maybe gathered and used by others. The company improves you to observe the privacy statements of websites you choose to gather, use and share your information. The company is not in charge of the privacy statements or other content on website outside of the Company's web page.
Private information is used for the Company's website to operate and deliver the services as you requested .The company's website also use your private identifiable information to notify you of other products or services available from the Company and its affiliates when requested through its mailing list. The company can also contact you via inspection to acquire your idea of current services or of involving new services that may be offered.
The company does not sell, rent or lease its client lists to third parties. Occasionally, the Company may contact you represent of external business partners about a particular supply that may be of interest to you. In cases, your only private identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. Otherwise, the Company may share data with trusted partners to complete the perform statistical analysis. All the third parties are banned to use your private information except to provide these services to the Company, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
The Company save track of the websites and pages our client's visit, in order to analyze what websites and services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising to clients whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.
The company's websites will reveal your personal information , without clew, just if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on the Company or the website; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of www.Power-level.net.com, but not limited to this Company's websites; and, (c) act under urgent circumstances to protect the private safety of users of the Company, its websites, or the public.
