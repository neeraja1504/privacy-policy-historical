Privacy | Ptengine
At Ptmind Inc we recognize that your privacy is of paramount importance. We value your trust and confidence and are determined to protect the privacy of our clients and visitors to our website including the information we gather. Therefore, we have developed a Privacy Policy that covers how we collect, use, disclose, transfer and store your information. Please read the following carefully and do not hesitate to contact us for any queries.
Ptengine (the "Service") refers to all websites, properties and services owned or operated by Ptmind Inc (the "Company"). Below is a briefing on how Ptengine collects information from you or your computer/device about visitors to sites tracked by Ptengine ("Visitor Information") as well as the information it gathers from Ptengine clients who visit www.Ptengine.com and use the service to track their website ("Client Information").
Please note that Ptmind uses its own service, Ptengine, to collect information that will allow insight into user activity on www.Ptengine.com.
1. Collection and Use of Personal Information
Personal information is data that can be used to uniquely identify or contact a single person.
i. What personal information we collect:
When registering with Ptengine we may obtain the following types of information from you (including for the purposes of obtaining a free trial of Ptengine services) or concerning your computer/device which may include information that may itself be used to identify and/or contact you (“Personally Identifiable Information”).
. Name
. Email Address
. Phone Number
. Website Name and URL
. Company Name (optional)
ii. Ptengine may also receive certain information that may be provided by your browser or mobile device including:
. Browser Information
. Operating System Information
. Mobile Device Information
. IP Address
. Pages Accessed
. Time of Visit
. Time of Last Visit
. Geographical Location
. Referring Site
2. Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information
We may also collect non-personal information – data in a form that does not permit direct association with any specific individual. We may collect, use, transfer and disclose non-personal information for any purpose. This is so that we can better understand customer behavior and improves our products, services and advertising. Aggregated data is considered non-personal information for the purposes of this Privacy Policy.
3. Visitors to Ptmind Clients’ Websites
Our Clients may use customized and proprietary Ptengine’s code, software and/or services (“Service”) to obtain information regarding the activities that users engage in while visiting their web pages (“Client’s User Data”). When a user visits a website that uses Ptengine’s Service, Ptengine codes contained on the website contacts Ptmind’s servers and enables Ptmind to collect Client’s User Data.
Ptengine’s collection and analysis of Client’s User Data helps our Clients to evaluate and understand how their users are interacting with their websites and potentially to modify their websites in order to make them more valuable or useful for their users. Ptmind’s Clients determine the types of Client’s User Data that are sent to Ptengine’s servers for analysis. This data may include or be linked to Personally Identifiable Information depending on how our clients use Ptengine Services on its website(s).
In addition, Ptengine may collect certain information about a Client’s Visitor’s computer that may be provided by the Visitor’s browsers. This includes browser information, operating system information, mobile device information, IP address, time of visit and the referring website, application, or service. Such information is collected even though not determined by the Client.
Because Clients have the sole discretion to determine what information Ptengine collects from Clients’ users, this Privacy Policy does not apply to any Client’s User Data, including Personally Identifiable information that Ptmind may collect, obtain or access in connection with operating the Ptengine service.
Ptmind recommends that each client obtain any necessary consent from visitors to its website(s), post an online privacy policy that provides each visitor with notice of its practices regarding data collection, use and disclosure.
4. Storing and transferring your personal information
4.1 Security. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, change or damage. All personal information we collect will be stored on secure servers. All electronic transactions entered into via our Website will be protected by SSL encryption technology.
4.2 International Transfers of your Personal Information. The personal information we collect may be transferred to and stored in countries outside of the jurisdiction you are in where we and other Ptmind group companies or our third-party providers have operations. If you are located in the European Union (“EU“), your personal information may be processed outside of the EU; these international transfers of your personal information are made pursuant to appropriate safeguards, such as standard data protection clauses adopted by the European Commission. If you wish to enquire further about the safeguards used, please contact us using the details set out at the end of this privacy policy.
4.3 We will take reasonable steps to ensure that your personal information is treated securely and in accordance with applicable law and this privacy policy.
5. YOUR RIGHTS IN RESPECT OF your personal information
5.1 If you are located in the EU, in accordance with applicable privacy law, you have the following rights in respect of your personal information that we hold:
(a) Right of access. The right to obtain access to your personal information.
(b) Right to rectification. The right to obtain rectification of your personal information without undue delay where that personal information is inaccurate or incomplete.
(c) Right to erasure. The right to obtain the erasure of your personal information without undue delay in certain circumstances, such as where the personal information is no longer necessary in relation to the purposes for which it was collected or processed.
(d) Right to restriction. The right to obtain the restriction of the processing undertaken by us on your personal information in certain circumstances, such as where the accuracy of the personal information is contested by you, for a period enabling us to verify the accuracy of that personal information.
5.2 If you wish to exercise one of these rights, please contact us using the contact details at the end of this privacy policy. You may also review and edit the personal information you have submitted to us by logging into your account on our Website.
5.3 You also have the right to lodge a complaint to the Supervisory Authority in your jurisdiction.
6. Cookies and Other Technologies
Ptmind uses first party cookies (small text files that Ptengine stores locally on the user’s computer) on our client’s website. We use cookies to access information in order to: identify unique visitors, assess usage patterns, perform traffic analysis, identify visitors’ preferences, diagnose problems with our servers, analyze trends and otherwise administer Ptmind products and services. Cookies placed on the user’s computer do not include Personally Identifiable Information such as name, phone number, e-mail address or mailing address, nor does Ptengine link cookies to such Personally Identifiable Information in its servers or databases. Internet Protocol (IP) addresses are considered personal information and are not individually targeted or disclosed but instead are shown as region or city.
Ptengine does not take responsibility for failure by you or your browser to accurately implement or communicate your browser preferences or settings.
For more information on amending your cookie settings, refer to your browser instructions. If you choose not to opt out of Ptengine cookies there will be a presumption of your consent.
7. Disclosure to Third Parties
Pt mind never sells or shares any Visitor Information with individuals or companies other than the specific client on whose website Ptengine collected the Visitor Information. All data Ptengine collects belongs to Ptmind.
The information is provided to clients in the form of aggregated reports and/or statistics that identify collective behavior of visitors to the client’s website, as opposed to the behavior or activity of specific or identified individuals.
If Ptmind becomes involved in a merger, acquisition or any form of sale of some or all of its assets, we will provide notice before personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
8. Protection of Personal Information
Ptmind takes adequate precautions to safeguard your personal information against loss, theft and misuse, as well as against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.
9. Integrity and Retention of Personal Information
At Ptmind we will retain your personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
10. Payment
When purchasing our product, payment will be requested via Stripe. Under no circumstances will we hold sensitive payment details such as your credit card number, expiry date or security code. All transactions are handled through Stripe. For more information on Stripe’s privacy policy please refer to: https://stripe.com/us/privacy/
No payment information will be requested of you when registering for the free trial.
11. Policy Concerning Children Under Age 13
Ptmind does not knowingly or intentionally collect Personally Identifiable Information from children under 13 or from websites that are directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have collected the personal information of a child under 13, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.
12. Changes to this Privacy Policy
Any significant changes to this privacy policy will be made aware to you via our login page for 14 days.
13. Queries
If you have any questions or concerns regarding Ptmind’s privacy policy please Contact Us.
