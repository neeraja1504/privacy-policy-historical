PTC Investigations: Privacy Policy
Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. If you do not agree with this policy, then you should stop using our service and our site. The following outlines our privacy policy.
When you leave a comment on our blog site, or contact us through email, a IP address is recorded for security purposes. Our site is targeted against scammers, and occasionally they will try and send us false info or leave false comments. In order to combat fraud we monitor those who try and leave fake info or comments on our site. Your IP will not be disclosed or shared to the general public.
Email addresses - If you choose to post your email address in our comment section it is at your own risk to do so. However we advise against it. We will not be held responsible if you disclose your email address in our comment section.
If you are a owner who has scammed or owns a scamming site, users will ask for a contact email. If the whois info or internet is showing your email address we will share it with others within our rights to do so. For it has become a legal matter and any info to combat fraud and scams will be shared with members or victims of the site in question.
If you do not want us to post the comment and want it to be for our eyes only be sure to state this at the start of the comment.
If you send us a complaint about a site through our email system or comment form, please be aware that we will use this as evidence against this site. The personal info will be removed or "XX"ed out. That includes email address, your name, screen names, and financial info. All we need is proof that a complaint was made and the statement given. Usually a screen shot or a quotation will be posted in the review to warn others about the site.
We are committed to conducting our blog site in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.
