Privacy
PadiAct is a service provided by PadiCode. PadiCode fully respects your privacy and confidentiality. We also encourage our clients, partners and website visitors to follow the applicable laws and continuously prohibit spamming and unethical marketing activities.
Any personal data that you may provide or that we collect from you will not be disclosed to others.
We collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, company and website address to create a PadiAct account for you and to be used for any necessary communication between PadiCode with you. We also collect the data automatically about your computer hardware and software which can include your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses that we use for the operation of PadiCode service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of PadiCode products.
Simply said, we do not intend to sell or to distribute your data to any third parties.
If you have any questions or comments, please contact us at legal@padicode.com.
