ParaEcho - Privacy Policy
Privacy Statement
Any information collected on this website is in the control of the user. We do not collect any information you do not provide to us. You have the right to revoke that permission at any time, as we have the right to suspend you from using our services if you misuse our product.
We do not share the information you provide through this website unless you provide permission to do so (in apps like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc). We do not collect and sell any information gathered through this website. All information remains the sole property & responsibility of the owner.
The only users who have unfiltered access to your accounts are the SuperAdmins of this website. No other users are granted access to your information unless you provide it to them through the Settings feature of your account.
We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of ParaEcho if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:
- meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
- enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations.
- detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.
- protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of ParaEcho, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.
This policy applies at all times when you are in use of ParaEcho. If you are using apps such as Facebook, Twitter, etc then you will need to consult their privacy policies.
The terms of this privacy policy may change and we encourage you to consult this page or contact us if you have questions.
