Types of Information GMA Deals Collects
Non-Personal Information
Non-personal information is data about usage and service operation that is not directly associated with a specific personal identity. GMA Deals may collect and analyze non-personal information to evaluate how visitors use GMA Deals’ website.
Aggregate Information
GMA Deals may gather aggregate information, which refers to information your computer automatically provides to GMA Deals and that cannot be tied back to you as a specific individual. Examples include referral data (the websites you visited just before and just after GMA Deals site), the pages viewed, time spent at GMA Deals website, and Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you access the Internet. For example, when you request a page from one of our sites, our servers log your IP address to create aggregate reports on user demographics and traffic patterns and for purposes of system administration.
Personal Information
Personal information is information that is associated with your name or personal identity. GMA Deals uses personal information to better understand your needs and interests and to provide you with better service. You may be able to request information, subscribe to mailing lists, participate in online discussions, provide feedback or submit information into registries on GMA Deals website. The types of personal information you provide to us on these pages may include name, address, phone number, company name or e-mail address.
How We Use Your Information
GMA Deals may use non-personal information in the aggregate in order to improve our services and for business and administrative purposes. Non-personal information that GMA Deals collects about you may be combined by GMA Deals with other information available to GMA Deals through third parties for research and measurement purposes, including measuring the effectiveness of GMA Deals’ services and/or services offered by GMA Deals’ business partners. This information from other sources may include age, gender, demographics, geographic, or other information. GMA Deals may also use or share any such aggregated data that does not contain personal information with third parties for any purpose.
Personal Information Sharing
GMA Deals does not sell, rent, or lease any individual’s personal information or lists of email addresses to anyone for marketing purposes. GMA Deals takes commercially reasonable steps to maintain the security of this information.
GMA Deals reserves the right to disclose personal information in connection with an investigation of fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, intellectual property infringement, piracy, other unlawful activity or as otherwise required by law. In such events, GMA Deals may disclose a user’s name, street address, city, state, zip code, country, phone number, email address and company name.
Security of Your Personal Information
GMA Deals implements physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the personal information to which it has access. GMA Deals follows generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information to which it has access. No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while GMA Deals strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, GMA Deals cannot guarantee its absolute security. Under no circumstances will GMA Deals be responsible or liable for any loss or damages, pecuniary or otherwise, caused by a third party’s unauthorized access to or use of your personal information. If you have any questions about security on GMA Deals’ website, you can email GMA Deals at help@gmadeals.com.
