Privacy Policy at Gods Girls.com
Gods Girls > Privacy Policy
While visiting our website information may be collected that enable us to tailor our website to better serve our clients. This information does not personally identify you or your computer but only provides general information: such as browser and operating system types, traffic patterns, referring urls, and other anonymous statistics.
Customers that join our website or complete surveys, or otherwise voluntarily submit personal information may receive email, or mail notifications or advertisements from us. We strive to make all such contact with you pertinent to products or news our data shows interests our customers.
We do not sell or rent any data or information collected to third parties. You may at any time opt out of such contacts by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email you receive.
By using our sites, you signify your agreement to the privacy polices described above. If you do not agree with our privacy policies, please do not use our sites. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages or we will e-mail you.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement or our online practices, you can contact support@godsgirls.com
