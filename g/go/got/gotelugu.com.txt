Privacy Policy | Gotelugu Weekly Magazine | Free Telugu Magazine
Gotelugu.com is a Free to browse website and do not require Login. The privacy policy is available to public at all times. We will notify you the changes made in the privacy policy in this page.
Like many other Web sites, www.gotelugu.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies and Web Beacons
www.Gotelugu.com does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense.
www.Gotelugu.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
For more information you may contact gotelugucontent@gmail.com
