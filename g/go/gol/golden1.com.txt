Golden 1 Credit Union | California Privacy Act
You have the right to control whether we share some of your personal information under certain circumstances. Please read the following information carefully before you make your choice.  For a more detailed description about our Privacy Opt-Out, please read our Privacy Policy.
Please note: If you previously selected your information-sharing preferences and have no changes, you do not need to take any action.
Your Rights
You have the following rights to restrict the sharing of personal and financial information with outside companies that we do business with. Nothing in this form prohibits the sharing of information necessary for us to follow the law, as permitted by law, or to give you the best service on your account(s) with us. This includes sending you information about some other products and services.
Your Choice
You may make your privacy opt-out choice at any time.  However, if we do not hear from you, we may share some of your information with companies with whom we have contract with to provide financial products and services.
To restrict information sharing with other companies we do business with to provide financial products and services, please submit the opt-out form digitally or physical copy.
