Privacy
GOLDPRICE.ORG Privacy Policy
The privacy of our visitors to goldprice.org is important to us. At goldprice.org, we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important.
Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use and visit goldprice.org, and how we safeguard your information.
We never sell, rent or share in any way your personal information with any third parties. goldprice.org may share non personal aggregate information with third parties such as the number of visitors per day received by goldprice.org or the number of visitors received from a specific country.
Log Files
As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service provider, such as Telstra or Optus), the browser you used to visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site. These log files are stored by google analytics.
Cookies and Web Beacons
Please read our Cookie Policy here.
Email Addresses
We do collect email addresses via google feedburner when you subscribe to receive updates of commentary from goldprice.org. These emails are stored on the google.com servers. We do not and will not sell or rent these emails to any other parties. You must double opt in in order to be subscribed to our email lists and there is an opt out link at the bottom of every email allowing you to click on them at any time to opt out and stop receiving email from goldprice.org. Should you have any problems in doing this please do not hesitate to contact us and we will manually unsubscribe your email and allow you to opt out of the emails.
Forum
The goldprice.org forum was closed in 2007 and may or may not resume operation in the future. We store your name and email address and password on the goldprice.org servers. If you would like to have your account deleted please contact us and we will be happy to delete your account.
