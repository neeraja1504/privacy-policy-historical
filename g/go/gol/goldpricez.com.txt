PRIVACY, TERMS, CONDITIONS AND COOKIES POLICY ETC
The privacy of our visitors is important to us. We are fully aware about privacy of your personal information which is very important to us. This documents describes what types of personal information we record about users when visiting this website. In any case, we never sell or share your personal information to third parties.
This is an informational website that diplay price information (on interbank market rates) of some commodities and currencies. We are not buying or selling any service from or to you (user). This website is for information purpose only, and not for commercial use.
We collect prices from various external sources.
We provide online rates of some commodities (e.g., Gold and Silver) in many weight units (e.g., Gram, Kg, Tola, Tael, Masha, Ratti, etc.) in all purity level in over 100 currencies of the world. The weight (or mass) of these units are calculated from Ounce and displayed on every page (e.g., 1 Gram Weight = 0.03210 Troy Ounce ). There might be deviation from the actual unit and weight appearing on this website.
We are also providing free API for some commodities and currencies. While generating API key, the user requires to insert an email address, website URL, and his/her name. We may utilize these information in the future to communicate the subscriber regarding our services. We do not use their(subscriber) data for marketing purpose.
In the future, we may stop this (or any other service) if we will wish to do so.
Time of updation is appearing on every page. That is the time, on which we are pulling data from external sources.
The gold price is being updated with 1-2 minute duration, while currency price with one-hour duration.
We provide calculators for some commodities, it gives price estimation only.
A user may contact us via email. We do not share your (user's) data (email address etc) with third-parties.
