Privacy Policy
This privacy policy document outline the types of individual information is received & collected by Gojobsx.in & how it is used & worked. We think that privacy of visitor’s personal information kept safe and it is important to not share with anyone. We never forward your personal details to anyone, all our visitors are strictly advised to go through the Official notification to check all the details for Eligibility Criteria/Selection Mode and satisfy themselves for the concerned posts.
The privacy of our visitors who visit our website Gojobsx.in is very important for us. If you require more details or have any query about our privacy policies, then you can freely share your suggestion & views with us through our Contact us web page.
Log Files
The details surrounded by the log file include IP (internet protocol) addresses,/type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP) date/time stamp/ referring/exit pages and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information.
Cookies and Web Beacons
Gojobsx.in is the web page which use the cookies to store details about visitor’s preferences, record user-detailed on which pages the user access or visit to customize Advertisements.
Double-click DART Cookie
Google, act as a third party supplier for us which uses cookies to serve ads on our website. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables, to serve ads to users based on their visit to Gojobsx.in and other sites on the Internet. Some of our advertising partners may also use these cookies. We have to tell all our users our website gojobx.in does not have any control over these cookies that are used by third party.
Google Adsense Or Infolinks
Google Adsense Or Infolinks act as a third-party ad servers or ad networks use different technology to the advertisements & links that appear on www.gojobx.in & send all the information directly to your browsers.
Every step has been taken to secure the precision of information appointed in this site. Gojobsx.in does not accept any responsibility for any loss to any individual for the result of any action taken on the basis of this information. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your particular browser options.
