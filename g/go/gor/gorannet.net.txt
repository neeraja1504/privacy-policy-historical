Privacy Statement
In order to be able to use Goran Net ISP Products and Services, certain personal information needs to be collected by Goran Net ISP employees or personnel. This information includes but is not limited to: Personal Details such as Name, surname and Lastname. Personal documents such as a driver's license, passport or national ID card. Address related information such as street, zip code, city and country. Service related information such as the type of service, the cost and duration of the service. Technology related information such as IP Address information, connection status etc.
All the above mentioned information including more details are subject to Goran Net ISP's privacy statement and will be kept and will not be disclosed to any third party (other than Goran Net ISP and the customer him/herself).
