Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy was last modified on October 13, 2014.
Goofap.COM / Goofap.ORG / Goofap.INFO / Goofap.COM.BR  ("us", "we", or "our") operates www.goofap.com (the "Site"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.
Goofap does not collect any information from you, so we have nothing to share.  We are dedicated to protecting our users information. When you use our search engine, we do not know who you are and what you searched for. You are anonymous.
IP Addresses:  at no time will your IP address be recorded, nor do we record or log the relationship between your IP address and search term or history. In fact, we have no search history at all, which means more privacy for you. Definition of IP Address - An Internet Protocol address (IP address) is a numerical label assigned to each device (e.g., computer, printer) participating in a computer network
Data Collection:  As stated above we do not collect any personal information, this includes but is not limited to; IP addresses, operating systems and platforms, browsers, email, where your coming from or going.
Cookies:   Goofap does not set any permanent identifying cookies.
Information Sharing:  As Goofap does not collect information, we have no information to share. We are here to protect your right to privacy.
Requests from Law Enforcement Agencies:  Bad news, if we are subpoenaed, we are legally obligated to comply. Good news, as we have not collected any information, we have no information to share.
Questions & Feedback: We always love to hear from our users. Please feel free to email us your questions and feedback about your experience.
