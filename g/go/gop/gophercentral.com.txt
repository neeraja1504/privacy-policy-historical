Privacy Statement
We are CAN-SPAM compliant, CAN-SPAM Act of 2003
This privacy statement in order to disclose our policies regarding the gathering and use of subscriber information.
It is our policy to only accept permission based subscription requests. To that end additional information about a subscriber may be kept on file including but not limited to: registering IP address, date and time of sign up, other source of registration, date of unsubscription, etc.
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (email address). Uses of this information includes but is not limited to: sending the user the mailings they have requested, occasional solicitation from us and 3rd party offers, etc. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time via our automated system. It is our policy to not mail to a subscriber unless they have given permission to recieve mailings from us or 3rd parties, and to not mail to a subscriber after they have requested removal from our mailing list(s).
