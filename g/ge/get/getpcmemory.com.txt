Privacy Policy
GetPcMemory.com respect the privacy of its visitor’s personal information. Although when you visit our website, we collect some information such as your comments can contain Name, Email address, IP address, browser information (such as Internet Explorer, Chrome, Safari or Firefox) and the time duration you spent here. But this information will never be sold or rented to any entity or individual outside GetPcMemory.com
Cookies
GetPcMemory.com also uses third party advertisements to support the site. Some of the advertisers may use cookies and web beacons for advertising on GetPcMemory.com. Google, through their Adsense Program, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to GetPcMemory.com users based on their visit to this and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
If you have any confusion or question about our Privacy Policy, contact getpcmemory [at] gmail.com.
