Privacy Policy By Girl Games Club
This is the official privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") for girlgamesclub.com ("Site," "we," "us," or "our"), an Internet website owned and operated by GCLUB Networks, Inc (GCLUB). This Privacy Policy governs only the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") collected by the Site. GCLUB is committed to protecting your privacy online. To further this commitment, we have adopted an Online Privacy Policy that guides how we collect, store, and use the information collected about you online.
100% Privacy 100% safe!
girlgamesclub.com is committed to protecting your privacy online. girlgamesclub.com does not collect any personal information. The visitor log files on our webserver are disabled for speed and privacy reasons.
IP address and no logs
Your IP address is never used to gather broad demographic information and girl games club do not track your general visiting paterns (how many pages you view while at one website, downloads, etc). There is no system on our server to store and process user information. We have no clue how many users are using our website and what our users are doing. Our webserver log files are disabled for speed, performance and privacy reasons. The current log level is 'emerg', meaning the server only make a log file in the event of a full server system crash. Our goal is to create a safe and free place to play girl games. Nothing less and nothing more then that!
Sharing Of Information

Third party advertisers might collect, store and use your information. These scripts are not property of our game portal website and are subject to the privacy policy of the third party. Find the links to the third party privacy policies in the sections below to learn more.

What are Cookies?
Cookies are digital text files that can be created by any remote connection that you make in your browser. A cookie can only store text or (binairy) numbers or a combinations of both. Because cookies are just text files, created and controlled by your browser, it's a harmless system for your computer health. However you need to be aware that websites, mobile apps and advertisers are able to write and read certain 'invisible' information on the background while you think you are just browsing some online or mobile App content. Our website is NOT using performance cookies and is NOT tracking your behaviour. However we are using third-party advertisers that might use cookies - please read the third party section of our privacy policy for more information about our third parties.
