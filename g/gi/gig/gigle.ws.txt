GIGLE.WS Privacy Policy
At gigle.ws we recognize that privacy is important. Ill keep this document brief and simple. The only information collected is:
What you choose to contribute to the site in form of posting
Info such as user agent and language settings send by you browser, this is used to help improve and customize the service for you
Email address on registration, we hate spam and your email will never be given to any third parties, email is needed to be verified so you can be notified of updates on threads you subscribe to
IP addresses are logged on login for short periods to make sure banned users and spammers don't mess up the site
Site statistics such as number of visitors and location is collecting using 3rd part analytics tool such as google analytics
3rd Party Advertising and Analytics
To try and keep the server fees paid, we have relationships with other companies like Google and Yahoo that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information via 3rd party cookies. For further information, consult the privacy policy of: http://www.google.com/privacy.html and http://info.yahoo.com/privacy/us/yahoo/details.html
Also the site uses Google Analytics ( http://www.google.com/analytics/ ) to provide site administration with traffic statistics which are used to help make decisions on how to run the site better.
