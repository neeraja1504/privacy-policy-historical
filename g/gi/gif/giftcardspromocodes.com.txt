Cookies and Web Beacons
As we stated that we collect your information and we do that by the mean of cookies to store your preferences, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends.

For further information, consult the privacy policy of: http://www.google.com/privacy.html
If don’t want to give you information then you can disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings. Please make sure that deleting cookies does not mean you are permanently opted out of any advertising program.  Unless you have settings that disallow cookies, the next time you visit a site running the advertisements, a new cookie might be added.
