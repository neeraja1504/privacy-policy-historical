Personal Data
Comments
When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor's IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.
Contact forms
When visitors send a message using the contact form, no information is stored on this site/server. Personal contact information is only stored in the email message and this is only used to reply to the message.
Third parties
Information (such as IP) and tracking cookies, may be shared with Google for the purposes of ad serving. For more information click here
