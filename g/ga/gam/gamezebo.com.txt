Privacy
Here at Gamezebo, we take your privacy very seriously. Please find below more information in regards to what information we collect from you and how we use and safeguard that information.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, Gamezebo collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Gamezebo’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Gamezebo’s visitors use its website.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to Gamezebo’s website choose to interact with Gamezebo in ways that require Gamezebo to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Gamezebo gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for our newsletter to provide their email address.  In each case, Gamezebo collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Gamezebo. Gamezebo does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
Gamezebo may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites.  However, Gamezebo does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
Gamezebo discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Gamezebo’s behalf or to provide services available at Gamezebo’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others.  Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Gamezebo discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Gamezebo believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Gamezebo, third parties or the public at large. Gamezebo takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.
Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Gamezebo uses cookies to help Gamezebo identify and track visitors, their usage of Gamezebo website, and their website access preferences. Gamezebo visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Gamezebo’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Gamezebo’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.  The National Advertising initiative has more information and an opt-out site at: networkingadvertising.org/choices/
Business Transfers
If Gamezebo, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Gamezebo goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Gamezebo may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.
Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies.  Cookies allows the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Gamezebo and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers. Links to Other Sites Gamezebo may create links allowing you to access third party sites.  Gamezebo is not responsible for the content that appears on those sites and you should refer to their policies in regards to their service. If you have any questions or comments related to this privacy policy, contact us at info@gamezebo.com.
Privacy Policy Changes
Although most changes are likely to be minor, Gamezebo may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Gamezebo’s sole discretion. Gamezebo encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
