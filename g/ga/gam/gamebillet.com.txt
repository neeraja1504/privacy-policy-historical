Privacy Policy
Controllers of Personal Information
Any personal information provided to or gathered by GameBillet.com is controlled by Gamebillet, Inc.  (2093 PHILADELPHIA PIKE #2732 CLAYMONT , DE 19703) and GB Digital Distribution OÜ (Sepapaja 6, Tallinn 15551, Estonia, VAT number: EE101997300).
What Personal Information About Customers Does GameBillet.com Gather?
The information we learn from customers helps us personalise and continually improve your shopping experience at GameBillet.com. We use the information to handle orders, deliver products and services, process payments, communicate with you about orders, products, services and promotional offers, update our records and generally maintain your accounts with us, display content such as wish lists and customer reviews. We also use this information to improve our store and platform, prevent or detect fraud or abuses of our website. We would like to assure you that we will never share your information with 3rd parties or send spam emails.
Here are the types of information we gather.
Information You Give Us: we receive and store any information you enter on our website or give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our features. We use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests, customizing future shopping for you, improving our stores, and communicating with you.
Automatic Information: we receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with us. For example, like many websites, we use "cookies" and we obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses GameBillet.com or advertisements and other content served by GameBillet.com.
Specific examples include:
IP:  When you log in, we record your  IP ** ** to direct you to the correct store front for your region.
Country: When an account is created we will store your country which was determined by your IP
Name: If you decide to sign up and create an account we also
Email address:  If you decide to sign up and create an account, you can choose whether you want to use an email and password combination or sign in through Facebook or Google. In the first case we will store your email, in the second case we will store your Email and ID. We only need you to have one of these.
We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent.
Does GameBillet.com share the Information it receives?
We do not share customer details with any 3rd parties. Information about our customers is an important part of our business and we are not in the business of selling it to others.
3rd Parties
We do not share your data but we use 3rd parties for their services or as data processors.
Amazon AWS (GDPR Ready) is a service we use for hosting whole of GameBillet.com. That's where your data reside.
Google Analytics with IP anonymization is used to provide us analytical data about GameBillet.com (like number of visits). We do not use any custom tracking to identify individual users.
Right to erasure - deleting your account
You have a right to erasure of all your personal data. When you decide to permanently delete your account your data will be immediatelly deleted with an exception of your user ID (random 32 character alphanumeric string) will be stored so it can't be used again. If you want to delete your account, you can contact us any time from: support@gamebillet.com.
All your information will be marked as deleted. Data that may have been stored in logs or backups will be removed during automatic rollover.
Notices and Revisions
If you have any concern about privacy at GameBillet.com, please e-mail us a thorough description and we will try to resolve the issue for you.
