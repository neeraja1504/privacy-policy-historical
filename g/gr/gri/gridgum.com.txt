Privacy Policy | Gridgum
Gridgum respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information which you provide to us and which we collect about you in connection with your accessing and use of this website and services associated with it.
Gridgum DOES NOT share personal information of any kind with anyone. We will not sell or rent your name or personal information to any third party. Any data that a user, author or business stores via our website is wholly owned by that user, author or business. At any time a user or business is free to take their data and leave, or can simply remove their data from our website.
Gridgum only collects such personal information that is necessary for you to access and use our services. This personal information can include, but is not limited to, full name, email address, phone number, credit card information, and other personal information necessary to generate legal documents. We also use Google Analytics to track and ensure a pleasant user experience is achieved on our website.
Gridgum may release personal information if Gridgum is required to by law or any investigations relating to fraud.
