INFORMATION WE COLLECT
We collect Non-personal and Personal Information in order to provide and operate Great Deal Compare and to make Great Deal Compare and related services customized as possible to your interests and preferences. We also use the information for statistical and analytics, research and technical support; this enable us to further develop, customize and improve Great Deal Compare based on Users’ common preferences and uses and to enable us to provide our Users with a better user experience, with more relevant products, offers and deals and other marketing materials. We make sure you get the best price every time! The following are the types of data and information from our Users: a. Non-personally identifiable information pertaining to a User, which may be made available or gathered via the User’s use of Great Deal Compare (“Non-personal Information”). We are not aware of the identity of the User from which the Non-personal Information was collected. Non-personal Information which is being collected consists of technical information and aggregated usage information, and may contain, among other things, the activity of the User on Great Deal Compare, type of operation system, User’s ‘click-stream’ on Great Deal Compare’s offers and any e-commerce websites operated by third parties (including Third Party Services, as such term is defined in the Terms), including search queries and related searches on search engines you make, browser type and version, language and keyboard language. b. The second type of information is personally identifiable information, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort identify an individual, or may be of private or sensitive nature (“Personal Information”).Personal Information which is may be gathered consists of the following: The User’s IP address or other persistent user identifier, which is allocated to your device by your Internet service provider and used mainly for enhancing the User’s experience and for geolocation personalization, as further detailed below; Any Personal Information provided voluntarily by the User via Great Deal Compare, such as the User’s e-mail address in order to be contacted or any Users interaction with us through Great Deal Compare, as may be updated from time to time by us. We do not collect any Personal Information from you or related to you without your consent, which is obtained, inter alia, through your active acceptance to the Privacy Policy. Please note that even if you will voluntarily submit your personal information to us, we do not merge such information with pseudonymous information we may have collected about you, and we do not supplement such information with other sources.

Cookies
When you access or use Great Deal Compare we use industry-wide technologies such as “cookies” (or similar technologies), which store certain information on your computer (“Local Storage”) and which will allow us to enable automatic activation of certain features, and make your service experience much more convenient and effortless. The cookies used by Great Deal Compare are created per session you launch when the application starts for the first time, and may persist until the application is removed from your computer. We do not include any information about you other than your session and anonymous globally unique identifier key, the date and time at which you have started using the application, information about how you have downloaded Great Deal Compare to your computer and information necessary to serve you different content, such as when performing user-interface so-called “A/B tests”. These cookies can be removed by using your browser’s cookie/Local Storage cleaning facilities. It is easy to prohibit the Local Storage altogether. Most browsers will allow you to erase cookies from your computer’s hard drive, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. However, if you block or erase cookies your online experience may be limited. The list below provides additional information regarding the types of cookies we use and their purpose: Functionality Cookies: used to identify you as an user and you preferences to provide you with customized offers and deals; we also use such cookies to label you as an opted-out user in case you will choose to opt out of our services; Analytics Cookies: used to collect information about how users use Great Deal Compare. We use the information to compile reports and to help us improve Great Deal Compare. Such information is collected in an aggregated form, including the number of Users of Great Deal Compare, where Users have access to Great Deal Compare from and the webpages they visited.
