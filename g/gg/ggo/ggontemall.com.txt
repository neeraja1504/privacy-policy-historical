Privacy Notice : ggontemall.com, Your online shopping store

We will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (e.g. name, address, telephone number and e-mail address), also referred to herein as "personal information," unless you provide it to us voluntarily.
Age Requirement
We do not sell or ship any items to anyone who is under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, please do not submit any personal information to us and reply on a parent or guardian to assist you.

Cookies
We may deposit "cookies" in your computer in order to identify you. Cookies are small data text files that are sent from a server computer during a browsing session. Cookies are typically stored on the computer's hard drive and are used by web-sites to simulate a continuous connection to the site. Security measures have been employed to prevent unauthorized access to visitor data. However, visitors acknowledge that our store does not control the transfer of data over telecommunication facilities including the Internet. Therefore, our store will not be responsible for any breach of security or the unauthorized disclosure or use of any such data on the Internet. You may configure your browser to reject cookies.
