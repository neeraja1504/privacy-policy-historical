Privacy Policy | Indian Council of Medical Research
We do not collect personal information for any purpose other than to respond to you. Our website never collects information or creates individual profiles for commercial marketing. While you must provide an e-mail address for a localised response to any incoming questions or comments to us, we recommend that you do NOT include any other personal information.
