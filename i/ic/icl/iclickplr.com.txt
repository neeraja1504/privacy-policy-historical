PLRGold: Videos
Privacy Policy
PLRGoldVideos.com values and respects the privacy of its visitors and members. This policy, known as the Privacy Policy, details the type of information we collect from you, and what we do with that information. The privacy policy shall be accepted as part of the Terms of Service.
Personal Information
PLRGoldVideos.com collects your Username, First Name, Last Name, and E-mail address when you first sign up with the site. We also ask for additional, optional information through our registration form, including but not limited to your mailing address.
Method of Information Collecting
PLRGoldVideos.com uses "cookies" to store and track visitor information. A cookie is a small text file that a server can automatically store on your computer and retrieve later. We instruct your browser to place a "cookie" into a file on your hard drive when you visit our site. Each time you return to the site, the cookie is re-sent to us so that we may track which of our services you use and what content you view (including advertisements). In addition, we collect the Internet Protocol address of each visitor to our site, which is a number that can be used to identify your internet service provider, among other things.
Use of Information
PLRGoldVideos.com may disclose information we have collected from you and/or content you have made available on our site in the following circumstances, and also may disclose such information and/or content to others when PLRGoldVideos.com believes in good faith that the law requires it, or to protect the rights or property of PLRGoldVideos.com or its visitors.
Upon request, PLRGoldVideos.com may disclose your contact information to customers of your website, government agencies, any trademark or copyright owner requiring to contact you in regards to transactions or content on your site.
Information submitted by you at the time of registration (including but not limited to your name and e-mail address) may be used but not limited to marketing and promotional purposes by PLRGoldVideos.com.
This information may also be shared with other companies who offer a co-branded or rebranded versions of our site, who offer services in conjunction with PLRGoldVideos.com , or who are business partners of PLRGoldVideos.com. PLRGoldVideos.com will not otherwise identify you personally to other companies, or to any advertisers. PLRGoldVideos.com provides demographic information in aggregate form to advertisers on our site, without identifying any user personally.
We may use information provided by you when you participate in contests or surveys on our site to send information about PLRGoldVideos.com to you, or to give information to advertisers in aggregate form.
PLRGoldVideos.com uses cookies to determine whether you are logged on to the site, to maintain your session, and to automatically log you out if your computer remains idle for a specified amount of time when using our service. We also use cookies to measure the traffic on our site, track which of our services you use, which content you view (including advertisements) and how long you view it. We collect IP addresses for administrative purposes (such as to help diagnose problems with our server) and to gather aggregate demographic information about our visitors.
By using PLRGoldVideos.com and disclosing information to us you expressly consent to the uses described in this Privacy Statement.
