This Cookies Policy is aimed at informing you clearly and precisely about the cookies that are used on our Website.
Cookies: What are they?
A cookie is a file that is downloaded onto your equipment (computer or mobile device) for the purpose of storing data, which can be updated and retrieved by the body responsible for its installation.
The information collected by the cookies may include the date and time of visit to the Website, the pages viewed, the period of time you have been on our Website and the sites visited just before and after.
Cookies used by us are stored on your equipment for a maximum period of 2 years from the date when they are initially installed or if they are subsequently updated, from the date of the update.
