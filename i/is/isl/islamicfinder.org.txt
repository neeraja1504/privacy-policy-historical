Privasi & Polisi | IslamicFinder
Protecting your privacy is very important to us. We’re telling you about our privacy policy so you know what information we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. This privacy notice is for IslamicFinder.org. This site is referred to as “IslamicFinder.org” or “IF” throughout the rest of this notice and is maintained and operated by IslamicFinder. Operating IslamicFinder.org, does not collect your name, email address, location or other similar information unless you choose to provide it. We do collect other limited information automatically from visitors who read, browse, and/or download information from our site. We do this so we can understand how the site is being used and how we can make it more helpful. If you create a user account by signing up from IslamicFinder, and use our module on the site (such as PrayerBook), we save your data of prayer logging that you added from our Athan application. We keep your data save of complete prayer logging throughout all past months and years. This information is collected in order to give you full information about your prayers progress in statistical form i.e. offered prayers, missed prayers, most offered prayers, most missed prayer and best day throughout the month. The important point is that IslamicFinder keeps users information confidential and does not sell any information that we got after your sign up process to any third party.
Types of information we collect
Information which is automatically collected:
When you browse
Certain information about your visit can be collected when you browse our website. When you browse IslamicFinder.org, we, and in some cases our third-party service providers, can collect the following types of information about your visit, including:
IP address (an IP or internet protocol address is a number that is automatically assigned to a device connected to the web)
Approximate geographic location based on the IP address of the user’s local system or internet service provider.
Operating system (which is software that directs a computer’s basic functions such as executing programs and managing storage) for the device that you are using and information about the browser you used when visiting the site.
Date and time of your visit.
Pages you visited.
Address of the website that connected you to IslamicFinder.org (such as google.com)
Device type (desktop computer, tablet, or type of mobile device)
Screen resolution
Browser language
Geographic location
Time spent on pages
Scroll depth – The measure of how much of a web page was viewed
User events (e.g. clicking a button)
Cookies of prayer times setting i.e. Calculation method, juristic method, Latitude and Longitude of your selected location.
We use this information to measure the number of visitors to our site and its various sections, to help make our site more useful to visitors, and to improve our public education and outreach through digital advertising. Also, this information is sometimes used to personalize the content we show to you on third-party sites.
Information which you may provide:
When you request information:
We collect information including your name, email address or mobile phone number to deliver Islamic newsletter and use mobile number to give you link of Athan Application on your cell number by taking your subscription consent first. We use this information to complete the subscription process and provide you with useful information. You can opt out of these subscriptions at any time by unsubscribe yourself from newsletter email. Link on unsubscribe has been given in newsletter

We write multiple cookies of your browser to calculate exact prayer times according to your current location. If you delete these cookies from your browser then we need to recalculate prayer times by using these cookies when you visit our page next time. When sending you IslamicFinder Emails or Messages:
IF uses the email address (or mobile phone number) you provide us to send emails or Short Message Service (SMS) messages related to latest uploaded Islamic content and Athan application installation, if you have given us permission to send you such emails and text messages.
When conducting surveys and improving services:
IF also uses online surveys to collect opinions and feedback. You don’t have to answer these questions. If you do answer these questions, do not include any personally identifiable information in your answers. We analyze and use the information from these surveys to improve the site’s operation and content. The information is available only to managers, members of the communications and other designated IslamicFinder staff and contractors who require this information to perform their duties.
When using third-party tools for website analytics:
IF uses a variety of third-party web tools for web analytics. IF uses these tools to collect basic information about visits to IslamicFinder.org. This information is then used to maintain the website including: monitoring site stability, measuring site traffic, optimizing site content, improving search engine optimization and may help make the site more useful to visitors. The staff analyzes the data collected from these tools. The reports are available only to managers and internal teams who implement core functionality of IslamicFinder.
When using third-party tools for outreach through digital advertising:
Uses third-party web services to conduct outreach and education through the use of digital advertising from the users Locations. These third-party services may collect information through the use of web beacons that are located on our pages. A web beacon is a see-through graphic image (usually 1 pixel x 1 pixel) that is placed on a web page and, in combination with a cookie, allows us to collect information regarding the use of the web page that contains the web beacon. We use web beacons to tell when a user is redirected to IslamicFinder.org because of a Locations advertisement that we ran on another website was clicked on or otherwise interacted with (known as “click tracking” or “conversion tracking”) and to better target Locations advertisements (known as “retargeting”). uses third-party tools to help deliver advertising. Vendors that operate the third-party tools may also gather information about your visits to third-party sites outside of IslamicFinder.org. However, while does not track your individual-level activity on the Internet outside of IslamicFinder.org, our vendors may collect information automatically collected by IslamicFinder.org (not information you provide),and combine it with data they collect elsewhere for targeted advertising purposes. You can opt out of this type of data collection via Privacy Manager, Ad Choices, and Do Not Track. See Your Choices about tracking and data collection below for methods to opt out of this type of collection. The outreach and education analytics tools provide reports which aggregate data such as the number of clicks on advertisements. The reports are available only to managers, teams who implement the insurance affordability programs represented on IslamicFinder.org, members of the IslamicFinder.org communications and web team, and other designated IslamicFinder staff and contractors who need this information to perform their duties.
Cookie Policy of IslamicFinder.org
This Cookie Policy was last modified on 20/05/2018. IslamicFinder Organization (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates https://www.islamicfinder.org (the “Site”). The IslamicFinder website uses cookies to improve the users experience while browsing/visiting the website. This website uses a cookie control system which enables the user to allow or disallow the use of cookies on their computer / device upon their first website visit. This complies with recent legislation requirements for websites to attain explicit consent from their users before leaving behind or reading files such as cookies on a user's computer / device. Cookies are small files that are saved to the user's computer's hard drive which track, save and store information about the user's interactions and usage of the website. This allows the website to provide its users with a personalized experience. In order for our users to disallow or discontinue the use and saving of cookies from this website on their computer's hard drive, necessary steps will have to be taken within their web browsers security settings which will block all website cookies and it's external serving vendors. This website uses tracking software to monitor it's visitors to better understand their website usage. This tracking software is provided by Google Analytics, which uses cookies to monitor visitor's usage. The software will automatically save a cookie to your computer's hard drive in order to track your engagement and usage of the IslamicFinder website, but it won't collect, save or store personal information of users. You can read Google's privacy policy for further information [http://www.google.com/privacy.html]. Other cookies might be saved to your computer's hard drive by external websites or vendors when this website uses referral programs, sponsored links or advertisements. Such cookies are used for conversion and referral tracking, which typically expire within 30 days, though some may take longer. No personal information is saved, collected or stored.

Your Choices About Tracking and Data Collection on IslamicFinder.org
IslamicFinder.org offers a Privacy Manager which gives you control over what tracking and data collection takes place during your visit. Third-party tools are enabled by default to provide a quality consumer experience. The privacy manager provides you with the choice to opt-in or to opt-out of the different categories of third-party tools used by IslamicFinder.org: Advertising, Analytics, or Social Media. The privacy manager prevents cookies, web beacons, and Local Storage Objects from being placed on your device. The privacy manager also prevents third-party tools from loading regardless of your cookie settings, which provides consumers with an additional layer of privacy that prevents the tool from loading at all. Because the privacy manager creates a cookie in your browser, the opt-in and opt-out choices you make through the privacy manager will only be effective on the device and browser you used to make your choices, and your choices will expire when the cookie expires Google AdSense Ads – includes the Ad Choices icon on all digital advertising that uses “conversion-tracking” or “re-targeting”. The Google AdSense Ads icon is usually at or near the corner of digital ads. When you click on the Google AdSense Ads icon it will provide information on what company served the ad and information on how to opt-out. For more information on Google AdSense Ads, please visit the Google AdSense Ads website. Do Not Track – automatically observes the “Do Not Track” browser setting for digital advertising that uses “conversion-tracking” or “re-targeting”. If “Do Not Track” is set before a device visits IslamicFinder.org, third party conversion tracking and retargeting tools will not load on the site. For more information on Do Not Track or information on how to set the Do Not Track setting in your browser go to the Do Not Track website.
