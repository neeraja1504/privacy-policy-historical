Privacy Policy
The following privacy policy (the Privacy Policy ) shall apply to your use of this TAQTAQ.com website (the " Website ") which is operated by [Red Mile Interactive General Trading LLC] (the " Company ") and should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Use. Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how it will be treated.
Where this Privacy Policy refers to "we", "us" and "our" we mean theCompany and where this Privacy Policy refers to "you" and "your" we mean the user of the Website.
By viewing and using a the Website you will be deemed to agree to this Privacy Policy.
If you do not accept the terms of this Privacy Policy, then you must immediately stop using or accessing the Website.
Collection
Companymay gather two types of information about you from the Website: (a) personal information, provided by you at the time of voluntary registration for the services offered by Company; and (b) aggregate information generated byCompany's systems, which track traffic to the Website but do not relate to you personally. Failure to provide necessary personal information when requested may result in certain services not being available to you. Company may collect additional information from you in relation to promotions, and also any information you provide when corresponding with Company.
Disclosure of Information
Company may disclose: (a) any information that it gathers about you to third parties for the purposes of providing you with the services that you request; (b) aggregate tracking information and other information that does not personally identify you to third parties; (c) your personal information to third parties, when Company believes in good faith that it is required to do so by law; or (d) your personal information to third parties, provided Company has your prior authorisation, which may be obtained at the time of collecting the information from you.
Use
Company uses the personal information it collects: (a) to verify your identity and assist you if you forget your password or login details for any of Company's registration services; (b) to assist in providing the services requested by you; (c) to provide further information to you about other websites and services or which Companyconsiders may be of interest to you; (d) for future marketing, promotional and publicity purposes, including to carry out direct marketing, market research and surveys; (e) for ensuring that you are shown the information that is most relevant to you and your interests; and (f) for any other use that you authorise.
Company will seek your consent if it wishes to use your personal information for purposes other than those you have agreed to previously.
Cookies and Tracking
Companyuses a “cookie” file containing information that can identify details of your IP address, operating system (e.g. Microsoft Windows, Mac O/S, Linux), browser (e.g. Internet Explorer, Safari, Firefox, plus the version of the Browser), domain (to determine which country you are accessing the Website from) and other user information (e.g. your username).
Companymay use the information generated by cookies to: (a) track traffic patterns to and from the Website;(b) ensure any advertising is being shown to the most appropriate person; and (c) enable you to enter the Website and use certain services and to visit registered user only areas of the Website.
Content Providers, Advertisers and Partners
You can choose to refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser and/or deleting them from your hard drive. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use the Website but you will need them to log on to the Website and to access personalised or secure content on the Website. Some webpages may not function properly if the cookies are turned off.
Content Providers, Advertisers and Partners
Companyis not responsible for the data collection practices, privacy policies or the content of websites other than this Website, including any websites that are accessible via a link from this Website. You should contact the operators of those websites directly if you wish to enquire about their privacy practices and policies before providing your personal information to any third party.
Opting In and Opting Out
If Company's intended collection, use or disclosure of your personal information is outside the collection, use or disclosure circumstances set out in this Privacy Policy, Companywill give you the option to: (a) opt out and neither receive certain services nor participate in certain interactive areas; or (b) opt in to agree to be contacted by Companyin relation to certain matters such as notification of new features to the Website, beta testing or promotional activities.
Protecting your Personal Information
The security of your personal information is important to Company. Companyhas implemented generally accepted technological and operational security measures to attempt to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized alteration or destruction. For example, all personal information you provide to Companyis stored on secure servers. All your personal information, including your credit card details, is encrypted before leaving your browser. Companywill keep these measures under review and enhance them from time to time as necessary.
Please note that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, therefore, Company cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.
Holding, Correcting and Updating Information
Your information will be held at Company's premises, or at the premises of an appointed third party. Company may allow other third parties to have access to your information, but only for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Company will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
