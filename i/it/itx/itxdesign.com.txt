Privacy Policy | ITX
Privacy
ITX Design is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its’ customers. Our primary goal is to contribute to providing a safe and secure web hosting environment for consumers and site visitors.
Disclosure
We do not disclose information about your individual visits with any other company, vendor or partner. Information such as your name, address, email address, telephone numbers, etc. are gathered for the sole purpose of establishing an account for web services to include but not limited to web hosting, domain registration, etc… ITX Design may share such information in response to any legal processes, such as a court order or subpoena, or in special cases such as a physical threat to you or others. Otherwise, this information is kept in secure confidentiality.
Information
The purpose of storing any personal information you may provide us when you visit our site or fill out any ITX Design forms, is to enable ITX Design to maintain communication with you. We will not sell, share, or give this information to any other company, vendor or parter. ITX Design will only disclose user information to the proper authorities, when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.
By using this site, you agree to the ITX Design Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our site. Your continued use of the ITX Design site following the posting of changes to these terms will mean you accept those changes.
ITX Design is a Web Hosting and Domain Registration provider. We provide dedicated servers, shared web hosting, and domain registration for small businesses including Reseller Hosting, FFmpeg Hosting and Christian Web Hosting.
