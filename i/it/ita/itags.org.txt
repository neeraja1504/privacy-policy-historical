By accessing or using the Site or the Services you are accepting the terms of www.todaysummary.com 's Privacy Policy.
Some of the Personal Information (for example your name and username) is listed publicly on blogs. www.todaysummary.com.com, App. www.todaysummary.com.com and links. www.todaysummary.com.com (collectively, the " www.todaysummary.com.com Service"), including on your profile page and in search results. The www.todaysummary.com.com Service is designed to help you receive answers and comments. Because most of the information you provide to the www.todaysummary.com.com Service is information you want made public, your questions and comments may be broadly and instantly disseminated. You should be careful about all information that may be made public by the www.todaysummary.com.com Service. Whatever you enter into the www.todaysummary.com.com Service text boxes may be published and could be publicly viewable immediately upon your clicking the submit button.

Query and Session Logging
www.todaysummary.com automatically records (or "logs") all search queries or questions, the results of all search sessions and responses provided and any communications by, to or with the www.todaysummary.com system. This information may include your browser type and version, your IP address, any referring URL, any URLs you visit from the Site, and the time and date associated with, and the content of, any communications by, to or with the www.todaysummary.com system.
When you submit a search query or question using a mobile phone, mobile device or other phone-based query, www.todaysummary.com 's servers may record information including the originating telephone number, the service provider, the audible or text content of any communications with, by or to www.todaysummary.com and the time and date of the communications. www.todaysummary.com may link Personal Information provided by you through the Web with a telephone number designated by you if you register online and provide www.todaysummary.com with both your phone number and email address.
