Privacy Policy
Privacy
We are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. We will not share, sell, discuss, rent, or lease any of the information you have given privately to our company, without your consent; unless required by law. All this information obtained is used strictly to provide you with quick, efficient service. Never worry about the safety of the information you have given us. Our checkout process uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) to ensure all data being transferred through the ordering process is encrypted.
Additional Uses
Your data is used to create a profile about you based upon your past purchases and/or to send you marketing communications.
Data Shared for Fulfillment
Your data is only shared with or collected by other companies only to fulfill your order or provide services you requested.
