Investor's Iraq Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting InvestorsIraq.com. Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains the InvestorsIraq.com online information policy.
We do not share your email address with anyone.
We do not disclose any information that you give us, or that we collect automatically, to any third party, unless we first ask your permission to do so. We will not give your email address, phone number, name or any information to anyone without your permission. Our Web servers do not automatically record e-mail addresses of the visitors to our site. InvestorsIraq.com may apply cookies, which are small computer programs, to increase the ease of use of our Forum. A cookie is a small bit of data sent to your Internet browser from a Web server. This data is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies do not damage your computer and are commonly used by advertisers and popular Web sites. You can adjust the settings on your browser and refuse to accept cookies but that may affect your experience at some Web sites.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information ( not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
