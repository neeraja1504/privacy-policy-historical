Web Browser Cookies
Our Site may use “cookies” to enhance best user experience. User’s web browser places cookies on their computer/mobile memory to track information about them. Users have full rights to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent.
DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.infocoverage.com. Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to www.infocoverage.com and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense.
Under Age Privacy Protection Act
Protecting the privacy of the child is especially important to us. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at www.infocoverage.com from those we actually know are under 14, and no part of this website is designed for a child under 14.
