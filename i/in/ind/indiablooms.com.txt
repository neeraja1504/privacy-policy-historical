Indiablooms (www.indiablooms.com) takes your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.
What this Privacy Policy Covers
When you register with www.indiablooms.com, we ask for your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with www.indiablooms.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
www.indiablooms.com also automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, www.indiablooms.com cookie information and the page you requested.
In addition, when you use www.indiablooms.com's services, you may make some of your personal information public. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you may receive unsolicited messages from other parties in return and www.indiablooms.com will not be responsible for the use or misuse of such information.
www.indiablooms.com uses information for three general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, to fulfill your requests for certain products and services promoted by www.indiablooms.com or other companies, and to contact you about specials and new products. www.indiablooms.com is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, veracity and reliability of any advertising and content, products and services which may be accessed or advertised to you and www.indiablooms.com will not be liable for any loss or damage arising out of the same.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
www.indiablooms.com will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.
www.indiablooms.com will send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:
We have your consent to share the information;
We need to share your information to provide the product or service you have requested;
We respond to requests from any Indian or foreign government, security, defence, revenue, regulatory or other authority, agency or officer;
We respond to subpoenas, court orders or legal process; or
We find that your actions on our web sites violate the www.indiablooms.com Terms of Service or any of our usage guidelines for specific products or services.
Cookies
www.indiablooms.com may set and access www.indiablooms.com cookies on your computer and you may not be able to disable these cookies. www.indiablooms.com ensures that all information collected and accessed by www.indiablooms.com through the access of these cookies will be maintained in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
www.indiablooms.com allows other companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies do not have access to www.indiablooms.com's cookies.
Your www.indiablooms.com Account Information is password-protected for your privacy and security.
In certain areas www.indiablooms.com uses industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
www.indiablooms.com may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on our pages.
Questions or Suggestions
If you have questions or suggestions write to us on info@indiablooms.com.
