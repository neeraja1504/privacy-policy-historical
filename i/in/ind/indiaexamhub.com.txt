Privacy Policy | IndiaExamHub.com
IndiaExamHub.com  may collects names, IP address and other details of our visitors from time to time for security purpose. Visitor only contribute through by comments in our website. Your contribution may be publicly displayed but without showing your personally identifiable information.
IndiaExamHub.com  web is the only educational news website run by us. The Most important part is that IndiaExamHub.com  makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on IndiaExamHub.com.
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to IndiaExamHub.com  and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on IndiaExamHub.com  send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
IndiaExamHub.com  has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
Our Privacy Policy page last updated on 12 March 2012. We rarely change our Privacy Polices. Thanks.
