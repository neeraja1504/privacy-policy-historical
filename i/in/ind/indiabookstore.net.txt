Privacy Policy | IndiaBookStore.net
IndiaBookStore.net's privacy policy states how we collect and use your personal information which you decide to share when using IndiaBookStore website or mobile app.
By using IndiaBookStore, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. For any queries, feel free to reach out to us at feedback@indiabookstore.net.
Information we collect
When using our site and services, you may provide information about yourself, such as name, address, phone, mobile, and e-mail. This may occur when you register on IndiaBookStore.net or take part in a survey, giveaway or submit a review etc. We may collect “Non Personal Information” – information that cannot be used to identify you – via Cookies, even if you have not registered with or provided any Personal Information to IndiaBookStore.
How do we use your information
We use your information for following uses :
- analytics and product development
- contact you with updates, offers, news etc from IndiaBookStore
- contact you to send you offers from our sponsors or advertisers
We do not disclose your personal information including email / mobile phone number to any third parties, unless required by law.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may amend this policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your information we will notify you by posting an announcement on our web pages.
