A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. Cookies identify you for our database, but provide no information other than that which you have already provided Indowebster. You may reject or delete the cookie, however, you may not be able to access your uploaded files if you have not already created a free account. You are solely responsible for safeguarding the secrecy of your password.
