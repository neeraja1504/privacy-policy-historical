Indobase.com :: Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Indobase.com respects the privacy of its users/visitors and is committed to protect it in all respects. The information about the user as collected by Indobase.com is: (a) information supplied by users & (b) information automatically tracked while navigation. To avail certain services on Indobase.com, users are required to provide certain information for the registration process namely (a) your name, (b) email address, (c) sex, (d) age, (e) PIN code etc., and / or your occupation, interests etc. The information supplied by the users helps us in improving our services and enhancing user experience.
Unless you voluntarily identify yourself (through registration), we will have no way of knowing your identity even if we assign a cookie to your computer. The only personal information a cookie can contain is information that you provide. A cookie cannot read data off your hard drive. Our web servers automatically collect limited information about your computer's connection to the Internet, including your IP address, when you visit our site. Your IP address does not identify you personally. We use this information to deliver our web pages to you upon request, to design Indobase.com to the interests of our users and also to measure traffic within our site.
Indobase.com may contain links to other websites. Such websites have their own privacy policies, which are not under our control. Once you leave Indobase.com, use of any information you provide is governed by the privacy policy of the web site you are visiting. The privacy policy of that web site may differ from ours. If you can't find the privacy policy of any of these sites (that you are visiting) via a link from the site's homepage, you may contact the website for more information regarding their privacy policy.
When you register with Indobase.com, we may contact you from time to time for updating you about such features that, we believe, may benefit you. All information collected on Indobase.com is securely stored within the Indobase.com database, which is secured and password-protected. We take utmost care regarding the security of our database but as no security system is impenetrable, we cannot guarantee the security of our database, nor can we assure that information you supply will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on the site.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to the site and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
It is to be taken into consideration that the internet is an ever evolving media and we may change our privacy policy from time to time to incorporate necessary future developments and changes. Our use of any information we collect from the users will always be consistent with the same policy under which the information was gathered, regardless of the content of the new policy.
