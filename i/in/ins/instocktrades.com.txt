Privacy policy
Effective Date: May 25, 2018
This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Discount Comic Book Service (DCBS) and our subsidiaries (collectively, "DCBS," "we" or "us") when you access or use our websites or otherwise interact with us.
We respect your privacy. Information about you will not be sold, rented, or shared with outside parties for any reason.
Where required by law, you have the right to review and delete the information DCBS has collected about you at any time the information is not needed to fulfill orders.
Information DCBS Collects
In the course of providing our services, DCBS collects and retains certain personal information. Some of this is required information you provide through the ordering process and some information we collect automatically, to improve your experience on the DCBS websites and the services we provide.
Order information. When you place an order on a DCBS website we collect information about each transaction. We collect the order and product information, including your name, email, billing information and shipping information. Additionally, we may log information about your location and IP address.
Analytic tools and cookies from third parties may be used to collect information in aggregate about who is visiting the DCBS websites. DCBS uses Google Analytics and cookies. This information includes the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, IP address, and possibly the page that referred you to the DCBS site. This information is used to help us improve our service and the website experience.
Additional site functionality requires the use of cookies to identify who you are, maintain your user cart, and remember layout preferences.
Use of Information
DCBS uses the collected information to fulfill orders, analyze and monitor site usage, and evaluate product offers and discounts. Your email address is used to communicate important order information and updates when necessary.
It is necessary to provide shipping information to our shipping partners (USPS, UPS, FedEx, etc.). We are also required to disclose shipment contents on International shipments.
Billing information may be validated and processed through third parties to complete transactions and perform services on our behalf.
Legal Requirements
When required by law, we may provide personal account information to law enforcement, public or governmental agencies, or private litigants, within or outside your country of residence. Additionally, in cases of suspected fraud or site abuse, we may provide your information to legal counsel and third parties in connection with potential litigation.
Your Consent
By continuing to use the DCBS site and services, you are consenting to your agreement with this Privacy Policy.
Security
The security of your information is very important to us. DCBS takes measures to protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
Contact and Data Controller
For additional information or requests please contact DCBS at customer.service@dcbservice.com.
