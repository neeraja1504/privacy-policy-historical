Instantz Privacy Policy
General site
Instantz Noobproduction is a free fansubs group who works to promote the popularity of Japanese Animation and Culture. Not only that, we also provide links to streaming videos of many animes through a third party video streaming website. Most of our media is contributed by the fans and members of the site who we have no control and responsible for the content they uploaded and provided. Please do know that, we do not take part in the making of any of the animes/videos on http://www.instantz.net. We repsects and support the rightful owners and producers of the anime. All video links point to content hosted on third party webites and we take no responsibility for it whatsoever.
If you are the rightful producer/owner of any of the content provided on http://www.instantz.net and wish to remove your contents please contact us through admin[at]instantz[dot]net with all the proof and I will be more than happy to do so.
Personal Information
Instantz Noobproduction will never ask, collects or stores any information such as names, phone numbers or addresses from users who visited the site. However, our systems do take note of your geographic location to use it as statistic to see where our users come from. Beside That, we will not store any of your private security information matter.
Forums/Chatboard
If you are using our site forums, you must acknowledge that anything you posts or submit there can be read from other people. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you choose to submit in these forums. However, we do take serious action to those who attempts to do bad things to our members.
Advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here
Donation
You are not forced to make any donation to the site. You make donation as your own will to help the site, and we can't give you refund
