Please read the following carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it. By accessing this Website and/or communicating with us you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information under the terms of this Privacy and Cookie Policy.

InboxPounds Limited is a subsidiary of CotterWeb Enterprises, Inc, a corporation registered in the United States of America.
Terms used in this Privacy and Cookie Policy will have the meaning given to them in the Terms and Conditions or, if applicable, in the Act, unless indicated otherwise in this Privacy and Cookie Policy.
Our Compliance Officer for the purpose of the Act is Tom Healy.

I. INFORMATION COLLECTION
Member data
InboxPounds® obtains and processes the following types of information (collectively referred to as "Member Data" )
1. All Members must provide the following personal information in order to join InboxPounds®: name, postal address, phone number, email address, and password. Your personal information will only be used in ways that are fair and in accordance with this Privacy and Cookie Policy. InboxPounds® will comply at all times with the Data Protection Act 1998 and all other applicable laws in relation to the processing of data.
2. Members may be required to provide subsequent demographic information in order to receive Signup Bonus(es) and/or qualify for other promotions. Demographic information includes, but is not limited to: birth date, gender, marital status, education completed, household income, and number of children in household.
3. If you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence.
4. InboxPounds® may also ask you to complete surveys that we use for research purposes, although you do not have to respond to them.
5. Details of visits to our Website, including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data, whether this is required for our own billing or payment purposes or otherwise, and the resources that you access.
6. We may collect information about your computer, including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system administration and to report aggregate information to our Advertisers. This is statistical data about our Members' browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual.
Means of Collection
Members may join InboxPounds® by completing one of our signup forms, through Co-Registration, or other means as we may determine at any time in the future.
II. USE OF INFORMATION
Use of Information
Member Data is used by InboxPounds® in all of the following ways:
1. to ensure that content from our Website is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;
2. to provide you with information, products or services that you request from us which we feel may interest you, where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes;
3. to carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us;
4. to allow you to participate in interactive features of our service, when you choose to do so;
5. to notify you about changes to our services;
6. InboxPounds® members automatically receive 1-3 PaidEmails™ per day. These emails inform members about the latest product and service offers from our advertisers and partners. Members are credited for confirming they have read their PaidEmail™ messages. InboxPounds® sends different types of PaidEmail™, some can be confirmed directly from the link within the email, while others require a subsequent action by the member. Follow the instructions in each PaidEmail™ to be sure you are credited properly. InboxPounds® members are required to subscribe to PaidEmail™. To stop receiving PaidEmail™ messages, members must cancel their membership. The "My Account" tab contains a "Cancel Account" link to stop your InboxPounds® membership.
7. your postal address may be used to send payments earned by you or otherwise communicate with you;
8. demographic information may be used to target advertisements to you;
9. membername, email address, and Password are used for your protection in order to restrict access to your account;
10. Member Data may be sold or disclosed to third parties as described in sections II.2 and III below;
11. Member Data may be disclosed to allow us to comply with our legal obligations where release of information relating to Members is required by law;
12. cookies may be used as described in Section IV below;
13. information may be used in additional ways as posted on this Privacy and Cookie Policy in the future.
Sharing of Information
InboxPounds® does not rent, sell, or share Member Data, except in the following circumstances:
1. We will release Member Data where it is necessary for us to do so in order to comply with any legal obligation or requirement.
2. We may release Member Data in order to enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions and other contractual agreements, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of InboxPounds®, our Members, our Advertisers or others. 3. In particular, we may share information with our Advertisers where such sharing is necessary for the provision of the InboxPounds® services and performance of the contractual obligations between InboxPounds® and the Advertisers.
4. We share Member Data when Co-registration Boxes (as described at section III and are checked during the course of registration.
5. We may share Member Data with third parties as reasonably necessary for us to operate this Website and to provide offers and services to Members that we think will be of interest to you, unless you indicate to us that you do not wish your Member Data to be disclosed for this purpose.

Your use of other websites is subject to the terms and conditions of those websites, including the privacy policies of those websites.
IV. COOKIES AND THIRD PARTY TRACKING TECHNOLOGY
About Cookies and Third Party Technology.
A "cookie" is a small data file that may be saved to and stored on your computer when you visit a website or open an email. Cookies are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. Cookies permit a website to "remember" whether your computer has visited the website before and helps the website create a more personalised experience for you and records activities taken by you, such as offers completed.
For a full list of all cookies used on the Website, please follow this link to cookie information http://www.inboxpounds.co.uk/ pages/cookie_info.
In addition to cookie technology, InboxPounds® and our Advertisers use additional third party technology and tracking tools to record offers and activities completed by you, friends referred by you, and to communicate information about you, such as anonymised demographic information (e.g., age, gender), in order to target offers and advertisements to specific groups as requested by Advertisers. Cookies and other tracking technology are collectively referred to as "Cookies and Recording Tools".
Your computer permits you to change the settings on your web browser to not accept and/or disable Cookies and Recording Tools. However, if you do not accept Cookies and Recording Tools used by InboxPounds® and our Advertisers, you may not be able to use certain functions on the Website or on certain links to external websites. More importantly, if you do not accept Cookies and Recording Tools you will not receive credit for Offers or other actions completed by you because we and our Advertisers are unable to track and record your activity. If you do not want to accept Cookies and Recording Tools, you must stop using this Website.
Except for essential cookies, all cookies will expire after the period of time provided at http://www.inboxpounds.co.uk/ pages/cookie_info.
Referral and Affiliate Tracking
InboxPounds® uses cookies in order to track new Member registrations. This ensures Members are properly credited for referring other Members, as well as to track earnings for our Advertisers.
Member Recognition
After a Member has logged into the InboxPounds® Member’s Section, cookies are used to remember their membername and Password so they do not need to be entered during every visit to our Website. Members can logout of the Member's Section if they do not wish to have this information saved.
