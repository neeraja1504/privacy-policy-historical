Ingram Micro: Privacy Statement
Ingram Micro Privacy Statement
This Statement of Privacy describes our practices regarding information we gather from you while you visit Ingram Micro's U.S. Web site, www.ingrammicro.com.
As a wholesale distributor, Ingram Micro provides products and services to technology solution providers, resellers and retailers. In order to better serve these customers, the majority of the content provided on our Web site is password-protected to enable a true business-to-business environment.
The Information We Collect
For all visitors to www.ingrammicro.com, Ingram Micro collects general data pertaining to each Web site visitor, such as the visitor's IP address, operating system, browser type and version number, and the web pages viewed. This information is aggregated with all other visitor information.
For our registered visitors, your site behavior characteristics are tracked and aggregated. This information may include site navigation paths, advertisements selected and business transactions conducted, and depending upon your business structure, may include personal data.
Use of the Information Collected
While the information that we collect enables us to offer better service, we may use the data for other purposes. Ingram Micro may share any information it collects with other parties including its business partners, suppliers and financial institutions. Ingram Micro may also disclose information in response to subpoenas, governmental requests or other legal process or to otherwise protect Ingram Micro's legal or contractual obligations.
"Cookies"
On your first visit to the registered portion of Ingram Micro's Web site, we may issue a "cookie," a small file stored on your computer's hard drive, in order simplify and ease your continued use of www.ingrammicro.com. The cookie may serve various purposes but is used primarily for establishing personal preferences for you while on our Web site.
Changes to the Statement of Privacy
Ingram Micro reserves the right to change its practices concerning information gathered from visitors to its Web site. If such changes occur, this Statement of Privacy will be updated.
Ingram Micro welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy; please contact us by e-mail at: webmaster@ingrammicro.com.
