THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT
In this site, you can order products, vote in polls or otherwise ask a question, subscribe to one of our services such as our online subscriber agreements, to fulfill a request by you for an online newsletter, or to contact you about your account with us.
We sometimes use the information that we collect from you and your use of the site to improve the design and content of our site and to enable us to personalize your Internet experience. We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze site usage, as well as to offer you products, programs, or services.
Subject to legal limitations, we may also use information you provide to us for marketing purposes.
All of this data is provided to us by you as you use the site, and we only use the data you provide to us for the purposes described in this Policy.
The type of data we collect generally consists of your first and last name, mailing address (including postal code), country of residence, e-mail address, employer, job title and department and telephone and facsimile numbers, and the products you purchase from us.
Depending upon the activity that you undertake, some of the information that we ask you to provide is identified as mandatory (generally required in circumstances where we need to verify that you are an authorised user of a product or where we need the information in order to provide the requested service, such as sending you a newsletter) and some as voluntary. If you do not provide the mandatory data with respect to a particular activity, you will not be able to engage in that activity.
COOKIES
To enhance your experience with our sites, many of our web pages use "cookies." Cookies are text files we place in your computer's browser to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering at one of our sites. However, once you choose to furnish the site with personally identifiable information, this information may be linked to the data stored in the cookie.
We use cookies to understand site usage and to improve the content and offerings on our sites. For example, we may use cookies to personalize your experience at our web pages (e. g. to recognize you by name when you return to our site), save your password in password-protected areas, and enable you to use shopping carts on our sites. We also may use cookies to offer you products, programs, or services.
Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.
OUR COMMITMENT TO SECURITY
We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security, and correctly use the information we collect online.
TRANSFERS ABROAD
As Adobe Romania is part of the Adobe group, data collected through this site may be transferred by Adobe Romania to Adobe companies and their agents across national boundaries and stored and processed in any of the countries around the world in which Adobe maintains offices, including the United States.
HOW TO CONTACT US
Adobe Systems Romania SRL is the data controller of all personal data gathered from this site. For credit card payments, Adobe Romania refers you to 2checkout.com, who is the supplier for purposes of your order and who will be the data controller of the order details you provide to them. If you have any questions or concerns about the Adobe Romania online policy for this site or its implementation or you wish to review, correct or delete any information about you, you may contact us at: privacy-officer@adobe.com
