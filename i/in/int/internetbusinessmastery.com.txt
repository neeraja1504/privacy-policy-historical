Privacy Policy
Internet Business Mastery, LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of our online visitors. On this site you can buy products & sign-up for our e-newsletter. To help you make decisions about the information you provide anywhere on this site, this notice has been provided to outline our practices.
Security
We use secure hosting accounts and security monitoring to protect our database and any information provided.
Email Privacy
We collect information from online visitors who ask to be on our newsletter mailing list and/or receive periodic email announcements. Any e-mail you receive from this site will contain the option to unsubscribe at the end of the e-mail. You can unsubscribe at any time.
Purchases
All credit card transactions occur over a secure connection.
When you order a product, the information you provide will only be provided to outside parties if it is necessary to complete and deliver the order. Your information will never be shared or disclosed to other parties for any other reason.
Third Party Vendors
Non-identifying information may be used to improve the site or shared with advertisers. For instance, information that is collected regarding traffic to the site or specific pages may be shared, but this will never include any identifying information.
Google’s advertising network displays Internet Business Mastery ads on other websites. Google uses cookies to display Internet Business Mastery ads based on our visitors’ past visits to the Internet Business Mastery web site.
You may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page.
Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have personal information on a child under the age of 13, we will delete that information from our systems.
