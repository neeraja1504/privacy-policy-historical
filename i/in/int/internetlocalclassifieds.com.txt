Privacy Policy | InternetLocalClassifieds.com
Dedicated
In an effort to provide and maintain an honest electronic environment, InternetLocalClassifieds.com has created a policy that protects users, affiliates and the general public. The following guidelines set forth have been established in order to provide consistent quality assurance. The brief information will explain how user information is protected, collected and sometimes used. All information contained herein will be updated by InternetLocalClassifieds.com when deemed necessary. InternetLocalClassifieds.com will notify users about any significant changes to this policy by posting a notice on the InternetLocalClassifieds.com website.
We Never:
Share your personal information with third parties.
Implement cross-marketing tactics in any way, shape or form.
Integrate tracking or spy-ware monitoring devices.
Knowingly collect any information from persons under the age of 18 years old.
Send you unsolicited communications for marketing purposes.
We Will:
Users who post information on our website may include directional links pointing to non-malicious third party websites, which may have different privacy practices. We do not take responsibility whatsoever, nor do we have any control over the Policies of those third party websites. We encourage all users to read each Privacy Policy in a careful manner.
We Reserve:
InternetLocalClassifieds.com reserves the right and may disclose information about any user if required to do so by law, or if such disclosure is reasonably necessary to respond to any legal course of action. InternetLocalClassifieds.com will also disclose information about its users to law enforcement officers and, or other officials if such disclosure is necessary to enforce our Terms of Use; respond to claims that any content posted on the said site violates the rights of third-parties; or protects the rights of our users and general public. InternetLocalClassifieds.com reserves the right to alter uploaded user images as we see fit without warning.
Google and the DoubleClick DART cookie:
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page. (Alternatively, you can direct users to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.)
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.
