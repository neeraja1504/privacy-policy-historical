Definitions
Affiliates                           Companies related by common ownership or control. They can be
financial and nonfinancial companies.
n   Our affiliates include Members Financial Management Center, Inc.
Nonaffiliates                        Companies not related by common ownership or control. They can be
financial and nonfinancial companies.
n   Interra does not share with nonaffiliates so they can market to you.
Joint marketing                      A formal agreement between nonaffiliated financial companies that
together market financial products or services to you.
n   Our joint marketing partners include companies offering products or
services from the following categories: insurance, investments,
credit cards, loans/student loans, bill pay and other electronic
services.
