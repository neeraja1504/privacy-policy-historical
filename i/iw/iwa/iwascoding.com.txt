Datenschutz
(1) Personenbezogene Daten werden von iwascoding nur zur Erfüllung des Geschäftszweckes erhoben, verarbeitet und genutzt. iwascoding gibt personenbezogene Daten ohne ausdrückliche Einwilligung des Kunden nicht an Dritte weiter. Der Kunde hat ein Recht auf Auskunft, Berichtigung, Sperrung und Löschung seiner Daten.
(2) Im übrigen gelten die Bestimmungen des Bundesdatenschutzgesetzes und des Telemediengesetzes.
(3) Ansprechpartner für die Rechte des Kunden nach (1) und (2) ist:
iwascoding GmbH, Weserstr. 18, 10247 Berlin,
Telefax: 030 - 46999-4707
Privacy Policy
Customer information we do NOT collect:
eBay Transaction Data
All your eBay communication goes directly from your Mac to eBay's API server, without any of our servers involved. We don't know what you are selling or buying, so there is nothing to collect for us.
Credit Card Data
As a small company we are trying to focus on what we can do best: creating software. That's why we have chosen to let specialized companies, like Kagi or PayPal, help us with processing your purchase. They charge your credit card and let us know that you bought our software. We will then send you your personal unlock code. As a result, we don't even know your credit card information. And if we don't know, we cannot lose it.
Customer information we do collect:
Customer Address Data
Depending on the method you are using to purchase a license for our software, we will collect personal information such as you name, your e-mail address, and your street address. We will only use this information to identify your purchase in case you lost your product license. We will not sell or share this information with anybody outside the company, nor will we send you unsolicited e-mail or other types of mailings.
