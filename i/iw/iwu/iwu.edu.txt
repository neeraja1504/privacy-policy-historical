Privacy Policy | Illinois Wesleyan
We Respect Your Privacy
Any and all information collected on this site will not be sold, rented, disclosed or loaned.
This website uses cookies to collect information about your computer, including your IP address, operating system and browser type. By using this website, you agree to the University’s use of cookies for statistical data purposes. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
Retargeting
We work with a third-party company to collect information about our website usage in order to increase the effectiveness of our communications. Recorded data is aggregated and shared with us. No personally identifiable information is collected or shared.
