Privacy Policy - IdealPrice
See also IdealPrice Service Terms and IdealPrice Cookie Policy
ASAP COMPARE LTD trading as www.idealprice.co.uk, a company registered in England and Wales under company number 07512660 whose registered address is at 1 The Square, Lightwater, Surrey, GU18 5SS, hereinafter referred to as ‘ IdealPrice ’ or ‘ We ’ have created this privacy statement (‘ Statement ’) in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy of the details that You provide to us when using the Site to access the IdealPrice Services. In this Statement, references to ‘ You ’ and ‘ Your ’ are references to the IdealPrice Member. When You use the Site to access the IdealPrice Services, You are consenting to the practices set forth in this Statement. Unless otherwise indicated, all definitions used in theIdealPrice Service Terms shall apply herein.
We know that Your privacy is very important to You which makes it very important to us. We aim to be as clear as possible in this Statement in respect of Your Personal Data. This Statement applies inter alia to Your Personal Data that We collect about You when You use the Site, how and when it is used, how We protect it and who has access to it. This Statement incorporates our Cookie Policy and the IdealPrice Service Terms by this reference (collectively called the ‘ IdealPrice Licence ’).
YOUR ACCEPTANCE OF THE STATEMENT
This Statement governs Your use of the Site to access the IdealPrice Services, including any dispute concerning privacy. By using the IdealPrice Services, You accept this Statement in full. You should read the Statement carefully and ensure that You understand its effect before proceeding to use the Site to access the IdealPrice Services.
We reserve the right to modify this Statement at any time without notice. Any changes We may make to this Statement in the future will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified to You by e-mail or when You next use the Site to access the IdealPrice Services, the new terms may be displayed on-screen and You may be required to read and accept them to continue. You agree that IdealPrice will treat Your use of the Site to access the IdealPrice Services as acceptance of this Statement including Your continued use subsequent to any changes.
WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED & HOW?
Personal Data
The Site asks You to provide Personal Data to enhance the quality of the IdealPrice Services We can provide to You and the more accurate the information You provide, the better We are able to respond to Your requirements! As part of the process of using the Site to access the IdealPrice Services, We may collect the following types of Personal Data about You (as applicable):
* Your email address upon Sign-Up as a IdealPrice Member on the Site to enable us to send our IdealPrice Product Alerts to You;
* Your visits to the Site and the IdealPrice Content that You download;
* any other information that You choose to send to us, including any request for further IdealPrice Services, general correspondence, reports of a problem with the Site or the IdealPrice Services.
2. **Other Data**
* De-personalised data which does not record Personal Data as such and concerns information about Your computer is used primarily to aid the technical administration of the Site, to better understand how the Site is functioning for IdealPrice Members and to draw conclusions upon demographic information. It includes information such as the date of Your visit to the Site, the path taken, the IP address, geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and Site navigation,
HOW IS THIS INFORMATION USED?
You hereby expressly consent to Your Personal Data being passed on to third party companies who act as our service providers (‘ IdealPrice Service Providers ’) for the sole purpose of fulfilling the daily operations pertaining to the IdealPrice Services only (including making improvements to the IdealPrice Services) and not for the purposes of those third parties making marketing communications to You, unless You have expressly consented to this being undertaken by a third party such as our selected third parties. Although You are under no obligation to provide such Personal Data to us, if You choose not to, some of the IdealPrice Services may not be available to You.
You acknowledge that Your Personal Data may be used by IdealPrice to contact a IdealPrice Member by email when necessary in connection with Your use of the Site and the IdealPrice Services.
From time to time, IdealPrice may contact You by email by way of sending You IdealPrice Product Alerts as well as newsletters, requests for feedback and related information. If You change Your mind, You can opt out of receiving these direct marketing communications from IdealPrice as described below.
Selected third parties: If You consented to be contacted by email or other means by selected third parties, We may provide Your information to the third parties We select and these third parties may use this information for their marketing or advertising purposes. If Personal Data has been passed to third parties with Your consent, You may need to contact them separately if You change Your mind in relation to their use of Your Personal Data.
Your Personal Data is used solely by IdealPrice (and IdealPrice Service Providers to the extent necessary to fulfil the IdealPrice obligations to You) for the sole purpose of enabling IdealPrice to act as a service provider to You and is not shared with any person or company without Your express consent.
Any Personal Data that is held with IdealPrice shall be retained in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998 and related Data Protection Legislation and/ or our retention and destruction policy culminating in its permanent deletion.
OPTING OUT:
If You do not wish to be contacted by IdealPrice in respect of future marketing communications, We will give You the opportunity to unsubscribe to the receipt of such communications in every communication that We send to You.
WHO HAS ACCESS TO YOUR PERSONAL DATA?
We may use the services of IdealPrice Service Providers to provide some of our business and operational functions so We may need to disclose Your information to them to provide You with the IdealPrice Services.
Disclosure of Your Personal Data in Compliance with Laws
You should be aware that We may release Your Personal Data when We believe it is necessary to comply with laws or regulations, to assist law enforcement, to enforce the terms under which You transact or communicate with or via IdealPrice, or to protect the rights, property or safety of IdealPrice, IdealPrice Members or third parties.
Transfer of Your Personal Data outside of the EEA
From time to time, We may transfer Your Personal Data to a related company, agent or contractor in order to improve our IdealPrice Services or to assist our security, credit risk or fraud protection activities. Such companies may be located outside of the EEA and You consent to the transfer of Your Personal Data to such companies for the purposes set out here in accordance with this Statement and as permitted by the Data Protection Legislation from time to time.
Transfer of Personal Data in the Event of the Sale of www.idealprice.co.uk or its Assets
In the event that www.idealprice.co.uk is sold or transfers some of its assets to another party, Your Personal Data could be one of the transferred assets. If Your Personal Data is transferred, its use will remain subject to this Statement. Your Personal Data will be passed on to a successor in interest in the event of a liquidation or administration of IdealPrice.
Other Sites and their Privacy Policies and Cookie Policies
The Site may contain links to other websites. IdealPrice is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites or for the privacy policies, cookie policies and practices of other third parties, so You should be careful to read and understand those website policies independently.
HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA & FOR HOW LONG?
The privacy and protection of Your Personal Data is important to us. In addition, any IdealPrice Member statistics that We may provide to prospective IdealPrice Service Providers regarding Your usage of the IdealPrice Services are provided in the aggregate only and do not include any individually identifiable data.
You acknowledge that email messages sent over the internet are not encrypted and are not secure. Despite efforts to protect Your Personal Data, We cannot ensure or warrant the security of any Personal Data You transmit to us, via, to, or from our Site. You accept that IdealPrice cannot be held liable for any breaches of confidentiality that may occur as a result of the use of email. If there is any sensitive or confidential Personal Data which You do not wish to communicate by email, please contact IdealPrice by telephone or post to arrange an alternative method of communication. You transmit all such Personal Data at Your own risk.
How long We keep Your Personal Data collected through our Site depends on the context in which You provide it and the purpose for which We use it. We will only retain it for as long as is necessary for such purposes. We may send You direct marketing communications for as long as You do not unsubscribe from receiving the same from IdealPrice. In the event that You choose to close Your IdealPrice Member Account, all Your Personal Data will be deleted from the Site.
Telephone calls: If You call any of the service telephone numbers quoted, We may record Your call. These recordings are used for training and quality control to ensure that We continuously monitor and improve our service standards.
We may disclose your Personal Data to any member of our group, which means our subsidiaries, our ultimate holding company and its subsidiaries, as defined in section 1159 of the Companies Act 2006.

We encourage You to bring it to our attention if You think that our collection or use of Personal Data is unfair, misleading or inappropriate. We would also welcome any suggestions for improving our procedures. This Statement does not provide exhaustive detail of all aspects of IdealPrice’s collection and use of Personal Data. However, We are happy to provide any additional information or explanation needed. Any requests for this should be sent to the address below. If We do hold Personal Data about You, We will let You have a copy of that Personal Data. To make a request to IdealPrice for any Personal Data that We may hold, You need to put the request in writing addressing it to the address provided below.
