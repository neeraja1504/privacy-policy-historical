Privacy Policy - Il Divo
This Privacy Policy was updated on 23rd March, 2019.
__This following Privacy Policy which applies to you if you live outside the European Union, Switzerland, Norway, Lichtenstein, Iceland, Australia, New Zealand and Canada.
If you are a citizen of a member country of the European Union, Switzerland, Norway, Lichtenstein, Iceland, Australia or New Zealand, this Privacy Policy will apply in your case.
IL DIVO Music LLC (hereinafter “Company”, “We”, “Us”, “Ildivo.com”) is concerned about privacy issues and wants you to be familiar with how we collect, use and disclose information.
This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection, use, share, and disclosure of your personal information in connection with your use of our Ildivo.com website (hereinafter “Site”, “Ildivo.com”), emails, our newsletter, our online store (together with the Site, the “Services”) owned and operated by the Company and any other services that display this Privacy Policy.
When using our Site or any of our Services your personal data will be processed as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, do not access or use the Services.
When this Privacy Policy uses the term “personal information,” we mean information that identifies a particular individual, such as full name, street address, telephone number or email address. When this Privacy Policy uses the term “Artist”, means We IL DIVO.
This Privacy Policy does not address, and we are not responsible for, the privacy, information or other practices of any third party, including Facebook, Inc., Apple Inc., Google LLC, Microsoft Corp. or any third party operating any site or property to which any Property contains a link. In addition, the inclusion of a link on any Property does not imply endorsement of, or affiliation with, the linked site or Property by Us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to any such information submitted by you to any third party.
If you are a California resident, you may request certain information once per year about our disclosure of certain categories of Personal Information during the preceding calendar year to third parties for such third parties own direct marketing purposes. To obtain such information free of charge, please send your request by email to privacy@ildivo.com, or by postal mail to: Il Divo Limited, LLC, Attn: Privacy Coordinator, 235, Park Avenue South, 9th Floor, New York, NY 10019. We will respond to these requests within thirty (30) days. We reserve our right not to respond to requests submitted other than to the email or mailing addresses specified in this section.
We therefore encourage you to review carefully the entire Privacy Policy before communicating any of your data to Us. Please click on any of the links below to review that particular section.

Information You Provide
You may provide personally identifiable information (“personal data”) about yourself when you use our Site or Services and, of course, when you register to become part of the Ildivo.com community, by providing your name, surname, email address and other identifying information. For instance, you may provide your personal data in order to join our newsletter, or to register as customer in our store to buy any of our products.
Information We Collect
We collect personal data automatically, which is any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, when you use our Site or Services.
We and our third-party service providers may collect the following Personal Information from you:
Contact information such as name, email address, street address, and phone number
Billing information such as name, company name, tax ID / VAT Number or NIF, credit card number and billing address
Demographic information such as age, gender, interests, country and zip code
Unique identifiers such as username and password
Preference and other information such as product wish lists, order history and marketing preferences
Location information such as your actual or approximate location
We collect personal data when you:
use our contact form to make an enquiry, comment or suggestion.
subscribe to our online newsletters, or register to participate in an online forum or community
purchase or return products offered through our websites
respond to one of our polls or surveys; or contact our customer service department with an enquiry or request.
Occasionally, We may as well collect Personal Information from you offline, e.g., if you provide information to us at a concert venue in connection with a service offered in our Site.
Some of the personal data we request from you is required in order for you to use our Services. If you do not wish to provide such personal data to us, you are not obligated to, but as a result you may not be able to use the particular Service. However, some of the personal data we request from you is optional. This means that you can elect not to provide it to us and you will still be able to use the Service. If not all the personal information we request on a form is required, we may identify the required personal data to you such as by displaying an asterisk (*) next to the field where we request the required personal data.
Unless specifically stated otherwise in this Privacy Policy, the data we collect may be used for any purpose set forth in “How We Use the Information” below.
Optional Information:
Occasionally, we may request demographic information (like your age or gender) or information about your interests and purchasing preferences. In most instances, you do not have to provide us with this information to receive the newsletter, product, or service you have requested. We use this demographic and preference information to, among other things, tailor or improve our Site, products and services offered to you where this is in accordance with this Privacy Policy, as well as for internal marketing research purposes.
How We Use the Information
Our primary goal in collecting information is to provide you with a customized experience when using our Services and to make them more valuable to you. We and our authorized service providers use personal data that We or they collect about you for our legitimate interests, including to:
Organize, manage and operate the website and online store;
Analyze, operate and improve our Services, and fulfill your requests for products, services, and information, including to send you emails and electronic newsletters;
Communicate with users in response to incidents, requests, comments and questions that you make through the Services or contact forms in our website (including chats or phone calls);
Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, information regarding changes to our policies, and support and administrative messages;
Save and remember any preferences that you set while visiting one of our Services;
Prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, comply with applicable law and enforce our terms;
Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, information regarding changes to our policies, and support and administrative messages;
Organize events to which users may decide to register using the Ildivo.com website;
Publish on the website of the information concerning the participation of the members to the events and activities carried out by IL Divo;
Measure and analyze visitor traffic and usage patterns and improve the quality and effectiveness of our website, content, features, advertisements, and other communications;
Analyze how subscribers interact with our email messages and click on links contained within those messages;
Send surveys which aim to gather information regarding the cyber security and privacy policy and regulatory framework in the European Union with the objective to assist Ildivo.com to provide recommendations to protect public and private organisations from cyber-attacks;
Commercial ads / advertising (Marketing): We may use your data to contact you, both electronically and non-electronically, to conduct surveys, polls, obtain your opinion on the service provided, and, occasionally, to notify you of changes or updates, or provide you with special offers, promotions, and information from companies within our corporate family; and
Display, develop, and deliver direct marketing.
You always have the choice (now and in the future) to avoid personalized advertising based on your web browsing activity by using the mechanism implemented for that purpose.

These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services.
All of our service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal data and to use it only in compliance with applicable data privacy laws, and are prohibited from using or disclosing your personal data for any purpose other than providing the services on our behalf or as otherwise not prohibited by applicable law; although we may permit them to use aggregate information which does not identify you or de-identified information for other purposes.

With reputable third parties in whose products or services we think you may have an interest, with your prior consent to do so
As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process

To any other third party with your prior consent to do so

Cookie Policy
Cookies are small text files that we place in your computer or device to store your preferences. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us your e-mail address or other personal information unless you choose to provide this information to us by, for example, registering with one of our Services.
By means of cookies, the websites record user actions and preferences (such as, for example, the language chosen, the character dimensions, other settings concerning the layout of the website, etc.) so as to avoid that the user has to enter them when returning to the Website at a later date or when the user surfs a different page of the same website. Cookies are therefore used to log in users, monitor browsing sessions, store information relating to the users who access the website and may contain a unique identification code that makes it possible to track the user’s navigation within the website for statistical and advertising purposes.
We may place “cookies” on your computer or device. We use “cookie” technology to collect additional website usage data as well as to improve our Services.
There are many types of cookies, among them, the technical browsing or session cookies which are strictly necessary for the service’s functioning. Please, be aware that if you disable technical/functional cookies the services might not work properly and some services or some of the functionalities may not be available or work properly; you could be required to modify or manually fill in some information or preferences each time you use the services.
Users may download cookies on their devices owned by sites and web servers different from the site that they are actually visiting (“third-party cookies”). Some cookies are necessary for the technical functioning of the Site; if you do not accept the latter cookies, some functionalities and features of the Site may not be accessible.
Some of the advertisements on this site may be generated by third party partners. These third parties may use their own “cookies” to track how many unique users have seen, or click on, a particular advertisement. These cookies cannot be used to identify you as an individual, and are used only for statistical purposes or, for example, to provide advertisements that are more relevant to your preferences.
Ildivo.com is not responsible for third party cookies, other than allowing them to be served via our websites. However, we may use statistical information arising from these third party cookies that is provided to us, if at all, by such third parties to improve the targeting of advertisements to users (see also “Information We Collect” above).
If you wish to block third party cookies, you can do so in your browser’s security settings.
