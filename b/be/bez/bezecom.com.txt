Invite a Friend. If you elect to use Bezecom referral service for informing a friend about any of Bezecom's Websites or any of Bezecom services or of the Software, Bezecom shall ask you for the friend's name and email address. Bezecom will automatically send the friend a one-time email inviting them to visit relevant Bezecom's Website. The friend may contact Bezecom to request the removal of the information from the database in a hyperlink available in such email or also Here.

If you do not opt-out such option, Bezecom shall include in Bezecom's Phonebook your full name as entered by you at registration and the phone number allocated to you by bezecom If at any time you wish that such information shall be removed from Bezecom's Phonebook, please contact Bezecom Here.

Children's Information. The Software or Bezecom's Websites or any of Bezecom services is not directed or intended for children under thirteen years of age. If you are under thirteen years of age, you should not install or use the Software or use any of Bezecom's Websites or any of Bezecom services. If you are the parent or guardian of a user under the age of thirteen whom you believe has disclosed Personal Data to Bezecom, please contact Bezecom Here. Bezecom shall make its reasonable efforts necessary by relevant applicable law to delete and remove such Personal Data from its system.
Cookies. Bezecom uses cookies to gather and store information. Cookies are pieces of data that a website transfers to user computer's hard drive solely for record-keeping purposes. Cookies help Bezecom make its Software or services or Websites more useful by storing information on your preferences. Cookies enable Bezecom to gain information about the use of its Websites, Software or services and to enhance such accordingly to the preferences of the users. The cookies do not personally identify you, but they will identify your computer. Your browser is most likely set up to accept cookies, but you can set yours to refuse them. If you do so, you may not be able to take full advantage of Bezecom's Website, Software or servises. Bezecom is not liable for any failure of your computer due to the use of cookies.
Security. Bezecom implies all reasonable security measures in order to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of Personal Data. However, you acknowledge that no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while Bezecom strives to protect your Personal Data, Bezcom cannot ensure and Bezcome shall not be held liable for the security of any information that you transmit or receive, and you transmit and receive information at your own risk.
Choice/Opt-out. Bezecom users are given the opportunity to 'opt-out' of having their information used for purposes not directly related to Bezecom's Websites, Software or services. In order to unsubscribe you may type your email address Here and your request will be recorded on our database.
