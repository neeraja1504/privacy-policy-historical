Privacy Policy at Betrally
Introduction
This Privacy Policy details the way in which ZapZap Marketing Limited handles and safeguards the information you provide us with through www.betrally.com.
We are committed to preserving the privacy of our visitors and complying with applicable data protection laws and regulations.
We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time and we will notify you of these changes by posting the modified terms on this website. We recommend that you revisit this Privacy Policy regularly.
You should read this Privacy Policy in conjunction with the terms and conditions found here.

Register with us or use our services;
Complete any forms on this website;
Correspond with us by email, telephone or via this website;
Make customer enquiries;
Respond to communications from us; or
Provide any information or communications on any forums on this website.
We reserve the right to verify any PII submitted by or collected from you. In the event that we learn that a minor has attempted to or has submitted PII or we suspect the use of a fraudulent identity, we may reject the information and may take steps to remove such PII.

Providing you with sports betting and gambling services;
Verifying your age and identity;
Setting up and managing your account;
Complying with applicable laws and regulations;
Dealing with any requests we receive from you for information;
Supplying customer support services;
Undertaking research and development;
Conducting surveys;
 Promoting and marketing our websites, services and products as well as those provided by any entity within our corporate group (the “ CL Group ”) or under other brands operated by us;

You may opt-out of receiving direct marketing offers from us or any entity within the “CL Group” when by emailing us at support@betrally.com.
Cookies
We use cookies in order to provide you with a high quality and personalised experience.
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer, laptop or mobile device when you visit a website or use applications. Cookies may be used for many different purposes such as remembering your preferences, following you use of the website and improving your user experience.
We use four different types of cookies:
Essential Cookies - These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly or to provide a service which you request. These cookies are usually only set in response to your actions, such as in the case of auto form filling or logging in. Your browser can be set to block these cookies but the website will not perform properly if you decide to do so.
Functionality Cookies - These cookies help to provide you with a more personalised user experience and are used by us and select third parties whose services feature on our pages. Blocking these cookies will disable these services and may affect your website experience.
Performance Cookies - These cookies collect information about how you use a website or application and assist us in improving the performance of our website. Blocking these cookies means we cannot work effectively to improve the usability of our website.
Cookies may be stored either for the duration of your visit to the website or for repeat visits:
Session Cookies - These cookies allows us to link your actions during a browser session. A browser session starts when you open the browser window and finishes when you close it. Session cookies are created temporarily and are deleted once the browser window is closed.
Persistent Cookie - These cookies remains on your device for a set period of time specified in the cookie and activated each time you visit the website that created that particular cookie.
You may modify your browser so that it will not accept cookies. Please see the "Help" section of your browser for instructions.
