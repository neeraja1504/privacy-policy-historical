Cookies
By accessing, continuing to use or navigating throughout the site you accept that we will use certain browser cookies. The cookies used by Betrush.com do not interfere your privacy. A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your device's hard drive. Once you agree, the file is added and the cookie helps analyse web traffic or lets you know when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow web applications to respond to you as an individual.
What cookies we do use:
Session cookies exist while you are logged in. They are needed for users to interact with the site ;
3rd party cookies (such as Google Analytics)- used for analyzing the traffic data and interests of our visitors while being on the site so we can provide you with better experience at Betrush.com;
Cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us. You can choose if to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but you can modify your browser settings to decline them. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website. If you would like to know more about cookies visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
