BestShareware.net Privacy Policy
1. BestShareware.net Committed to Protect the Privacy of Users : BestShareware.net is committed to ensuring that every user's needs are addressed. In the course of delivering this site to you, we will NOT collect any personally-identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address from users of this web site without your approval, and we are also taking measures to protect your privacy.
2. BestShareware.net Ensure the Integrity of the Internet : You can send email to ask for the help of BestShareware.net. Your email will NOT be shared in any way with any third parties, and will only be used to send replies to you.
3. BestShareware.net Ensure to Protect Personal Information of Users : When you purchase products form BestShareware.net, additional information will be needed to fill your order. Any information provided will NOT be sold or given to any third parties in any way, and will only be used to send the Registration Information and the Free upgrade to you via email, and all order forms are run on a secure server, utilizing powerful encryption technology to safeguard all transactions (credit card or online purchases) from unauthorized access.
**4. BestShareware.net Order Security & Purchase Privacy Guarantee : ** We use RegNow for software selling, one of the most trusted names in e-commerce and Web-based technology, to respect the privacy of the partners, clients and their customers. It uses standard SSL (Security Socket Layer) encryption and all information collected through the system will be treated with the utmost care. Read more RegNow's Privacy Guarantee here.
5. BestShareware.net Download Security Policy : We have strict policies for checking all software which is available to be downloaded through the use of up-to-date virus scanning technology. But you should understand that our company CAN NOT and DOES NOT guarantee or warrant that files available to be downloaded through our site will be free of infection of viruses, worms, Trojan horses or other codes that manifest contaminating or destructive properties. You are responsible for implementing sufficient procedures and checkpoints to satisfy your particular requirements for accuracy of data inputs and outputs, and for maintaining a feasible means external to our site for the reconstruction of any lost data.
6. BestShareware.net Anti-spam Policy : While BestShareware.net does not condone the use of Unsolicited Commercial E-mail (UCE or SPAM), Usenet or message board postings or other similar methods of electronic, unsolicited mass messaging, we cannot prohibit our publishers from legitimately promoting their own web-sites or products as they see fit.
7. Inquiries : If you have direct questions regarding the usage of this site and requests for special usage permissions, please send email to the below address: support@BestShareware.net.
