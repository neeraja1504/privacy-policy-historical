Privacy Policy
This is the privacy policy document of Bestmobs.com. It outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by www.bestmobs.com and how it is used.
Logs
Bestmobs.com makes use of log files, just like most websites on the Internet. The information inside the log files includes visitors’ IP addresses, user agent (browser), date/time stamp, referring pages. We use this information to monitor the website’s growth. The information we collect will never be given out.
Cookies
Bestmobs.com makes use cookies to store information about visitors preferences and user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
External Links
Bestmobs.com sometimes puts links to other websites. We may be recommending the website for a specific reason or as the source of a resource we used. Whatsoever the case, Bestmobs.com is not responsible for the content or business practices of these websites.
Third Party Ad Servers
We make use of third party ad servers to bring ads to your browser. These companies may use information about your visits to this website in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. In as much as we try to make sure only safe advertisements are displayed, we do not fully control the type of advertisements our third party ad servers may show.
Questions
If you have any questions about Bestmobs.com privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
