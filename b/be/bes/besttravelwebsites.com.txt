Websites For Sale or Lease at Dot Commerce Incorporated
Privacy Information for all Dot Commerce Incorporated Websites
At Dot Commerce Incorporated, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to other companies. We may use the information we collect about you to process subscriptions and to provide a more personalized experience. Dot Commerce Incorporated will not collect any personal information about you except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information.
It may be helpful for you to know how we use the information we collect:
When you subscribe or purchase goods or services, we request your name, e-mail address, mailing address, and telephone number. We may also request your credit card number and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your request and to notify you of the status of your purchase.
We may also use the information we collect to notify you about important changes at Dot Commerce Incorporated and information we think you will find important.
We may collect certain technical information, which does not allow us to identify you personally, but pertains to your use of the Internet. For example, on the Web such information may include the type of Internet Browser that you are using, and through e-mail the information may include the type of messaging capabilities. This information enables us to enhance the content you receive from Dot Commerce Incorporated.
By using any of our Web sites, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Dot Commerce Incorporated. If we change our privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. Currently, we may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third-party vendors, but these statistics will include no personal identifying information. If you are under 18 years of age, you must have your parent or guardian's permission to order from any of our websites or their affiliates.
