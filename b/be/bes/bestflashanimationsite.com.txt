Flash animations
This statement discloses the privacy practices for the Best Flash Animation Sites web site.
At Best Flash Animations Site we respect your right to privacy and we understand that, as a visitor or client of bestflashanimationsite.com, you prefer to control your own personal information. For this reason, we may ask you to provide personal information when you submit your web site to be listed, request an update, or exchange a reciprocal link with us.
We will guard the personal information you share with us to ensure your privacy. When you provide B.F.A.S with your name (or alias), or e-mail address, B.F.A.S may use your e-mail address to alert you to updated information and new services.
B.F.A.S never releases e-mail addresses to its representatives. B.F.A.S also does not offer, or allow for the selling of, any user-provided information to third parties. B.F.A.S will always protect the personal information that you share with us. B.F.A.S stores information internally in a controlled, secure environment.
