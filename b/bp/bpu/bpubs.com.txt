Privacy
Privacy Statement for BPubs.com
BPubs.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.bpubs.com.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information. Our site uses cookies to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly.
This site contains links to other sites. BPubs.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
We also use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we may use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by the ad companies, and we do not have access to this information.
Public Forums
This site may make chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. When content is submitted to this site, BPubs.com requires a contact name and e-mail address. We endeavour to limit modification requests to the original
contact name and e-mail address. Modification requests by publishers, authors, or copyright holders will take priority over, and supersede submissions/modifications from other parties.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where we request information about the visitor. Users can send requests by e-mail to admin@bpubs.com.
Publication Inclusion Choice/Opt-Out
BPubs.com actively develops our collection of Business Publications. We also accept recommendations from our users. The nature of our service requires the use of “deep links”. Correct/Update
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided. Users must use the submission URL located at:
http://www.bpubs.com/cgi/links/modify.cgi
The user must provide a matching “E-mail” and “Contact Name” for submission requests to be honoured. As mentioned above, requests by Publishers or Authors will be given priority to modification requests.
