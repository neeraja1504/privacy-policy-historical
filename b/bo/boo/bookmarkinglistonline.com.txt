Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy and therefore to protect all your personal information. To easily discover and read all the privacy guidelines, we have put it on our homepage.
When visiting our site, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Most importantly, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
Cookies
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. Our site don’t use cookies. However, Some of our business partners use cookies on our site (for example, www.google.com ). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies, once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising. If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options.
Links
This Web site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for any content, products or services offered on any third-party sources. All the links are displayed to make them approachable to you easily. This Privacy notice will not recount to any Private Information that you provide to, or that is gathered by, any third-party website operator.
Advertisers
We use outside ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies and/or web beacons to collect data in the ad serving process and are collected by our ad companies, and we do not have access to this information. We work with the ad company: Google Adsense . Please check their websites for respective privacy policies.
We have included links on this site for your use and reference. We are not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from our own.
