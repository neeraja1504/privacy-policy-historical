Bet on Sports and Online Casino at Bookmaker.com.com - Privacy Policy
Bookmaker.com will always strive to provide an accountable and secure gaming and banking experience.
Our customers can be confident that, in doing business with this site, their personal details and financial information will be kept private and will never be misused.
Information gathered on this website is used only in the business of this site and will never, under any circumstances, be shared, sold, or divulged to any other persons or entities.
Your Security
We are proud of our reputation for fair play and secure and timely transfers of funds. Bookmaker.com uses Windsor Processing Services Limited for financial transactions. The information relating to each transaction is protected by our unique encryption system. For more information on our deposit methods visit the secure Banking section on our website.
The quality of your account details and financial information relating to your account is assured as Bookmaker.com records all of your transactions. We provide you with a fully transparent comprehensive audit trail on all the games you play detailing the sums wagered, won or lost. We believe that this will give you absolute confidence in all your dealings with Bookmaker.com.
Your Privacy
Bookmaker.com respects and appreciates your decision to play our games and we wish to ensure your total privacy. We guarantee that access to your account information is restricted to you by your unique username and password.
Your customer details are stored and managed under the strictest supervision. We adhere to the stringent tests set out by the Data Protection Agency in England, verified by Thawte to ensure your peace of mind.
For further information on how Bookmaker.com uses, protects and stores information we receive from our site visitors and our customers please do not hesitate to contact our Technical team by sending an email to help@Bookmaker.com.
