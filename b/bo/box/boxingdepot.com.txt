Privacy Policy
We will never sell or share your personal or email information with any third party
We will only contact Mailing List members with offers from Boxing Depot and will never rent, share or sell their names or emails
We will contact those not on the Mailing List only about their specific orders
This site uses ClickTale web analytics service. ClickTale may record mouse clicks, mouse movements, scrolling activity as well as any text you type in this website. ClickTale does not collect personally identifiable information that you do not voluntarily enter in this website. ClickTale does not track your browsing habits across web sites which do not use ClickTale services. For more information see http://www.clicktale.com/privacy_service.aspx - Privacy Policy for Information Collected by the ClickTale Web Analytics Service.
You can choose to disable the Service at http://www.clicktale.net/disable.html
