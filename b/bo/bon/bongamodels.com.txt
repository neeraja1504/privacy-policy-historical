Privacy Policy for Models and Studios
I hereby authorize BongaCams to process technical data related to my visit to the BongaCams website and my personal data, including sensitive data (e.g. relating to my sexual life), provided by me or collected during the registration process in accordance with the present Privacy Policy, during the period of my subscription with BongaCams and after cancellation of my subscription during the period necessary for BongaCams to comply with legal requirements.
I have not and will not provide any false information and/or documents to BongaCams. Therefore, I recognize that BongaCams has the right to, immediately and unilaterally, terminate the present Agreement upon the slightest suspicion of forgery.
I allow all my data to be inspected by BongaCams, randomly, resorting to any existing means for such effect. Notwithstanding, I am aware that it is not their duty to proceed with such inspection and said entities will not be considered responsible, either jointly or severally, in case of my unlawful conduct.
I agree and authorize BongaCams to obtain and store information automatically from my computer used to visit BongaCams website (with use of cookies and similar technologies). BongaCams may track the visitor’s visit to the website by giving a choice to accept Cookies when entering. Cookies help to collect anonymous data by tracking user trends and patterns.
I agree and allow BongaCams to collect, process and communicate to its processors, including the processors situated in countries not ensuring an adequate level of protection according to European Commission, the following types of data/information related to visitor:
information that the visitor voluntarily provide to and/or authorize to view, such as names, email address, address, date of birth and other miscellaneous account information submitted through BongaCams Submission Forms.
number of visits and areas of BongaCams pages visited by the visitors. Software and hardware attributes might get logged too, along with any other data that can be gained from the general internet environment, such as browser type, IP address, etc.
private communications, such as telephone conversations, chat logs, faxes and letters to BongaCams Staff along with e-mail messages to Adult Service Providers (ASP) or to our Staff. BongaCams also keeps Adult Service Providers (ASP) chat client logs for a limited period of time.
BongaCams will use the data collected from the visitors for mainly general purposes, such as improving services, contacting the visitors, customizing the website content and for promotional marketing purposes, to the extent allowed by law.
By accepting this Agreement, the visitor also agrees that BongaCams may also research behavior patterns and trends to improve the visitors’ user experience.
BongaCams takes serious security measures to grant maximum protection to information against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or deletion of data. The visitors’ details are always protected by highly sophisticated security system.
BongaCams guards the visitors’ information on electronic and physical levels.
Besides using its own security software and mechanisms, BongaCams also incorporates the most advanced security technologies available in order to ensure maximum safety of its visitors.
BongaCams employees' access to any personal information of BongaCams visitors is extremely limited and they are bound by confidentiality obligations. Therefore, they might be subject to disciplinary measures, including the termination of their contracts and in serious cases even criminal prosecution should they fail to meet these strict obligations.
BongaCams does not rent, sell, trade, share or otherwise transfer information to outside parties except the communication to BongaCams group companies and services providers in order to ensure the good functioning of BongaCams services.
BongaCams does not allow its performers to record or capture the members’ personal camera feeds during private shows. Such action is prohibited on the website.
The visitors may obtain a copy of any personal information that BongaCams process and ask for rectification of any incorrect personal data, upon written request to the contacts provided in the present Agreement and indication of the address to which the information must be sent. If the visitor would like to oppose the processing of personal data by BongaCams, he/she is entitled to cancel his/her account on BongaCams at any time as described above.
BongaCams, upon reception of the above mentioned request, will communicate the personal data concerning the visitor to the email address provided during the registration process on BongaCams. BongaCams shall be held harmless in case the right, foreseen in the previous clause, is exercised and the personal data is lost or subject to unauthorized access.
