Privacy and security
At GohalfPrice England, we consider your confidential personal information right as paramount as possible and we commit ourselves to protect this information.
This engagement of confidentiality flows between both parties to create a bond of confidence between us. The personal information collected within the framework of the remote sale is obligatory ;this information which is required for the processing and delivery of orders results as an invoice. This information is strictly confidential. Any alterations of this information automatically rejects the order.
Personal information
Information and data concerning you is necessary to manage your order and our trade relations. Information required by you is certainly necessary for us to carry out your requests. It is important for us to know you. This will help us improve the services we offer you on www.brandalley.com

Cookies
Cookies are very tiny text files containing information stored on your computer when you visit certain web pages that record your preference. The cookies on brandalley.com automatically deletes any information once the web page is closed.
Web code(html)
Certain web pages of the site can sometimes contain electronic images or " web code", which makes it possible to keep track of the total number of visitors that visit the webpage. The web codes can be used by some of our partners to measure and improve the effectiveness of some publicities. In any circumstance, the information obtained via these web codes is strictly anonymous and simply makes it possible to gather statistics on the frequencies of visitors on the web site. This enables us to provide a better service to our site users.
Financial security
We do not store any financial information concerning your credit card details. All this information is preserved in a secured way by our CIC bank. It is specified that with an immediate payment by bank card, (the only means of payment suggested by Brandalley on its web site), the user will carry out his/her payment via Brandalley site using the TPE system (Terminal Electronic Payment) of CIC bank.
The Brandalley site is secured by encoding S.S.L. (Secure Socket Layer) signed by VERISIGN organization ,in order to most effectively protect all possible data relating to the means of payment. Thus, the banking data of the user are re-transmitted at the bank which makes it secured by encoding S.S.L . Consequently, the banking data of the user will not be transmitted on the information processing system of Brandalley.
The responsibility for Brandalley is consequently released for this reason.
