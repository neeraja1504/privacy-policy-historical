Privacy Policy
All thoughts and views are the author’s opinions and are not endorsed by any other party.
Information Collected
Your Email address is required when leaving a comment on this blog. Your Email will never be made available to the public, nor will it be sold to any third party vendors.
Brown Thoughts uses website analytics software which report your I.P., browser, operating system, and geographic location. These statistics are used to fine-tune this blog’s content for optimal performance.
Cookies are saved in your browser to identify you as a returning visitor. You are welcome to delete cookies from this blog anytime.
Google uses cookies to collect data for their ad serving process.
Comments Policy
While comments on this blog are not moderated, Brown Thoughts reserves the right to delete or edit comments that are deemed unacceptable. Examples of unacceptable comments are racism, foul language, links to adult/gambling/pharmacy/spam blogs, and abusive comments that disrupt the normal flow of conversation.
