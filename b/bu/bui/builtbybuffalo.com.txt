Buffalo - Brighton based interactive web design, development & e-commerce
Privacy
Your use of this site implies your agreement and acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree please refrain from providing us with any personal information.
Personal information
We may ask for personal information when you interact with the site. This information is held securely and can only be accessed by Buffalo.
Despite these measures, please make sure you keep your information safe at all times as we are not responsible for the actions of third parties.
Logs
We store information on site visitors only to provide usage statistics. None of the information is personal or could in anyway be linked to an individual or company. The information gathered will be used to improve the site and the user experience.
Personal details
We do not sell, trade or transfer your personal information in any form to third parties unless ordered by a legal entity. We are the only people who have access to this data and we promise to keep it that way.
Links
There are external links on our site and we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these websites. We cannot be liable for the contents and activities of these websites.
Cookies
We do not currently use cookies on this site, however this may change in the future. Rest assured though, we will not use this data for anything other than monitoring our site. No personal details are recorded.
Choices and opt-out
Should you no longer wish to hear from us (we will be sorry to see you go!) send us an email to hello@builtbybuffalo.com and we will remove you from our correspondence list. Likewise if you want to update your details - let us know and we will be happy to oblige.
Future policy changes
This policy may evolve over time and we reserve the right to carry out any changes and request that you visit this page regularly, as these changes may affect you as a visitor to our site.
Questions?
If you have questions about this privacy policy or the privacy aspects of our services please contact us by email at hello@builtbybuffalo.com or phone us on +44 (0)845 466 0030.
