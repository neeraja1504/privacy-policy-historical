Privacy Policy
We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate
our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses
our information gathering and dissemination practices for this
Web site
This web site will not disclose cardholder information to third
parties, other than to our Processing Bank and their agents; this
is for the sole purpose of completing a Credit Card or Online
Check transaction. We will not store any confidential information
except where we have taken appropriate industry standard
security measures to protect this information Confidential
information may be stored behind a secure firewall and/or
in encrypted form.
In the event that that this web site is discontinued, fails or
otherwise ceases to process, any database containing
Cardholder s personal information, Card numbers, Checking
account information or other transaction information will not be
considered an asset of the business and will not be sold or
disclosed to a third party. This web site/business is required
to immediately notify our processing Bank if a loss of transaction
information is experienced or if we otherwise suspect that the
information may have been compromised
This site contains may contain links to other sites We are not
responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web
sites. We do not share merchant information with these partners
except in the normal course of business when it is necessary for
the two companies to fulfill a customer request
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss,
misuse and alteration of the information under our control
A state-of-the-art firewall separates our Web pages and Web
data from unauthorized access. The credit card information
provided in the course of on-line transactions between our
merchant processor and customers is received from and
transmitted to our merchant processor s secure servers from
our secure server. All transaction data is encrypted using 128 bit
SSL encryption, except for those transactions which originate
from outside the United States, which are encrypted using
40 bit SSL encryption.

modifying information previously provided Please send your
request to the email or mail address shown on our Contacts
Page

Thank you for preferring our company   m.businessnameusa.com.  We have done our best to ensure the security and privacy of your transactions with us. Rest assured that your credit card information and other personal data will be treated with the highest standards of safety, security, and confidentiality.
We've made every effort to make our system a secure one.  In order to provide our customers with the most secure and private online experience our company uses state-of-the-art technology to protect credit card numbers and other confidential information you provide when placing an order. The "padlock" symbol on the browser bar at the bottom of your monitor indicates that you have entered our secure zone. Our security system automatically encrypts your personal information, including credit card number, name, address, and telephone number, so that it can't be read while over the Internet. This is the industry standard for securing web transactions. Any information that you send to us while at our secure site is "encrypted" before it is transmitted over the web.
Personal Information Security
For obtaining a seller's permit, a federal tax id or a state employer number, we require your Social Security number and, for Texas residents, a date of birth.  As with all applications for legal documents, we are required to obtain this information before we are able to provide service. All information submitted is protected by our secure server, which automatically encrypts your personal information, name, address, and telephone number, so that it cannot be read while traveling over the Internet.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance
This policy complies with the California online privacy protection act. In essense, the act requires that we don't share the information we collect from you with third parties.
You can log in and change your personal information at any time by going to your control panel and clicking on the 'Edit Profile' page.
Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance
We fully comply with COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act), because we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.
Online Privacy Policy Only
