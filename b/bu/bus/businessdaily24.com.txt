Privacy and Policy - Business Daily 24
**Privacy and policy forhttps://businessdaily24.com/ **
If you are considering for more information or have any queries or question about our privacy and policy, please feel free to contact us via email at info@businessdaily24.com
At businessdaily24.com, the privacy of our visitor is tremendously important to us. This privacy policy document summaries the types of personal information is received and collected by businessdaily24.com and how it is cast-off.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, businessdaily24.com makes use of log files. The information private the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, referring/exit pages, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, , and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies and Web Beacons
businessdaily24.com does use cookies to collection information about visitors favorites, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
DoubleClick DART Cookie
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on businessdaily24.com
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to businessdaily24.com and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
Businessdaily24.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party publicists
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. Businessdaily24.com privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so finished your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
