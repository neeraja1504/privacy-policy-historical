Privacy Policy
This notice discloses the privacy practices for the BinaryTranslator.com website. This privacy notice applies to information collected by this web site.
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing
We do not store any information submitted to this site for the purposes of translation. We only ever have access to information that you may use to contact us with. We do not rent or sell this information to anyone, we only use it to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.
Cookies
Cookies are an industry standard tool used by most websites. Advertisers on this website will use cookies to deliver ads that are more relevant to you. We have no access to or control over these. That said, they should in no way cause damage to your computer.
