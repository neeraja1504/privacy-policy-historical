Information We Collect and How We Use It:
This website may collect certain information from and about its users by creating a browser Cookie or dropping a Web beacon through third party websites.
A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a Web Server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
The information collected is non-personally identifiable information such as website visited, approximate location, time of visit, browser information, and/or website related content. This data is collected over time to build a user profile based on user browsing history, behaviors, and interests.
Our data collection is designed to collect and categorize only anonymous, non-personally identifiable information. We will never collect or use any personal information such as name, address, social security number, or credit card number.
Opt Out:
Opting out of our services will prevent us from collecting data when a user visits any of our partner sites. By doing so, advertisements will no longer be tailored to your interests.
To opt out of having data collected and written to cookies placed by our website or third party partners, please click here.
Note that after choosing to opt out, if you use a different computer or a different browser, or if you delete or block browser cookies, you may need to repeat the opt-out steps. Most web browsers also enable you to block browser cookies. You can refer to your web browser's documentation to get more information on how to block or delete cookies directly through your browser settings.
