This is the web site of Blackberry Freeware.
We can be reached on our contact us page should you have any questions.
We do not seek to collect any private information of those visiting our sites and web properties. Customers may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by e-mailing us at the above address.
