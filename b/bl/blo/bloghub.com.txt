Bloghub.com - blog directory & blog search engine : Privacy Policy
Internet user privacy and confidentiality is of the utmost importance to Bloghub.com. Our reputation and integrity is directly related to how we serve our clients and customers. Please find below a description of how Bloghub.com strives to protect the privacy of Internet users, and maintain the Internet as a highly credible medium.
User Information
Personal information submitted by users to Bloghub.com is used for the purposes of maintaining the integrity of the website and its contents, contacting our users when necessary, and improving the quality of service provided by Bloghub.com. This information is not provided to 3rd parties, except when requested by the user or legally required. Bloghub.com may offer opt-in opportunities with 3rd party partners - this information cannot be protected by Bloghub.com after being released (with your permission) to the 3rd party.
Bloghub.com servers also collect information which is specific to how you use the Internet. Bloghub.com servers note details such as the server you are logged onto, the geographic location of the ISP and type of browser you use, and your IP address. This information is obtained solely for the purpose of maintaining the site's integrity and improving our service.
There are instances where a user may provide specific personal information in response to an ad, survey, or registration questionnaire. For such programs, Bloghub.com may employ this information in a compiled format to provide to clients a precise profile of the type of individuals using the Bloghub.com website. Under no circumstances will this information be released on an individual basis.
The Cookie Principle
Bloghub.com may use cookies to improve and better understand your Web browsing experience. When you first log in to certain Bloghub.com services, you may be assigned a unique random ID to your computer browser - commonly known as a "cookie." We do this to understand the way a user uses our site and to improve the quality of your experience with Bloghub.com.
3rd Party Measurement Services
Often, advertisers or other Bloghub.com partners will employ a third-party measurement service other than Bloghub.com for tracking the performance of an ad program. In these instances, we have no control over the information collected by the third party, and may not be held responsible for the actions of the third party measurement service where collected information is concerned.
Contacting Us
If you have any questions about the above privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact privacy(at)Bloghub.com .
