Privacy Policy | Blog.co.in - Create Blog is Easy
Blog.co.in doesn’t sell / disclose the information collected about the users during registration process.
We may aggregate your information with information from other users (without personally identifying you) and share it with our business partners or others.
We automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookies, and the pages you request.
We use the services provided by StatCounter.com and Google Analytics for web traffic analysis.
Advertisements appearing on Blog.co.in may be delivered to users by Blog.co.in or one of our advertising partners. Our advertising partners may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement. In this way, advertising partners (or “ad networks”) may compile information about where you, or others who are using your computer, saw their advertisements and determine which ads are clicked on. This information allows an ad network to deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This privacy policy covers the use of cookies by Blog.co.in and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers. We do not share any personally identifiable information with advertisers.
