Batrus Hollweg
Batrus Hollweg International knows that you care how information about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting Batrushollweg.com, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.
What Personal Information About Clients Does Batrushollweg.com Gather?
The information we learn from clients and future clients helps us personalize and continually improve your experience at Batrushollweg.com. Here are the types of information we gather.
Information You Give Us: We receive and store any information you enter on our Web site or give us in any other way. You can choose not to provide certain information. We use the information that you provide for such purposes as responding to your requests for information, and customizing future features on our site.
E-mail Communications: To help us make e-mails more useful and interesting, we often receive a confirmation when you open e-mail from Batrushollweg.com if your computer supports such capabilities. If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us, please let us know by emailing us at info@batrushollweg.com.
Information from Other Sources: We might receive information about you from other sources and add it to our account information.
What About Cookies?
Batrushollweg.com does not employ the use of cookies. Our online applications BHIOASIS.com and BHISurvey.com use minimal "session" cookies only, that are used to be sure that information is dispatched to our server efficiently and are removed when you have logged out of the application.
Does Batrus Hollweg.com Share the Information It Receives?
Information about our clients is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others.
