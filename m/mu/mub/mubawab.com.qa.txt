The data collected are adequate, pertinent and not excessive in relation to the ambit, the determined, explicit, legitimate ends and services of Mubawab.

The sending of personal data through the use of the electronic forms of Mubawab or, as the case may be, electronic mail messages, supposes the express consent of the sender to the automated treatment of the data included in the indicated media, services and products related to the objects of the Mubawab website, and the electronic sending of communications with information related to Mubawab and its initiatives. 4- How the information is used and disclosed to third parties.
As a general practice, we do not provide your personally identifiable information to third parties. The major exception to this practice is that if you provide information to us in connection with a product or service, we may share your information with the owner/manager of the product owner or the service company. We do not sell your email address or your name and personal demographic information to mass marketers, however, on particular pages where we ask for your personally identifiable information, we may explicitly tell you that the data we are collecting on that page will be shared with third parties, in which case those disclosures shall override anything to the contrary in this policy.
In some cases, we use your personally identifiable information to provide information to you, such as to send to you requested newsletters or to provide to you requested services.
In some situations, we share your personally identifiable information with third parties who provide services to our site in order to enhance the functionality available to you on our website, and to provide you with new products and services. We use third party vendors to conduct and/or facilitate a survey of our clients and customers to obtain information on our services.
We use your information on an aggregated basis to do such things as operate our site, enhance our site, and sell and deliver advertising. We also use this data, both aggregated and personally identifiable; to enhance your on-site experience by displaying content and marketing messages that we believe will be of interest to you.
We may use your information to notify you about information related to your inquiry. We may also use your information to contact you about updates to our site and services, and to inventory available from our products owners and services companies.

7- Cookies and IPs
The user accepts the use of cookies and IP follow-ups. Our website traffic analyzer uses cookies and IP follow-ups that allow us to collect data for statistical purposes, such as: date of first visit, number of visits, date of last visit, URL and domain from which it proceeds, explorer user and screen resolution. However, the user may decide to deactivate and/or eliminate these cookies by following the instructions given by their Internet browser.
Advertising on this site includes Google AdSense, a system that uses "cookies" to display publicity content that relates to what pages the user is viewing.
When a user accesses a site that utilizes Google AdSense, it introduces a cookie into his browser allowing Google to collect the information about the user's activity and publish Google AdSense advertising relevant to the user's browsing experience.
The user can deactivate Google cookies by deactivating cookies in their browser.
