MusicBabylon.com - Privacy Policy
The following policy is intended to explain how your personal information will be treated as you use of our site. Personal information includes your name, e-mail addresses, click-through activity and any other personal information you may provide here.
Traffic data
Each time a visitor comes to a MusicBabylon.com Web site, our server logs some basic technical information such as browser and platform, visitor's domain name and referral data. We are also processing the visitor's activity through our site. We may present the results of this processing to outside companies, but no one will be able to identify you or contact you because of this data.
Cookies
Companies which place advertising on our site have the ability to assign cookies to you. We may use cookies in some parts of our site (in forms to remember nickname and e-mail you used last time), but we cannot control that cookies coming with banner ads.
Other sites
MusicBabylon.com may contain many links to other sites on the Web. We cannot control and are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these other Web sites.
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement, please, complete our contact us form.
