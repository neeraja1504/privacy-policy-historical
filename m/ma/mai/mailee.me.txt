Email Marketing - Know our Privacy Policy | Mailee.me
We collect the email address of those who communicate with us via email, we add information about which pages clients have accessed or visited, and information given voluntarily by the client (such as research or registration data). The information we collect are used to improve the content of our web pages and the quality of our services. Those are not shared or sold to other parties for commercial goals, except for providing products or services you have solicited, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
It is necessary to share information to investigate, prevent or take actions regarding illegal, suspiciously fraudulent activities, situations involving potential threats to the physical security of any individual, violation of the Terms of Use, or any other form required by law
We transfer information about you if Panop or Mailee are acquired by or are merged with other company. In this case, Panop will notify you before your information are transferred and become subject to other privacy policies.
Collecting and using information
When you open an account at Mailee we solicit information such as your name and email address. If you register for a paid account, we solicit billing information, whether directly or by a billing service (such as PayPal).
Panop uses the collected information for the following purposes: providing product or services, billing, identification and authentication, improvement of services, contact and research
Cookies
A cookie is a little set of data, frequently including a unique anonymous identifier, sent to a computer browser of a given website and is stored on the hard drive of your computer.
Cookies are required to use Mailee's service.
We use cookies to record information from the current session, but we do not use permanent cookies. It is necessary to login on Mailee's site after a certain amount of time to protect you against accidental access from others to the content of your account.
Data storing
Panop uses third party services of hosting and infrastructure to provide hardware, software, network, storage and the needed technology to run Mailee. Although Panop retains all code, databases, and all the rights to Mailee's application, you retain all the rights to your data.
Disclosure
Panop may disclose personal identifiable information under special circumstances, such as to answer subpoenas or when your actions violate the Terms of Use.
Changes
Panop may periodically update this policy. We will notify your abot significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a warning to the email address provided in you Mailee.me account, or by placing a warning in our site.
Questions
Any doubt or question surrounding this Privacy Policy should be addressed to support@mailee.me.
