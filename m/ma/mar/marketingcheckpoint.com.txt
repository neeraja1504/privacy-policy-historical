Privacy Policy - Marketing Checkpoint
(Updated May 3rd, 2016)
By visiting this site, you are agreeing to the terms of this Privacy Policy and the accompanying terms.
Collected Information
Information collected includes three types: usage data, personal identifiable data, and marketing/advertising data. Regular activity using the website may include collecting additional information such as your IP address, web browser type & version, language settings, URLs visited, and time spent on the website.
Financial transactions are recorded including amount spent, amount earned, and amount owed. Credit Card information is not recorded nor stored on our site or servers.
We allow third-party companies to collect certain information when you visit our web site. These companies may utilize cookies, pixels or other technologies to collect and use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., hashed data, click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can visit the websites of the Digital Advertising Alliance at www.aboutads.info and Networking Advertising Initiative at www.networkadvertising.org/choices/.
California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits customers of Shrawk LLC who are California residents to request certain information regarding its disclosure of their personally identifiable information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. You may request a list of all of the third parties with whom we share your PII by submitting a support request and placing “California Third Party Disclosure” in the subject line. Please allow up to thirty days for a response.
Use of Cookies
The use of cookies on the website reflects a selective need to remember who has visited. The purpose is to reward affiliates with appropriate opportunities to earn a sale commission based on the purchase from a member who they’ve referred to the website. In addition, cookie data helps us record necessary website analytics.
Changing Your Information
You may change your information at any time. If you wish to remove your account, please contact us.
How We Use Your Information
Updates and notifications are sent to you, mostly via email.
Necessary contact for tax documents and other legal information required by law.
Anonymous usage of the website is used to make future changes and improvements. We also use the data to track popularity among content
Operated In The USA
The website is operated in the United States. Data from anyone outside the US will be transferred to the US. Use of the website is an agreement to this transfer.
External Websites
By using the website you may encounter links/hyperlinks to external websites. We do not control nor have control over the privacy policies of third party websites nor their content. We are not responsible for such privacy and content of those websites.
Policy Changes
As stated at the top of the page, this policy is active as of the posted date. Changes may be made periodically and unannounced. It is your responsibility to check this page for the changes without notification. By accessing the website after we make any changes to this policy, you acknowledge and accept any all of the changes.
