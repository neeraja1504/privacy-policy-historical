The updated list of these parties may be requested from Marijuana Central at any time.
Place
The Data is processed at Marijuana Central's operating offices and in any other places where the parties involved with the processing are located. For further information, please contact Marijuana Central.

The Data is kept for the time necessary to provide the service requested by the User, or stated by the purposes outlined in this document, and the User can always request that Marijuana Central suspend or remove the data.

The Data concerning the User is collected to allow the Application to provide its services, as well as for the following purposes: Registration and authentication. The Personal Data used for each purpose is outlined in the specific sections of this document.

The rights of Users
Users have the right, at any time, to know whether their Personal Data has been stored and can consult Marijuana Central to learn about their contents and origin, to verify their accuracy or to ask for them to be supplemented, cancelled, updated or corrected, or for their transformation into anonymous format or to block any data held in violation of the law, as well as to oppose their treatment for any and all legitimate reasons. Requests should be sent to Marijuana Central at the contact information set out below. This Application does not support “Do Not Track” requests. To determine whether any of the third party services it uses honor the “Do Not Track” requests, please read their privacy policies.
Changes to this privacy policy
Marijuana Central reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time by giving notice to its Users on this page. It is strongly recommended to check this page often, referring to the date of the last modification listed at the bottom. If a User objects to any of the changes to the Policy, the User must cease using this Application and can request that Marijuana Central erase the Personal Data. Unless stated otherwise, the then-current privacy policy applies to all Personal Data Marijuana Central has about Users.
