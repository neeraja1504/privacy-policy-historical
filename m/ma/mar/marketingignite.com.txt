Internet Marketing Company in Bangkok, Thailand
Marketing Ignite Co. Ltd.,  believes that strong internet privacy is crucial for the ongoing success of the Internet.   Marketing Ignite will never release your personal information to anyone else without your consent. Your privacy is very important to us and we will not jeopardize your trust.
Information Collection and Use:
Marketing Ignite only collects information that you enter on our site so we will be able to respond to your questions and requests.  We do by no means distribute or share (sell or rent) this information to anyone ever.
Use Of Traffic Data
**** Every time a visitor comes to Marketing Ignite website, we collect some information to improve the overall quality of the user's online experience at our site. At Marketing Ignite, we collect referral data, which means we record the address of the last URL a user visited prior entering Marketing Ignite website. Marketing Ignite receives your IP address each time you view a web page from our site. Your IP address is not linked to your email address or any other personally identifiable information. Your IP address may be used for various purposes such as collect browser and platform type, visitor information for purposes of service improvement, but no one will be able to identify you or contact you. Website visitor behavior information is compiled in aggregate and does not represent individually identifiable information.
Use Of Data From Paid Submissions Or Optimization Services
All website information details from our advertisers provided for our pay per click search engines services will only be forwarded to Yahoo, Google, and MSN.  However, we will not provide your email address to these engines.  Instead we will provide them our own email address.
Policy Amendments
Marketing may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes in the way we use your personal information, or introduce new products and services, this policy will be updated and we will notify you by posting a prominent announcement on the site.
