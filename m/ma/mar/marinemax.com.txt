Privacy Policy: MarineMax
At MarineMax, we are committed to protecting and preserving your privacy. MarineMax is the sole owner of any information collected via its website, mobile app, including, without limitation MarineMax Service Express, mailings, off-site events, or marketing activities. Any information collected by MarineMax will not be rented, sold, or traded to any outside sources. This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed by MarineMax when you access or use our website, mobile app, sign up for emails, or otherwise interact with us.
Collection and Use of Information
MarineMax does want to communicate information that is interesting, newsworthy and relevant to you. We do this through our website, emails and mailings. The information we collect while you are on our website allows us to communicate information to you that is personalized to your interests.
As you use the MarineMax website we may collect information about you including the following:
Log Information: We collect information about your use of our website including the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address, and the page you visited before navigating to our website.
Device Information: We collect information about the computer or mobile device you use to access our website or mobile app, including hardware model, operating system and version, mobile network information and unique device identifiers.
Location Information: We may collect information about your location when you access our website or mobile app.
Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: MarineMax.com uses "cookies" which are small text files that are stored on the user's computer and gather and keep track of information related to the user. We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about you and your interactions with our website or mobile app including browsing behavior and engagement with our website or mobile app. This information is used to provide you with an experience on our website or mobile app that is relevant to your boating lifestyle and interests.  Although many website browsers are set to accept cookies, you can set your website browser to reject cookies, but this may prevent you from taking full advantage of our website features or receiving personalized information and services.
Information that you give us may be combined with other personally identifiable information (such as updated address and demographic information) from third-party sources to make our marketing efforts more efficient. Examples of other information we receive from third-party sources include updated delivery and address information, which we use to correct our records and deliver your next mailing more easily.
We use the information we collect for a variety of purposes, including to:
Personalize your experience with our website to be relevant to your boating lifestyle and interests
Communicate with you about news, events, MarineMax and partner offers, and other information we think will be of interest to you
Provide, maintain and improve the website
Monitor and analyze the website usage and trends
Process and deliver contest or sweepstakes entries and prizes
While on our website, you may choose to receive emails from MarineMax. Additionally while on our website, if you fill out a form requesting more information, you will be added to our email list. You may also sign up to receive our emails by contacting us at marketing@marinemax.com or 727-531-1700. Upon signing up for MarineMax emails, you will receive the MarineMax monthly newsletter and emails about news, events, partner offers and other information we think will be of interest to you. As we learn more about you and your boating interest, we will strive to send you emails that are relevant to your boating lifestyle and interests.
General Data Protection Regulation: We value your privacy and are currently working on making our site better to ensure we meet the new data protection regulations in the European Union. Check back for more information.
Sharing of Information
MarineMax is the sole owner of any information collected via its website, mobile app, mailings, off-site events, or marketing activities. Any information collected by MarineMax will not be rented, sold, or traded to any sources outside of MarineMax. MarineMax may share information with you from selected companies that we believe offer products or services of interest to you and are complimentary to your relationship with MarineMax. In doing this, we will not share your information with these companies; all such communications will originate from MarineMax.
