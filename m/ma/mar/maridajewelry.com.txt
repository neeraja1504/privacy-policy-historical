Privacy Policy
MARIDA Jewelry is commited to protecting the security of our customers’ personal information. Access to your personal information is restricted to those who are directly in charge of providing products to you. We maintain physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect your personal information.
Whenever you shop online with MARIDA Jewelry, we obtain all information necessary to complete and process your order including; your name, address, phone number and email address. Under absolutely no circumstances will we sell, loan, rent, trade or release your name, address, email address or any other personal information to a third party.  Your credit card is processed by Paypal, and we do not have access to your credit card details. We may retain details of your product purchases with us.
MARIDA Jewelry uses your email and occasionally your phone number to communicate with you regarding your current order. We may also send you promotional details via email if you have asked to receive such information. Each time you receive an email from us you will be given the option to be removed from our list.
