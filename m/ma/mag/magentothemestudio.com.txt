Privacy Policy - Peerforest - Peerforest
We respect your right towards privacy. Hence, this privacy policy will cover information, we will be collecting from you, while keeping your privacy intact:
We will collect personal information such as name, phone number, email address, correspondence address, only for the communication purpose through personal visit, email, phone call, or messenger chat. We will collect the general website information for enhancing the performance and looks of your website.
We will provide you with a proper explanation for using your personal or technical information. The information will be strictly used for fulfilling your orders, responding to your inquiries, supplying information to you within no time. We might also use the information, for informing you about our latest updates, new products, or services, only if you choose to do so.
We are not responsible for the privacy policies of the sites whose links are present on our website. In addition, we have cookies in our webpages which is the data stored on your computer when you visit our webpages. It is meant to recognize you whenever you visit our site again in the future.
We reserve the rights to modify this privacy policy at any given time. The modifications will be reflected immediately on our web pages.
We may release your personal information, pertaining to the government or judicial investigations, when required by the law.
