Math-Drills.Com Privacy and Cookies Policy
This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by Math-Drills.com and how it is used.
Math Drills.com and its partners set cookies on your computer or device as you access the website. This is done by almost all websites and should not be a surprise.
Math-Drills.com tracks our visitors for two specific purposes: 1. to gain insight into how the website is used and 2. to comply with requirements of our advertising partners. Our third party partners track our visitors for their own purposes which you can read about in their privacy policies.
What does Math-Drills.com do?
. The information inside the server access logs includes Internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. We use this information to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. We do not share this information with any third parties other than in the most general sense. For example, we might tell someone how many visitors we had on a certain day.

What does Math-Drills.com do to protect your privacy?

What do third parties on Math-Drills.com do?

You will most likely still see advertisements on the website, but tracking cookies from those advertisements will not be set. Adsense may set a Math-Drills.com cookie even if you have third party cookies disabled in your browser. This cookie may look like it came from Math-Drills.com, but it was set by a third party.
