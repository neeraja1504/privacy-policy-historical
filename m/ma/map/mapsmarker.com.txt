Collection and Use of Personal Information
When purchasing a product, we collect the following personal information from you:
Contact information, such as name (required), email address (required), mailing address (optional), phone number (optional)
Unique identifiers, such as user name, account number, license number, password
As is the case  for most web sites, we automatically gather information about your computer/device  such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and operating system.
We use this information to
Fulfill your order
Send you an order confirmation
Respond to customer service requests
Respond to your questions and concerns

Plugin update checker data
Maps Marker Pro uses a plugin update checker library, which checks every 24 hours if a new version is available. The following unpersonalized info is transmitted and gets logged on our server with those requests:
IP address and domain of the server (e.g. 1.2.3.4, www.domain.com)
timestamp of the request (e.g. 2017-05-31 17:10:33)
Maps Marker Pro version number (e.g. 3.1)
WordPress version number (e.g. 3.1)
PHP Version (e.g. 7.2.1)
locale (e.g. en_US)
This data is only used in the context of the plugin update mechanism and is not shared with third parties.

User generated location data
All the location data (like markers, layers, longitude, latitude, pop-up text…) that you create with your plugin instance within your WordPress installation is stored in your database only – we do not collect this data, nor do we get notified about it.

Information Sharing
We will share your personal information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
We may also disclose your personal information
as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process
* when we believe in good faith, that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request
* if MapsMarker.com is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our web site of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information, as well as any choices you may have regarding your personal information
* to any other third party with your prior consent to do so.

Payment provider
You have to choose a payment processor like PayPal, Mastercard, Visa, American Express, Diners Club or Sofort AG through our payment provider payunity.com to finalize your order. You have to provide your credit card number directly to this provider and its privacy policy will apply to the information you provided on the corresponding website. We don’t store any credit card or PayPal login information on our servers nor can we access those data on Payunity´s servers.

Comments
When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection. An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After approval of your comment, your profile picture is visible to the public in the context of your comment. Affiliate program
If you choose to register for our affiliate program, you can use link to get provisions for sales on your site. If one of your links embedded on your sites is clicked, a so called return cookie is saved on the visitors computer so that in case he or she buys a license, this sale is referred to your affiliate account. We can clear those traffice logs on demand in total for your affiliate user or on IP basis for individual visitors.

Security
When we collect personal information directly from you, we follow generally accepted industry standards – like SSL encrypted transmission – to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission, and when we receive it. No method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have any questions about security on our website, you can contact us.
In order to make a purchase from us, you must use our shopping cart provider to finalize and pay for your order. Its privacy statement and security practices will also apply to your information. We encourage you to read that privacy statement before providing your information.
