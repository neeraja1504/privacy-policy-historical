Online-Bills | Online payment solutions for local government
Online-bills.com is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the www.online-bills.com web site and addresses data collection and usage guidelines. Using the Online-bills.com web site constitutes your consent to the privacy policies addressed herein.
The Online-bills.com web site does not require any personally identifiable information from visitors. Our web server software may log the IP address of the computer you are using to visit this web site, but this information is not shared and is only used to compile statistics on site traffic. Online-bills.com will only collect personal information that is voluntarily provided. Any information collected will be used solely to provide the information you are requesting. We will not share or sell your information.
Online-bills.com reserves the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy at any time. If you have any questions or concerns regarding how Online-bills.com collects, uses, or protects your personal information, please contact us.
