What are cookies?
Cookies are small pieces of information that websites stored in the user's browser. Websites will then have access to that information to analyze the effectiveness of our services and meet the needs of the user.
How are they used by MercadoJobs ?
MercadoJobs can troubleshoot problems that the user has while visiting our website.
In the case that the cookies generated spam, they are not viruses, spyware or anything similar.
MercadoJobs uses these cookies to improve the user experience. Cookies are used as a safety feature to prevent fraudulent operations and protect users; to get to know the preferences and interestes of the user and provide offers at the time of the connection; to be ablet o solve problems that may arise to improve the the performance of the website. These tools do not collect data about your name or last name or the address from where you connect .
MercadoJobs works with third-party websites that also may store cookies on the computers of our users . These websites are Facebook, Google analytics.
These tools do not collect data about your name or last name or the address you connect from. The information obtained is related to the number of pages visited , language, social networks in which our news are published , the city that the IP address is assiged to upon agreeement , the number of new users , frequency and recurrence of visitors , time of visit, the browser and type of the operator or terminal from which the check is performed.
