MediaArea - Privacy
Personal data
MediaArea tries to collect only the minimun personal data, they are not used to track users and they are not sold to other companies.
Here is the list of personal data we collect
User account (when you register as a user)
Email address
Name (not mandatory)
Username (not mandatory)
MediaConch API (when you connect MediaConch to MediaConchOnline)
IP address
Payment system (when you made a payment to support us)
IP address
If you want to retrieve all the data we have about you or if you want to delete your account you can contact us.
Cookies
MediaArea uses Google AdSense to display ads (you can avoid ads by supporting us), this service use cookies.
