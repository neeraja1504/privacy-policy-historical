MessageMagic Privacy Policy
MessageMagic is committed to customer service and we intend to uphold this by providing a private and secure browsing experience. The following policy will help you to understand how we will treat your personal information. As we continue to improve our site and as we take advantage of developments in technology, this policy may change from time to time so please check this policy on an ongoing basis for revisions and updates.
MessageMagic collects information from our web site in several ways. During registration, we ask for information such as your name, email address, a valid password, etc. you will be able to take full advantage of everything we have to offer, including free use of the software for a 7 day trial. You may also be asked for personal information when you report a problem with our site, or services; or, if you otherwise contact us.
MessageMagic is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, rent or otherwise distribute this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. Information is collected with the sole purpose of providing a working product, customer service, and payments to affiliates.
