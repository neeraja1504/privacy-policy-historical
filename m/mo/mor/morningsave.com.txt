By visiting this website, you accept this privacy policy. Bam! If you don't like it, don't visit, simple as that. This privacy policy also applies to any affiliated or linked domains, pages, features, content, or mobile applications.
We collect information about you, including:
Personally identifiable information (PII) that you provide us, such as name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and credit card number.
Information from third-party sites and services (e.g. Amazon, Google, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, etc) when you interact with our Site using your accounts from third-party sites and services. Information we may collect includes your basic profile information, username, email address, age, gender, location, and other demographic and geographical information.
Log data, such as your internet protocol (IP) address, internet service provider (ISP), browser type and version, operating system, time and date stamp, referring and exit pages, and clickstream data.
Aggregate information collected from cookies and pixel tags, such as visit duration, pages visited, and other data about your usage of our site.
Mobile device information, such as device type and location, if you use one of our mobile applications and enable (or do not disable) location services in the device settings.
Information you volunteer through our surveys or other methods, such as interests, preferences, and additional demographic information.
We use the information we collect to:
Enable you to participate in community forums, surveys, contests, and other site features.
Send you emails, tweets, newsletters, and similar communications about our sites, services, content, products, and offers.
Improve our sites, services, content, communications, products, and offers.
Conduct data analysis, statistical studies, and market research.
Display content based on your interests.
Enable you to purchase products, and us to fulfill your order.
Respond to your customer service requests or other communications.
Administer your account.

Mediocre's sites are not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we find that we do possess any such information, we'll delete it.
We use physical, electronic, and administrative measures to protect the information we collect. However, we cannot guarantee that our measures will never be breached or defeated. Be careful with the information you share in our forums, and use a robust password to safeguard your account.
