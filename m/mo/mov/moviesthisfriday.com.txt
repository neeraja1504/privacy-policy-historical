Moviesthisfriday.com Privacy Policy - Moviesthisfriday
At moviesthisfriday.com, we are totally responsible for your privacy terms.
Moviesthisfriday.com will never advertise, replace or reveal any of your individual information to the third party without your permission.
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to troubleshoot of your problem with our server. We assure you that your IP address or any information never be revealed to anyone or never be shared to the third party.
Cookies
As other sites, moviesthisfriday.com also uses cookies. Cookies are those data files which are to be stored on your computer. Cookies contain identification information that ensures us to see how our visitors are interacting with our website and how frequently they are move on to other site.
We also use cookies to deliver information according to our interest and save that so you don’t have to re-enter it again and again when you visit on our site. Information or data which is collected from cookies will never be shared or disclosed to third party.
Advertising
We use different advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our site. These advertisements may also contain some cookies. These cookies get banners ads which are collected by advertising networks and we don’t have information to access this.
If you have any query and question about our privacy policy then free to write us on moviesthisfriday.com.
