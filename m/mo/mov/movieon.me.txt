Every access to our website and each download of a file on our website are logged. The storage serves internal system-related and statistical purposes. 2. Cookies
The Internet pages use so-called cookies in several places. They serve to make our service more user-friendly, effective and safer. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer and saved by your browser. Most of the cookies we use are “session cookies”. They are automatically deleted after your visit. Cookies do not damage your computer and do not contain viruses.

Our pages integrate plugins of the social network Facebook, 1601 South California Avenue, Palo Alto, CA 94304, USA. The Facebook plugin, you agree to the Facebook logo or the “Like-Button” (“Like”) on our side. You will find an overview of Facebook-plugins here: http://developers.facebook.com/docs/plugins/
