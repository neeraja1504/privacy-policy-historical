Privacy and Policies
Privacy
In today's technological age, Mom Masterminds™ knows you care about how information about you is used and shared and ensures you that any information you provide is used only as necessary to conduct Mom Masterminds™ business.
IP Addresses & Cookies
Mom Masterminds™' website statistics program does keep track of IP addresses that visit this website. It also uses cookies to determine unique visits from a particular IP. This information is used to collect general statistics about website visitors and does not to track individual behavior. Our message board uses cookies to keep track of registered members preferences and to provide you with information on posts since your last visit. We do not use this information to track your individual behavior.
Community Participation
Please note that information you place in your "profile" within the community can be viewed by other members. If you do not wish to make your email address or personal information public, you can still register for the community. You should omit any personal information from the "profile" that you don't want seen by the general public.
By participating in the community, you agree that we may reproduce any of your contributions elsewhere on the website, in advertising or in our newsletter. However, we will only publish the information that you make available to other members.
