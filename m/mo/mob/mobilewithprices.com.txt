Privacy Policy - Mobile with Prices
Privacy carries paramount importance for mobilewithprices.com. We do not ask for any personal information for browsing to website. Only generalized limited information may be required just to get an idea of community wise or region wise visitors purely from business point of view; like advertisers and business partners. Any personal information of the readers is always optional and not a compulsory requirement for the visitors to enter the website and it is never provided to any third party. We ensure that email addresses of the users are kept saved from those who are collecting the addresses for bulk mail or newsletters etc. we modify these addresses to prevent them from unauthorized persons or parties. However if there are forum sections email address is required for completion of the registration. Still the email addresses are never provided to any third party. When you visit our website you may find third party ads. Such third parties may use your information but not giving your personal details like email address, telephone number name etc. mobilewithprices.com may contain advertisements of goods and services which may interest you the companies maybe employing cookies and clear GIFs to collect such information but information collecting like this is not identifiable unless you specifically it is suggested that you read privacy policies of the companies just to find out how they can serve you better. MobilewithPrices.com always respects and maintains your privacy. Any of your personal information is safe with us.
