Privacy Policy for MT4 Stats
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at info@mt4stats.com.
At www.mt4stats.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.mt4stats.com and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, MT4 Stats makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track userâ€™s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Emails
MT4 Stats will not and never sell email addresses for the use of spam, MT4 Stats will only sent emails regarding important website notices of MT4 Stats itself and it's partners. Also all email addresses are kept safe on a secure server and will never be shared or sold to anyone. MT4 Stats does not like spam either.
Cookies and Web Beacons
MT4 Stats does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include Google Adsense and Clickbank.
These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on MT4 Stats send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.
MT4 Stats has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. MT4 Stats' privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
