Privacy Policy - Mind360 Games
Privacy Policy
MIND360 is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our continued support for the responsible management of Internet users' data.
Non-personally identifiable information
MIND360 does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as your browser type or the type of computer operating system you use.
Personally identifiable information
Besides this, MIND360 also collects personally identifiable information. This information is stored when you register for the site. This information is used to provide customized content and for other means.
Cookies
This site requires the use of cookies. The use of cookies is an industry standard and you'll find cookies at most major web sites. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web site and is stored on your computer's hard drive. Cookies are used on this site to enhance the user experience.
Sharing of information
MIND360 does not share personal information with unrelated third parties. We may use personal information to improve our site. We may use your information, in aggregate form to do certain types of analysis. All data is used in aggregate form so at no point will you be personally identifiable.
Terms of Service
The Terms of Service found here (Terms of Service) are the rules that govern this website. Additional information may be found within the Terms of Service.
Changes in the privacy policy
MIND360 may amend this policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.
