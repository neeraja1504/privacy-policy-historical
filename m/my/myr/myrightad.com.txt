MyRightAd - Earn online, Work from Home, Earn commissions from Advertisers
1. Privacy Statement
At MyRightAd we are committed to protecting your privacy. We also want to make sure that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and under which circumstances, if any, we disclose it. This Privacy Policy outlines such information practices and describes the way MyRightAd handles your personal identifiable information and data. All information entered into or collected through www.MyRightAd.com will be treated accordingly with this policy.
2. TRUSTe Program
MyRightAd is a licensee of the TRUSTe Web Privacy Seal Program. Our privacy policy and information practices have been supervised by TRUSTe, which is an independent organization whose mission is to build confidence in the internet by reviewing privacy policies and security practices
3. Scope
This privacy policy covers the website www.MyRightAd.com. The TRUSTe program covers only the information that is collected through this website, and does not cover any other information that may be collected through software downloaded from www.MyRightAd.com.
4. Contact Information
If you have any questions, concerns or complaints regarding our Privacy Policy or MyRightAd´s information practices, please contact us.
5. Information Collection and Use
This section describes the information you provide to us, the information we collect from you when you navigate through our website, and the way we use such information.
5.1. Personal Information
Before using our service, users are required to first complete a registration form and provide MyRightAd with personal identifiable information such as complete name, date of birth, ID number, address, email, and phone number. This information can be changed, updated and/or corrected according to Section 9 of this Privacy Policy.
This personal identifiable information is not supplemented with information from other sources. However, in some cases, the information provided by the users on www.MyRightAd.com might be subject to internal verification processes for security and compliance reasons. Under these processes, we may request users to submit documents to verify some of the information provided on our website.
Said personal identifiable information is used by MyRightAd only to provide the electronic transfer service and fill customer’s orders. Then, when such orders are taken out, users are notified based on the personally identifiable information provided by them. This information is also used to tailor the website to the person to whom the information pertains; to send customers strictly administrative messages in the event a problem with their account arises, and to provide customer-service in response to their inquiries.
All personally identifiable information provided by users is retained for no longer than it is necessary to (i) fulfill the purpose for which it was collected and (ii) comply with De Macau laws and regulations. Under said laws and regulations we must keep such information from the moment users open an account to 5 years after they close it.
5.2. Cookies Information
A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer. We use both session cookies and persistent cookies. You can remove cookies by following directions provided in your browser, but in some cases, that may impact your ability to use MyRightAd´s website.
Session cookies collected are used to ensure that you are recognized when you move from page to page within MyRightAd´s website and that any information you have entered is remembered during your session. Persistent cookies collected are used to remember you on subsequent visits, speeding up your experience throughout the website. We do not tie cookies to personal information about the user.
We use another company to place cookies on your computer to collect non-personally identifiable information to compile aggregated statistics for us about visitors to our site.
5.3. Log Files Information
As most standard website servers, MyRightAd.com uses log files. These Log Files store information such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and transaction history.
The information stored in Log Files is used to examine traffic patterns within our website, analyze trends, administer the website and track user’s movement in the aggregate.
5.4. Usage of Information
MyRightAd is the sole owner of both the information provided by the users during the registration process and the one collected through cookies and file logs.
5.5. Information and Third Parties
Notwithstanding the Legal Disclaimer stated in Section 7 of this Privacy Policy, MyRightAd will not share, sell or rent users´ personally identifiable information with any third party, neither for marketing purposes nor for any other reason not strictly related to the primary purpose for which the information was collected, unless permission to do so is granted by the user.
5.6. Profile
MyRightAd does not collect or store information from third parties to create a profile of our users.
5.7. Children's Privacy
MyRightAd does not knowingly collect information from children under 18. We encourage parents and guardians to check their children’s activities online and to teach them to never give out personal information.
6. Communications from our Website 6.1. Service Announcements and Opt/outs
On rare occasions it is necessary to send out a strictly service-related announcement as indicated in Section 5.4 of this Privacy Policy. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance we might send users an email. Generally, users may not opt-out of these communications because they are not promotional in nature. However, they can deactivate their account by sending a request via contact us form. Those users who request the deactivation of their accounts will receive a confirmation email within the next 48 hours and will be effectively removed from the list of users to whom these communications are sent.
6.2. Customer Service
We communicate with users on a regular basis to provide requested customer-services and solve issues related to their accounts. These communications are usually made via email or through the live chat service provided in our website.
7. Legal Disclaimer
Though we make every effort to preserve user´s privacy, we may need to disclose all the personal identifiable information we have collected from our users when required by law, to comply with a current judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process.
8. Choice
Users have the opportunity to share personally identifiable information placed in www.MyRightAd.com with other users of our service. For example, when making a transaction, users may share their name, address, and email address with the receiver of their transactions Users are able to choose which personally identifiable information they wish to share. However, minimum information such as their MyRightAd account name, type of account, account number, and creation date shall always be shared.
9. Access
Users are able to correct material inaccuracies in their personally identifiable information at any time. To make those corrections user can visit the Personal Information page placed within their MyRightAd Account. However, users shall consider that both dates of birth and email addresses are subject to special procedures.
This information cannot be changed by users themselves. For security reasons, users willing to update their email addresses shall send a request through the contact us form or Just by Sending A Message From Our Website. Then, a support agent will request users to provide us with the following: date of birth, current e-mail address and the new e-mail address. Dates of birth, in turn, cannot be changed for compliance reasons. They can be corrected though, but only if the user remembers the date entered when filling the registration form and requests the correction through the contact us form provided in our site. Otherwise, users will be required to open a new account and use a correct date of birth as stated in the FAQ Section of our website.
10. Security
Our website takes every precaution to protect our users’ information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, their information is protected both online and off-line. When our website asks users to enter sensitive information using forms, it is encrypted for their protection. Aside from using SSL encryption, our website is analyzed and certified every day by THAWTE , which means our site is free from viruses, malwares and other online threats.
We also do everything in our power to protect user-information off-line. All of our users’ information, not just the personally identifiable above mentioned, is encrypted and restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job, for example, account managers or service representatives, are granted access to users´ information. They are also notified any time new policies are added or modified to ensure our users’ information is protected. In addition, all the servers that store personally identifiable information are in a secure environment, in a locked facility.
11. Notification of Changes
We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. If, however, we decide to make material changes to our privacy policy, or if we are going to use our users’ personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time of collection, we will post a notice on www.MyRightAd.com. Such notification notices will be posted and delivered at least 7 days prior to the implementation of the material changes. Users will have a choice as to agree whether or not we use their information in this different manner.
12. Links
This website may contain links to other websites which are outside our control and not covered by this policy. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of these (and all) websites because this privacy statement applies solely to information collected by www.MyRightAd.com as previously stated.
13. Defined terms
In this policy:
"We", "us" "our" or "MyRightAd" means MyRightAd, H.K, a Hong Kong corporation.
"you" means you, the person using our services, or visiting our website.
"Service" means the service that allows user to send and receive payments online.
“Website”, or “site” means www.MyRightAd.com.
