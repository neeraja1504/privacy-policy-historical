Privacy Policy - myEcon
The types of personal information collected on this website are:
Your Name
Your Mailing/Shipping Address
Your Email address
Your Billing Address
Your Credit Card Information
Your Social Security Number
We will not share this information with outside parties except with those related to completing transactions with myEcon, Inc. This includes our merchant card processor, online payment gateway, and banks.
We use your email address to communicate with you regarding your association with myEcon, Inc. Such addresses are not used for any other reason and are not shared with any outside party.
To maintain data accuracy and security, we have in place the appropriate procedures and technologies to protect and secure the data that we collect online.
