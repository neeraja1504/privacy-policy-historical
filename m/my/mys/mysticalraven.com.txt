Privacy Policy – Mystical Raven
This  ** Website Is GDPR Compliant**
Section 1 –  How do we use your information?
Most websites will collect information from their visitors. Usually this will include IP addresses, browser type, and referring pages, things that Google Analytics or tracking codes would track. We use cookies to remember our visitors preferences and to help create a better experience for them when they are using our website. When we occasional do use advertisements, those cookies help us make sure that we are only creating relevant advertisements for our users. We do NOT sell any information to 3rd parties and we keep any data that we collect extremely confidential within our organization. Your email address will not be sold to third parties.
We allow third-party companies to serve  ads and/or collect certain anonymous information when you visit our web site. These companies may use non-personally identifiable information (e.g., click stream information, browser type, time and date, subject of advertisements clicked or scrolled over) during your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services likely to be of greater interest to you. These companies typically use a cookie or third party web beacon to collect this information.
By browsing this site, you hereby accept the use of cookies.
To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice or to opt-out of this type of advertising, you can submit a request to mysticalraven@outlook.com
Section 2 – Consent
If you access this website, you are consenting to use of cookies and accept that there will be advertisements.
Section 3 – Our disclosure requirements
We do not disclose personal information.
Section 4 – Links
Any time you follow a link from our website to another, we aren’t responsible for the privacy policies of those other sites. We encourage you to read their private policies.
Additionally, we use Google Analytics to help us learn about you, the visitor! It doesn’t tell us too much, just what you’ve been checking out on the site, and general location. This information helps us to create a more enjoyable experience for everyone.
Section 5 – Third-party services
Third-party groups will only be allowed the information voluntarily collected from you to allow them to provide a service to us (see: advertising). We will not sell your email address to third parties under any circumstance.
Section 6 – Age of consent
By using this website, you acknowledge that you are the age of consent in your state, province, or country, or that you are a consenting adult giving consent to a minor to use this website.
Section 7 – Changes to this policy
We reserve the ability to change this privacy policy at any time, so review it frequently. Changes take effect immediately upon posting on this website unless otherwise specified.

If you have any questions or comments please contact us here
