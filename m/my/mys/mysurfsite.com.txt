MySurfSite.com - TrafficDX Turnkey Traffic Exchange Systems
We Respect Your Privacy
Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information that you give to us will be held with the utmost care, and will never be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.
Resale Or Disclosure Of Information To Third Parties
MySurfSite.com does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our website, including membership forms or email lists.
Browser Information Collected On Our Website
MySurfSite.com analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Use Of Cookies
MySurfSite.com does not use cookies on this site except in the case of a registered member logging into a members area, in which case, one session cookie is placed on the member's computer.
Privacy Of Email Addresses
MySurfSite.com maintains records of the email addresses of all customers solely for the purpose of corresponding with customers to coordinate the delivery of purchased products or services. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses to anyone. UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCES will these email addresses be used for solicitations from any 3rd party.
