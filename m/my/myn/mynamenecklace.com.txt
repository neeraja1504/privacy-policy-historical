Privacy Policy and Terms of Service | MyNameNecklace
|  |
Respecting your privacy is of utmost importance to us, here at My Name Necklace. Any information that you submit to our website, or that is gathered by a “cookie” during your visit, is used strictly by MyNameNecklace for maintaining a record of your purchases and for promotional reasons. We do not sell, transfer, rent or trade your information to any third party.
When you register on our site, you will be asked to submit certain personal information, such as your name and contact information.
MyNameNecklace will safeguard your personal information. Under no circumstances will we disclose or share your information with any unauthorized third party.
