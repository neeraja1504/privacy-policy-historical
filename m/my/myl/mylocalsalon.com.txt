Privacy Policy
Shortcuts Software Inc. is committed to protecting an individual’s privacy. This policy covers the personal information that Shortcuts Software Inc. holds and sets out the way in which Shortcuts Software Inc. will use, manage and protect personal information that comes into the possession of Shortcuts Software Inc. via the websites mylocalsalon.com, mylocalsalon.co.uk and mylocalsalon.com.au.
Collecting and Using Personal Information
Shortcuts Software Inc. will only collect and use personal information that is necessary for its business purposes. All information collected is treated as confidential, and will only be used for the purpose for which it was supplied, unless permission has been obtained from the individual. Information will be collected by lawful, fair and unobtrusive means. Any sensitive information will be collected with the individual’s consent.
Disclosing Personal Information
Shortcuts Software Inc. will not disclose any personal information to any third party except with the consent of the individual or where required by law to do so. Shortcuts Software Inc. considers the act of an individual using the website to book an appointment as the individual's consent to disclose the individual's personal information to the third party receiving the booking.
Retention and Disposal of Information
Shortcuts Software Inc. will only keep personal information it needs. Personal data will be retained for no longer than 2 years after non-use. Reasonable steps will be taken to remove or permanently de-identify personal information when it is no longer required.
Accessing and Updating Personal Information and Right To Be Forgotten
Shortcuts Software Inc. will take reasonable steps to make sure that the personal information collected, used or disclosed is accurate, complete and up to date. Individuals may access and update their personal information at any time or notify us of any required correction. Individuals may assert their right to be forgotten at any time by accessing and deleting their account (via website) or by contacting Shortcuts Software Inc. and asserting their right to be forgotten to which Shortcuts Software Inc. will comply within 30 days.
Data Security and Data Location
Shortcuts Software Inc. will take all reasonable steps to ensure all data collected is stored in a secure computer storage facility. Shortcuts Software Inc. uses Amazon Web Services in the United States to store all data collected.

AUTOMATIC COLLECTION OF INFORMATION/COOKIES
The Shortcuts Software Inc. website's server, or the servers of companies that are used to operate this site, may place a "cookie" on your computer in order to allow you to use the website and to personalize your experience. A "cookie" is a small piece of data that can be sent by a web server to your computer, which then may be stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Cookies allow us to recognize your computer while you are on our website and help customize your online experience and make it more convenient for you. Cookies are also useful in allowing more efficient log-in for users, tracking transaction histories and preserving information between sessions. The information collected from cookies may also be used to improve the functionality of the website.
Most web browser applications (such as Microsoft Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator) have features that can notify you when you receive a cookie or prevent cookies from being sent. If you disable cookies, however, you may not be able to use certain personalized functions of this website.
INFORMATION COLLECTED AND HOW IT IS USED
We do collect personal information directly from individuals who volunteer to use some of our services. Collection of this information is required to deliver the specific services, but use of these services is voluntary.
If you do nothing during your visit to the website but browse or download information, we automatically collect and store the following information about your visit:
The Internet Protocol address and domain name used, but not the e-mail address. The Internet Protocol address is a numerical identifier assigned either to your Internet service provider or directly to your computer. We use the Internet Protocol address to direct Internet traffic to you and generate statistics used in the management of this site;
The type of browser and operating system you used;
The date and time you visited this site;
The web pages or services you accessed at this site;
The website you visited prior to coming to this website;
The website you visit as you leave this website
