Summary
Thank you for visiting the MyJobHelper.co.uk website located at www.myjobhelper.co.uk (the "Site").  The Site is owned and operated by Surfin Mouse LLC, d/b/a MyJobHelper.co.uk and d/b/a MyJobHelper.com, and its affiliated companies ("MyJobHelper.co.uk," "MyJobHelper," "we," "our" or "us").  The following MyJobHelper.co.uk Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") sets forth our policies for the collection, storage and usage of your "Personal Data."  For purposes of this Privacy Policy, "Personal Data" means any information concerning the personal or material circumstances of an identified or identifiable individual. This includes information such as your name, your address, your phone number and your email address.
Please note, MyJobHelper.co.uk is considered the "Data Controller" in connection with the Personal Data collected pursuant to this Privacy Policy for purposes of applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").  We only collect, store and use your Personal Data if you have given us your express consent to do so or, in the alternative, to the extent that you have explicitly granted us permission, e.g. pursuant to the contractual relationship between you and MyJobHelper.co.uk.
Background
Without limiting the foregoing, this MyJobHelper.co.uk Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") outlines the privacy practices associated with the Site. You agree to this Privacy Policy, in its entirety, when you: (a) access or use the Site; (b) access and/or view any of the Site's viewable blog posts, text, graphics and/or other material (collectively, "Content"); (c) utilize any of the interactive areas of the Site, such as blog and comment sections, that enable users to interact with third parties, as well as MyJobHelper.co.uk personnel ("Interactive Services"); (d) utilize the searchable database ("Database") in order to view job listings ("Listings") as made available by MyJobHelper.co.uk's third party employment advertising partners ("Third Party Employers") (collectively, the "Listing Services"); and/or (e) sign up to receive e-mail from us, including alerts about Listings that meet your preselected criteria (the "E-mail Alert Services," and together with the Site, Content, Interactive Services, Database, Listings and Listing Services, the "Site Offerings").  Capitalized terms not defined herein shall have the meaning set forth in the MyJobHelper.co.uk Terms and Conditions ("Terms and Conditions").  IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY IN ITS ENTIRETY, YOU MAY NOT ACCESS, DOWNLOAD OR OTHERWISE USE THE SITE OFFERINGS, AS APPLICABLE.
If you are a resident of the State of California and would like to opt-out from the disclosure of your Personal Data to any third party for marketing purposes, please e-mail us at privacy@myjobhelper.co.uk. Please be advised that where California residents opt-out from permitting their Personal Data to be shared, such individuals may still receive selected offers directly from us, in accordance with the law.

MyJobHelper.co.uk is responsible for the processing of Personal Data that it receives under the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and subsequently transfers to a third party acting as an agent on its behalf. MyJobHelper.co.uk complies with the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Principles for all onward transfers of Personal Data from the EU, including the onward transfer liability provisions.
With respect to Personal Data received ** ** or ** ** transferred pursuant to the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework, MyJobHelper.co.uk is subject to the regulatory enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission.   In certain situations, MyJobHelper.co.uk may be required to disclose Personal Data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.
If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) at: https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request.
Under certain conditions, more fully described on the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield website located at https://www.privacyshield.gov, you may invoke binding arbitration when other dispute resolution procedures have been exhausted.
Personal Data Collected
We collect your Personal Data when you sign up for our E-mail Alert Services. The types of Personal Data that we collect at the Site include, without limitation, your: (a) name; (b) e-mail address; (c) telephone number; and (d) postal address. In conjunction with our partner program, we also collect banking information and Social Security or Tax Identification number.
Use of Personal Data
By submitting your Personal Data on the Site in connection with a job listing inquiry, you agree that we may send you Listings, as well as other e-mail messages describing the types of job opportunities available in your area. If you wish to opt out of the E-mail Alert Services, please follow the unsubscribe directions in the applicable email or contact us at: privacy@myjobhelper.co.uk.
We will share your Personal Data with third parties only in the ways that are described in this Privacy Policy. We may also employ other companies and individuals to perform certain functions on our behalf. Examples include sending direct and electronic mail, removing duplicate information from user lists, analyzing data and providing marketing analysis. The agents performing these limited functions on our behalf shall have access to our users' Personal Data solely as needed to perform their functions for us, and we do not permit them to use your Personal Data for other purposes. If you do not want us to share your personal information with these companies, contact us at privacy@myjobhelper.co.uk.
We will also use your Personal Data for customer service, to provide you with information that you may request and to customize your experience on the Site. We may also use your Personal Data for internal business purposes, such as analyzing and managing our business. We may use third parties to help us operate our business and the Site or to administer activities on our behalf.
In certain situations, MyJobHelper.co.uk may be required to disclose Personal Data in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements. We also reserve the right to release current or past user information in the event that: (a) we believe that the user is in violation of the Terms and Conditions, this Privacy Policy or any other of our operating rules, policies, price schedules and other supplemental terms and conditions or documents that may be published by us from time to time; (b) we believe that a particular user may commit unlawful acts; (c) such information is subpoenaed or requested pursuant to a judicial or regulatory proceeding or inquiry; (d) we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others; (e) we are sold, merge with a third party, are acquired or are the subject of bankruptcy proceedings; provided, however , that if MyJobHelper.co.uk is involved in a bankruptcy proceeding, merger, acquisition or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on the Site of any change in ownership or uses of your Personal Data, as well as any choices that you may have regarding your Personal Data; and/or (f) to comply with a legal obligation, including sharing your name/e-mail address with third-parties to assist you with your opt out requests in compliance with applicable law, including the GDPR and the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003, as amended from time-to-time.
Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Solely where you agree to the terms of our cookie policy as same is made available when you access the Site, technologies such as cookies, beacons, tags and scripts may be used by MyJobHelper.co.uk  and those of our partners who you have provided explicit consent to, including marketing partners, affiliates and/or analytics or service providers. Solely where you have agreed to the terms of this aspect of our cookie policy, our partners may link the Personal Data we share with them to the cookie stored on your browser or device, and they may collect information such as your IP address, browser or operating system type and version, and demographic or inferred-interest information. Solely where you have agreed to the terms of our cookie policy, these technologies will be used in connection with analyzing trends, administering the Site Offerings, tracking users' movements around the Site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.  In addition, solely where you have agreed to the terms of our cookie policy, our partners will use this information to recognize you across different channels and platforms over time for advertising, analytics, attribution and reporting purposes.  We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies on an individual as well as aggregated basis.
We also use cookies to remember users' settings, advertisement targeting, and content customization. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. In addition, you can opt-out of allowing us to use cookies in connection with your computer/mobile device at any time by contacting us at: privacy@myjobhelper.co.uk; provided, however, that certain cookies are strictly necessary to generate the Listings that meet your specified search criteria (such as job type and geographical area).  These cookies are deemed "Necessary Cookies."  If you reject cookies (other than Necessary Cookies), you may still use the Site Offerings, but your ability to use some features of the Site Offerings or areas of the Site may be limited.
Analytics/Log Files
As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information may include Internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp and/or clickstream data.  We use the non-personally identifiable information that we collect to improve the design and content of the Site Offerings and to enable us to personalize your Internet experience.  We also may use this information in the aggregate to analyze usage of the Site Offerings.
Behavioral Targeting
We partner with third parties to display advertising on our Site and to manage our advertising on other sites. Solely where you have agreed to the terms of this behavioral tracking policy, our third party partners may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on the Site and other websites in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests. If you would like to opt-out of having this information used for the purpose of serving you interest-based ads, you may opt-out at any time by clicking on the cookie consent manager in the lower right hand corner of MyJobHelper.co.uk (visit by clicking here
).  Please note this does not opt you out of being served ads. You will continue to receive generic ads, but such ads will not be based on your browsing activity or other analytics. ****

Individual Rights: Deleting, Modifying and Updating Your Information
At your request, we will: (a) inform you, in a machine readable format, of what Personal Data we have on file for you; (b) amend the Personal Data that we have on file for you; and/or (c) remove Personal Data that you have provided to us, or that we have collected, from our databases and the databases of any third party partners with whom we have shared such information.  You may do so by e-mailing us at: privacy@myjobhelper.co.uk.  We ask individual users to identify themselves and the information requested to be accessed, corrected or removed before processing such requests, and, to the extent permitted by applicable law, we may decline to process requests that are unreasonably repetitive or systematic, require disproportionate technical effort, jeopardize the privacy of others or would be extremely impractical (for instance, requests concerning information residing on backup tapes).
Please be advised that deleting your Personal Data may terminate your access to certain of the Site Offerings.  If you wish to continue using the full complement of Site Offerings, you may not be able to delete all of the Personal Data that we have on file for you.
Please be further advised that, after you delete your Personal Data, residual copies may take a period of time before they are deleted from our active servers, and the services of our third party partners, as applicable, and may remain in our backup systems.
Data Retention
We only collect Personal Data that you provide to us for the purpose of handling the job search relationship that you have with MyJobHelper.co.uk, and only to the extent necessary for those purposes.  We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Site Offerings. Please contact us if you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you with the Site Offerings. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Security
We endeavor to safeguard and protect our users' Personal Data.  When users make Personal Data available to us, their Personal Data is protected both online and offline (to the extent that we maintain any Personal Data offline).  When we store and transmit our users' Personal Data, that information is encrypted with advanced technology.
Access to your Personal Data is strictly limited, and we take reasonable measures to ensure that your Personal Data is not accessible to the public.  All of our users' Personal Data is restricted in our offices, as well as the offices of our third-party service providers.  Only employees or third-party agents who need user Personal Data to perform a specific job are granted access to user Personal Data.  Our employees are dedicated to ensuring the security and privacy of all user Personal Data.  Employees not adhering to our firm policies are subject to disciplinary action.  The servers that we store user Personal Data on are kept in a secure physical environment.  We also have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of Personal Data under our control.
Please be advised, however, that while we take every reasonable precaution available to protect your data, no storage facility, technology, software, security protocols or data transmission over the Internet or via wireless networks can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.  Computer hackers that circumvent our security measures may gain access to certain portions of your Personal Data, and technological bugs, errors and glitches may cause inadvertent disclosures of your Personal Data; provided, however, that any attempt to breach the security of the network, our servers, databases or other hardware or software may constitute a crime punishable by law.  For the reasons mentioned above, we cannot warrant that your Personal Data will be absolutely secure.  Any transmission of data at or through the Site, other Site Offerings or otherwise via the Internet or wireless networks, is done at your own risk. In compliance with applicable laws, we shall notify you and any applicable regulatory agencies in the event that we learn of an information security breach with respect to your Personal Data.  You will be notified via e-mail in the event of such a breach.  Please be advised that notice may be delayed in order to address the needs of law enforcement, determine the scope of network damage, and to engage in remedial measures.
