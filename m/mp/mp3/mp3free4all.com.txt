MP3free4all.com Privacy Statement
****Mp3free4all respects your privacy. Our privacy policy is simple and unwavering:
Mp3free4all does not store server logs that record your IP address or how you
use the site. Mp3free4all stores only non-identifying statistics to analyze
trends with our site and to help diagnose problems.
Mp3free4all does not require or collect any personal information for the use of
the services on this site. If Mp3free4all adds additional services to the site
in the future that would require some minimal personal information
(such as username, password and email address), Mp3free4all will not sell,
trade or otherwise disclose your personal information to other companies.
Third Party Advertising
We sometimes use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit
our Web site. These companies may use information (not including your name,
address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other
Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about
goods and services that may be of interest to you.
Third Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party
advertiser may place or recognize a unique “cookie” on your browser.****
