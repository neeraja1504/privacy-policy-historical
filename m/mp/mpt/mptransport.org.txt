Cookies:
In an effort to improve the quality of our Application, we track information provided to us
by your device when you view or use the Application, such as the applications you came

We track this information using cookies or small text files which include an anonymous
unique identifier to enhance your experience. Cookies are sent to a user’s browser from
our servers and are stored on the user’s device for record-keeping purposes and sometimes
to track information about them. Sending a cookie to a user’s browser enables us to collect
information about that user and keep a record of the user’s preferences when using our
Application . We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand
how you use our Application . A session cookie enables certain features of the Application
and is deleted from your computer/mobile/device when you disconnect from or leave the
Application. A persistent cookie remains after you close the Application and may be used
by your device on subsequent visits. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most
web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser
setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage
of the Application.

VII.   OUR POLICY TOWARDS CHILDREN
If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with data or
information without his/her consent, he or she should contact us at
[mpstatetransport@gmail.com ].
We do not knowingly collect data or information from persons under the age of 18
(eighteen) years. If we become aware that a person under the age of 18 (eighteen) years has
provided us with his/her data or information, we will delete such information from our
files.
If you are under the age of 18 (eighteen) years, please do not access our Application and
submit any of your data or information through ourApplication. We encourage parents and
legal guardians to monitor their children's internet usage and to help enforce our Privacy
Policy by instructing their children not to access our Application and provide any data or
information.
