How Information is used and stored:
1. TalkCharge uses the gathered information for the following purposes:
The information helps us to administer and manage your TalkCharge account.
The information allows us to give users access to certain areas of website and provide services that they have requested;
The information allows us to give users access to make payments, do recharges, buy Giftcards and make transactions to wallet.
The information helps us verify if the user has sufficient funds in their TalkCharge wallet to make payments;
The information helps us to send notifications to users about new services and offers;
The information might be use to give our development and support team internal training.
The information helps us to regulate financial policies, fraudulent activities and security;
2. TalkCharge retains or store the user information for a minimum period of 5 years after the closure of your users TalkCharge account, which includes the personal data such as user name, contact details, user registered number and transaction history, etc.. Other than this Retained(Stored) Data, we will delete and destroy all Personal Information that we hold about user when he terminates his TalkCharge account. We do not store any online banking login PIN, TAN and/or password.
3. Please note that TalkCharge will not store user credit card information on closure of account.

What's Phishing & How it works?
Phishing is the process to attempts to steal personal details and financial account details from a website user. "Phishers" use fake or "spoof" emails to lead users to counterfeit websites where the user is tricked into entering their personal details, such as credit card numbers, user names and passwords. TalkCharge never ask users to disclose any personal or security details by e-mail. If you receive any e-mail supposedly from TalkCharge requesting your personal or security details, please do not respond to it. Please forward any Such E-mail to Care@TalkCharge.com and thereafter delete the e-mail immediately.
