Privacy Policy
This is to inform the visitors of Taleem-E-Pakistan.com
+ For each visit to the Web page, information about your browser, IP address, City/State/Country is collected by storing cookies on your computer.
+ The information collected is used for internal review only. No part of your personal data such as emails, contact name, phone number, etc are not shared with anyone.
+ We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit my website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
+ We are allowing Google as
– As a third party vendor, which enables cookies on your computer to serve ads on this site.
– Google’s uses Double Click DART cookie by which it serve ads on this site.
– You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
This privacy policy is subject to change, might vary as changes are made from time to time.
