Privacy Policy
Tamriel Foundry is a proud partner of the Launch Media Network who contributes to the maintenance and ongoing improvement of the site. Protecting your privacy is important to Launch Media Network, and this statement is intended to make you aware of our commitments to you as a visitor to Tamriel Foundry. This policy may change from time to time, so please check back periodically.
When accessing Tamriel Foundry, Launch Media Network will learn certain information about you during your visit. How we will handle information we learn about you depends upon what you do when visiting our site.
If you visit our site to read or download information on our pages, we collect and store only the following information about you:
The name of the domain from which you access the Internet
The date and time you access our site
The Internet address of the website you used to link directly to our site.
If you identify yourself by sending us an e-mail containing personal information, the information contained in your email is not collected and will be solely used to respond to your message.
The information collected is for statistical purposes. Launch Media Network may use software programs to create summary statistics, which are used for such purposes as assessing the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, what information is of most and least interest, determining technical design specifications, and identifying system performance or problem areas. Launch Media Network will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, nor will personal information be sold or transferred to unaffiliated third parties without the approval of the user at the time of collection.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit Tamriel Foundry. This helps to pay for the hardware costs of keeping Tamriel Foundry running smoothly. The companies which serve advertisements on Tamriel Foundry may use aggregated information (not personal information) about your visits to Tamriel Foundry or other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please click here http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
