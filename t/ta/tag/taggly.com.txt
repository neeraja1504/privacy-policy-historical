Taggly
Privacy policy
Taggly is committed to protecting your privacy and respects the privacy of individuals who use Taggly's software and services. This Privacy Statement covers how Taggly treats personal information that is either collected or received. This includes, but is not limited to email addresses, phone numbers, names, addresses, age, gender, and interests.
Use of your personal information
Taggly currently collects personal information, including email addresses, when a user of the Taggly software registers to use the optional synchronized bookmarks and link sharing services. Taggly also monitors usage information from web servers owned by Taggly, including page views, product usage and download counts, obtained during the normal course of use of Taggly's software and services. Unless required by law, Taggly will not share your personal information, except in aggregate or anonymous form, with third parties.
Use of third party affiliate programs and ad networks
Taggly may allow other companies (affiliates) to display ads and services on Taggly web pages and software. Some affiliate partners or programs may place a persistent cookie on your computer or retrieve an IP address. Although Taggly makes every effort to ensure your privacy, Taggly does not have any control over the policies of affiliate partners. Users should see individual partner's privacy statements for their privacy policies.
