Privacy and Security
_** Tattoos By Design is 100% committed to protecting your privacy.  Tattoos By Designwill not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others
The information collected about you when you purchase through the shopping cart is solely used to process your order.
Tattoos By Design conforms with the requirements of the Data Protection Act, 1998
You may receive emails from us in the following circumstances...
When you place an order you will receive a confirmation to set out the details of your order and confirm that the order and delivery details are correct.
You may also receive additional emails to inform you of the progress of your order should there be any unlikely delay.
The information you give us is used purely to contact you about your order or to send you emails if you have requested to receive updates. If you wish to stop receiving the site updates, simply e-mail us to let us know and we will immediately remove your details from our mailing list.
Tattoos By Design do not send marketing emails to non-customer, personal email addresses (‘spam’).  Tattoos By Design uses cookies to keep track of the items in your shopping basket to ensure they are attributed to you and only you. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer's hard drive by your browser. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifying information but allow us to provide features to make your shopping on our website easier. Most browsers allow the cookie function to be turned off. If you want to know how to do this look at the help menu on your browser.
For maximum security in online shopping we use Worldpay, one of the worlds leading providers of secure on line credit card transactions.
The transfer of the purchase details from our site to Worldpay, are encapsulated using their own encrypted and digitally-signed protocol. This uses a combination of standard methods such as PGP, RSA and MD5 to ensure that the information passed is secure and tamper-proof.
Any communication between the Customer and Worldpay is also encrypted to the maximum strength, supported by the Customers browser using TLS or 128 Bit SSL. The customer is also protected from fraudulent use of their card, in a “card not present” environment, by their card issuers. The card issuers provides the right for the Customer to dispute a transaction, if the goods/service did not arrive, or if the card was used fraudulently.
