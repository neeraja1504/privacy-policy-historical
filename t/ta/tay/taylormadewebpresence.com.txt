The TaylorMade Web Presence Privacy Policy
TaylorMade Web Presence values your interest, willingness and trust in our services.  We vow to serve you at the best of our ability, and this includes our commitment to safeguard your privacy and security while doing business with us.
Collecting information makes it possible for TaylorMade Web Presence to deliver the kind of service you expect. At TaylorMadeWebPresence.com, you will be asked to share your identifiable information for various purposes, including:
1-     To request products and services
2-     To request information
3-     To sign up for updates
4-     To provide feedback  on our products and services
Upon voluntary form submission, your IP Address is recorded. Your visit details are traced for statistical purposes. In addition, content that is transmitted through e-mail correspondence is retained in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.
TaylorMadeWebPresence.com is not directed toward minors. TaylorMade Web Presence does not knowingly attempt to solicit or receive any information from children.
You will never be asked for sensitive information while filling out a form at TaylorMadeWebPresence.com.
The information collected is used to:
1-     Provide customers products and services requested
2-     Respond to your inquiries
3-     Personalize your experience
4-     Supply information about and promote our services
5-     Resolve problems actively and proactively
Personal or business information provided via TaylorMade Web Presence.com is transmitted securely, considered strictly confidential, and will NOT be shared, disclosed, or sold to any third party without your express consent to such action.
Please be warned that when you click on a link that takes you to another Web site, any and all information you provide, is subject to the privacy policy of that site. TaylorMade Web Presence does not control and is not responsible for the practices of such sites.
By visiting or using TaylorMadeWebPresence.com, you agree to the provisions of this Privacy Promise.  If you are not in agreement with the provisions of this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using TaylorMadeWebPresence.com.
