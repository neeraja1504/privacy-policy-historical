Privacy Notice : Medieval Clothing & Costumes, Medieval Dresses, Clothes - TudorDressing
What information do we collect?
• We collect information from you when you register on the site or place an order.
• When ordering or registering, we may ask you for your name, e-mail address, mailing address and phone number. You may, however, visit our site anonymously.
What information do we NOT collect?
• We never collect or store your Credit Card details.
• All payments are processed on secure servers of Paypal.
How do we use your information?
• To personalize your site experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content and product offerings in which you are most interested.
• To allow us to better service you in responding to your customer service requests.
• To quickly process your transactions.
• To let you know about the new products or offer that we are offering to our customers.
How do we protect visitor information?
• All customer information is stored on our secured servers behind limited access. Only the site administrator has the right & authority to view these details.
• No customer, employee or third party has access to the information.
Do we disclose the information we collect to outside parties?
• We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information.
• The term "outside parties" does not include our associate companies, which follow similar Privacy Policy.
