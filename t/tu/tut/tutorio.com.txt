Tutorio.com Privacy
Privacy Policy
Tutorio.com is committed to protecting your online privacy while providing you with the most useful and enjoyable Web experience possible.
How we use "cookies"
As you probably know, "cookies" are small pieces of information that some Web sites store on your computer's hard drive when you visit them. Like many other Web sites, Tutorio.com uses cookies to identify members so they donâ€™t have to enter a user ID and password upon each visit. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies but allow you to modify security settings so you can approve or reject cookies on a case-by-case basis.
What we provide to third parties
While tutorio.com does not provide any third-party access to your personal information, we may provide third parties with aggregate statistics about our visitors, traffic patterns, and related site information. This data reflects site usage patterns gathered during hundreds of thousands of visits to our Web site each month, but it does not contain behavioral or identifying information about any individual visitor.
Tutorio.com uses a third-party advertising company to serve ads when you visit our sites. Those company may use information from cookies about your visits to our and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. In addition, we share aggeregate Web site usage information about visitors to our site with this company for the purpose of managing and targeting advertisements on our sites.
Obtaining your consent
By using our Web site, you give tutorio.com permission to collect information about the pages served to you as an anonymous user for the purpose of calculating aggregate site statistics.
Tutorio.com welcomes your questions and comments about our privacy policy. You may contact us using our contact page.
