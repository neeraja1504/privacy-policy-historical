Privacy Policy | Tuts Publication Network
Thank you for using Tuts Publication Network! We wrote this policy to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. Because we're an internet company, some of the concepts below are a little technical, but we've tried our best to explain things in a simple and clear way. We welcome your feedback on this policy.
What information do we collect?
We collect information in a few different ways:
When you give it to us or give us permission to obtain it
When you sign up for or use our products, you voluntarily give us certain information. This can include your name, profile photo, the email address you used to sign up, and any other information you provide us. If you're using Tutpub on your mobile device, you can also choose to provide us with location data.
We also get technical information when you use our products
These days, whenever you use a website, mobile application, or other internet service, there's certain information that almost always gets created and recorded automatically. The same is true when you use our products. Here are some of the types of information we collect:
Log data , When you use Tutpub, our servers automatically record information ("log data"), including information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website or your mobile app sends when you're using it. This log data may include your Internet Protocol address, the address of the web pages you visited that had Tutpub features, device information, browser type and settings, the date and time of your request, how you used Tutpub, and cookie data.
Cookie data , Depending on how you're accessing our products, we may use "cookies" (small text files sent by your computer each time you visit our website, unique to your Tutpub account or your browser) or similar technologies to record log data.
Our partners and advertisers may share information with us
We may get information about you and your activity off Tutpub from our affiliates, advertisers, partners and other third parties we work with. For example: we are using Google Analytics and Google adsense.
How do we use the information we collect?
We use the information we collect to provide our products to you and make them better, develop new products, and protect Tutpub and our users. For example, we may log how often people use two different versions of a product, which can help us understand which version is better.
We also use the information we collect to offer you customized content, including: Suggesting post you might like. or, Showing you ads you might be interested in.
Transferring your information
Tutpub is a worldwide service. By using our products or services, you authorize us to transfer and store your information outside of your home country, including in the United States, for the purposes described in this policy. The privacy protections and the rights of authorities to access your personal information in such countries may not be equivalent to those in your home country.
How and when do we share information?
Anyone can see the posts you create, and the profile information you give us. But wo do not publicize the information that we declare private when getting the information.
Our policy on children's information
We do not verify your age. If you have an email address, we think you are able to browse online.
