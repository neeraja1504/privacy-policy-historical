What we record about every visitor
For each visitor to this website, our Web server recognises information regarding the consumer's E-mail address and other personal details.
We collect this information volunteered by the consumer via site registration.
How we use the information
We may use your information to:
Facilitate use of this website
Manage your account and preferences
Customize advertising and content on this website
Identify and protect against fraudulent transactions and other misuse of this website
Enforce our Terms of Use.
E-mail
We will send you regular E-mail updates, including but not limited to:
- New profiles that match your Ideal Partner criteria
- New profiles that are local to you
- Notifications of problems with your account
- Notification of new features
- We will NOT send you E-mails unrelated to your use of this website
- We will NOT share your E-mail address with 3rd parties for the purposes of marketing (see Disclosure below)
If you do not want to receive E-mail from us in the future, login to your account, go to Main Settings and change your E-mail options.
Alternatively, please let us know by sending an E-mail to the above address.
Disclosure
Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information [including proprietary information] that we maintain about them, unique identifier information (e.g., user ID or password) that we maintain about them, contact information (e.g., name, E-mail address) that we maintain about them, and a description of information that we maintain about them.
Consumers can access this information by E-mail us at the above address.
We will NOT share your personal data / E-mail address with others except as indicated below:
- Service providers, such as credit-card payment processors, performing services on our behalf
- Third parties in limited circumstances, such as preventing fraud, complying with legal requirements and protecting the safety of other users.

Cookies
We use cookies to store visitors preferences, record session information, customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends.
Ad Servers
To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. For further information, consult the privacy policies of: http://www.ukbanners.com.
We do not provide any personal information to these companies.
Change of Policy
From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future we will contact you before we use your data for these new purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.
Corrections
Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information.
Consumers can have this information corrected by or logging into their account and modifying their settings.
Security
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number.
