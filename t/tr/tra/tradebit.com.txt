Privacy Policy - By Tradebit.com/.de/.es/.it
Privacy Policy
What This Privacy Policy Covers
This policy covers how Tradebit.com treats personal information that Tradebit.com collects and receives, including information related to your past use of Tradebit.com products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.
This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Tradebit.com does not own or control, or to people that Tradebit.com does not employ or manage.
Information Collection and Use
General
Tradebit.com collects personal information when you register with Tradebit.com, when you visit Tradebit.com pages or the pages of certain Tradebit.com partners. Tradebit.com may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.
When you register we may ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. For some services we may also ask for your address, Social Security number, and other personal information. Once you register with Tradebit.com and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.
Tradebit.com collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about your use of financial products and services that we offer.
Tradebit.com automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Tradebit.com cookie information, and the page you request.
Tradebit.com uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the advertising and content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.
Children
Tradebit.com does not allow a child under age 13 to register with our services.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
Tradebit.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:
We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Tradebit.com under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Tradebit.com communicate with you about offers from Tradebit.com and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims
We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Tradebit.com's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
We transfer information about you if Tradebit.com is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, Tradebit.com will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
Cookies
Tradebit.com may set and access Tradebit.com cookies on your computer.
Your Ability to Edit and Delete Your Account Information and Preferences
General
You can edit your Tradebit.com Account Information at any time.
We reserve the right to send you certain communications relating to the Tradebit.com service, such as service announcements, administrative messages and the Tradebit.com Newsletter, that are considered part of your Tradebit.com account, without offering you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving them.
You can delete your Tradebit.com account by visiting the Account Deletion page in the member area for the free accounts or just cancel your subscription with PayPal.
Confidentiality and Security
We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
Softlayer, our hosting company, has physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
Tradebit.com may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Tradebit.com account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
