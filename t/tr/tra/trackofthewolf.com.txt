Track of the Wolf - Privacy Policies
Our Privacy Policy
Track of the Wolf is committed to protecting your privacy. We keep your information confidential. We will not sell, trade, rent, loan, or disclose information about our customers, to anyone, or any business.
Track of the Wolf uses the information supplied by customers and guests, to serve them, answer questions, and fill their orders.
Track of the Wolf uses cookies. Cookies are bits of information that our Web site sends to your computer, while you are viewing our site. These reminders allow our Web site to remember important information, that make our site more useful.
We use cookies to remember the part numbers and quantities of the items in your shopping cart.
You may choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is sent, or you may turn on, or turn off all cookies. You do this through your browser (like Netscape Navigator or Internet Explorer) settings. Each browser is a little different, so look at your browser Help menu to learn the correct way to turn on cookies. If you turn cookies off, you cannot access many of the features that make our website more efficient.
Our site uses a secure on-line order form, for our customers to order products. We collect information, like email addresses, bank card numbers, and contact information, to process customer orders. The customer contact information, and email address, is used to contact customers when necessary, to clarify orders, or correct errors. Bank card information is only for billing customer orders.
Email/Feedback information is used to provide our visitors with occasional e-mail updates, about special offers, and new products. However, if you do not wish to receive these, please tell us. We will remove your e-mail address from our database. We enforce our 'no-spam' policy, that renounces unsolicited commercial email.
All of the customer information is protected against unauthorized access, and is available only to specially trained customer service staff members, when assisting a customer with their order.
This site is not intended for children, especially children under 13 years of age. Parents, please supervise your children.
If you have questions about our privacy policy, please contact me, using our on-line form.
Thank you,
David S. Ripplinger
President
Track of the Wolf, Inc.
