Privacy Policy
Personal Information
We collect personal information from you when you create an account and from the messages you send to free recycling groups you are a member of.
We don't sell, rent, or lend personal information to anyone under any circumstances.
You may contact us at any time to remove your account and any personally identifying information you provided.
Non-Identifying Information
We do report non-identifying information about your use of the site to reputable 3rd parties like Google to generate aggregate statistics and advertisements to support this site and help us improve it.
