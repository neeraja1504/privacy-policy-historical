Get Paid To Complete Offers At TreasureTrooper.com!
All personal information collected by TreasureTrooper.com will be kept confidential. Your information will not be given, sold, or traded to any outside sources unless requested by yourself. When signing up for TreasureTrooper, you may be asked if you'd like to join certain coregistration opportunities. If you click "yes" next to any of these, your info will be sent to the company that you have selected. Your personal information will not be viewed by any member or person other than the administration of TreasureTrooper.com. If we need to ask for additional information, that information will be covered by this privacy policy as well. We will use your e-mail address to send out occasional updates and offers.
This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Resigned and terminated members of TreasureTrooper.com will remain protected by this privacy policy
