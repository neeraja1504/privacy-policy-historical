What choices are available to you regarding collection, use and distribution of your information?
Sharing your sensitive personal identifiable information is entirely voluntary. You need not register with us in order to use Truepush. However, we offer push-notification services only to visitors who register. You may opt-in or opt-out of push notification services. You may update your information and change your account settings at any time.
Upon request, we will remove/block your sensitive personal identifiable information from our database, thereby cancelling your registration. However, your information may remain stored in archive on our servers even after the deletion or the termination of your account.
Cookies are small data files containing a string of characters, such as an anonymous unique browser identifier. Cookies are stored on your computer or other device and act as unique tags that identify your device or browser. However, cookie data shall be anonymous data intended to track user behaviour. We may use third party-services such as Google Analytics, Fresh Chat.
You can accept or decline cookies. All sites that are customizable require that you accept cookies.
In addition, cookies placed on your computer or your customer’s computer help us to understand what you or your customers are interested in. We or our ad-service platform may deploy online cookies to tracks users across websites or to associate users (and these cookies) with Mobile IDs We may do so in order to better or more accurately target relevant and contextual ads to you Our ad-serving platform may then help us retarget ads to your customers based on their interactions with Truepush. However, you may opt-out of receiving push notification by going to “Settings” and clicking on “Notifications” and then changing those settings for some or all of the apps on your device. Further, different device configurations or updates to devices may affect or change these settings work). Your choice to opt- out of “Notifications” from Truepush shall not affect ads places by any other platform or organisation
