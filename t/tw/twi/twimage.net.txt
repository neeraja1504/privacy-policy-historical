Privacy Policy | Twimage
Information Collection and UseThis privacy policy sets out how twimage.net uses and protects your data that you give to twimage.net when you use this website.
What we collect is, your name, Twitter nickname, contact information including email address, preferences and interests and other information relevant to end user interactions.
We require and gather this information to understand your needs and provide you a better service, and in particular for the following reasons; Internal record keeping, use the information to improve our products and services, to send promotional emails about new services, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided.
Security
twimage.net is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
Changes to Privacy Policy
twimage.net may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are agreed and happy with any changes. This policy is effective from December 2016.
Contact
If you have any questions about this Terms and Conditions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
