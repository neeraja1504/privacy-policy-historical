It may also be used to provide you with information concerning our industry news, special events or features and offers suited for you.
Thinktankinfo.com recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of personally identifiable information collected about our clients. The purpose of this Privacy Statement, the word clients include visitors to our web sites, purchasers of our products, subscribers and users of our services, and advertising customers. We take your privacy very seriously and we will never sell, rent or share the information submitted on our web site. You can assure that your personal information, including your email address will be kept in strict confidence.
