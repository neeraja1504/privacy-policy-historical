Privacy Policy — Christopher Ross
We are committed to safeguarding your privacy online. This Privacy Policy applies to your information that we collect and receive on and through this website; it does not apply to practices of businesses that we do not own or control or people we do not employ.
At no time will we sell or otherwise distribute your information or your personally-identifiable data without your permission. You can visit this website without revealing who you are or providing any personal information about yourself.
By using this website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read the following document to understand how your personal information will be treated as you use this website and its services. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.
This website contains links to other websites. These include links to websites that we think may be of interest to you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.
We do not collect, or store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any 3rd parties.
Visitor information collected by Google Analytics
This website uses Google Analytics to help analyze how users use the site. The tool uses _web browser cookies  _to collect and log information about visitor behavior. The information is stored in an anonymous form, including your IP address and is transmitted to Google.
The information is used to improve website performance, and compile statistical reports on general website activity, we will never use statistical data to track or collect personal identification information of unique visitors to our website.
Visitor Information collected by PayPal
The website makes use of PayPal to handle financial transactions. No information associated with your PayPal account is stored on the website.
Contact
If you require more information about our Privacy Policy or feel any part needs more clarification, please feel to contact us for more information.
