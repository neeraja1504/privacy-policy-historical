Your continues usage of our services shall be construed as you consent to this Privacy Policy. In case you do not agree to any of the clauses or Privacy Policy as a whole, please DO NOT use our services.
Your privacy is highly important for us.
We acknowledge the privacy of the users of our services, and is determined to rationally protect it by all means. The user’s information that is gathered by us includes:
(a) Information provided by users
(b) Information automatically traced while browsing the website
(c) Information gathered from any other source (Third party)
(d) Name, Email id,Phone Number
In case you realize that the information provided by you is inaccurate or incomplete, you can rectify the same by contacting us at contact@thevoiceofnation.com We shall update your information at earliest.

We shall process or share your information with your consent only. Without your prior approval, we shall neither use your data nor share it with anyone unless required in below mentioned circumstances.

** ** Cookies
To enhance the responsiveness of the website for our users, we might use “cookies”, or parallel electronic tools to accumulate information in order to allocate a unique, random numeral as a User Identification (User ID) to each visitor. This will help us understand the individual interests of every user browsing from an identified computer/device. Till the time you willingly identify yourself (through registration), we by no means can know who you are, even though we allocate a cookie to your device.
The only personal detail a cookie can contain is the information that you provide. By no means can a cookie read data off your hard drive. Our advertisers could also dole out their own cookies to your browser (in case you click on their ads), a procedure that we do not manage. We obtain and accumulate certain sorts of information whenever you communicate with us on website though any of your device.
** ** Information Security
We take all suitable security measures to guard against unauthorized access or modification, leak or destruction of data. These measures include in-house reviews of our data compilation, storage and dealing practices and security procedures, including apposite encryption and physical security procedures to safeguard against illegitimate access to systems on which we accumulate personal data.
All information gathered on The Voice of Nation is guardedly stored inside the Company controlled database. We store the database on servers protected behind a firewall; plus the server access remains password-protected and is stringently limited. However, despite highly efficient security measures in place, no security system can be completely impenetrable. We do not warranty the security of our database, nor can we assure that details you provide will not be interrupted while transmitting them to us over the Internet. Furthermore, obviously, any information you post in the discussion columns is accessible to anyone with the Internet access.
