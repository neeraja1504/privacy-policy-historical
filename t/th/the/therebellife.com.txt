The Canadian National Newspaper: About Us - The Rebel Life | Canada's eclectic fashion magazine | therebellife.com
The Canadian is a not-for-profit organization. We seek to respect the privacy of our readers, and everyone in general, in relation to the vital human rights principles that we support.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies,
please see: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
Please be aware that email, and websites in general, are not a secure medium, and any personal information you send to us by email or through the website could be intercepted. If your communication is very sensitive, or includes information such as your credit card number, you should not send it electronically unless the email is encrypted or your browser indicates that the access to our website is secure.
Materials posted to online forums such as bulletin boards or comment sites are public, not secure and may be viewed by anyone. Any personal information you post may be collected and used by anyone and may result in unsolicited messages from other internet users.
The Canadian is not responsible for personal information you choose to post or submit in any online forum on any of its websites.
Please feel free to send questions to editorial@agoracosmopolitan.com.
