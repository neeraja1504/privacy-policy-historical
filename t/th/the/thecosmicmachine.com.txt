Refund Policy
Are you unsatisfied with our products? Send us an e-mail, let us know what went wrong and we'll be happy to try and resolve the issue, or refund your purchase (within 30 days of purchase).
Cancellation Policy
Orders are processed immediately by our backend system and you receive your serial number instantly thus an order cannot be cancelled. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we can offer a refund. For more information, refer to our refund policy.
Privacy Policy
We greatly value our customers' privacy. In short summary:
We will never sell or distribute your personal information to a third party (unless required by law).
We will never email you without your request.
We will never store or retain your financial details (including credit card numbers).
We only keep the licensing information for the products you have bought (name, email and serial numbers).
Data Protection Act
As a UK based company, we are subject to the Data Protection Act of 1998 and we have the legal duty to protect any information we collect from you. We will never disclose your information to another organisation without your prior permission unless there is a legal requirement to do so.
Web Site Usage
We keep logs of activity on our website including IP addresses and host names. This information is used internally to help us improve our services and is not shared with anyone.
Personal Information
When you buy our software, we keep a record of your personal details such as your name, email address and your serial numbers. Details of each transaction is also collected and held by our payment processor PayPal. When you are purchasing a product, your IP address is recorded for security purposes.
Information Request
You have the right to request a copy of any personal data we hold about you to check its accuracy.
