Privacy Policy
PLEASE READ!!!
The privacy and security of your personal information is very important to us. Any personal information you provide us will be kept in the strictest of confidence.
Your billing information will only be used to identify your purchase order. It will never be given or sold to anyone for any reason without your consent.
We offer discreet credit card billing. Your order will appear as The Paper Experts Inc. on our statement.
Information is stored through the use of cookies to help facilitate the ease of use within the site. No malicious ad-ware or spy-ware products are ever used by us.
All custom works are guaranteed to be original in content and creation. All sources used are documented according to strict and proper guidelines in the format of your choice (APA, MLA, Chicago Style, etc.)
Your business is appreciated! Please feel free to contact us regarding any questions you might have about the security of your information.
