Privacy Policy of The Elder Geek
Last Update -  July 08,2002
At the present time, no information is collected from visitors to this site, other than those who choose to opt in to the Content Notification Service. Users of the Content Notification Service supply only their e-mail address. If that policy should change, this notice will be updated and I will only gather information I need to serve you better and will not - without your consent, - sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement except in cases where in good faith I believe that the law requires me to do so, it is necessary to protect The Elder Geek's rights and/or property, or if circumstances necessitate acting to protect the personal safety of myself or of the public.
