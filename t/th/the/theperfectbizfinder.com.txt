Privacy
We are committed to the protection of the rights of visitors to this site. Any data which is collected from you throughout this site will be used solely for the purpose intended, and will not be given, lent, or sold to any third party.
None of the personal information gathered about our users will be published on this site or anywhere else.
The secure payment system
We use the services of a number of merchant processing services to handle secure ordering on this website.
Each has extensive anti-fraud measures in place to protect your personal and credit-card details.
The information collected by our merchant service providers is for the purposes of completing the transaction and will not be used by them for any other purpose.
Your rights regarding your personal data
We are based in the USA and our website is bound by American law.
We will never Sell, Barter, or Rent your email address to any unauthorized third party.
