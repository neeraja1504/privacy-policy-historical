Privacy statement for the-landscape-design-site.com
the-landscape-design-site.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: the-landscape-design-site.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.
This site contains links to other sites. the-landscape-design-site.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
the-landscape-design-site uses its own privately owned email service for the landscape design newsletter which safely stores your name and email address when you subscribe. the-landscape-design-site agrees not to sell or rent the email addresses of any List Owner or List Member to any third party.
We also store the same information whenever you join any membership on the site such as the forum or video directory. Again, this information is safe and will never be shared.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving communications from us and our partners at the point where the information was given about the visitor.
This site gives users the following option for removing their information from our database to not receive future communications or to no longer receive our service. You can send an email to the-landscape-design-site at contact the-landscape-design-site.com , you can use the unsubscribe link provided at the bottom of each email or ezine issue, or you can unsubscribe to any specific membership in that respective directory.
Third Party Cookies - We also use third party advertisements on the-landscape-design-site.com. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers (such as Google through Google AdSense ads program) information including your IP address, your ISP , the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used to show you ads better suited to your geographic area or search history by showing certain ads based on specific sites visited. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security.
Contacting this Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, feel free to use the form at Contact Us
