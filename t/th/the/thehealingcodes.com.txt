Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC has created this privacy statement with respect to our websites in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. We DO NOT sell, rent or give your name or contact information to third parties without your permission. When you purchase a product or product package which is provided in part or wholly by a third party, we will provide your contact information to those parties, so they may provide the products and/or services you have purchased. We consider your payment for such products or product packages your permission to share your contact information with such parties.
Our site, from time to time, may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.
Our site's registration form(s) may require(s) you to give us contact information (like your name and email address), unique identifiers, and optional information (like your mailing address) when you request products or services from us. Your contact information is used to contact you when necessary, such as sending opted-in promotional material. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings; or emails

We use this data to tailor your experience at our site, showing you content that we think you might be interested in, and displaying the content according to your preferences.
This particular site, may from time to time, contain links to other sites outside the scope of Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC. Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Public Forums
This site, from time to time, may make chat rooms, forums, message boards, and/or news groups available to its users. Please remember that any information that is disclosed in these areas becomes public information and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information. We have provided the user with the capability to select a username to protect their privacy on the public message boards.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC does not personally maintain your information. Your information is maintained with one or more of the following companies: Infusionsoft, 1ShoppingCart and AWeber. Those companies have security measures in place to protect your information and are GDPR compliant.
What you can do
According to the General Data Protection Regulation you can request the following:
To be informed about all of the personal information Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC has on file about you
To have your personal information fixed and/or updated
To ask for the source where your personal information was obtained
To request the removal of your personal information from our systems (please be aware that some information needs to be on file for certain time periods due to country specific laws, rules and regulations)
To not be contacted anymore or to be taken off the mailing list. Remember, you can easily remove yourself by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link that is provided in each email you receive from Dr. Alex Loyd Services, LLC.
