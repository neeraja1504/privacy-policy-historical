Privacy Policy
The Coding Studio takes your privacy seriously. This privacy policy describes which personal information we collect and how we use it.
We can be reached through our contact page.
For each visitor to our web site, our web server automatically recognizes terms that may have been provided to search engines.
We collect aggregate information on which pages visitors access, as well as information volunteered on our quote request form.
The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web site, and is shared with other reputable organizations to help them contact visitors for marketing purposes.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please contact us.
Persons who supply us with their contact information on-line will only be contacted from us with information regarding quote requests. Persons may also be contacted from other reputable organizations. Visitors can, however, have their names put on our do-not-share list by contacting us.
Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in their contact information. Visitors can have this information corrected by contacting us.
With respect to security: We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from our visitors on our web site.
With respect to cookies: We use cookies to record past activity in order to provide better service as visitors navigate through our web site.
With respect to Ad Servers: Advertising partners and other third parties may also use cookies, scripts and/or web beacons to track visitors to our site in order to display advertisements and other useful information. Such tracking is done directly by the third parties through their own servers and is subject to their own privacy policies. Any advertisements served by Google, Inc., and affiliated companies may be controlled using cookies. These cookies allow Google to display ads based on your visits to this site and other sites that use Google advertising services. Learn how to opt out of Google's cookie usage. As mentioned above, any tracking done by Google through cookies and other mechanisms is subject to Google's own privacy policies.
If you feel that our web site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us.
