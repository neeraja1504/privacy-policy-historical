tossdown | Privacy Ploicy
What information do we collect?
We collect information from you when you fill a form or signup.
What do we do with your information?
Your information is used as follows:
As for your personal information a limited amount of that is shared with other users to endorse you as a contributor to the website.
Information such as reviews/photos/tips and any other contribution is used on our website to help other people in finding great food around them and also used by our third party services such as a API and Apps.
Periodic emails/newsletter are sent to keep you updated on your products/services or any other information that helps to improve your dining out experience.
Information Security:
We implement an array of security features to help protect your information.
Cookies:
The website uses cookies to help save your basic selections such as the city of your choice etc.
Third Party:
Occasionally, at our discretion we may include third party links or advertise these products and services. They have independent privacy policies and we are at any given instance are not responsible for these. However, if you may have any feedback regarding these services/products you may advise us.
Online Privacy Policy:
This privacy policy applies to all information collected online and not to information collected offline.
Consent:
By using our site, you agree to our privacy policy.
