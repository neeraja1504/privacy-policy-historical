Today's BIG Fail: Privacy Policy
The following Privacy Policy applies to this website, http://www.todaysbigfail.com (the "Website", "Our", "Us", "We"), and all of its visitors ("You").
We use cookies across the website to: (a) store which videos you have voted on; (b) store which videos you have viewed.
We allow visitors to submit their email addresses to Us to receive a 'Daily FAIL'. We will not share, sell or make public any information We hold on the visitors to any third parties; including, but not limited to, your email address.
You can opt-out of the 'Daily FAIL' emails by clicking the link in the email. Your email address will then be permanently removed from this service and our databases.
We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit Our Website. Some advertising companies may store information on your visit in order to serve You relevant adverts.
Aforementioned advertising companies may store Cookies on your web browser in order to track which adverts have been served and how You interacted with them. DART cookies may be used to service you adverts based on previous visits to the Website, as well as other Websites you have visited. You can opt-out of the DART cookie at http://www.google.com/privacy/ads.
When you use our "applications" on Social Networking sites such as Facebook, you allow Us to access certain information from your profile for that site. The information We can see is limited by the privacy settings you established when "allowing" the application. We may use and store information such as:
- Your first and last name
- Your User ID for the Social Networking site
- Your email address used at the Social Networking site
