Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy. This policy explains the accepted privacy practices for the services available at www.toolbarhome.com (the " Website ") and the use of our related software services and tools (collectively: " ToolbarHome ").
Information that we collect
You will not need to provide us with any personally identifying information in order to use ToolbarHome. When you use ToolbarHome, whether as a publisher or any other user, we may collect certain information, such as session durations, page impressions, advertisements viewed or clicked on, the Internet protocol (IP) address, the domain name that served you to access ToolbarHome, and the geographic location of the computer system that you are using when you access ToolbarHome. We may also track further information pertaining to you, when you access ToolbarHome, such as the frequency and the extent of your use of ToolbarHome. We may automatically collect information through the use of log files and other means. We will not knowingly make use of any information which personally identifies you, except as specifically indicated in this policy, or on the Website.
What do we do with the information?
We may use the information for the following purposes:
To provide the ToolbarHome services and operate the Website;
To properly operate ToolbarHome, to improve the quality and functionality of the ToolbarHome services and the website, to enhance your experience, to create new services, including customized services, to change or cancel existing content or services and for other internal and statistical purposes.
To present you relevant content, marketing materials and advertisements, by analyzing your interests from the web pages and you visit and online services that you use;
To provide you with support and handle inquires;
To enforce the ToolbarHome EULA;
To comply with any applicable law and assist law enforcement agencies as required;
To conduct surveys and market researches;
To take any action in any case of dispute, or legal proceeding of any kind between you and us, or between you and other users, publishers or third parties with respect to, or in relation with ToolbarHome;
We may use anonymous, statistical or aggregated information about ToolbarHome's use and share, publish, post, disseminate, transmit or otherwise communicate or make available such information, to suppliers, business partners, sponsors, affiliates and any other third party, at our sole discretion, provided however, that such information will not identify you personally and that we will not knowingly, or intentionally use such information to reveal your identity without your consent, under the terms of this policy;
Data retention
We reserve the right at all times to review, retain and disclose any information as we deem necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request, as well as our internal needs. However, by doing so we do not either expressly or impliedly endorse affirm or in any manner assume any responsibility for such information.
Information security
We implement information security systems and procedures to secure the information stored on our servers. Such systems and procedures reduce the risk of security breaches, but they do not provide absolute security. Therefore, we cannot guarantee that the ToolbarHome will be immune to unauthorized access to the information stored therein and to other information security risks.
Cookies
We may use cookies when operating ToolbarHome. Cookies are packets of information sent by our servers to your web browser and then sent back by the browser. We may use cookies for various purposes, such as to facilitate the use of ToolbarHome, to collect information for statistical and analytical purposes, to verify information, to customize ToolbarHome to your personal preferences and for information security purposes.
Some of the cookies may expire when the session ends and you exit ToolbarHome. Other cookies may be stored on your computerâ€™s hard drive. If you wish to block the cookies, then please use the help button in your browser and follow the necessary instructions. However, bear in mind that disabling cookies may complicate or even prevent your use of ToolbarHome, or certain features thereof.
Advertising campaigns
We may permit certain other companies to conduct advertising campaigns on ToolbarHome. The advertisements that you see when accessing ToolbarHome are redirected from the servers of those advertising companies. Some advertisers may use cookies on your computer. The cookies enable them to collect information about websites that you have accessed, advertisements posted on those sites and the advertisements that you have viewed. In this manner advertisers try to display advertisements tailored to your preferences. The use made by the advertisers of cookies is subject to their own privacy policies and not to this Policy. If you wish to study the privacy policies of those advertisers you should visit their websites, or contact them directly.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may change and update the terms of this policy from time to time. Therefore, it is recommended that you read it periodically. All substantial changes made to this policy will be notified on the Website, at our sole discretion, and take effect 30 days following their initial posting.
Contact us
You may send requests, responses, questions and complaints, by sending an e-mail message to: info@toolbarhome.com.
