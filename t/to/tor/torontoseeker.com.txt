Privacy Policy
Torontoseeker.com will not sell or share personally identifiable information on its individual customers to third parties without the prior permission of the user. Torontoseeker.com may use information provided on a voluntary basis for its own statistical analyses to measure the interest in and use of its site. The personal information of those who advertise with  Torontoseeker.com is kept confidential and will not be disclosed under any circumstance. This confidential information may include, but is not limited to,  credit card information, e-mail addresses, and contact information.
