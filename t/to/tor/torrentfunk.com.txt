TorrentFunk - Privacy Policy
TorrentFunk doesn't record personal information.
We do not require a username or password from you to use our site, so your privacy is 100% guaranteed!
Advertisements
We generate advertisements locally based on the session's search history. Users clicking on these adverts are forwarded to third-party advertising companies.These companies may use information about your visit to this and other web sites in order to provide goodsand services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choicesabout not having this information used by these companies, please consult the privacy policy of the advertiser concerned.This policy can be found on the advertiser's website.
Cookies
TorrentFunk does not generate any cookies.
IP Logging
Generally TorrentFunk doesn't record IP addresses of it's visitors. There are two exceptions to this non-IP-logging policy.For the purpose of informating the administration with insights into TorrentFunk's traffic and marketing effectiveness, we usethird-party applications that do log the IP addresses of our visitors temporarily. Secondly, we record parts of the IP address of a user,when the user uploads a torrent onto TorrentFunk. These parts of the IP address can't be linked back to the individual user, butit does provide us with enough information to prevents the abuse of our systems.
