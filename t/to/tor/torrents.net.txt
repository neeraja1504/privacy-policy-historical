Torrents.net - Privacy Policy
Policy as of January 1st, 2010.
Torrents.net does NOT record personal information.
Where identification is required for purposes of administration and/or user login, we use an encrypted string based of the user's username and password which are then encoded using our own secret algorithm. Such string is used solely by Torrents.net and cannot be decoded.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements on Torrents.net. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or IP number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please consult the privacy policy of the advertiser concerned. This policy can be found on the advertiser's website.
