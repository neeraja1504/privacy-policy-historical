Top Secret Recipes | Privacy Policy
Top Secret Recipes®, Inc. (Topsecretrecipes.com) uses its best efforts to respect the privacy of its on-line visitors. Top Secret Recipes may use "cookie" technology only to obtain non-personal information from its on-line visitors in order to improve your on-line experience. For example, we track the total number of visitors to our site in the aggregate.
Top Secret Recipes only gathers personally identifiable data, such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses, etc. when voluntarily submitted by a visitor. This information is used solely by Top Secret Recipes or other entities who are involved in the operation of this site for internal purposes and is not sold or transferred to third parties. Top Secret Recipes reserves the right to change this policy at any time. Be sure to check this page periodically for changes.
NOTE TO PARENTS ON OUR PRIVACY POLICY
We urge parents to regularly monitor and supervise their children’s on-line activities.
ADVERTISEMENTS
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
