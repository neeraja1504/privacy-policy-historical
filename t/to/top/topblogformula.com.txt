Privacy Policy | Top Blog Formula
We DO NOT share your information with others. We will not solicit you unless you have given us your consent. We use collected information about you for 2 reasons: firstly, to process your service/order and second, to provide you with the best customer service possible.
The type of information we will collect about you includes:
Your name, address, phone number, and email
Credit/debit card information
Information in cookies
Your information is maintained by SZ Creative Solutions, LLC.
767 Willamette St Ste 203 Eugene OR, 97405
We will do the best to keep your information we hold accurate and up to date. You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies we will make a correction promptly.
The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy and the law.
If we intend to transfer your information outside the company, we will always obtain your consent first.
We may use technology to track the patterns of behavior of visitors to our site. This can include using Google Analytics and a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser, which you can usually modify your browser to restrict.
If you have any questions/concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at info@topblogformula.com.
