Privacy Policy | Digital Commerce 360
DigitalCommerce360.com (the “Site”, “Service” or “DC360”) is brought to you by  Vertical Web Media, LLC  (“we” or “us”). This Privacy Policy describes the types of personal and non-personal information we collect on the Site, how we use such information and to whom and under what circumstances we may disclose it. By using the Site, you agree to the collection, use and disclosure of your personally identifiable information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Site.
GDPR Compliance
Digital Commerce 360 respects your right to privacy. In compliance with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)  we ensure all data is managed lawfully, fairly and transparently. By using our website and services you consent to our collection and use of your information as described in this policy.  Email us at dataprotection@verticalwebmedia.com to request removal of your personal data.
Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation
Digital Commerce 360 complies with Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation. We only email contacts with whom we have had a business relationship within the last 2 years, individuals who have inquired about any of our products in the last 6 months or who have opted in to receive our marketing and third-party communications. Should you receive an unsolicited email from Digital Commerce 360, please email dataprotection@verticalwebmedia.com and we will remove your personal data.
Collection of Information
Personal Information
We collect personal information from you when you:
Use the Site
Access our content, including articles, resources, webinars and podcasts
Register or update an account on the Site
Complete a form including but not limited to:
DC360 newsletters
Sponsored content
Magazine subscriptions
Contact us forms
Order products and services
Roundtable events
Request for a quote
What we collect
The personal information that we collect and store may include but is not limited to:
First name, last name, job title, business type, job role
Contact information including email address and phone number
Demographic data
Any other information you provide during registration or completion of any forms or purchase of product(s)
This personal information may be collected from you during either online or offline transactions or during any contact with us. We do not share this information directly with any advertisers or sponsors unless it explicitly is stated at the time of collection.
Use of information
We may use the information we collect from and about you for any of the following purposes:
To fulfill, deliver services, products, or transactions that you have requested
To fulfill or respond to any requests you make with us
To provide you with targeted offers and advertising on the Site
To contact you with information and promotional materials and offers from our company as well as from our subsidiaries, affiliates, marketing partners, advertisers/sponsors and other third parties
To contact you when necessary
To review Site usage and operations
To address problems with the Site, our business or our products and services
To protect the security or integrity of the Site and our business
To comply with the law
Access to Information You Provide to Us
You may access any personal information you provide to us by clicking “Update Email Preferences” at the bottom of any email from the Site, correct or modify any such personal information, or direct us to make no further use of your personal information. To do so, please send an email with your request to dataprotection@verticalwebmedia.com.
Non-personal Information
As part of the standard operation of the Site, we may collect certain non-personal information from you, including but not limited to your browser type, operating system, IP (Internet Protocol) address and the domain name from which you accessed the Site. In addition, we may collect information about your browsing behavior, such as the date and time you visit the Site, the areas or pages of the Site that you visit, the amount of time you spend viewing the Site, the number of times you return to the Site and other click-stream data.
This non-personal information may be collected through various technologies, including but not limited to server logs, cookies, and web beacons.
We may use information gathered from our multiple content vehicles to create a single user record. In addition, we may add additional information from third-party sources to expand, enhance or better service your interests.
When we may share your data:
We may share information collected about you under the following conditions:
With your consent and knowledge of the data to be shared and to whom. As a publisher we provide multiple kinds of content services for your access. The content may be sponsored by an advertiser(s). Some typical examples are webinars, executive briefings, industry reports and whitepapers. The form for these registrations or downloads will identify the data that could be shared with the third party. If you do not want your data shared then you should not register or request that content.
To third-party service providers supporting the Site and all related products and publications. Examples include but are not limited to:
Our email service provider, which we use to send our newsletters and email campaigns
Our credit card processor, which processes your paid product order
Our fulfillment and mailing provider for our printed publications
Our webinar provider, which collects registration information and hosts online presentations
Online analytic tools such as Google Analytics, which provides analytics about website usage
Other service providers assisting us with our business
With government entities as needed to provide our services and where required by law
As part of an acquisition or merger with another company, a partial or total sale of assets, or another corporate change, including bankruptcy
To investigate, respond to or resolve problems or inquiries (including law enforcement or legal requests) or to protect, enforce, or defend our rights, assets and/or interests
To prevent or respond to a violation of any law, regulation, or our Terms of Service, or as necessary to protect and defend our network security
Cookies
We may use cookies to collect the non-personal information described above. A cookie is a small text file that may be stored on the hard drive of your computer when you access the Site. We may also use cookies to: (1) provide you with customized content and advertising; (2) monitor Site usage; and (3) conduct research to improve our content and services. You are free to decline cookies, but by doing so, you may not be able to use certain features on the Site or take full advantage of all of our offerings.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may change this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the change(s) on our website. If we change the policy in a material way, we will provide an appropriate online notice to you.
Security
We have taken reasonable steps to safeguard and secure the information we collect from Site visitors.
Children
We do not direct the Site to, nor do we knowingly collect any personal information from, children under 13.
Third-Party Websites
The Site may contain links to third-party websites, which may have privacy policies that differ from our own. We are not responsible for the activities and practices that take place on these sites. Accordingly, we recommend that you review the privacy policy posted on any site that you may access through the Site.
