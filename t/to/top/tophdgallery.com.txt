Privacy Policy - Top HD Photos Gallery
Our Privacy Policy
Your privacy is very important with us. To protect your privacy in a better way, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
For your convenient in finding our privacy policy, we place this notice on every our webpages.
We do not capture and store personal information.
Cookies and Third Parties
Cookies are information that stored by your browser. They are primarily used to store information about your preferences and other information which you may need when you visit a website. We do not collect your cookies but some third parties (such as Google Adsense, DoubleClick, ect...) which are used on our website may use your cookies to serve their displaying ads.
