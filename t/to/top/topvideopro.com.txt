| Topvideopro.com Privacy Statement
Topvideopro.com is committed to protecting privacy of visitors to our web page.
We will never collect your email address!
We will never sell or give away any information that my be collected!
We will never send any cookies!
Customers who buy through Topvideopro.com are shopping on a secure server mostly hosted by Regnow, which encrypts all of the customer's personal information including name, address and credit card number. Encryption ensures that no one can access or use personal information in an unauthorized manner. Please view Regnow's privacy policy.
Topvideopro.com welcomes your comments regarding this privacy statement. If you believe that Topvidepro.com has not adhered to this privacy statement, please feel free to contact us.
