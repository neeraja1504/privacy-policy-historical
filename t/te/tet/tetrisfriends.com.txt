PRIVACY POLICY
Last Revised: May 22, 2018
​Tetris Online Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") recognizes your privacy is very important, and we take it seriously. This Privacy Policy ("policy") explains how personal information is collected, used and disclosed by us with respect to your use of any website, service, game, or application offered by Tetris Online, Inc ("TOA Products").
By accessing or using TOA Products, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy, including consenting to TOA Products and our advertising partners' use of your data for targeted advertising purposes as described in this policy. This policy also explains how you can withhold or withdraw your consent to the use of your data for targeted advertising purposes. If you have any other concerns about providing information to us, or how the information is being used as described in this policy, you should not use any TOA Products.

DEFINITIONS ​
​ Personal Information  | Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. An identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person.

**PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT ​​ ****
Personal information may be collected in a number of ways through use of TOA Products, such as when you voluntarily provide it in connection with registering, creating a profile, posting content, sending an email or message to another user or participating in the interactive games, forums or features of TOA Products. If you leave a comment on a forum or provide a testimonial on TOA Products, you should be aware that any personally identifiable information you submit there can be read, collected, or used by other users, and could be used to send you unsolicited messages.
From time to time we may collect personal information that you provide in connection with your participation in surveys, sweepstakes, contests, games, promotional offers, and other activities on TOA Products. If we collect information from you offline, and it is linked with personal information, we will treat that information as personal information.
Some personal information may also be automatically collected through the use of TOA Products, as is described within this policy.
When you use TOA Products on a social network, we may record information regarding the use of that TOA Product, and we may collect other information that is provided in accordance with the relevant terms of use associated with that social network. It is your responsibility to read and accept social networking sites’ Terms of Use, as well as their Privacy Policy prior to continuing to use such sites.

When you invite new members into your network, or send messages through TOA Products, we may collect and maintain the information associated with those messages, included email address and content.
HOW WE USE PERSONAL INFORMATION ​​​
****In general, we use personal information that we collect to process your requests or transactions, to provide you with information or services you request, to inform you about other information, events, promotions, products or services we think will be of interest to you, to facilitate your use and our administration and operation of TOA Products, to learn more about and improve TOA Products. For example, we may use the information we collect:
i. To send you a welcoming email or contact you about your use of TOA Products, such as for administrative purposes or to provide you with customer service;
ii. To provide you with the services, products or information you request and to complete any transactions or facilitate the use of any features in connection there with, such as matching users for gameplay;
iii. To contact you about or to advertise our or a third party's services, products, activities, special events or offers and for other marketing, informational, product development and promotional purposes;
iv. To respond to your emails, submissions, comments, requests or complaints;
v. To notify contest winners; and
vi. To request feedback and to enable us to develop, customize and improve TOA Products and new offerings.
In addition, when you use TOA Products on a social networking site, we may record information regarding the use of that TOA Product, and we may collect other information that is visible on your social networking site in accordance with the relevant terms of use associated with that social network (See Facebook's
Terms of Use  | Data Policy ). This information may include, but is not limited to, your name, profile picture, the pictures of your social networking site "friends," gender, and game scores. We may retain this information to enhance your experience when playing our games. For example we may post the high scores and profile pictures of you and your friends while you play one of our TOA Products.​ **
STORAGE AND HISTORY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION ​​
****We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the TOA Products you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our policies.
How long we retain personal information can vary significantly based on context of the services we provide and on our legal obligations. The following factors typically influence retention periods:
How long is the personal information needed?
This includes such things as maintaining and improving the performance of our products, keeping our systems secure, and maintaining appropriate business and financial records. This is the general rule that establishes the baseline for most of our data retention periods.
Is the personal information sensitive?
If so, a shortened retention time is generally appropriate.
Have you provided consent for a longer retention period?
If so, we will retain data in accordance with your consent.
Are we subject to a legal, contractual, or similar obligation to retain your personal information?
​Examples can include mandatory data retention laws in the applicable jurisdiction, government orders to preserve data relevant to an investigation, or personal information retained for the purposes of litigation.
After it is no longer necessary for us to retain your personal information, we will dispose of it in a secure manner according to our data retention and deletion policies. *  ***​

**PROTECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
​​
We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to help protect the security of your information both online and offline and to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this policy. These measures vary based upon the sensitivity of your information.
We strive to protect personal information while it is under our control. Your account information is accessible online only through the use of a password. To help protect the confidentiality of personal information, you must keep your password confidential and not disclose it to any other person. Please advise us immediately at support@tetrisonline.com if you believe your password has been compromised or misused.
​​ ​ COOKIES AND  SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES​
​A cookie is a small piece of data that is stored on your computer or mobile device. Like many websites, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect additional website usage data and to operate some TOA Products.
Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, many browsers’ settings can be set to decline cookies or alert you when a website is attempting to place a cookie on your computer. However, some TOA Products may not function properly if you disable cookies.
When your browser or device allows it, we use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with TOA Products, to monitor aggregate usage patterns, and to personalize and otherwise operate TOA Products such as by providing account security, personalizing the content we show you including ads, and remembering your language preferences.
Advertising companies we work with generally use cookies and similar technologies as part of their services. To learn more about how advertisers generally use cookies and the choices they offer, you can review the following resources:
Digital Advertising Alliance
Digital Advertising Alliance of Canada
European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance
