Log files
As far as the security of the website is concerned then http://www.techbase.in/ will use of the log files. The information  given inside the log files includes the Internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), type of browser,  date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable and you do not need to panic while visiting the techbase website.
