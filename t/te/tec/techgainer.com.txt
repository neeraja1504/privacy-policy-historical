Privacy Policy
At TechGainer.com, we take the privacy of our users very seriously. Please note that any personal information you provide us or we collect ourselves, is never shared or sold with any entity or individual. You can find details below about when, how and what we may record some of personal information of our users. Read on.
Visitor Logs
Just like web browsers, TechGainer uses logs that includes IP (internet protocol) address, ISP (internet service provider), geographic location, time/date, browser info, referral links etc. This helps us understand how users are interacting with our website and helps us improve our service. These information are anonymous and not identifiable personally.
Cookies
In case you not sure, “cookie” is a small data that is stored in your browser locally and allows TechGainer to recognize your each visit. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, rather visitor specific preference.
External links
TechGainer is never responsible for external links that is not hosted on our site. It is advised to read specific privacy policies for external links/sites.
Ads
We uses ads served by Google, a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TechGainer. Google’s uses DART cookie that enables it to serve ads based on users visit and interest. You can opt out the use of Dart cookies by visiting Google ad privacy and terms. Our other advertising partners may also use cookies and web beacons on our site to serve ads. These third-party ad servers uses technologies like JavaScript, web beacons, IP address to serve relevant ads and may gather anonymous data to measure their performance. TechGainer has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You can disable cookies if you wish using your browser settings.
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by contact us page.
