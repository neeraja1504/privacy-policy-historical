Google Adsense and the DoubleClick DART Cookie
To serve ads on the site Google, the third party advertisement vendor use cookies. The use of DART cookies serves adverts to visitors based on their visits to sites on the internet. Tracking users by using DART cookie mechanisms are subject to Google’s own privacy policies. Through this http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html URL you can visit content network privacy policy and Google ad. To track users other third parties and ad networks also use cookies. There is no control to IndianOnlineMedia Blogs (IOM) over these cookies. Third parties may use the cookies.
Collection of Personal Information
With the date and time, the IP address access the site will be logged while visiting IndianOnlineMedia Blogs (IOM). It is used to gather broad demographic information, administer the site, track users’ movement, and analyze trends for internal use. It is not linked the IP address to personally identifiable information.
