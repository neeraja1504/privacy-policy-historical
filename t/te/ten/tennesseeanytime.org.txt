Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy covers how www.tennesseeanytime.org collects, uses and discloses information collected from users of the www.tennesseeanytime.org website. This privacy policy applies to www.tennesseeanytime.org.
Personal information
We may collect personal information when it is submitted to us voluntarily by our users.
Non-personal information
We may collect non-personal information from users whenever they access our website. This information may include standard information provided by the web browser such as browser name, version and IP address.
Cookies
We or 3rd party partners may use cookies to improve the user experience of our site.
How we use the information we collect
We use user information to improve our site and to provide support to our users.
Sharing your personal information
We do not provide the personal information of users to other parties. We may share aggregated user information that does not include any personal information with our business partners.
Advertising
We may display ads on our site from third party advertisers. They may use cookies to improve the performance of their ads.
Third party sites
Visitors to www.tennesseeanytime.org may find advertising or other content on our site that links to third party websites. We do not control the content or the links that appear on these third party sites. These third party sites may have their own privacy policies and we encourage our users to review them.
Your acceptance of these terms
By using www.tennesseeanytime.org you are agreeing to accept this privacy policy. We ask that you not use our site if you do not agree to this policy. We may change the terms of this privacy policy at any time without notice. Your use www.tennesseeanytime.org will signify your acceptance of any changes made to this privacy policy.
