Privacy Statement for TerraGame.com
TerraGame.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.TerraGame.com.
Our online surveys ask visitors for contact information (like their email address) and rarely demographic information (like their Name, Gender and age). We may use contact data from our surveys to send the user technical and promotional material from us. Any time users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the Correct/Update section below. We use this data and cookies to enhance our visitor's experience of our site, displaying content that we think they might be interested in, and formatting the content according to their preferences.
Financial information such as Credit Card No etc. is NOT collected from our users and is NOT used on TerraGame site. Your payment is proceeded by financial vendors and operates directly to the bank.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
Correct/Update
This site gives users options for changing and removing information previously provided. Users also are able to remove their information from our database not to receive future communications or to discontinue our service.
We use an outside ad company to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. While we use cookies in other parts of our Web site, cookies received with banner ads are collected by ad company, and we do not have access to that information.
This site contains links to other sites, however TerraGame.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of other Web sites.

These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
