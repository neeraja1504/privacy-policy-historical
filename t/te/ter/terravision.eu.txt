Privacy Policy
Any information provided by you through this website is provided to Terravision London Ltd.("us"), which administers this website.
Your privacy is important to us and we will not disclose information about you to any other person or organisation without your consent unless there is an emergency situation that requires it or we are legally required to do so. We will use the personal information you give us as a research tool to develop the content of this website, to develop our understanding of our visitors and to determine how best to serve them. We may occasionally use the information we collect to notify you about new services and special offers we think you may find valuable. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties unless you consent to us doing so. We will use return e-mail addresses to answer the emails we receive. The website collects some anonymous information about you automatically as you move through the site. This information is used to help us monitor how you and other visitors use the site, allowing us to provide an improved service throughout the site. The information collected automatically does not, however, contain anything that can identify you personally.
In using this website, you consent to the collection and use of personal information by Terravision London Ltd. In the ways described above. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it and in what circumstances we disclose it
If you have any questions about privacy, data accuracy or security please contact Terravision on +39 06 3244152 helpdesko@terravision.eu
