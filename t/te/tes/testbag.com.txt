TestBag
PRIVACY POLICY
Collection of Information
In order to provide you with our online and offline services , we collect your personal information such as your full name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone number . The personal information is collected only in order to serve you and contact you in connection with services provided by us. When we collect personal information from you it is because you are voluntarily submitting the information to us in order to avail services such as test trials, online testing, discussion forums, contributions and other service/information provided by us on our website testbag.com
Use of Information
We may use information provided by you for billing to subscriber/other users, making payments to contributors and attending to queries, for service enhancement, attending to service issues etc. The information collected is used for our internal use and for contacting/serving you better. None of the information collected is sold or passed to third party
At present we are using email to contact our existing subscribers. We are not using email to contact other users although we reserve the right to do so in future. If you do not want to receive promotional email, please let us know by sending email to email@testbag.com
Purchases using Credit Card
To facilitate your online purchases , we collect your personal information such as name, address, phone number and email ID and pass on the same information to Credit Card Service Provider linked to our website. The Credit Card Service Provider further collects information about your credit card number, date of expiration etc and forwards to relevant authorization entity ( Credit Card Company/Bank) for validation. The information on success or failure of your transaction is provided online by credit card service provider. Please go through provisions privacy policy of Credit Card Service Provider
Purchases using Net Banking
To facilitate your online purchases , we collect your personal information such as name, address, phone number and email ID and pass on the same information to Net Banking Service Provider linked to our website. The Net Banking Service Provider further collects information about your bank account number, date of expiration etc and forwards to relevant authorization entity (Bank) for validation. The information on success or failure of your transaction is provided online by Net Banking service provider. Please go through provisions of privacy policy of Net Banking Service Provider
Our Commitment to Privacy
RKS Learning Private Limited is committed to preserve individual privacy rights on the Internet and electronic commerce. RKS Learning Private Limited uses reasonable administrative, technical, personnel and physical measures to safeguard your personal information in its possession against loss, theft and unauthorized use, disclosure or modification
Privacy Provisions of Linked Internet Sites
Kindly note that other Internet sites to which you link from www.testbag.com may contain privacy provisions that differ from the provisions of our Privacy Policy
