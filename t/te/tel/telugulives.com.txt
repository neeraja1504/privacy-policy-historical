Privacy Policy - Telugu Lives
Personal Information :
Telugulives.com does not collect or save any personal information who visits to our site and won’t ask any personal information.
we won’t handle any cookies of visitors who visits our website on their computers.
we won’t ask or give information of visitors to third party for any reason.
There is no database to handle visitors information in our website.
Privacy Policy:
Telugulives.com fully committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to this website.
Aggregate Information:
Telugulives.com uses a web statistics to track the performance of the site and this program help us to improve site and it doesn’t reflect any visitors.
