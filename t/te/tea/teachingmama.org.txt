Privacy Policy - Teaching Mama
Angela Thayer is the writer and creator of Teaching Mama. She values the privacy of her readers and believes in providing you with a safe and secure online experience. Here is more information about Teaching Mama’s private policy.
By visiting, you hereby accept the policies and practices described in this privacy policy and agree to Teaching Mama’s use and disclosure of the information that you provide, or that is collected about you. Anonymous visitor information is recorded and used by Teaching Mama to track blog growth and learn how you found me.  This information is not shared or sold.  Teaching Mama uses Google Analytics to learn which browser you use, how you found this blog, and to see how many hourly/daily/weekly/monthly visitors there are.
To leave a comment, enter a giveaway, or subscribe by email, you provide your name and email address. This information is never shared with anyone. It is used to to notify winners of giveaways, reply to comments and send email newsletters. As always, you may unsubscribe to the newsletter at any time.
Some of my advertising partners may use cookies to store information about your preference, record user-specific information on which pages you access or visit, and to customize web page content based on your browser type or other information that you send via their browser. Teaching Mama has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
Teaching Mama publishes ads for Mode Media on this website.  Mode uses a third party to collect cookies from you that help them show ads that are relevant.  You can opt out of the Mode Media network cookie here:http://www.aboutads.info/choices
Teaching Mama publishes Google Adsense ads on this website.  Google and third-party vendors show ads on this site that reflect your interests.  In order to determine what interests you, Google tracks your behavior on this website and other websites using DoubleClick DART Cookies.  You can opt out of the Adsense Partner network cookie at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
Personal information that is collected on this site through Google Analytics, Google Adsense, or Lijit ads has never been sold, and will never be sold.  You will not be contacted through direct marketing.
To learn more about this behavioral advertising practice visit the NAI at http://www.networkadvertising.org. To opt out of this type of advertising, you can visit http://www.aboutads.info/choices/
Teaching Mama reserves the right to change this policy at any time without notice.
