Adsense
Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google. teachingbd24.com  uses the “Doubleclick” Cookie, which tracks the use of teachingbd24.com  and user behavior concerning ads, products and the services we offer.
Personal Data collected: Cookie and Usage Data. Place of processing: USA. You can find the AdSense Privacy Policy here.
You may decide to disable all the Doubleclick Cookies by clicking on: google.com/settings/ads/onweb/optout?hl=en.
What do we do with your data?
The Data concerning the User is collected to allow the Application to provide its services, as well as for the following purposes: Analytics and Interaction with external social networks and platforms. By any mean, we don’t use your data for any other purposes.
We don’t sell your data, or modify it or use it by any other means.
Privacy
teachingbd24.com respects your privacy.
Any personal information you provide to us including and similar to your name, address, telephone number and e-mail address will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of teachingbd24.com.
Credit card details
teachingbd24.com  will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter it on any of the forms on teachingbd24.com.
External Sites.
teachingbd24.com  is not responsible for the content of external internet sites. You are advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.
Cookies
A “cookie” is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows teachingbd24.com  to recognize you each time you visit this site(customisation etc). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and teachingbd24.com  does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds.
