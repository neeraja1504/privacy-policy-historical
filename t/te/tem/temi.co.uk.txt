You supply certain information when you sign up with us as a customer. This information includes your name, the name of your business, your postal address, email address, telephone number, fax number and how you heard of us. This information will be used in the management of our database and to enable us contact you about the service you have requested. We may also use your email address to send you one special offers. If you wish you may opt out from receiving these offers. In addition, when you use our website we may store your IP address and may use this information to administer our website and help to diagnose any network or server problems. Our website does not use cookies at present but we may introduced the use of cookies in the future to make your use of velnet.co.uk a better experience. If you do not want Velnet.co.uk or any other site to store cookies in your browser you may configure your browser NOT to accept cookies Information we share with others Velnet.co.uk will not divulge your personal information to a third party. The following circumstances are however an exemption: In order to register a domain name we are required to send your name or/and business name, postal address and email address to organisations that formally record the registration; this information may be available to others via a "Whois" search; If we are required to do so by law or a statutory body; To a financial institution with whom we hold an account for the purpose of verifying credit status and / or validating financial transactions. Velnet.co.uk is not responsible for the privacy policy of other companies or website. Credit Cards WorldPay processes credit card payment for Velnet.co.uk. WorldPay collects your credit card details over a secure server to guarantee your privacy. Velnet.co.uk does not collect or store your credit card details. Links to other sites and banner advertisements Velnet website contains advertising and links to other websites. No personal information is passed to other websites. Velnet.co.uk is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of linked websites. We would encourage you to read the privacy statements of other websites if you are concerned about your privacy. Consent Use of the velnet.co.uk website and services indicates your consent to the collection and use of information as described in this document.
