Privacy Policy
TemplateKunena.com is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. TemplateKunena.com does not collect personal information from Web users, unless the personal information is provided by you. A Privacy Policy will apply in each case where you may provide personal information to TemplateKunena.com through our Web site..
We will never sell or rent your personal information to outside parties.
Credit card information is not stored or collected at the TemplateKunena.com web site.
All credit card payment transactions are securly processed by paypal.com and at no time does TemplateKunena.com have access to your credit card details.
