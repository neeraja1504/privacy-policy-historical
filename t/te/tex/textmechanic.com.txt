Privacy Statement
Each text tool has it's own "Privacy of Data" statement.
TextMechanic.com uses third party advertisements to support it's website.  These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TextMechanic.com.  Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to TextMechanic.com users based on their visits to TextMechanic.com and other websites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
