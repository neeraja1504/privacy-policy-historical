Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to us.  This Privacy Policy is here so that you may review how we collect make use of the information gained by your visitation to our servers.  The following outlines our privacy policy.
TickerReport.com collects information from visitors accessing our servers.
This information gained by your visitation to our servers are things such as keyword or keywords used to enter, IP address, web browser.
TickerReport.com shares some of this information with advertisement agencies for the purpose of attempting to provide you with advertisements that may be of interest to you.
TickerReport.com does not sell any of this information about you or share it outside of the advertisement agencies we have agreements with, exception being to court orders to do so.
TickerReport.com or our representatives will not ask for your name or address without your consent.
Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this website.
Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads and collect information when users visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements on our site, other websites and other forms of media about goods and services of interest to you. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, check out
http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.
