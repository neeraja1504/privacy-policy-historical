Privacy Policy
This document explains how we collect, use and share data about our users. If you require any more information or have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at tickcounter@tickcounter.com.
Collected data
Email address
Your billing address, first and last name, four last digits and expiry date of your credit card
Cookies
Automatically Collected Data such as your IP address, connection information such as browser type and version, information about your device including device-type and device identifier, operating system and platform, mobile network data, a unique reference number linked to the data you enter on our system, login details, the site from which you arrived at our service, details of your activity with date and time stamps.
Legal basis
We collect data about you because it's necessary for our contract in order to provide you with our service and because we use them to manage and improve our service. Among others, we use your data to send you invoices, process payments or create anonymized traffic analysis.
Cookies
As is common practice with almost all professional websites this site uses cookies, which are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. You can configure your browser to prevent these cookies from being stored, however this may downgrade or break certain elements of TickCounter functionality. For more general information on cookies see  this Wikipedia article.

Privacy Policy
Data retention
Your data is kept for the time necessary to provide you our service and to comply with legal requirements. You can always send us a request to remove your personal data.
Sharing your data
We may share your personal information with companies that supply us with a service:
PythonAnywhere LLP (our hosting provider)
ZOHO (our email provider)
Braintree (PayPal) (processes payments)
Facebook (provides comment system)
Google (provides anonymized traffic analysis and delivers ads)
These companies are authorized to use your personal information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
Although our business is based in Poland, your personal data may be transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area. These transfers will be protected by appropriate safeguards.
Changes to this document
We recommend to check this page often as our Privacy Policy may change at any time. If you object to any new changes, please stop using TickCounter and contact us to remove all your data from our servers.
