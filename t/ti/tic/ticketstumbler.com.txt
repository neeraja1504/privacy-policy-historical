Privacy Policy | TicketStumbler
At TicketStumbler we understand the importance of your privacy. This page describes the types and uses of personal information we collect when you use our services.
Types of Information We Collect
On some of our pages we allow you to enter your email address to receive future product notifications and/or updates from our blog. These can unsubscribed to at any time.
We set and access cookies on your computer.
TicketStumbler's servers automatically record information when you visit our website. This information includes your IP address, browser type, and time of your page request.
Uses of Information
We use personal information to provide the pages and services that you request.
TicketStumbler also uses personal information to analyze site usage and improve the quality of our services.
We do not share any of your personal information with third parties outside of TicketStumbler.
If you have additional questions regarding your privacy and TicketStumbler please contact us.
