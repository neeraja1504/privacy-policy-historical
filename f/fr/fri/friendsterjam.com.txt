Privacy Policy | FriendsterJam.com
FriendsterJam.com makes every effort to protect your privacy and identity. FriendsterJam.com does not actively collect information about it’s users. We do not ask for your email address, name, or any other identifying information.
Like all web sites, our servers do log each visit to FriendsterJam.com. Our logs are stored in the standard web log file format. The only data retained is a visitor’s IP address, operating system, browser type, and pages visited. This data is only used internally for statistical purposes. It is used to improve our visitors’ experience and site content.
We do not under any circumstances provide any of this information to any third parties.
Should this privacy policy change at anytime, details will be posted here.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, click on the “Contact Us” link that is provided on every FriendsterJam.com’s web page (always on the bottom of the page area).
Lastly, thank you for supporting FriendsterJam.com.
