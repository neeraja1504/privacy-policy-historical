Privacy Policy of APEC. Bottled water at home by R.O. Manufacturer of reverse osmosis water filters, membranes
Privacy
E-mail and Mailing Lists
We DO NOT SELL OR RENT your name, e-mail address or any other information to any third parties. APEC does not engage in any telemarketing or e-mail spam activities. If we contact you, it is because we need to clarify an order you placed with us or we are just returning your call or e-mails.
Cookies, Web Beacons, and other Technologies by Advertisers
We occasionally uses the AdRoll display advertising network to collect visitor data on our site and then serve targeted display ads to you on other sites. Our partners use technology such as cookies and web beacons to collect this data. For more information or to opt-out of this type of advertising, please visit AdRoll's privacy policy.
Customer Feedback
We may use portions of your comments as well as your first name or initials, city, and state and country of origin in the "Customers' Comments" section.
Security
Secure, Safe Shopping
We encrypt your credit card and all personal information for security when ordering through our online shopping cart. We utilize the newest secure shopping cart systems and the highest industry standard technology, SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), to assure your transactions are protected.
