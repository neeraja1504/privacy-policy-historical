Privacy Policy | Free People Directory
This policy outlines the collection, use, and sharing of any information you provide to FreePeopleDirectory, as well as information that is published in our white page directory.
DO NOT USE FREEPEOPLEDIRECTORY.COM IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY.
1. Public Information
FreePeopleDirectory aggregates information from a number of public sources, such as: white page directories, phone books, and other sources. We do not guarantee the accuracy of this information.
2. Information from Site Use
Aggregate information for analyzing our services is collected from your use of the web site for a period of time (i.e. browser type, searches), but we have no obligation to maintain this information. If you wish to be opted out of any re-targeting advertising (also known as interest-based advertising), you can visit the Network Advertising Initiative website.
3. Children’s Privacy
FreePeopleDirectory follows the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not target the collection of information for anyone under the age of 13. We also do not knowingly publish information for anyone under the age of 13. Please contact us if you are aware of any such information so we may promptly remove it.
4. Contact Us
If you need clarification or have questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us by email.
5. Removing your listing from FreePeopleDirectory
To remove a listing from FreePeopleDirectory, please use our Opt Out form.
