Cookies:
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. W2.FreeEducationGuide.com and its partners use cookies or similar technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website, and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, but if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our website or service.
We use cookies on our sites. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site.
We use session ID and persistent cookies. We use session cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our site. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for an extended period of time. You can remove persistent cookies by following the directions provided in your Internet browser's "help" file.
We set a persistent cookie to store your preferences so that each time you visit the site you do not have to set your search preferences more than once. Persistent cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site.
The use of cookies by our third party partners or affiliates is not covered by our privacy policy. We do not have access or control over these cookies. Our partners and affiliates use session ID cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our site and provide search results based on your preferences.
Targeted or Behavioral Advertising:
We partner with a third party to [display advertising on our website or to manage our advertising on other sites]. Our third party partner may use cookies or similar technologies in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests. Targeted advertising (also known as Behavioral Advertising) uses information collected on an individual's web browsing behavior such as the pages they have visited or the searches they have made. This information is then used to select which pch.com advertisement should be displayed to a particular individual on websites other than W2.FreeEducationGuide.com. For example, if you have shown a preference for nursing while visiting W2.FreeEducationGuide.com, you may be served an advertisement from W2.FreeEducationGuide.com for nursing related programs when you visit a site other than W2.FreeEducationGuide.com. The information used for targeted advertising either comes from W2.FreeEducationGuide.com or through third party website publishers. If you would like to opt out of targeted advertising from W2.FreeEducationGuide.com that occurs when visiting our third party advertising publishers, please click here. Please note that this will opt you out of targeted ads from W2.FreeEducationGuide.com and any other participating advertisers. If you opt out, you will continue to receive online advertising from W2.FreeEducationGuide.com; however, these ads may not be as relevant to you.
