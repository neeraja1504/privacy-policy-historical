Homepage.org Privacy Policy
Thank you for reviewing our Privacy Policy. Your Privacy is very important
to us and we’re committed to protecting it.
What type of information we collect
The server on which the web site is located collects and saves the
default information customarily logged by web server software. Such
information usually includes the date and time of your visit, originating
IP address, and pages and images requested.
The web site and third party advertisers may use cookies to keep track
of web searches you make while visiting this web site as well as your
web surfing behavior on this web site and other web sites you may visit
to enhance your online experience and learn more about how you use
this web site in order to improve the quality of services.
Third Parties Disclosure
We may use third party advertising companies to serve ads when you
visit our web site. These companies may use aggregated non-personal
information about your visit to this and other web sites in order to
provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
Such use is not based on personal identifying information and does not
include your name, address, email address or telephone number. In such
cases we require that third parties comply with our privacy policy and
any other confidentiality and security measures.
We may use the information you provide us for auditing, research
and statistical analysis to improve our web site and its services such
as customizable hyperlink option and content. Again such use is not
based on personal identifying information.
In limited circumstances including legal processes, fraud prevention
or imminent harm we may have to share some information with legal
authorities or third parties to ensure network security and services.
Use of Cookies
A “cookie” is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent
to your computer when you visit a web site. Cookies used by this
web site may store information such as user preferences. You can
reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is
being sent. However, without cookies some web site features or services
may not function properly.
DoubleClick Dart Cookies
We feature Google custom search on this web site and as a result, we
may use DART cookies for ad serving through Google’s DoubleClick which
is sent to your computer when you’re browsing the web and visit a site
using DoubleClick advertising (for instance for Google AdSense ads). The
purpose of this cookie is to identify and serve ads specifically selected to
serve your interests based on your previous browsing history; this is also
known as interest based targeting advertising. Again, such use of DART
cookies is not based on personal identifying information and does not
include your name, address, email address, telephone number or any
credit card information.
Web Site Servers
This web site processes personal information on servers in the United
States and Canada. The origin of this web site is in Canada.
Use of Contact Information
We may use your contact information to (1) send you updates on new
features or services of this web site, or (2) to inform you of the launch
of new web sites that we believe are of significance to the theme and
culture of Homepage.org.
