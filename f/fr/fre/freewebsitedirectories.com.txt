freewebsitedirectories
Any personal information that www.freewebsitedirectories.com collects, such as e-mail addresses  etc will be kept confidential. www.freewebsitedirectories.com will not sell, trade or share information with any other company, organization or individual, nor will this information be used for advertising purposes, unless the visitor explicitly expresses consent.
www.freewebsitedirectories.com respect your privacy.
www.freewebsitedirectories.com does not spam or sell/lease its user information to third parties.
EMAIL COMMUNICATIONS: www.freewebsitedirectories.com generally communicates with you as a member of www.freewebsitedirectories.com  . Your email will never shared with anybody within or outside www.freewebsitedirectories.com.  www.freewebsitedirectories.com uses this email address to send you three types of communications:
Sending you emails related to the proper functioning of your account (e.g. when you receive a response to a classified / URL  you have posted on www.freewebsitedirectories.com  or when you receive an invitation to join the personal network of another person)
Sending you newsletters that you have subscribed to .
Sending you occasional announcements about changes in www.freewebsitedirectories.com  's policies or promotions introduction of new services
Please note that these email communications may contain commercial messages of advertisers; however, www.freewebsitedirectories.com does not provide your personal information or emails to any of the advertisers; we just include the advertising banners they provide us in the emails that we send. As always you can opt out of the newsletters or promotional announcements.
DISCLOSURES REQUIRED BY LAW: It is possible that we may need to disclose personal information when required by law. We will disclose such information wherein we have a good-faith belief that it is necessary to comply with a court order, ongoing judicial proceeding, or other legal process served on our company or to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
