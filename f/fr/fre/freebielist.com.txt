Cookies
FreebieList.com uses cookies, which are small pieces of data that are stored on your computer to identify your machine or device. We use these for tracking visitor sessions and also for display advertising purposes.
You cannot be personally identified by cookies at FreebieList.com, but if you prefer not to accept cookies, these can be turned off in your Web browser. Usually, in most popular browsers, this involves clicking "Settings" and then locating the “Privacy” section. From here, you should be able to find options to block first and third-party cookies and sites from collecting any data. More details on how to control cookies can be found at AboutCookies.org.
We use cookies for the following reasons:
Google Analytics - This allows us to monitor the number of visitors to the website, country of origin, referring pages, percentage of returning visitors, and other anonymous information. You cannot be personally identified from this data and it is simply used to monitor visitor traffic levels.
Google Adsense - We use this service to display advertising across the website and to personalise advertisements. Again, you cannot be personally identified from these cookies and they are merely used to build up a profile to tailor adverts to match your recent searches.
Privacy
Personal information (information that you can personally be identified from), is readily misused over the Internet. The vast majority of Web sites will, at some point, ask you to provide your e-mail address and perhaps other details. We do not collect any personal information from our visitors, although if you contact us by e-mail or via one of our Web forms, or subscribe to our newsletter, we may request your e-mail address. In any case, we will not disclose your e-mail address to any third party.
Web Statistics:
Our Web statistics package collects information such as your time of visit, IP address and country of origin. Again, we do not share this information with anybody else.
Opt-in Newsletter Service:
We require your e-mail address if you wish to receive our opt-in newsletter. We will never disclosure your e-mail address to third parties or use it for any other purpose.
External Domains:
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other Web sites. Due to the nature of the Internet, we cannot be held responsible for any information being intercepted by a third party.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with freebielist.com, please contact us and we'll be more than happy to help you out!
