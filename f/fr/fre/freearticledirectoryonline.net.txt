EUA
We keep in our members database the details of all member organisations and the professional details of the main contact people in each of them. That informationÂ is available to everyone on the EUA website. We also keep information for internal purposes about participation in EUA events. Non-members can subscribe to our newsletter and therefore we keep in our database email addresses of subscribers for dissemination of this service. The information will not be re-used for an incompatible purpose.
EUA will only disclose information to third parties if that is necessary for the fulfilment of the purpose(s) identified above and to the mentioned (categories of) recipients. EUA will not divulge your personal data for direct marketing purposes.
EUA only keeps the data for the time necessary to fulfill the purpose of collection or further processing.
Adequate security measures have been taken to safeguard your information against possible misuse or unauthorised access as much as it is feasible.
For any query or complaint, please contact us at info@eua.be
