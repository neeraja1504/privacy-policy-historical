Fresh & Easy Online Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy governs the websites (each a "Site" and collectively "Sites") owned by Fresh & Easy LLC ("Fresh & Easy") where this Privacy Policy is posted. Fresh & Easy is committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our Sites. This Privacy Policy informs you about the information we collect on our Sites and how we use, share, and protect it. YOUR USE OF THIS SITE SIGNIFIES YOUR ASSENT TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT USE THIS SITE.
Your use of this Site is also subject to our website Terms and Conditions. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into, and made a part of, the Terms and Conditions.
SUMMARY
We provide the following summary with links to specific sections of our Privacy Policy for your convenience. For more detailed information, click on the links below and you will be directed to the relevant section of the Privacy Policy.
Information Collected Automatically
This Site logs IP addresses in order to improve security, analyze trends and administer the Site. Our web servers automatically recognize a visitor's domain name, browser type, the web page from which a visitor enters our Site, which pages a visitor visits on our Site, access times, and how much time a visitor spends on each page. We only use this information in aggregate form, and do not link it to you personally.
Use of Cookies and Other Technologies
We and our analytics service providers may use session and persistent "cookies," web beacons, and other technologies to collect, store and track information for analytical purposes, improve our products and services, improve our marketing and promotional activities, tailor and personalize content, and better understand your preferences and interests.
You can set your computer to warn you each time a cookie is being sent or turn off cookies through your browser. Check your browser's Help menu to find out how. If you reject cookies, you may still use our Site, but your ability to use some features may be limited.
Information Voluntarily Provided by Users
This Site collects personally identifiable information voluntarily provided by you, which may include your first name, last name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone (or mobile) number, fax number, and credit card information. Certain features may also require that you provide a user name and password, and we may also request demographic information, such as your age, gender, or interests, to help serve you better.
If you register to join our Rewards Program, we may collect your name, Zip Code, and email address, and you may be required to create a password. You may have the option of providing addition information, such as your full address, number of household members, date of birth, and dietary preferences. After you activate your program membership, we will automatically collect and maintain information about points you earn and redeem, purchase information and redemption history.
When you shop online, the Site collects information associated with the transaction, such as your name, mailing address, shipping address, products ordered, and credit card information. In addition, customers may have the option of registering certain information for future orders.
Use of Your Information
We use your information to: provide and personalize our products and services; communicate with you about products and services; administer our Rewards Program and promotions; fulfill orders; send you news, information, and offers; process job applications and surveys; respond to your inquiries and requests; and communicate with you for administrative or operational purposes.
We may combine the information we collect from you and/or supplement it with information that we obtain from public databases or third parties.
Disclosure of Your Information to Third Parties
If you participate in interactive features on this Site that permit you to submit or post user-generated content, any personally identifiable information provided by you may be visible to others. If you voluntarily post contact information, you may receive unsolicited communications from third parties.
We do not share or sell personally identifiable information with or to third parties for their marketing purposes without your consent. We do share personally identifiable information with third parties who perform services on our behalf. We will also share your information if you request or authorize it.
Your information may also be disclosed in exceptional circumstances, such as a merger, sale of assets, insolvency, bankruptcy or receivership, where Fresh & Easy or an affiliate is seeking financing, investment, support or funding, where necessary to comply with laws, subpoenas, warrants, orders, or legal requests, to enforce our Terms and Conditions, and to protect the rights, property, or safety of Fresh & Easy or others.
Account Access, Choice and Opt-Out
Rewards Program members can access their membership account by visiting the Program page. Other customers who have established an account online can also access their account information at our website. You may also inquire about your account by calling our telephoneÂ helpline at (877) 338-6546.
If you wish to correct any personally identifiable information maintained by Fresh & Easy, or change your contact information, you can contact us.
If you no longer wish to receive commercial e-mail communications from us, you may opt-out of receiving them by following the instructions included in each e-mail we send or by contacting us.
How We Protect Your Information
We employ reasonable security measures to protect your personally identifiable information and prevent unauthorized access, and we use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that our third party agents who make this Site and our services available and fulfill your orders agree to protect your information and honor your preferences about contacts you receive. No method of transmission or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, however.
Other Web Sites; Social Plugins
Your use of any linked site on our Sites is subject to the privacy policy imposed by the operator of the linked site.
Some of our Sites offer you the ability to interact with social plugins from social networks. Your use of social network plugins is subject to each network's privacy policy, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.
Changes to Privacy Policy
We may change, modify, add, or remove portions of this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will take effect upon posting, provided that the use of information previously collected will continue to be governed by the policy in effect when the information was collected.

PRIVACY POLICY
Information Collected Automatically
This Site logs IP addresses (a unique number assigned to every computer on the Internet) in order to improve security, analyze trends and administer the Site. To gauge the effectiveness of our Site, we do collect some generic information about our visitors. Our web servers automatically recognize a visitor's domain name, browser type, the web page from which a visitor enters our Site, which pages a visitor visits on our Site, access times, and how much time a visitor spends on each page. We only use this information in aggregate form, and do not link it to you personally.
Use of Cookies and Other Technologies
We and our analytics service partners may use "cookies," web beacons, and other technologies to collect, store and track information for analytical purposes, to improve our products and services, to improve our marketing and promotional activities, to tailor and personalize content, and to better understand your preferences and interests.
"Cookies" are small pieces of information sent by a web server to a web browser, which enables the server to collect information from the browser. Visitors to some of our Sites are sent anonymous cookies to keep track of their browsing patterns and build up a demographic profile. Sites that offer online shopping use cookies to remember and process items in your shopping cart.
We use session ID cookies and persistent cookies. We use session cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our Sites. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. Persistent cookies are used to customize the content of certain sections of the Site and enable us to track and target the interests of our users and to enhance the experience on our Site. In addition, any visitor who has clicked on an advertisement for this Site on another site that links to this Site will be sent a cookie that will allow us to analyze advertising effectiveness and manage our relationship with affiliate sites. Persistent cookies do not expire when you close your browser.
You can set your computer to warn you each time a cookie is being sent or turn off cookies (except flash cookies) through your browser (e.g., Internet Explorer or Google Chrome). Check your browser's Help menu to find out how. If you reject cookies, you may still use our Site, but your ability to use some features may be limited.
Some of our Web pages and e-mail updates may contain electronic images called clear gifs, web beacons, or pixel tags. On our Sites, clear gifs allow us to count visitors who have viewed our pages. In promotional e-mail messages or newsletters, they allow us to count how many subscribers have read them, which provides important statistical information about the articles, features or activities of most interest. They are not used to access personally identifiable information.
Information Voluntarily Provided by Users
This Site collects personally identifiable information voluntarily provided by you, which may include your first name, last name, mailing address, e-mail address, telephone (or mobile) number, fax number, credit card information, and other information when you use the services and features made available on the Sites.
Certain features on our Sites may require that you provide a user name and password. We may also request demographic information, such as your age, gender or interests, to help serve you better.
We collect this information when you register for contests, sweepstakes or other promotions, loyalty programs, and e-mail updates, news, offers, and information offered by us, search and/or apply for jobs online, make purchases, give us feedback, ask questions, fill out surveys, or submit content or comments to the Site.
If you register to join our Friends of Fresh & Easy Rewards Program we may collect your name, Zip Code, and email address, and you may be required to create a password. You may have the option of providing addition information, such as your full address, number of household members, date of birth for yourself or household members, dietary preferences or other information. After you activate your program membership we will automatically collect and maintain information about the points you earn and redeem. Fresh & Easy will also collect purchase information and redemption history.
When a customer puts an item in a shopping cart or places an order online, the Site collects information associated with the transaction, which may include the customer's name, mailing address, shipping address, products ordered, and credit card information. In addition, customers may have the option of registering certain information for future orders while completing their online order. As explained below, when you make purchases at the Site using a credit card, your credit card information is securely sent to and processed by a third party service provider.
Use of Your Information
We will use your information to provide and personalize our products and services, communicate with you about our products and services (including orders you have placed), respond to your inquiries and requests, send you news, information, coupons, and offers about Fresh & Easy's products and services that we think may be of interest to you, register you for sweepstakes, contests, and other promotions, process job applications, and fulfill orders for products.
We may combine the information we collect from you and/or supplement it with information that we obtain from public databases or third parties, in which case the combined information will be treated in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
We may use the information collected in connection with your Friends of Fresh & Easy Rewards Program membership to provide membership rewards to you and contact you about special programs and incentives offered to program members.
We may contact you by post, email, telephone, or other methods for these purposes. You may opt-out of receiving commercial communications from us as described below.
We like to hear your views to help us improve our service. From time to time, we may contact you to ask your opinions. If you do not want to be contacted for this purpose, please let us know.
Please note that there may be instances where it may be necessary for us to communicate with you, in any event, for administrative or operational reasons relating to our Sites or services, for example, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance, or in connection with an account or registration.
