Privacy Policy | FX Commander.com
All data collected from browsing this website is confidential and will not be disclosed to any 3rd party. We collect personal data that will help us improve our services and provide you with better content. Such data might be your IP, operation system, type of browser, time on site, etc. All the data collection isn’t linked personally to any visitor, it is used in order to better understand the needs of our visitors and better maintain our servers and uptime. If you choose to share your personal details with us, through registration, contact request or any other interaction, such details may be shared with our partners in order to offer you with better products and services.
Cookies:
Cookie definition: A small amount of data that a website puts on your computer or mobile device’s hard drive in order to identify the visitor’s online traffic flow for improving the experience on future visits.
We use cookies to track the behaviour in order to bring our visitors better offers and relevant content. If you do not wish to accept cookies you may turn off the option from your browser.
