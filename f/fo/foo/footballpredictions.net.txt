Privacy
European Union Visitors
Visitors from the European Union are protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) legislation.
At FootballPredictions.NET we have put in place a number of changes to make sure that, as a European Union located visitor, you are provided with adequate privacy settings as required by the GDPR.
We do not collect and store any personal information anywhere on our website through entry forms, registration forms, or any other form of data entry.
We have updated our Google Analytics settings to ensure that your IP address will not be stored by Google in a format that will make you personally identifiable (click here for more technical information about Google Analytics Anonymization)
We now provide you with the ability to add and remove your consent for us to use cookies (see below to update your consent).
What We Collect
We may collect the following information when you use our website:
Demographic information, namely the country from which you are viewing our website
Statistical information about the pages you are viewing, by use of Google Analytics
Preferences and settings - for example your odds style preference, mobile or desktop preference, and your consent (or not) for us to use cookies.
Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
How FootballPredictions.NET Uses Cookies
We use cookies to personalise content and advertisements to make our website relevant to you, and to analyse our traffic. We also use cookies to ensure that our website is legally compliant with laws and regulations in different countries and regions of the world. For example, we may be restricted from showing you links to certain sportsbooks where these companies do not have a licence to operate in your country.
What is a cookie?
A cookie is a small text file which is placed on your computer's hard drive when you visit websites.
Almost all websites use cookies. Cookies are used to help analyse web traffic, store your preferences, and track the path of your visit to a website from page to page to assist in caching and performance when you visit a particular site. Cookies allow websites to respond to you as an individual. The web site can tailor its operations to your needs, likes and dislikes by remembering information about your preferences.
FootballPredictions.NET uses cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us to improve our website in order to tailor it to your needs. We use this information for statistical analysis purposes, in particular Google Analytics.
Overall, cookies help us provide you with a better website, by enabling us to see which pages you find useful and which you do not. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any specific and personal information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us.
Your Agreement To Allow FootballPredictions.NET To Set Cookies
We are required by relevant laws and directives to inform you that FootballPredictions.NET uses cookies and we are required to gain your consent to allow us to place cookies on your device. This is why we have explicitly placed a message about the use of cookies on our website pages to direct you to this page to make you fully aware of how our website operates.
The law states that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of our website. For all other types of cookies we need your permission.
This site may use different types of cookies now, or in the future as defined by this policy. Some cookies may also be placed by third party services that appear on our pages.
Your consent applies to the following domain only: www.FootballPredictions.NET
What To Do If You Do Not Want Cookies
You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser, or for our website only. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies by default, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. If you would like to learn more about how to manage the acceptance of cookies on your own browser or device, please visit http://www.aboutcookies.org/default.aspx
