Privacy policy | foodora
We collect personal information from our customers in the regular course of doing business.
We collect the following personal information about you:
Name
Address
Postal Code
Phone Number
Email Address
When you visit our web site, we also collect:
Information about your computer, including your IP address, the type of operating system and browser you use,
and your computer's location
What pages you visit on our site and what links you click on
What other sites you've visited recently
The main reasons we collect personal information from you are:
To complete a sale/transaction
Delivery services
Customer service
Customer relationship management
Marketing
If it's a necessary part of any of these transactions, we may disclose your information to another company.
We also pass on your name, phone number, and address to a courier to complete a delivery.
Sharing of Personal Information with Third Parties
We do not share your personal information with any third parties.
