Privacy Policy - Foodie Home Chef
We promise you that any personal information you provide us here at FoodieHomeChef.com is safe, as we take the protection of your privacy very seriously. Furthermore, we do not sell, rent, trade or give out any information about you in any way to others.
When purchasing items on this website all your personal, banking & credit card information is collected by PayPal. Please refer to their privacy policy for information as to how your personal information is handled. We have no access to any information collected at PayPal other than your name, e-mail address & shipping information for the purpose of delivering your orders and as stated above we do not give this information out to anyone for any reason.
