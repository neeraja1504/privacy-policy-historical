ForexVerified
Information Collected
Email address / contact information
If you subscribe to our mailing list or purchase one of our services, be sure that we will never, under any circumstances share your email address or other contact information with any third parties. We never share, rent, sell, or abuse our clients contact information.
Payment information
If you purchase one of our services, you will pay using a Visa, Mastercard, or Discover card. We do collect your Name, Billing Address, the Exp date and only the last four digits of your credit/debit card. Neither the owner of Forex Verified nor any of our employees have access to your full credit card number. The information that we do collect is only used to help troubleshoot any billing issues you may have. No payment information is ever shared, rented, sold, or abused by Forex Verified. Your full credit card number is pushed immediately and solely to the Authorize.net payment gateway and is not collected by Forex Verified.
