Call Center | BPO | Business Process Outsourcing | Customer Care
Fusion BPO Services is committed to protecting your privacy as well as developing technology for offering you an exceptional online experience. This Statement of Privacy is applicable to the Fusion BPO Services website and governs data collection and usage. By using Fusion’s website, you consent to the data practices mentioned in this statement.
Collecting Personal Information:
Fusion BPO Services collects personally identifiable information, such as your name, e-mail address, home/work station address, or telephone number. Fusion also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age, gender, ZIP code, preferences, interests and favorites.
Information is also provided about your computer hardware and software, which is automatically collected by Fusion BPO Services. These details may include your IP address, browser type, domain names, timings of access, and website addresses for referral. This information is used by Fusion BPO Services for operations, and for maintaining the quality of service, and for providing general statistics regarding use of the Fusion website.
Note that if personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data is disclosed by you through Fusion‘s public message boards, it can be used by others. Keep in mind that Fusion BPO Services does not read any of your private online communication.
Fusion BPO Services motivates you in reviewing the privacy statements of websites that you choose to link to from Fusion, so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Fusion is not responsible for the privacy statements or any other content on websites outside Fusion BPO Services’ family of websites.
Using your Personal Information:
Fusion BPO Services collects and uses your personal information to operate the Fusion website and deliver services you have requested for. Fusion also uses personally identifiable information to inform you of other products/services available from Fusion and its affiliates. Fusion BPO Services may also contact you via surveys for conduct research on your opinion about current services or potential new services that might be offered.
Fusion BPO Services does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Fusion may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In these cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Fusion may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except for providing services to Fusion and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
