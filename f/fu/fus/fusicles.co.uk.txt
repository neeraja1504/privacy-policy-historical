Fusicles - Free Article Submission encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Fusicles - Free Article Submission so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Use of your Personal Information

Fusicles - Free Article Submission does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Fusicles - Free Article Submission may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. Fusicles - Free Article Submission does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.
Fusicles - Free Article Submission keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Fusicles - Free Article Submission, in order to determine what Fusicles - Free Article Submission services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Fusicles - Free Article Submission to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Use of Cookies
The Fusicles - Free Article Submission Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Fusicles - Free Article Submission pages, or register with Fusicles - Free Article Submission site or services, a cookie helps Fusicles - Free Article Submission to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Fusicles - Free Article Submission Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Fusicles - Free Article Submission features that you customized.
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Fusicles - Free Article Submission services or Web sites you visit.
Security of your Personal Information
Fusicles - Free Article Submission secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Fusicles - Free Article Submission secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
