Privacy Statement
No information is sold or released to anyone about visitors to  FunnyPart.com without prior consent or a court order.  In other words, no information is released to anyone unless you give permission first.
FunnyPart.com does not attempt to collect personal information from children. Note that  FunnyPart.com cannot always determine that a user is a child.   FunnyPart.com does not maintain databases of children or adults.
When visiting any Internet site, your unique address called an "IP address" is recorded.   FunnyPart.com does not release any information about the collection of this address to any third party except under court order.  FunnyPart.com archives the log files in order to create aggregate statistical reports, detect errors at the web site,  and for security reasons.  The log files are eventually deleted.
IP reports for advertisement clicks are shared with the advertiser.  However, they usually have this information already since it is captured by their server when you view/click an ad.  This is done to verify billing for the ads.  No additional information associated with any specific user is provided to the advertisers.
Over View
Your privacy is important to us. FunnyPart.com service is free for all to use, and prior registration is not needed for use of FunnyPart.com
FunnyPart.com will explicitly ask when it needs information that personally identifies you. All the personally identifiable information we collect are only gathered with your voluntary act. FunnyPart.com keeps all the personal information private and does not, and will not, share it with any third parties except under these circumstances.
When such action is necessary to conform to legal requirements or comply with legal process
When such action is necessary to protect and defend rights or property of FunnyPart.com
When such action is necessary to enforce FunnyPart.com terms of service
FunnyPart.com  uses cookies as part of tracking process for advertisement banners so you do not see the same ad repeatedly, but cookies will not be used to personally identify you without your prior consent.
If any of the section of FunnyPart.com include your work or you own it, then contact FunnyPart.com and an action will be taken right away from there.
