Privacy Policy - FunShun.com
We at FunShun are responsible to keep your privacy and understand your requirement for protection of information you provide us and which can identify you.
According to our privacy policy:
No information, about you, will be obtained without your permission and notice.
Your identifiable information will not be disclosed anywhere and any time until you allow.
In case, you are our valued client, prior permission will be taken from you to include your identifiable information in the list of our valued clients on this website.
Unwanted emails will not be delivered to you.
We may set and access FunShun Forum cookies on your computer.
FunShun lets other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.
You can edit your FunShun Forum Account Information at any time.
Questions and Suggestions: If you have questions or suggestions, please complete a feedback form or you can contact us.
Regards
