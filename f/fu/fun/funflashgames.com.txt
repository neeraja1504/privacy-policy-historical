Privacy Policy
We are glad of your interest in http://www.funflashgames.com site. We have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
We can be reached via e-mail at webmaster@funny-spot.com
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no information regarding the domain or e-mail address.
With respect to cookies :
We use cookies to record past activity at a site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site so they will have fasting loading and better quality at the time of coming back again.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending us e-mail at or webmaster emaill address that is webmaster@funny-spot.com
With respect to Ad Servers :
To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information to have a demographic information of what kind of people and from what country watches their ads. For further information, consult the privacy policies of companies such as:
http://www.advertising.com , http://www.fastclick.com and others.
We will not give any of your info away to anyone starting for your email addresses.
With respect to security :
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
With respect to links :
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we at FunFlashGames.com are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other web sites.
