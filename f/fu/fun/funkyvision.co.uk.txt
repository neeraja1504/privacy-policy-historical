Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
This privacy policy sets out how we (Funky Vision) use and protects any information that you give when you use our website.
We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected, should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy statement.
We have the right to change this privacy policy statement at any time without any notice, it is your responsibility to check this privacy policy statement to ensure you are happy with any changes that we may of made.
You can request the details of personal information which we have about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. A small administration fee will be payable to collect this data. If you would like a copy of the information about you use our contact form quoting DP1998.
Data Collected
Some of the services we provide may collect some information about you.
First & Last Name
Your Valid Email Address
IP & ISP Address Information
We collect the above information when you use some of our free webmaster services, this data will never be passed on or sold unless it becomes a legal issue..
