Privacy Policy for  fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at brokwebb@gmail.com.
Atfulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by YOURWEBSITE.com and how it is used.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies and Web Beacons
fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com does not use cookies.
DoubleClick DART Cookie
.:: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com.
.:: Google’s use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to WaysToAvoidScamsOnline.com and other sites on the Internet.
.:: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL – http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.
You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. WaysToAvoidScamsOnline.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.
You can opt out of all communication with  fulltimejobfromhome.siterubix.com simply be leaving this page.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers’ respective websites.
