Cookies
Some ads displayed may come from our servers. Ads loading from our servers may also contain cookies, the purpose of these cookies
is just to introduce and select the proper advertisements to be shown for your interests. We only have access to information of
visiting frequencies of our visitors from cookies you might receive while viewing ads from our servers.
A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows Filma24.TV to recognize you each time you visit this site(customisation etc).
Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Filma24.TV does not use cookies to collect personal information.
Cookies may also be used by 3rd party content providers such as newsfeeds.
