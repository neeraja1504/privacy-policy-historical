FileZigZag | Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Logging IP Addresses
For each visitor to our web page, our web server does not recognize any information regarding the domain or email address. IP addresses are logged and used by filezigzag for measuring usage.
Collecting Email Addresses
We collect the email addresses of filezigzag recipients and senders for the purpose of logging and measuring usage. filezigzag does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with non-affiliated companies.
Certain Exceptional Disclosures
We may disclose your information if necessary to protect our legal rights or if the information relates to actual or threatened harmful conduct or potential threats to the physical safety of any person. Disclosure may be required by law or if we receive legal processes.
Use of Cookies
filezigzag uses cookies to store the preferences of users, only if they so choose.
File Transfer Security
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Files stored for delivery are only accessible by filezigzag and through the clickable link generated for your recipient. All files stored for delivery are deleted when they expire.
Third-Party Advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our site. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other web sites in order to provide advertisements on this site and other sites about goods and services that may be of interest to you.
Third-Party Cookies
In the course of serving advertisements to this site, our third-party advertisers may place or recognize a unique "cookie" on your browser.
