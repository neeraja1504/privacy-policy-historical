FileHook - Earn More Money With File Hook
Privacy Policy of FileHook:
1. Information we collect
The only personal information FileHook collects and requires is an email address and payment information/
2. Use of information
FileHook will only use your email address for sending out essential news and updates regarding FileHook's services. Payment information will only be used to send payments.
3. Disclosure to others
FileHook will not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. FileHook may release account information when we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to (i) comply with law, (ii) enforce or apply the terms of any of our user agreements.
