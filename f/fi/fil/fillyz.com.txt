Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining it. When you shop on our shopping store, we will ask you to input your following information:
- Personal information
- Your interests and preferences.
- Your contact information like email, postal address, phone/mobile etc.
- Your log of visit Fillyz.com
- Your testimonials, opinions or any information you supply to us additionally by participating in offers/contests/surveys etc.
We recognize your right to confidentiality and are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not furnish any identifiable information at the individual level regarding its customers to any third party. The information you give to us is held with the utmost care and security. This information is collected for following reasons:
- To keep serving you related products and services based on your subject of interests.
- To keep in regular communication with you before/during/after your purchase with us.
- For shipping and mailing purpose.
- To enhance Fillyz.com and make it more easy and useful to you, by studying your uses of Fillyz.com and make it more easy to use and painless experience shopping with us.
What we do with this information?
- We keep your information safe with our company records and use it to study the interests and preferences of our customers to make your shopping experience with Fillyz.com more cheerful.
- All contact / personal preferences information used to keep you updated with latest offers / promotions or in case of any issues with your order with us.
What we don't do with this information?
- We don’t sell/exchange/barter/handover/supply this information to any of the third party, until otherwise permission from you.
- We don’t SPAM using your contact details in any irritating behavior.
We use 'Cookies' to keep track of your current shopping session to personalize your experience and so that you may retrieve your shopping cart at any time. 'Cookies' are tiny text files which our Website places on your computer's hard drive to store information about your shopping session and to identify your computer. Cookies cannot harm your computer and do not contain any personal or private information.
We do not hold any of your credit card or any other payment option information. This information is securely collected by our authorized payment processors. These companies are very well reputed themselves and millions of people worldwide trust their services. These companies are: Paypal, CC Avenue etc.
For query regarding the privacy policy, you may Contact Us.
