Last Updated As Of December 9, 2011
This privacy policy (the "Policy") covers how A.V.M. Software Inc. (together with its affiliates, "we" "our" or "us") treat the information that we collect and receive when you subscribe for and use the Firetalkâ„¢ services (the "Firetalk Services") offered at www.firetalk.com. This Policy explains the practices that apply to information ("Personal Information") that you provide to us that can be used to easily identify or contact you or others personally.
Collection of Your Personal Information
In order to provide the Firetalk Services, we must collect your phone number so we can identify you when you interact with our system, and to route telephone calls from other users to your phone. We also obtain a copy of your entire contact list, which we use to provide the Firetalk Services to you and to identify relationships between users in your contact list and other Firetalk users. Never, under any circumstances, do we reveal your contact list to other Firetalk users. As with regular phone calls, your telephone number is revealed to people you call.
How Your Personal Information Is Used
Your Personal Information is used to operate the Firetalk Services' features and offerings, to personalize the Firetalk Services provided to you, and to communicate with you and respond to your inquiries.
Your Personal Information will never be shared with third parties except to provide the Firetalk Services that you have requested.
Firetalk is not to be used for illegal activity. In the event that legal authorities deem it necessary to investigate a Firetalk user's activity, by way of legal processes (court order, search warrant or subpoena), we will comply as required by law.
We may use cookies, web beacons or similar technologies to enhance and personalize your experience with the Firetalk Services. This includes helping authenticate your identity when you login to Firetalk; remembering your preferences and registration information; and measuring and researching the effectiveness of the Firetalk Services and promotions.
In the event Firetalk is acquired, consolidates or merges with another company, we will share your Personal Information with the acquiring company, but only so long as that company agrees to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Policy.
Our Commitment to Security
We are committed to keeping your Personal Information private, and will use that information only for purposes of operating Firetalk Services and improving them. We have implemented security guidelines to help safeguard the privacy of your Personal Information from unauthorized access or improper use.
How to Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about the Firetalk Privacy Policy or its implementation, you can send email to info@firetalk.com
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may change or revise this Policy at any time, for any reason, and if you use or access the Firetalk Services at any time on or after the "Last Updated As Of" date set forth above, you are deemed to have been notified of, and your use or access of the Firetalk Services after that date signifies your acceptance of, any changes or revisions arising on or before that date. For this reason, please visit this page on a regular basis to ensure that you are familiar with the most recent version of this Policy.
This Policy is effective as of the "Last Updated As Of" date set forth above. This Policy does not apply to any other website that you may be able to access from this website, which may have its own data collection and use practices and policies.
Please review this Policy periodically at www.firetalk.com. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
