Site Rules
Simply put: be nice, no cheating!
FlyOrDie is a friendly online community for those who love play! Please be on your best behavior as you play and interact with others. Failure to follow these rules will result in your account being closed.
User interaction
Without respect toward others, no online community can function properly. Please be aware you are entering a community comprised of different age groups, heritages, races, social levels and educational backgrounds. There should be no abusive language, personal attacks, threats, or any other unkind behavior.
Keep it clean
You may not post any offensive content on FlyOrDie including your user name, avatar, or personal description. You may not add any offensive comments, chat, or other content. The following behavior is strictly forbidden and can result in your being kicked or banned: cheating, using offensive/vulgar language, making personal attacks, spamming or advertising competitive websites, religious or political debate, intentionally pointless/distracting comments, disclosing other's personal data without consent, attempt to solicit account information from other players, and discussion of illegal activities (drugs, etc).
No cheating
Any form of unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated, FlyOrDie reserves the right to reset points to zero and/or ban your nickname for this offense. It includes artificially adjusting your rating, encouraging your opponent to leave the match, intentionally losing, accepting your opponent's behavior who is intentionally losing, using other's account, using more than one nicknames.
Engines/programs
FlyOrDie members have the right to know if they are playing against a computer. Therefore FlyOrDie uses a system - including analysis of client computing environment - to detect suspected program use, which will be indicated at concerned player's nick with a small [!] icon.
Only one account
Members may only have ONE FlyOrDie.com account, unless explicitly allowed to have more. Members who create more than one account may have all accounts closed.

Personal data we collect
FlyOrDie collects data to operate effectively and provide you the best experiences with our products. You provide some of this data directly, such as when you create a FlyOrDie account, participate in various activities on FlyOrDie such as chat, posting messages in the forum or contact us for support.
We get some of it by recording how you interact with our products by, for example, using technologies like cookies, and using passive tracking technologies to log information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and website usage.
There are basically three levels of users using FlyOrDie:
simple visitors, or guests, who use the services without registering
registered users
subscribed members
as a guest you do not provide any information directly to us, but we might collect some data while you interact with our services. The data we might collect from guests are IP address, browser types, time and date at each login. We use these data to analyse trends and create statistical records.
as a registered user you have to provide some data when signing up. These include your email address, a username and password. These data are collected to protect your user account from unauthorized access. While using our game you also have the option to provide your age, gender and country, additionally you have the option to make these three data public to everyone who visits our site, or keep them private. These data are collected in order to make your experience more enjoyable
Any message you send in the chat rooms, or post in the forum will be public and will be logged. Any match results, tournament results, forum posts are public, and cannot be deleted.
Besides these data that you provide, we also collect some additional data when signing up for or while playing our games. These data include IP addresses, browser types, time and date of any interaction with our service, the amount of time you spend on our game, moderator notes, moderation history, your game results and statistics for each game you play. The aim to collect these data is fraud detection/prevention.
as a subscribed member you also have to provide your billing address and name, which we need due to legal obligations. The data mentioned in section (b) refer to subscribed members as well.
In case of credit card payments, your credit card information is not revealed to us, it is processed by our payment processing partners.

Cookies
This site, like many others, uses small files called cookies to help us customise your experience.
What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored by the browser (for example, Internet Explorer or Safari) on your computer or mobile phone. They allow websites to store things like user preferences. You can think of cookies as providing a ‘memory’ for the website, so that it can recognise you when you come back and respond appropriately.
How does FlyOrDie website use cookies?
A visit to a page on FlyOrDie website may generate the following types of cookie:
Site performance cookies
Anonymous analytics cookies
Registration cookies
Third party advertising cookies
Site performance cookies
This type of cookie remembers your preferences for tools found on the FlyOrDie’s website, so you don’t have to re-set them each time you visit. Examples include: your favourite games are remembered so you can start them easily.
Anonymous analytics cookies
Every time someone visits our website, software provided by another organisation generates an “anonymous analytics cookie”. These cookies can tell us whether or not you have visited the site before. Your browser will tell us if you have these cookies and, if you don’t, we generate new ones. This allows us to track how many individual users we have, and how often they visit the site. We use these data to gather statistics, for example, the number of visits to a page.
Registration cookies
When you register with FlyOrDie, we generate cookies that let us know whether you are signed in or not. Our servers use these cookies to work out which account you are signed in with, and if you are allowed access to a particular service.
Third party advertising cookies
Ads keep our content FREE for everyone. We use additional third-party advertising companies who serve ads to help keep content free.  These third party vendors, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. The use of advertising cookies enables third party vendors and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. The companies that generate these cookies have their own privacy policies, and we have no access to read or write these cookies. These organizations may use their cookies to anonymously target advertising to you on other websites, based on your visit to FlyOrDie.

On some pages of our website, other organisations may also set their own anonymous cookies. They do this to track the success of their application, or to customise the application for you. Requesting to delete your personal data
You have the right to ask us to delete your personal data at any time. We have the right to refuse the request where we have legal obligations, or legitimate interest to keep them, or we are unable to identify you.
By deleting your account on the game settings page, your personal data will be made unavailable, but they will be kept in order to enable us investigating any illegal or suspicious activities. Your personal data will be deleted one year after being marked for deletion.
By disabling your account (this can be done from the original registration email, when someone else used your email address unauthorised), your personal data will be made unavailable, but they will be kept in order to enable us investigating any illegal or suspicious activities. Your personal data will be deleted one year after being marked for disabling. This will also prevent creating a new account using your email address. Your email address will be stored in an unidentifiable way for this purpose.
