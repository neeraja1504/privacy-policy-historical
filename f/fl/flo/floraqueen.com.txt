Like other commercial websites, the FloraQueen website utilizes Web server logs to collect information regarding how our website is used. Information gathered through cookies and web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the pages viewed, time spent at our website, and the websites visited just prior to and after our website. Cookies are tiny text files that are stored on your computer when you visit our website. Thanks to the information stored in cookies, your preferences are remembered when you return to the website and you thereby enjoy a better browsing experience.
If you prefer to disable cookies, you can do so by modifying your browser settings. However, this may prevent some parts of our site from functioning properly, as they play an essential role in the provision of our services.
If you do not enable cookies on your computer, access may be denied to some parts of the site and your experience on our website may be limited in some areas:
- When you log into your account
- Content on the website customised to your preferences
- A record of your tastes and interests on your next visit
Because you will be able to enjoy many of the site’s essential functions, enhancing your experience on our website. For instance, in the customised recommendations according to your tastes, access to exclusive areas such as your account, keeping a record of the products stored in your basket and improving security by preventing fraudulent activities.
Accepting cookies optimises browsing; therefore, most browsers are already configured to accept them by default. However, if you prefer, you can change this setting. Please see the details in your browser settings.
