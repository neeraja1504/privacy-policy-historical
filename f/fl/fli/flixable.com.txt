Privacy Policy | Flixable
Cookies
In order to provide our service, we may place cookies on you as well as partner with third parties including advertising partners, who may use cookies or other similar technologies to provide you with advertising based on your browsing activities and interests. We use cookies to store information about your preferences and to customize customize page content based on information that you send via your browser.
Advertising Partners
Our advertising partners include:
Google AdSense
We have no access to or control over cookies that are used by our advertising partners. You can consult the respective privacy policies of our advertising partners for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your browser options.
Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to understand how our site is used in order to improve the user experience. You can read more about how Google collects and protects data by clicking here.
Logs
We receive and record information from your browser when you visit our site, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address. This information is stored in secure logs and is collected automatically. We use this information to analyze and understand how our site is used and to optimize such usage.
