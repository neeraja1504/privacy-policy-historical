PRIVACY POLICY
FluxAdvertising ("FluxAds") is a property of TrekEight, LLC, a California Limited Liability Company.
Registering for FluxAds
Signing up for a FluxAds affiliate account requires signing up with your name, email address, company name, web address, business phone and business address.
We gather this information to provide tracking and access for campaigns you are running or have run, to enable us to track your performance over time, and to process payments for monies you have earned. By registering with FluxAds you will be entered into the DirectTrack affiliate tracking system.
We collect personally identifiable information, like name, company, postal address, telephone number, email address, etc. when voluntarily submitted. If at any time you wish to opt out of any communication or to be deleted from our database, simply contact us at: support@fluxads.com
Personally Identifiable Data
New affiliates will sign up for FluxAds from links on our Web site or directly through a FluxAds representative. All access to your account is secure and password protected. FluxAds holds all data gathered in strict confidence. Your email address, mailing address, phone number or other personally identifiable information will never be sold to, supplied to, or otherwise made available to any third party.
Questions regarding this statement should be directed to: support@fluxads.com
