Privacy Policy
Fatboy the original B.V. (Fatboy) will not collect, process or use any personal information without your prior consent. This website does collect statistic information that is important for improving the website, however, this information cannot be lead to any personal information.
Fatboy will only use your personal information to send you information or process your order from the Fatboy shop. Fatboy will not share any personal financial information you provide (such as credit card information) with other companies, other than companies affiliated to Fatboy.
Fatboy takes a great effort to protect your personal information and uses security techniques such as servers and firewalls.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, or the personal information you have submitted to us, please feel free to contact us.
